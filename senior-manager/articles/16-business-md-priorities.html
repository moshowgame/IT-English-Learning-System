Business MD Conversation on IT Priorities — Portfolio Trade-Off, Capacity, Regulator vs Revenue

Article 16 · Module 4: Cross-Function Alignment · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Business MD conversation, portfolio trade-off, capacity, regulator vs revenue, regret analysis

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 10:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You have a 60-minute Business MD 1:1 with Rachel (Group Head of Retail Banking). The agenda: the IT portfolio trade-off for next year, capacity vs demand, regulator-mandated work vs revenue-generating work, and a regret analysis on the digital onboarding programme. Rachel wants more IT capacity for a new mobile investment feature, but the cloud migration and regulator cap are absorbing 60% of the IT portfolio. You need to defend the trade-off, propose a path for the mobile feature, and protect the regulator cap.

周五 10:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你与Rachel（集团零售银行负责人）有 60 分钟业务 MD 1:1。议程：明年 IT 组合权衡、容量 vs 需求、监管驱动 vs 创收、数字开户项目群的遗憾分析。Rachel 希望为新的移动投资功能争取更多 IT 容量，但云迁移和监管 cap 吸收了 60% IT 组合。你需要捍卫权衡、为移动功能提出路径、保护监管 cap。

A Business MD 1:1 is the portfolio trade-off conversation. The pattern: portfolio first, capacity second, regulator vs revenue third, regret analysis fourth, decision last. Speak the MD's language: customer impact, revenue, NPS, regret.

业务 MD 1:1 是组合权衡对话。模式：组合第一、容量第二、监管 vs 收入第三、遗憾分析第四、决策最后。说 MD 的语言：客户影响、收入、NPS、遗憾。

2. The Business MD 1:1 — 4 Sections, 60 Minutes

Rachel (MD Retail): Wei, the new mobile investment feature. It's a $14M revenue opportunity next year, and we've been told it has to wait until Q3. I need it in Q1. Walk me through the trade.Wei，新的移动投资功能。这是明年 1,400 万收入机会，我们被告知要等到 Q3。我需要 Q1 上。讲讲交易。
You (CIO): Portfolio today. Total IT project FTE: 312. Regulator cap: 96 FTE (31%) — HKMA, MAS, GDPR, NYDFS. Cloud migration: 92 FTE (29%). Run cost and incident: 38 FTE (12%). Other: 86 FTE (28%) — split across 14 initiatives. Mobile investment feature: 14 FTE for 6 months = 7 average FTE, 2% of portfolio. Capacity for Q1: 0 FTE, all 78 average FTE allocated.当前组合。IT 项目 FTE 总计：312。监管 cap：96 FTE（31%）——HKMA、MAS、GDPR、NYDFS。云迁移：92 FTE（29%）。运行成本和事件：38 FTE（12%）。其他：86 FTE（28%）——分布在 14 个项目。移动投资功能：6 个月 14 FTE = 年均 7，组合 2%。Q1 容量：0 FTE，78 平均 FTE 全部已分配。
You (CIO): Regulator vs revenue. Regulator cap of 96 FTE is not negotiable — by definition. If we cut regulator FTE, we miss HKMA Cyber Resilience, MAS TRM, and the supervisor calls. Cloud migration 92 FTE is not negotiable for 18 months — we have committed exit cost and two-cloud direction. Run cost 38 FTE is not negotiable — it's the operating model. The 86 FTE 'other' is the only place to trade.监管 vs 收入。96 FTE 监管 cap 不容商量——按定义。如果我们削减监管 FTE，会错过 HKMA 网络韧性、MAS TRM，supervisor 会来电话。云迁移 92 FTE 18 个月内不容商量——我们有承诺的退出成本和双云方向。运行成本 38 FTE 不容商量——这是运营模型。86 FTE"其他"是唯一可交易的地方。
You (CIO): Regret analysis on the 86 FTE. Across 14 initiatives: 3 are run-rate, can pause, recover in 60 days. 4 are in-flight, can defer by 90 days, recover in 30 days. 4 are vendor-dependent, can renegotiate scope, save 8 FTE. 3 are non-recoverable, will not defer. Recoverable FTE for Q1: 14. Your mobile investment feature needs 7 average FTE. Net: it can be in Q1, if we defer 2 in-flight initiatives by 90 days and renegotiate 1 vendor scope.86 FTE 遗憾分析。14 个项目：3 个是运行率，可暂停，60 天恢复。4 个进行中，可推迟 90 天，30 天恢复。4 个依赖厂商，可重谈范围，节省 8 FTE。3 个不可恢复，不会推迟。Q1 可恢复 FTE：14。你的移动投资功能需要 7 平均 FTE。净：可以 Q1 上，前提是 2 个进行中项目推迟 90 天、1 个厂商范围重谈。
You (CIO): Two names, Rachel. Initiative 1 to defer: the regional CRM replacement (90 days, in-flight, recovery in 30). Initiative 2 to defer: the e-statements redesign (90 days, in-flight, recovery in 30). Vendor renegotiation: the data lake contract with Vendor X, scope cut 30%, save 4 FTE for 6 months. Trade approved in principle at the portfolio SteerCo last month. Two trade-offs: CRM is your regional MD's flagship, e-statements has a Q2 customer NPS commitment. Both are recoverable. Recommendation: approve mobile investment feature for Q1, with named deferrals and named vendor renegotiation.Rachel，两个具名。推迟项目 1：区域 CRM 替换（90 天、进行中、30 天恢复）。推迟项目 2：电子对账单重设计（90 天、进行中、30 天恢复）。厂商重谈：与厂商 X 的数据湖合同，范围削减 30%，6 个月节省 4 FTE。交易上个月组合 SteerCo 原则批准。两个 trade-off：CRM 是你区域 MD 的旗舰，电子对账单有 Q2 客户 NPS 承诺。两者都可恢复。推荐：批准移动投资功能 Q1 上，附具名推迟和具名厂商重谈。
Rachel (MD Retail): Rachel: Q1 mobile investment feature approved. I'll talk to my regional MD about the CRM defer, and to the Q2 NPS owner about e-statements. One condition: monthly FTE burn-down at our 1:1 until the 7 FTE are net-additive, not reallocated from the deferred initiatives.Rachel：Q1 移动投资功能批准。我会与我的区域 MD 谈 CRM 推迟，与 Q2 NPS 负责人谈电子对账单。一个条件：每月 FTE 消耗在我 1:1 上评审，直到 7 FTE 净新增，不是从推迟项目重分配。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Portfolio today, FTE by category Total IT project FTE: 312. Regulator cap 96, cloud 92, run 38, other 86. IT 项目 FTE 总计 312。监管 96、云 92、运行 38、其他 86 Portfolio is FTE by category, not initiative. The MD buys the category breakdown. Always name the 4 categories.
  • Not negotiable, by definition Regulator cap of 96 FTE is not negotiable — by definition. 96 FTE 监管 cap 不容商量——按定义 "By definition" is the deal term for regulator cap. The MD knows it; the phrase moves the conversation past debate.
  • Regret analysis, recoverable vs not Regret analysis: 3 run-rate, 4 in-flight, 4 vendor-dependent, 3 non-recoverable. 遗憾分析：3 运行率、4 进行中、4 厂商依赖、3 不可恢复 Regret analysis separates recoverable from not. The MD buys the recoverable count. Always name the four buckets.
  • Named deferrals, named renegotiation Two named deferrals: CRM, e-statements. One named vendor renegotiation: Vendor X. 两个具名推迟：CRM、电子对账单。一个具名厂商重谈：厂商 X Trade-offs are named, not abstract. "We'll defer some things" loses; "CRM, e-statements" wins. The MD owns the named deferrals.
  • Net-additive, not reallocated 7 FTE net-additive, not reallocated from the deferred initiatives. 7 FTE 净新增，不是从推迟项目重分配 "Net-additive" is the MD's deal term. The MD is OK with deferrals if the new feature is truly net-additive, not a re-allocation.
  • Monthly FTE burn-down Monthly FTE burn-down at our 1:1 until the 7 FTE are net-additive. 每月 FTE 消耗在我 1:1 上评审 "FTE burn-down" is the MD's metric. Pair monthly review with the named condition (net-additive).

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Portfolio n. 组合 The set of all IT projects and their FTE allocation.
  • FTE by category n. 按类别 FTE FTE split by category: regulator, cloud, run, other.
  • Regulator cap n. 监管 cap The FTE reserved for regulator-mandated work.
  • Run cost n. 运行成本 The cost of running the existing systems.
  • Regret analysis n. 遗憾分析 A review of which projects can be deferred without permanent loss.
  • Recoverable n. 可恢复 A project that can resume within 30-60 days of restart.
  • Non-recoverable n. 不可恢复 A project that, if deferred, will lose its window entirely.
  • Vendor renegotiation n. 厂商重谈 Renegotiating scope or price with a vendor.
  • Net-additive FTE n. 净新增 FTE FTE that is genuinely new, not reallocated from elsewhere.
  • FTE burn-down n. FTE 消耗 The monthly tracking of FTE consumption against plan.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Not negotiable, by definition" — the regulator cap deal term

Regulator cap is always not negotiable, by definition. The MD knows it; the phrase moves past debate. "We need to protect the regulator" is opinion; "by definition" is the deal term.

监管 cap 总是 不容商量，按定义。

② "Regret analysis" — the four-bucket frame

Regret analysis separates projects into four buckets: run-rate, in-flight, vendor-dependent, non-recoverable. The MD buys the recoverable count. Always name the four buckets and the recoverable FTE.

遗憾分析将项目分为四类：运行率、进行中、厂商依赖、不可恢复。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Portfolio is FTE by category, not initiative. The MD buys the category breakdown. Always name the 4 categories: regulator, cloud, run, other.
  • "Not negotiable, by definition" for regulator cap. The phrase moves past debate. The MD knows regulator cap is sacred.
  • Regret analysis, four buckets. Run-rate, in-flight, vendor-dependent, non-recoverable. The MD buys the recoverable count.
  • Named deferrals, named renegotiation. "We'll defer some things" loses; "CRM, e-statements, Vendor X" wins. The MD owns the names.
  • Net-additive is the MD's deal term. The MD is OK with deferrals if the new feature is truly net-additive, not reallocated. Pair net-additive with monthly FTE burn-down.
  • Trade-offs are recoverable. The MD buys deferrals when recovery time is named. "30 days" is the deal term.
  • End with a monthly review condition. The MD closes with a date, not a feeling. "Monthly FTE burn-down" is the named condition.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the regret analysis

Translate the regret analysis.

"Regret analysis on the 86 FTE. Across 14 initiatives: 3 are run-rate, can pause, recover in 60 days. 4 are in-flight, can defer by 90 days, recover in 30 days. 4 are vendor-dependent, can renegotiate scope, save 8 FTE. 3 are non-recoverable, will not defer. Recoverable FTE for Q1: 14."

"86 FTE 遗憾分析。14 个项目：3 个是运行率，可暂停，60 天恢复。4 个进行中，可推迟 90 天，30 天恢复。4 个依赖厂商，可重谈范围，节省 8 FTE。3 个不可恢复，不会推迟。Q1 可恢复 FTE：14。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the MD mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a Business MD 1:1? Why?

  1. "We'll see what we can do."
  2. "Portfolio today: regulator 96, cloud 92, run 38, other 86. Regulator cap not negotiable, by definition."
  3. "Regret analysis: 3 run-rate, 4 in-flight, 4 vendor-dependent, 3 non-recoverable. Recoverable 14 FTE."
  4. "Two named deferrals: CRM, e-statements. Net-additive, not reallocated. Monthly FTE burn-down at our 1:1."

① is the credibility-damager. "We'll see what we can do" is not a portfolio answer. ② names the four categories and protects regulator cap. ③ names the four buckets and the recoverable count. ④ names the deferrals and the net-additive condition. MD 1:1s run on FTE categories, regret analysis, and named deferrals — not on "we'll see".

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute MD trade

You are the CIO. MD wants $8M AI customer-service feature in Q1. Portfolio: regulator 96, cloud 92, run 38, other 86. Recoverable 14 FTE. AI feature needs 6 average FTE. Pitch.

"Portfolio today. Regulator cap 96 FTE, not negotiable, by definition. Cloud 92 FTE, committed. Run 38 FTE, operating model. Other 86 FTE, the only place to trade. Regret analysis: 3 run-rate (recoverable in 60 days), 4 in-flight (recoverable in 30 days from 90-day defer), 4 vendor-dependent (renegotiable, save 8 FTE), 3 non-recoverable. Recoverable FTE for Q1: 14. AI feature needs 6 average FTE. Net: it can be in Q1, if we defer 2 in-flight initiatives (named: regional dashboard rebuild, branch analytics refresh) and renegotiate 1 vendor scope (named: Vendor Y, 30% scope cut, save 4 FTE). Trade-offs: regional dashboard is a regional MD flagship; branch analytics has Q2 NPS commitment; both recoverable. Net-additive, not reallocated. Monthly FTE burn-down at our 1:1."
