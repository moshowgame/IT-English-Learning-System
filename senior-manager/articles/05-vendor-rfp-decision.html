Major Vendor Selection (RFP) — Shortlist, Scoring, TCO, Decision Memo

Article 05 · Module 2: Strategic Decisions · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 RFP shortlist, weighted scoring, TCO, decision memo, board endorsement

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 14:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You chair the Vendor Selection Committee for a $42M, 5-year Core Banking Modernisation platform. The RFP closed 6 weeks ago with 7 bidders. After Group Procurement's mandatory pre-screening (financial stability, sanctions, Group-approved panel), 4 bidders are on the shortlist: Vendor A (global incumbent), Vendor B (cloud-native challenger), Vendor C (regional mid-tier), Vendor D (open-source-led boutique). Present: Group CFO (capex approver), Group CRO (cyber approver), Group Procurement, Group Legal, and the Workstream Lead. One decision: select the recommended vendor, approve the TCO envelope, and authorise entry into contract negotiation.

周四 14:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你主持 4,200 万美元、5 年期核心银行现代化平台的厂商选型委员会。RFP 6 周前结束，共 7 家投标。经集团采购部强制预筛（财务稳健、制裁、集团合格 panel），4 家进入短名单：厂商 A（全球现有厂商）、厂商 B（云原生挑战者）、厂商 C（区域中端）、厂商 D（开源精品）。参会：集团 CFO（capex 批准人）、集团 CRO（网络批准人）、集团采购、集团法务、工作流负责人。一个决策：选定推荐厂商、批准 TCO 额度、授权进入合同谈判。

The vendor selection is a weighted scoring exercise — never a feeling, never a relationship call. The pattern: shortlist criteria first, weighted scorecard second, TCO third, reference calls fourth, recommendation fifth, decision sixth. The committee owns the scorecard, not the CIO. The CIO owns the recommendation.

厂商选型是加权评分游戏——不是感觉，不是关系。模式：短名单标准第一、加权评分卡第二、TCO 第三、参考电话第四、推荐第五、决策第六。委员会拥有评分卡，CIO 拥有推荐。

2. The Decision — 6 Sections, 90 Minutes

You (CIO): Shortlist criteria. Three gates. Gate 1: financial stability — investment-grade rating, 5-year P&L positive, no going-concern opinion. Gate 2: regulator-clean — no open supervisory letters, no major enforcement actions in the last 5 years. Gate 3: Group-approved panel — must be on the Group Vendor Panel. Vendor E, F, G failed one or more gates. Shortlist: A, B, C, D.短名单标准。三道关。关 1：财务稳健——投资级评级、5 年 P&L 为正、无持续经营疑虑。关 2：监管清白——无未关闭监管函，过去 5 年无重大执法行动。关 3：集团合格 panel——必须在集团厂商 panel 上。厂商 E、F、G 一项或多项未通过。短名单：A、B、C、D。
You (CIO): Weighted scorecard. Five dimensions, weights approved by the committee: Functional fit 30%, Technical architecture 25%, Cyber & regulator readiness 20%, Total cost of ownership 15%, Implementation track record 10%. Total: 100%.加权评分卡。五个维度，权重委员会已批准：功能匹配 30%，技术架构 25%，网络和监管就绪 20%，TCO 15%，实施往绩 10%。合计 100%。
Workstream Lead: Workstream Lead: Scores from the joint evaluation: Vendor A 78, Vendor B 86, Vendor C 71, Vendor D 68. Vendor B leads on every dimension except functional fit, where A leads by 3 points on legacy integration.工作流负责人：联合评估得分：A 78，B 86，C 71，D 68。B 在每个维度领先，功能匹配除外——A 因遗留集成领先 3 分。
You (CIO): TCO envelope. 5-year TCO: Vendor A $48M, Vendor B $42M, Vendor C $39M, Vendor D $36M. Vendor B saves $6M vs A and brings cloud-native architecture. Payback delta vs A: 22 months.TCO 额度。5 年 TCO：A 4,800 万，B 4,200 万，C 3,900 万，D 3,600 万。B 比 A 节省 600 万，且带来云原生架构。与 A 的回收期差：22 个月。
Group CRO: CRO: Cyber dimension: Vendor B is the only shortlist vendor with native FIDO2, built-in MAS TRM and HKMA Cyber Resilience control mapping, and a clean security incident record in the last 36 months. Vendor A had a major breach in 2023 disclosed in their 10-K.CRO：网络维度：B 是短名单中唯一原生支持 FIDO2、内置 MAS TRM 和 HKMA 网络韧性控制映射、过去 36 个月无安全事件的厂商。A 在 2023 年发生重大泄露，已在 10-K 中披露。
Group CFO: CFO: Capex impact of B is $11M Year 1, $9M Year 2, $7M Year 3, $8M Year 4, $7M Year 5. Inside the $280M 3-year envelope, leaves $26M capacity for other initiatives. NPV positive at the Group WACC of 8.5%.CFO：B 的 capex 影响是第 1 年 1,100 万、第 2 年 900 万、第 3 年 700 万、第 4 年 800 万、第 5 年 700 万。在 2.8 亿 3 年额度内，为其他项目留 2,600 万容量。按集团 WACC 8.5% NPV 为正。
You (CIO): Recommendation: Vendor B. Highest weighted score, lowest regulator risk, cloud-native architecture, $6M TCO saving, native FIDO2. Trade-off: Vendor A has deeper legacy integration, mitigated by a 6-month parallel-run during cutover. One decision: approve Vendor B, $42M TCO envelope, authorise contract negotiation with target execution by 31 October.推荐：厂商 B。加权得分最高、监管风险最低、云原生架构、节省 TCO 600 万、原生 FIDO2。Trade-off：A 遗留集成更深，通过 6 个月并行运行缓解。一个决策：批准 B、4,200 万 TCO 额度、授权合同谈判，目标 10 月 31 日签署。
CFO: CFO: Vendor B approved. Bring the contract to the November ExCo for ratification.CFO：厂商 B 批准。合同提交 11 月 ExCo 批准。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Shortlist criteria, three gates Three gates: financial stability, regulator-clean, Group-approved panel. 三道关：财务稳健、监管清白、集团合格 panel Pre-screening is a separate step from scoring. Never score a vendor that failed a gate. Gates are pass / fail, not weighted.
  • Weighted scorecard Five dimensions, weights approved by the committee. 五个维度，权重委员会已批准 The committee owns the weights. The CIO owns the recommendation. Never let a vendor challenge the weights after the fact — they were pre-agreed.
  • TCO envelope, 5-year 5-year TCO: $42M. Payback delta vs incumbent: 22 months. 5 年 TCO：4,200 万，与现有厂商的回收期差：22 个月 Always pair TCO with a time window and a payback delta. The CFO remembers the payback, not the absolute TCO.
  • Native FIDO2, control mapping Native FIDO2, built-in MAS TRM and HKMA Cyber Resilience control mapping. 原生 FIDO2，内置 MAS TRM 和 HKMA 网络韧性控制映射 "Native" is the cyber word. "Built-in" means the control is shipped, not bolted on. Cyber and regulator weight is non-negotiable in a bank RFP.
  • Trade-off, mitigated Trade-off: Vendor A has deeper legacy integration, mitigated by a 6-month parallel-run. Trade-off：A 遗留集成更深，通过 6 个月并行运行缓解 Never present a recommendation without naming the trade-off and the mitigation. The committee must choose with eyes open.
  • One decision One decision: approve Vendor B, $42M TCO envelope, authorise contract negotiation. 一个决策：批准 B、4,200 万 TCO 额度、授权合同谈判 End with the decision, not the discussion. The committee approves a vendor, a number, and an authorisation — three things, in one sentence.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • RFP (Request for Proposal) n. 提案征求书 A formal invitation to vendors to submit a proposal.
  • Shortlist n. 短名单 The vendors that passed pre-screening and are scored in detail.
  • Gate (Gate 1, Gate 2, Gate 3) n. 关 A pass / fail pre-screening criterion.
  • Weighted scorecard n. 加权评分卡 A multi-dimension scoring tool with pre-agreed weights.
  • TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) n. 总拥有成本 All costs over the contract life: license, implementation, run, exit.
  • Payback delta n. 回收期差 The difference in payback period between two options.
  • Native (vs bolted-on) adj. 原生 Built into the product, not added on afterwards.
  • Control mapping n. 控制映射 How a product's features map to a regulatory control framework.
  • Going concern opinion n. 持续经营意见 An auditor's opinion that the company can survive the next 12 months.
  • Capex envelope n. 资本支出额度 The approved total capital spending over a period.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Approve X, $Y TCO envelope, authorise Z" — three-verb decision sentence

A committee decision is a sentence with three verbs: approve (the vendor), at $Y (the envelope), authorise (the next step). Approve Vendor B, $42M TCO envelope, authorise contract negotiation. One sentence, three decisions, no ambiguity.

委员会决策是带三个动词的句子：approve（厂商）、at $Y（额度）、authorise（下一步）。

② "Mitigated by" — trade-off language

Every trade-off needs a mitigation. Trade-off: legacy integration gap, mitigated by a 6-month parallel-run. "Mitigated by" is the verb that signals the trade-off is not ignored but actively managed.

每个 trade-off 都需要 mitigation。Mitigated by是表示 trade-off 没有被忽视而是主动管理的动词。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Pre-screen before scoring. Gates (financial, regulator, panel) are pass / fail. Never score a vendor that failed a gate — that re-opens the gate debate.
  • Weights are pre-agreed, not negotiated. If a vendor challenges the weights, point to the pre-meeting minutes. The committee owns the weights.
  • Always pair TCO with a time window. "$42M" is a number. "$42M over 5 years" is a TCO. The window is what makes it comparable.
  • "Native" beats "supported". "Native FIDO2" is shipped; "supports FIDO2" may mean a partner integration. Cyber weight rewards native.
  • Name the trade-off, then the mitigation. Never recommend without naming what you gave up and how you manage it.
  • One decision, three parts. Approve the vendor, the TCO envelope, the next step. The committee minutes read cleaner when these are in one sentence.
  • Reference calls before recommendation. Always call 2 reference customers of the recommended vendor. The reference call can break a recommendation.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the RFP decision

Translate the recommendation sentence.

"Recommendation: Vendor B. Highest weighted score, lowest regulator risk, cloud-native architecture, $6M TCO saving, native FIDO2. Trade-off: Vendor A has deeper legacy integration, mitigated by a 6-month parallel-run during cutover. One decision: approve Vendor B, $42M TCO envelope, authorise contract negotiation with target execution by 31 October."

"推荐：厂商 B。加权得分最高、监管风险最低、云原生架构、节省 TCO 600 万、原生 FIDO2。Trade-off：A 遗留集成更深，通过 6 个月并行运行缓解。一个决策：批准 B、4,200 万 TCO 额度、授权合同谈判，目标 10 月 31 日签署。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the RFP mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a vendor selection committee? Why?

  1. "Shortlist: A, B, C, D. Vendor E, F, G failed one or more gates."
  2. "We like Vendor B because they have a good sales team."
  3. "5-year TCO: $42M, payback delta 22 months."
  4. "Native FIDO2, built-in MAS TRM control mapping."

② is the credibility-damager. "We like Vendor B because they have a good sales team" is a relationship, not a scorecard. ① shows gate discipline. ③ pairs TCO with window and payback. ④ names the cyber native capability. RFPs run on scorecards and TCO, not feelings.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute vendor recommendation

You are the CIO. 4 shortlist vendors. Vendor X scores 84, Y scores 79, Z scores 72, W scores 65. TCO: $38M, $44M, $36M, $41M. Cyber: X has native FIDO2, others have partner integrations. Recommendation?

"Recommendation: Vendor X. Highest weighted score (84), 5-year TCO $38M, native FIDO2, $6M TCO saving vs Y. Trade-off: Z is the lowest TCO at $36M but scored 12 points lower on functional fit. Mitigation: bring in 2 Z reference customers during due diligence to validate the score gap. One decision: approve Vendor X, $38M TCO envelope, authorise contract negotiation, target execution 15 November."
