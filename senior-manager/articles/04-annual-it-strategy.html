Annual IT Strategy Review — 3-Year Roadmap, Capex, Headcount, Regulator

Article 04 · Module 1: SteerCo & Board Communication · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Annual strategy, 3-year roadmap, capex plan, headcount, regulator, exit/enter

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 10:00 HKT, mid-November. You are the Group CIO. The Annual IT Strategy Review with the Group CEO and the Group Executive Committee is in 90 minutes. The committee has 60 minutes to approve the IT strategy for the next 3 years: the roadmap, the capex envelope, the headcount envelope, the regulator trajectory, and the major exits and entries. You have 30 slides; the CEO has read the pre-read. The CFO is watching the capex envelope; the CRO is watching the regulator trajectory; the Business MDs are watching the entries (what new technology you will adopt). One decision: approve the $280M capex envelope for the next 3 years.

周二 10:00 HKT，11 月中旬，你是集团 CIO。与集团 CEO 和集团执行委员会的年度 IT 战略评审90 分钟后开始。委员会有 60 分钟批准未来 3 年的 IT 战略：路线图、capex 额度、headcount 额度、监管轨迹、重大退出和进入。你有 30 张幻灯片；CEO 已读预读。CFO 关注 capex 额度；CRO 关注监管轨迹；业务 MD 关注进入（你将采用什么新技术）。一个决策：批准未来 3 年 2.8 亿 capex 额度。

The Annual IT Strategy Review is the highest-stakes 60 minutes in a CIO's calendar. The pattern: strategy first, roadmap second, capex third, headcount fourth, regulator fifth, exits/entries last. The CEO reads the strategy; the CFO reads the capex; the CRO reads the regulator; the MDs read the entries.

年度 IT 战略评审是 CIO 日历上风险最高的 60 分钟。模式：战略第一、路线图第二、capex 第三、headcount 第四、监管第五、退出/进入最后。CEO 读战略；CFO 读 capex；CRO 读监管；MD 读进入。

2. The Review — 6 Sections, 60 Minutes

You (CIO): Strategy. Three pillars for the next 3 years. Pillar 1: bank-grade resilience — 99.99% availability, regulator-ready cyber, zero P1 incidents from preventable causes. Pillar 2: digital at scale — digital channels handle 80% of customer interactions (currently 62%), onboarding time down from 4 days to 4 hours. Pillar 3: AI-enabled productivity — $120M annualised productivity gain by Year 3, 40% of operations automated.战略。未来 3 年三大支柱。支柱 1：银行级韧性——99.99% 可用性，监管就绪网络，可预防原因的零 P1 事件。支柱 2：大规模数字化——数字渠道处理 80% 客户互动（当前 62%），开户时间从 4 天降至 4 小时。支柱 3：AI 驱动生产力——第 3 年年化 1.2 亿生产力提升，40% 运营自动化。
You (CIO): Roadmap. Year 1: regulatory baseline — MAS TRM, HKMA Cyber Resilience, GDPR readiness, FIDO2 rollout (Article 18). Year 2: digital scale — onboarding 2.0, mobile platform rewrite, cloud migration 60%. Year 3: AI productivity — GenAI assistants for operations, fraud detection at scale, predictive customer service.路线图。第 1 年：监管基线——MAS TRM、HKMA 网络韧性、GDPR 就绪、FIDO2 推广（文章 18）。第 2 年：数字规模——开户 2.0、移动平台重写、云迁移 60%。第 3 年：AI 生产力——运营 GenAI 助手、大规模欺诈检测、预测性客户服务。
You (CIO): Capex envelope. 3-year plan: $280M. Year 1: $95M, Year 2: $98M, Year 3: $87M. Breakdown: $98M regulatory (35%), $112M digital (40%), $56M AI (20%), $14M cyber resilience (5%). Payback period blended: 28 months. NPV positive across all 3 pillars.Capex 额度。3 年计划：2.8 亿。第 1 年：9,500 万，第 2 年：9,800 万，第 3 年：8,700 万。细分：9,800 万监管（35%），1.12 亿数字（40%），5,600 万 AI（20%），1,400 万网络韧性（5%）。混合回收期：28 个月。三大支柱 NPV 均为正。
You (CIO): Headcount envelope. Current: 1,420 FTE. Year 3: 1,520 FTE (+100 net). Mix shift: +80 in cyber / risk / regulator, +60 in data / AI, -40 in legacy infrastructure. Net cost: $14M annualised by Year 3, covered by the $120M productivity gain.Headcount 额度。当前：1,420 FTE。第 3 年：1,520 FTE（净增 100）。结构变化：网络/风险/监管 +80，数据/AI +60，遗留基础设施 -40。净成本：第 3 年年化 1,400 万，由 1.2 亿生产力收益覆盖。
You (CIO): Regulator trajectory. Year 1: HKMA C-RAF Tier-1 attestation, MAS 14 cyber hygiene attestation, FIDO2 rollout for all employees. Year 2: HKMA iCAST 100% pass, NYDFS 23 NYCRR 500 full certification, MAS TRM deep dive. Year 3: zero open Tier-1 findings, zero open supervisory letters, all 8 regulators with annual attestation on file.监管轨迹。第 1 年：HKMA C-RAF Tier-1 合规声明，MAS 14 个网络卫生合规声明，所有员工 FIDO2 推广。第 2 年：HKMA iCAST 100% 通过，NYDFS 23 NYCRR 500 完整认证，MAS TRM 深度审查。第 3 年：零未关闭 Tier-1 发现，零未关闭监管函，8 个监管全部年度合规声明归档。
You (CIO): Exits and entries. Exits: 3 legacy mainframes (Year 2), 2 on-prem data centres (Year 3), 14 high-cost vendor contracts (Year 1-3, $22M savings). Entries: cloud-native core banking (Year 2 pilot), GenAI assistants (Year 1 pilot, Year 2 scale), embedded finance APIs (Year 3). Net effect: $22M vendor savings, $120M productivity, $14M net headcount cost. Net Year 3 benefit: $128M annualised.退出和进入。退出：3 个遗留主机（第 2 年），2 个本地数据中心（第 3 年），14 个高成本厂商合同（第 1-3 年，节省 2,200 万）。进入：云原生核心银行（第 2 年试点），GenAI 助手（第 1 年试点，第 2 年规模化），嵌入式金融 API（第 3 年）。净效果：2,200 万厂商节省，1.2 亿生产力，1,400 万净 headcount 成本。第 3 年净收益：年化 1.28 亿。
You (CIO): One decision. Approve the $280M 3-year capex envelope. Without the sign-off, the regulator trajectory is at risk — FIDO2 cannot roll out, MAS attestation slips, and the bank remains the only one of the 5 peer banks without phishing-resistant MFA for privileged access.一个决策。批准 3 年 2.8 亿 capex 额度。不签批，监管轨迹有风险——FIDO2 无法推广，MAS 合规声明推迟，银行仍是 5 家同业中唯一未为特权访问部署抗钓鱼 MFA 的。
CEO: CEO: $280M approved. Quarterly check-in at the ExCo, first one in March. Good strategy, Wei.CEO：2.8 亿批准。ExCo 季度检查点，第一次在 3 月。战略不错，Wei。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Three pillars Three pillars for the next 3 years: bank-grade resilience, digital at scale, AI-enabled productivity. 未来 3 年三大支柱：银行级韧性、大规模数字化、AI 驱动生产力 Three pillars is the cognitive budget. Each pillar has a number (99.99%, 80%, $120M) and a target. The CEO remembers the pillars; the CFO remembers the numbers.
  • Payback period blended, NPV positive Payback period blended: 28 months. NPV positive across all 3 pillars. 混合回收期 28 个月，三大支柱 NPV 均为正 CFO language. Payback in months, NPV positive across the portfolio. Always name the blended payback and the NPV direction; never a number without a unit.
  • Net headcount cost, covered by productivity Net cost: $14M annualised by Year 3, covered by the $120M productivity gain. 净成本：第 3 年年化 1,400 万，由 1.2 亿生产力收益覆盖 The pairing that closes the headcount chapter. $14M cost on the left; $120M productivity on the right. The CFO signs when the right side covers the left.
  • Zero open Tier-1, zero open supervisory letter Year 3: zero open Tier-1 findings, zero open supervisory letters. 第 3 年：零未关闭 Tier-1 发现，零未关闭监管函 The CRO's target. "Zero" is the trajectory. Anything above zero is a finding; the committee remembers the count.
  • Exits and entries, with net effect Exits: 3 mainframes, 2 data centres, 14 vendors. Entries: cloud-native, GenAI, embedded APIs. Net effect: $128M annualised by Year 3. 退出：3 主机、2 数据中心、14 厂商。进入：云原生、GenAI、嵌入式 API。净效果：第 3 年年化 1.28 亿 Exits and entries are the strategic narrative. Net effect is the financial proof. The committee reads both; the CFO signs the net effect.
  • One decision, with consequence Without the sign-off, the regulator trajectory is at risk — FIDO2 cannot roll out, MAS attestation slips. 不签批，监管轨迹有风险——FIDO2 无法推广，MAS 合规声明推迟 The decision + the consequence. The CEO signs the capex; the consequence is the regulator trajectory. The two are linked.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Annual IT Strategy Review n. 年度 IT 战略评审 The yearly ExCo meeting that approves the 3-year IT plan.
  • ExCo (Executive Committee) n. 执行委员会 The senior-most decision-making body, chaired by the Group CEO.
  • Three pillars n. 三大支柱 The 3 top-level strategic themes for the IT plan.
  • Roadmap n. 路线图 The year-by-year delivery plan for the strategic pillars.
  • Capex envelope n. Capex 额度 The total capital expenditure approved for the period.
  • Headcount envelope n. Headcount 额度 The total FTE approved for the period.
  • Payback period n. 回收期 The time for the investment to recover its cost against the benefit.
  • NPV (Net Present Value) n. 净现值 The discounted value of future cash flows, minus the initial investment.
  • Regulator trajectory n. 监管轨迹 The year-by-year plan for regulator attestations and findings.
  • Exits n. 退出 The legacy systems and contracts being decommissioned.
  • Entries n. 进入 The new technologies and capabilities being adopted.
  • Net effect n. 净效果 The total annualised financial benefit by Year 3, after costs.

5. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 6 sections, 60 minutes. Strategy, roadmap, capex, headcount, regulator, exits/entries. The ExCo reads the structure, not the prose.
  • Three pillars, each with a number. Three is the cognitive budget. Each pillar has a number (99.99%, 80%, $120M). The CEO remembers the pillars; the CFO remembers the numbers.
  • Payback period blended, NPV positive. CFO language. Always name the blended payback in months and the NPV direction. Never a number without a unit.
  • Net headcount cost, covered by productivity. The pairing that closes the headcount chapter. $14M cost on the left; $120M productivity on the right.
  • "Zero open Tier-1" is the regulator target. The CRO signs when the trajectory shows zero. Anything above zero is a finding; the committee remembers the count.
  • Exits and entries, with net effect. Exits and entries are the narrative; net effect is the financial proof. The committee reads both.
  • One decision, with consequence. The decision + the consequence. The ExCo signs the capex; the consequence is the regulator trajectory. The two are linked.

6. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the capex slide

Translate the capex slide into natural Chinese.

"Capex envelope. 3-year plan: $280M. Year 1: $95M. Year 2: $98M. Year 3: $87M. Breakdown: $98M regulatory (35%), $112M digital (40%), $56M AI (20%), $14M cyber resilience (5%). Payback period blended: 28 months. NPV positive across all 3 pillars. Headcount envelope. Current: 1,420 FTE. Year 3: 1,520 FTE (+100 net). Mix shift: +80 in cyber / risk / regulator, +60 in data / AI, -40 in legacy infrastructure. Net cost: $14M annualised by Year 3, covered by the $120M productivity gain."

"Capex 额度。3 年计划：2.8 亿。第 1 年：9,500 万。第 2 年：9,800 万。第 3 年：8,700 万。细分：9,800 万监管（35%），1.12 亿数字（40%），5,600 万 AI（20%），1,400 万网络韧性（5%）。混合回收期：28 个月。混合 NPV 均为正。Headcount 额度。当前：1,420 FTE。第 3 年：1,520 FTE（净增 100）。结构变化：网络/风险/监管 +80，数据/AI +60，遗留基础设施 -40。净成本：第 3 年年化 1,400 万，由 1.2 亿生产力收益覆盖。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the strategy review mistake

Which line would damage the strategy review the most? Why?

  1. "Three pillars: resilience, digital, AI. Roadmap Y1 regulatory, Y2 digital, Y3 AI. Capex $280M / 3 years. Payback 28 months blended. NPV positive. Headcount +100 net, cost $14M covered by $120M productivity."
  2. "IT is important and we have a lot of plans. We will invest in technology and do our best. Thank you."
  3. "Exits: 3 mainframes, 2 data centres, 14 vendors. Entries: cloud-native, GenAI, embedded APIs. Net effect: $128M annualised by Year 3. Regulator trajectory: zero open Tier-1 by Year 3."
  4. "One decision: approve the $280M capex envelope. Without the sign-off, the regulator trajectory is at risk — FIDO2 cannot roll out, MAS attestation slips."

② is the credibility-damager. "IT is important and we have a lot of plans" replaces the three pillars, the capex, the headcount, the regulator trajectory, and the exits/entries with generalities. The ExCo walks out with no strategy, no number, no decision. ① names the three pillars, the roadmap, the capex with payback and NPV, and the headcount covered by productivity. ③ names the exits, the entries, the net effect, and the regulator trajectory. ④ names the decision and the consequence. ② turns a 60-minute strategy review into a 6-month delay.

Exercise 3 — Build a strategy review

You are the CIO of a mid-size bank. 3 pillars: customer obsession (NPS 60 by Y3, currently 45), operational excellence (cost-income ratio down from 62% to 52%), regulator trust (zero open findings). Capex $200M / 3 years. Headcount +60 net. Build the 6 sections: (1) strategy, (2) roadmap, (3) capex, (4) headcount, (5) regulator, (6) exits/entries.

"Section 1 — Strategy. Three pillars. Pillar 1: customer obsession — NPS 60 by Year 3 (currently 45), driven by digital onboarding, in-app service, and predictive personalisation. Pillar 2: operational excellence — cost-income ratio down from 62% to 52% by Year 3, driven by AI automation of ops and vendor consolidation. Pillar 3: regulator trust — zero open Tier-1 findings, zero open supervisory letters, all 5 regulators with annual attestation on file. Section 2 — Roadmap. Year 1: customer baseline (digital onboarding 2.0, NPS measurement framework), operational baseline (process automation 30%), regulator baseline (HKMA C-RAF, MAS 14 cyber hygiene). Year 2: customer scale (in-app service, predictive personalisation), operational scale (process automation 60%, vendor consolidation 50%), regulator deep dive (HKMA iCAST, NYDFS 23 NYCRR 500). Year 3: customer leadership (NPS 60), operational leadership (cost-income 52%), regulator leadership (zero open findings). Section 3 — Capex envelope. 3-year plan: $200M. Year 1: $70M. Year 2: $70M. Year 3: $60M. Breakdown: $80M customer (40%), $70M operational (35%), $40M regulator (20%), $10M cyber resilience (5%). Payback period blended: 32 months. NPV positive across all 3 pillars. Section 4 — Headcount envelope. Current: 1,100 FTE. Year 3: 1,160 FTE (+60 net). Mix shift: +50 in data / AI / digital, +30 in cyber / risk / regulator, -20 in legacy infrastructure. Net cost: $8M annualised by Year 3, covered by the $45M operational savings. Section 5 — Regulator trajectory. Year 1: HKMA C-RAF Tier-1 attestation, MAS 14 cyber hygiene attestation. Year 2: HKMA iCAST 100% pass, NYDFS 23 NYCRR 500 full certification. Year 3: zero open Tier-1 findings, zero open supervisory letters. Section 6 — Exits and entries. Exits: 2 legacy mainframes (Y2), 1 on-prem data centre (Y3), 8 high-cost vendor contracts (Y1-3, $12M savings). Entries: cloud-native onboarding (Y2), GenAI ops assistant (Y1 pilot, Y2 scale), embedded finance APIs (Y3). Net effect: $12M vendor savings, $45M operational savings, $8M net headcount cost. Net Year 3 benefit: $49M annualised. One decision: approve the $200M 3-year capex envelope. Without the sign-off, the regulator trajectory is at risk — HKMA C-RAF cannot land, MAS attestation slips, and the bank remains in the regulator's Tier-2 list."
