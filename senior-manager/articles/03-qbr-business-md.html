QBR with Business MD — Alignment, Satisfaction, Top 3 Asks

Article 03 · Module 1: SteerCo & Board Communication · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 QBR, business-IT alignment, satisfaction score, top 3 asks, joint commitment

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 15:00 HKT. You are the IT Director, Retail Banking Technology. The Quarterly Business Review (QBR) with Margaret, MD of Retail Banking HK, is in 30 minutes. Margaret is the largest internal customer for your IT organisation: $28M annual IT spend, 42 active projects, 18 FTE embedded in her franchise. The QBR covers: satisfaction score, project portfolio health, top 3 business asks, top 3 IT asks, regulator milestones, and joint commitments for next quarter. Margaret will want numbers, not narratives; she has 4 peer IT directors competing for her time.

周五 15:00 HKT，你是IT 总监，零售银行技术部。与零售银行香港 MD Margaret 的季度业务评审（QBR）30 分钟后开始。Margaret 是你 IT 组织的最大内部客户：年 IT 支出 2,800 万，42 个在运行项目，18 名 FTE 嵌入她的业务条线。QBR 涵盖：满意度、项目群健康度、前 3 大业务请求、前 3 大 IT 请求、监管里程碑、下季度联合承诺。Margaret 要数字而非叙述；她有 4 位同业 IT 总监在竞争她的时间。

A QBR is a two-way accountability conversation. The IT Director is accountable for delivery and satisfaction; the Business MD is accountable for prioritisation and resourcing. The pattern: satisfaction score first, portfolio second, business asks third, IT asks fourth, regulator fifth, joint commitments last. Margaret remembers the satisfaction score; you remember her asks.

QBR 是双向问责对话。IT 总监对交付和满意度问责；业务 MD 对优先级和资源问责。模式：满意度第一、项目群第二、业务请求第三、IT 请求第四、监管第五、联合承诺最后。Margaret 记住满意度；你记住她的请求。

2. The QBR — 6 Sections, 60 Minutes

You (IT Director): Margaret, thanks for the slot. Q2 QBR, 60 minutes, 6 sections. I'll keep the numbers up front.Margaret，感谢档期。Q2 QBR，60 分钟，6 个部分。数字优先。
You (IT Director): Satisfaction score. Q1: 7.2 out of 10 (52 respondents). Q2: 7.8 (58 respondents). Up 0.6 points, driven by the Onboarding 2026 launch (NPS 64, target 60) and the App Performance Improvement (crash rate down 42%). Top detractor: "change advisory board takes too long" — CAB median approval 5 days, target 3.满意度。Q1：7.2 / 10（52 份回复）。Q2：7.8（58 份回复）。上升 0.6 分，由 2026 开户发布（NPS 64，目标 60）和应用性能改进（崩溃率下降 42%）驱动。首要不满："变更咨询委员会耗时过长"——CAB 审批中位 5 天，目标 3 天。
You (IT Director): Portfolio. 42 active projects. 34 Green, 7 Amber, 1 Red. Red: Mobile App 7.0 — vendor delivery slipped by 3 weeks, mitigation in flight (parallel UAT start). Capex: $28M plan, $19M committed, $27.6M forecast. Variance $400K under plan.项目群。42 个在运行项目。34 绿，7 黄，1 红。红：移动应用 7.0——厂商交付推迟 3 周，缓解进行中（并行 UAT 启动）。Capex：2,800 万计划，1,900 万已承诺，2,760 万预测。差异比计划少 40 万。
Margaret: Margaret: Three asks from me. One: the CAB approval cycle — 5 days is too long, target 3. Two: the Mobile App 7.0 slip — I need it before the Q4 customer event on 12 December. Three: a named IT partner for my Wealth business, same as the Retail Onboarding model.Margaret：我有三个请求。一：CAB 审批周期——5 天太长，目标 3 天。二：移动应用 7.0 推迟——我需要在 12 月 12 日 Q4 客户活动前上线。三：为我的财富业务配具名 IT partner，与零售开户模式一样。
You (IT Director): Three asks from me. One: confirm the Q3 priority list by 5 July — I'm holding 8 FTE until the list is confirmed, and the unconfirmed work is at risk of slip. Two: name a business lead for the Mobile App 7.0 UAT — current business lead (James) is on leave in August. Three: back the App Performance SLA increase at the next SteerCo — the SLA change protects the customer NPS but adds 2 FTE to the on-call rotation.我有三个请求。一：7 月 5 日前确认 Q3 优先级列表——我保留 8 FTE 直到列表确认，未确认的工作有推迟风险。二：为移动应用 7.0 UAT 具名业务负责人——当前业务负责人（James）8 月休假。三：在下次 SteerCo 支持应用性能 SLA 提高——SLA 变更保护客户 NPS，但 on-call 轮值增加 2 FTE。
You (IT Director): Regulator. MAS TRM cyber hygiene attestation Q3, on track. HKMA Cyber Resilience iCAST October, on track. PDPA data-breach playbook refresh Q4, on track. No regulator milestone is at risk for retail.监管。MAS TRM 网络卫生合规声明 Q3，按计划。HKMA 网络韧性 iCAST 10 月，按计划。PDPA 数据泄露预案刷新 Q4，按计划。零售无监管里程碑有风险。
You (IT Director): Joint commitments for Q3. One: CAB target to 3 days, owned by me, in flight by 15 July. Two: Mobile App 7.0 UAT start 1 September, owned by Margaret (named lead). Three: named IT partner for Wealth, owned by me, named by 22 July. Review at the next QBR.Q3 联合承诺。一：CAB 目标 3 天，由我负责，7 月 15 日前进行中。二：移动应用 7.0 UAT 9 月 1 日启动，由 Margaret 负责（具名负责人）。三：财富业务具名 IT partner，由我负责，7 月 22 日前具名。下次 QBR 复核。
Margaret: Margaret: All three asks approved. The satisfaction score is good — the team did well on Onboarding. One thing I want to flag: the CAB issue. 5 days is the top complaint, and I need it fixed by Q3 close.Margaret：三个请求都批准。满意度不错——团队在开户上做得好。我想提一件事：CAB 问题。5 天是首要投诉，我需要 Q3 关闭前修复。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Satisfaction score, with the top detractor Q2: 7.8 out of 10. Top detractor: "CAB takes too long" — median 5 days, target 3. Q2：7.8 / 10。首要不满："CAB 耗时过长"——中位 5 天，目标 3 天 Satisfaction score is the headline. Always name the top detractor — the one issue the business cares about most. The MD remembers the top detractor; you remember the score.
  • Portfolio: Green, Amber, Red, with the Red named 42 projects: 34 Green, 7 Amber, 1 Red. Red: Mobile App 7.0. 42 个项目：34 绿，7 黄，1 红。红：移动应用 7.0 The RAG count is the headline. The Red is the one the MD will ask about. Always name the Red project; the count is the headline; the Red is the conversation.
  • Three asks from me, three asks from you Three asks from me. Three asks from you. 我有三个请求。你有三个请求。 The discipline that keeps the QBR balanced. Three asks from each side. Anything more, and the QBR drifts into a wish list.
  • Named lead, with date Mobile App 7.0 UAT start 1 September, owned by Margaret (named lead). 移动应用 7.0 UAT 9 月 1 日启动，由 Margaret 负责（具名负责人） A joint commitment without a named owner and a date is a wish. Pair owner with date. The QBR review will ask "did it happen?".
  • Top detractor, by name Top detractor: "CAB takes too long". 首要不满："CAB 耗时过长" The MD is giving you a gift — the one thing that, if fixed, will move the satisfaction score the most. Take it. Do not deflect.
  • I need it fixed by Q3 close I need it fixed by Q3 close. 我需要 Q3 关闭前修复 The MD's deadline. The deadline is what the joint commitment will be measured against. The QBR review will ask "did it land by Q3 close?".

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • QBR (Quarterly Business Review) n. 季度业务评审 The quarterly meeting between IT and a Business MD to review performance and align on next quarter.
  • Satisfaction score n. 满意度 A 0-10 score collected quarterly from the business, with a top detractor question.
  • Top detractor n. 首要不满 The single issue the business identifies as the most painful.
  • CAB (Change Advisory Board) n. 变更咨询委员会 The governance body that approves production changes.
  • Embedded FTE n. 嵌入式 FTE IT staff dedicated to a specific business line, reporting matrix-style.
  • Named lead n. 具名负责人 A specific person, by name, who owns a deliverable.
  • Joint commitment n. 联合承诺 An agreement between IT and the business, with named owner and date.
  • QBR review n. QBR 复核 The next QBR, which checks the joint commitments from the prior QBR.
  • NPS (Net Promoter Score) n. 净推荐值 A customer-loyalty metric from -100 to +100.
  • Portfolio n. 项目群 The complete set of active IT projects for the business.
  • IT partner n. IT partner A senior IT leader who pairs with a business leader as the single point of contact.
  • Two-way accountability n. 双向问责 The principle that IT is accountable for delivery and the business is accountable for prioritisation.

5. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 6 sections, 60 minutes. Satisfaction, portfolio, business asks, IT asks, regulator, joint commitments. The MD reads the structure, not the prose.
  • Numbers up front. "Q2: 7.8 out of 10" is the headline. The detail is the backup. MDs remember numbers; they forget prose.
  • Satisfaction score with the top detractor. The score is the headline; the top detractor is the gift. Take the gift; do not deflect.
  • Portfolio: count by RAG, name the Red. The count is the headline; the Red is the conversation. The MD will ask about the Red; be ready.
  • Three asks from each side. The discipline that keeps the QBR balanced. Three is the cognitive budget.
  • Named owner + date for every joint commitment. A commitment without an owner and a date is a wish. The next QBR will check it.
  • The MD's deadline is the deadline. "I need it fixed by Q3 close" is the MD's commitment to their own franchise. The QBR review will hold them to it; they will hold you to it.

Exercise 1 — Translate the satisfaction slide

Translate the satisfaction slide into natural Chinese.

"Satisfaction score. Q1: 7.2 out of 10 (52 respondents). Q2: 7.8 (58 respondents). Up 0.6 points, driven by the Onboarding 2026 launch (NPS 64, target 60) and the App Performance Improvement (crash rate down 42%). Red: Mobile App 7.0 — vendor delivery slipped by 3 weeks, mitigation in flight (parallel UAT start). Capex: $28M plan, $19M committed, $27.6M forecast. Variance $400K under plan."

"满意度。Q1：7.2 / 10（52 份回复）。Q2：7.8（58 份回复）。上升 0.6 分，由 2026 开户发布（NPS 64，目标 60）和应用性能改进（崩溃率下降 42%）驱动。首要不满：'变更咨询委员会耗时过长'——CAB 审批中位 5 天，目标 3 天。项目群。42 个在运行项目。34 绿，7 黄，1 红。红：移动应用 7.0——厂商交付推迟 3 周，缓解进行中（并行 UAT 启动）。Capex：2,800 万计划，1,900 万已承诺，2,760 万预测。差异比计划少 40 万。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the QBR mistake

Which line would damage the QBR the most? Why?

  1. "Satisfaction 7.8, up 0.6. Top detractor CAB. Portfolio 34 Green, 7 Amber, 1 Red. Three asks from me, three asks from you. Joint commitments with named owner and date. Q3 close on the CAB."
  2. "We had a good quarter, the team worked hard, we delivered a lot of things, thank you."
  3. "Three asks from me: confirm Q3 priority list by 5 July, name business lead for Mobile App 7.0 UAT, back the App Performance SLA increase at SteerCo."
  4. "Joint commitments for Q3: CAB target to 3 days, owned by me, in flight by 15 July. Mobile App 7.0 UAT start 1 September, owned by Margaret. Named IT partner for Wealth, owned by me, named by 22 July."

② is the credibility-damager. "We had a good quarter" replaces the satisfaction score, the portfolio, the asks, and the joint commitments with a thank-you. The MD walks out with no number, no ask, no commitment. ① leads with the satisfaction score, names the top detractor, names the Red, and pairs the joint commitments with owner and date. ③ names the three IT asks. ④ pairs every joint commitment with owner and date. ② turns a 60-minute QBR into a 3-hour follow-up email chain.

Exercise 3 — Build a QBR

You are the IT Director for Wealth Management Tech. Margaret's satisfaction dropped from 7.4 to 6.9 (Q1 to Q2). Top detractor: "platform instability" (3 P1 incidents in 60 days). Portfolio: 28 projects, 22 Green, 5 Amber, 1 Red (Wealth Platform Migration). Capex: $18M plan, $12M committed, $18.4M forecast (slight over). Build the 6 sections: (1) satisfaction, (2) portfolio, (3) business asks, (4) IT asks, (5) regulator, (6) joint commitments.

"Section 1 — Satisfaction. Q1: 7.4. Q2: 6.9. Down 0.5 points. Top detractor: 'platform instability' — 3 P1 incidents in 60 days (trading hours, customer impact). Driver: the Wealth Platform Migration project (currently Red) is the source of the instability. Section 2 — Portfolio. 28 projects, 22 Green, 5 Amber, 1 Red. Red: Wealth Platform Migration — vendor integration slipped 4 weeks, mitigation in flight (parallel cutover plan). Capex: $18M plan, $12M committed, $18.4M forecast, $400K over plan (1 FTE contractor for 3 months, $400K). Section 3 — Business asks. Ask 1: name a business lead for the Migration cutover — current lead (Lisa) leaves in August. Ask 2: approve the $400K variance at next SteerCo. Ask 3: cap any new Wealth projects in Q3 until the Migration is stable. Section 4 — IT asks. Ask 1: confirm the cutover date of 15 September by 22 July — beyond that, the regulator milestone is at risk. Ask 2: freeze any new vendor selection for Wealth until Q4. Ask 3: back the P1 incident review process change (root cause within 5 days, owned by engineering). Section 5 — Regulator. MAS TRM attestation Q3, on track. HKMA iCAST October, on track. No regulator milestone at risk for Wealth. Section 6 — Joint commitments. Commitment 1: Wealth Platform cutover 15 September, owned by IT (technical) and Margaret (business sign-off). Commitment 2: cap new Wealth projects in Q3, owned by Margaret. Commitment 3: P1 incident root cause within 5 days, owned by IT, in flight by 1 August. Review at the next QBR. Bottom line: satisfaction dropped 0.5; the driver is the Migration Red; the joint commitment is the cutover date."
