COO Conversation on IT Operations — SLA, MTTR, Change Failure Rate, BAU vs Project

Article 15 · Module 4: Cross-Function Alignment · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 COO conversation, SLA, MTTR, change failure rate, BAU vs project, productivity

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 14:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You have a 60-minute COO 1:1 with Mark (Group COO). The agenda: IT operational SLA performance, MTTR trend, change failure rate, BAU vs project mix, and the productivity programme (Article 04 $42M annualised target). The COO has signalled the bank's P1 incident count is up 22% YoY and customer-impacting incidents are up 14%. He wants a plan to reverse the trend without slowing cloud migration.

周一 14:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你与Mark（集团 COO）有 60 分钟COO 1:1。议程：IT 运营 SLA 表现、MTTR 趋势、变更失败率、BAU vs 项目组合、生产力项目（文章 04 4,200 万年化目标）。COO 已示意集团 P1 事件数同比上升 22%、客户影响事件上升 14%。他希望在不减缓云迁移的前提下扭转趋势。

A COO 1:1 is the operational excellence conversation. The pattern: SLAs first, trend second, root cause third, plan fourth, productivity fifth. Speak the COO's language: MTTR, change failure rate, BAU cost, productivity per FTE.

COO 1:1 是运营卓越对话。模式：SLA 第一、趋势第二、根因第三、计划第四、生产力第五。说 COO 的语言：MTTR、变更失败率、BAU 成本、人均生产力。

2. The COO 1:1 — 4 Sections, 60 Minutes

Mark (COO): Wei, the IT operational story this year. P1 incidents up 22% YoY, customer-impacting up 14%. Walk me through the trend and the plan.Wei，今年 IT 运营情况。P1 事件同比上升 22%，客户影响上升 14%。讲讲趋势和计划。
You (CIO): SLA performance. Customer-facing availability: 99.94% against 99.95% target. MTTR P1: 78 minutes against 60-minute target. Change failure rate: 11% against 8% target. Top 3 systems by P1 volume: core banking (32%), digital onboarding (24%), payments (18%).SLA 表现。面向客户可用性：99.94% 对 99.95% 目标。P1 MTTR：78 分钟对 60 分钟目标。变更失败率：11% 对 8% 目标。P1 数量前 3 系统：核心银行（32%）、数字开户（24%）、支付（18%）。
You (CIO): Trend. P1 up 22% YoY, but 67% of the increase is concentrated in 4 systems: core banking mainframe (EOL 2027), digital onboarding (cloud migration in flight), payments (vendor change), and a single legacy CRM. Customer-impacting up 14% is more concerning — it's a 1.5x multiplier of P1, against the historical 0.7x.趋势。P1 同比上升 22%，但 67% 增量集中在 4 个系统：核心银行主机（EOL 2027）、数字开户（云迁移中）、支付（厂商变更）、单一遗留 CRM。客户影响上升 14% 更值得关注——是 P1 的 1.5 倍乘数，对历史 0.7 倍。
Mark (COO): Root cause?根因？
You (CIO): Root cause, three. Cause 1: change failure rate is up because the cloud migration is changing the deploy pattern — 2 of 3 deploys now touch both on-prem and cloud, doubling the failure surface. Cause 2: MTTR is up because the on-call rotation is 6 engineers across 4 squads, no dedicated incident commander during business hours. Cause 3: customer-impacting 1.5x is the customer notification lag — we detect fast, we notify the customer 38 minutes late on average.根因，三条。原因 1：变更失败率上升，因为云迁移改变了部署模式——3 个部署中 2 个同时涉及本地和云，失败面翻倍。原因 2：MTTR 上升，因为 on-call 轮值是 4 个 squad 6 个工程师，工作时间无专职事件指挥。原因 3：客户影响 1.5 倍是客户通知延迟——我们检测快，但客户通知平均延迟 38 分钟。
You (CIO): The plan. Three actions, 90 days. Action 1: introduce canary deploys for the 2 mixed-pattern deploys — 10% traffic for 30 minutes, rollback automated, target change failure rate back to 8%. Action 2: name a dedicated incident commander during business hours, rotating weekly, 5 named commanders. Target MTTR back to 60 minutes. Action 3: automate customer notification from the SIEM alert — target notification lag from 38 minutes to 5 minutes. No slowdown to cloud migration.计划。三个行动，90 天。行动 1：对 2 个混合模式部署引入金丝雀发布——10% 流量 30 分钟、自动回滚、目标变更失败率回到 8%。行动 2：工作时间具名专职事件指挥，每周轮值，5 名具名指挥。目标 MTTR 回到 60 分钟。行动 3：从 SIEM 告警自动化客户通知——目标通知延迟从 38 分钟降至 5 分钟。不减缓云迁移。
You (CIO): Productivity. $42M annualised target from Article 04 is on track for Year 3. Year 1 actual: $11M realised. $31M remaining across Years 2-3. BAU FTE: 1,180 (83% of IT), project FTE 312 (17%, includes cloud migration). BAU cost: $182M annualised, down 4% YoY despite headcount stable — productivity per FTE up 6%.生产力。文章 04 4,200 万年化目标第 3 年可达。第 1 年实际：已实现 1,100 万。第 2-3 年剩余 3,100 万。BAU FTE：1,180（IT 83%），项目 FTE 312（17%，含云迁移）。BAU 成本：年化 1.82 亿，同比下降 4%，尽管 headcount 稳定——人均生产力上升 6%。
Mark (COO): Mark: 90-day plan approved. One condition: monthly P1 and customer-impacting review at our 1:1, with the trend chart, until both metrics are back to target. Productivity on track.Mark：90 天计划批准。一个条件：P1 和客户影响每月在我 1:1 上评审，附趋势图，直到两个指标回到目标。生产力达标。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • SLA performance, against target Customer-facing availability: 99.94% against 99.95% target. 面向客户可用性 99.94% 对 99.95% 目标 Always pair the actual with the target. "We're 99.94%" loses; "99.94% against 99.95% target" wins. The COO buys the gap.
  • Top 3 systems by P1 volume Top 3 systems by P1 volume: core banking 32%, digital onboarding 24%, payments 18%. P1 数量前 3 系统：核心银行 32%、数字开户 24%、支付 18% Concentration analysis. "P1s are up" loses; "67% of the increase is in 4 systems" wins. The COO buys the concentration.
  • Customer-impacting 1.5x multiplier Customer-impacting up 14% is a 1.5x multiplier of P1, against the historical 0.7x. 客户影响上升 14% 是 P1 的 1.5 倍乘数，对历史 0.7 倍 Multipliers are COO language. "1.5x of P1 against 0.7x historical" is a sharp trend. The COO buys the multiplier.
  • Named incident commander, 5 named Name a dedicated incident commander, rotating weekly, 5 named commanders. 具名专职事件指挥，每周轮值，5 名具名指挥 Names, not "a team". The COO buys the named owner. "5 named" is the deal term.
  • Notification lag, 38 to 5 minutes Automate customer notification from SIEM — target lag 38 minutes to 5 minutes. 从 SIEM 自动化客户通知——目标延迟 38 分钟降至 5 分钟 Customer-impacting is a notification problem as much as a detection problem. The fix is automated notification, not faster humans.
  • Productivity per FTE, up 6% BAU cost down 4% YoY despite headcount stable — productivity per FTE up 6%. BAU 成本同比下降 4%，headcount 稳定——人均生产力上升 6% Productivity per FTE is COO language. Pair cost trend with headcount stable, then name the productivity gain.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • SLA (Service Level Agreement) n. 服务等级协议 The committed performance target.
  • MTTR (Mean Time To Recover) n. 平均恢复时间 The average time to recover from an incident.
  • Change failure rate n. 变更失败率 The percentage of changes that cause an incident.
  • Canary deploy n. 金丝雀发布 A small-percentage rollout to detect failure early.
  • On-call rotation n. On-call 轮值 The schedule of engineers responsible for incidents.
  • Incident commander n. 事件指挥 The person accountable for managing an incident.
  • Customer-impacting n. 客户影响 An incident that affected end customers.
  • Notification lag n. 通知延迟 The time between detection and customer notification.
  • BAU FTE n. 日常运营 FTE FTE allocated to business-as-usual work.
  • Productivity per FTE n. 人均生产力 The output per FTE, the COO's metric.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "X against Y target" — the SLA sentence

SLA performance is always X against Y target. 99.94% against 99.95% target. "We're at 99.94%" loses; the gap is the story.

SLA 表现总是 X 对 Y 目标。

② "X multiplier against Y historical" — the trend sentence

Trend language is X multiplier against Y historical. The multiplier is sharper than a percentage change. 1.5x of P1 against 0.7x historical is a 2x deterioration.

趋势语言是 X 倍数对 Y 历史。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Pair actual with target. "We're 99.94%" loses; "99.94% against 99.95% target" wins. The COO buys the gap.
  • Concentration analysis on the trend. "P1s are up" loses; "67% of the increase is in 4 systems" wins. The COO buys the concentration.
  • Multipliers are sharper than percentages. "1.5x of P1 against 0.7x historical" is a 2x deterioration. The COO buys the multiplier.
  • Three root causes, named. "Things are bad" loses; "deploy pattern, on-call rotation, customer notification lag" wins. The COO buys the three named causes.
  • Three actions with named owners and dates. "We'll fix it" loses; "5 named incident commanders, rotating weekly, 90 days" wins. The COO buys the named action.
  • Productivity per FTE pairs cost with headcount. "BAU cost down 4% YoY, headcount stable, productivity per FTE up 6%" is the COO's metric. Pair cost trend with headcount stable.
  • End with a monthly review condition. The COO closes with a date, not a feeling. "Monthly P1 review until back to target" is the named condition.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the root causes

Translate the three root causes.

"Root cause, three. Cause 1: change failure rate is up because the cloud migration is changing the deploy pattern — 2 of 3 deploys now touch both on-prem and cloud. Cause 2: MTTR is up because the on-call rotation is 6 engineers across 4 squads, no dedicated incident commander during business hours. Cause 3: customer-impacting 1.5x is the customer notification lag — we detect fast, we notify the customer 38 minutes late on average."

"根因，三条。原因 1：变更失败率上升，因为云迁移改变了部署模式——3 个部署中 2 个同时涉及本地和云。原因 2：MTTR 上升，因为 on-call 轮值是 4 个 squad 6 个工程师，工作时间无专职事件指挥。原因 3：客户影响 1.5 倍是客户通知延迟——我们检测快，但客户通知平均延迟 38 分钟。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the COO mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a COO 1:1? Why?

  1. "Things are not great, we need to improve."
  2. "Customer-facing availability 99.94% against 99.95% target. MTTR 78 minutes against 60-minute target."
  3. "Top 3 systems by P1 volume: core banking 32%, digital onboarding 24%, payments 18%."
  4. "Productivity per FTE up 6%, BAU cost down 4% YoY."

① is the credibility-damager. "Things are not great" is opinion, not COO language. ② pairs actuals with targets. ③ names the concentration. ④ pairs cost with productivity. COO 1:1s run on actuals-vs-targets, concentration, and productivity-per-FTE — not on feelings.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute COO plan

You are the CIO. COO wants P1 reduction. P1 up 18% YoY, 4 of 5 in 2 systems. MTTR up 25%. Change failure rate 14%. Pitch the plan.

"SLA performance. P1 up 18% YoY, 4 of 5 in 2 systems (core banking + payments). MTTR 92 minutes against 60 target. Change failure rate 14% against 8% target. Top 2 systems by P1: core banking 56%, payments 24%. Root cause, three. Cause 1: change failure up because both systems have legacy change windows, no canary. Cause 2: MTTR up because on-call is 4 engineers, no dedicated IC. Cause 3: customer notification lag 42 minutes. Plan, 3 actions, 90 days. Action 1: canary deploy for core banking and payments, target change failure back to 8%. Action 2: 4 named incident commanders, rotating weekly, target MTTR 60 minutes. Action 3: automate customer notification, target lag 42 to 5 minutes. Monthly P1 review at our 1:1 until both metrics back to target."
