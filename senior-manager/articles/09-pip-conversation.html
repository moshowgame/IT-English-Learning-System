Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) Conversation — Specific Gaps, 90-Day Plan, Exit Trigger

Article 09 · Module 3: Difficult Leadership Conversations · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 PIP, specific gaps, 90-day plan, named support, exit trigger, HR partnership

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 14:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You have a 60-minute Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) conversation with Marcus, a 14-year veteran and Senior Engineering Manager who runs the Core Banking platform squad. Over the last 9 months: 3 production incidents Marcus was the named incident manager for, 2 of which breached the MAS reporting window by 2 hours each; Marcus missed 4 of 6 monthly platform reviews; 2 senior engineers in his squad have resigned citing his management style. The decision: 90-day PIP with named support, observable exit triggers, and a clear "if not improved, exit" line. Sarah (Group HR Business Partner) is on the call, taking notes.

周一 14:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你与Marcus——一位 14 年老兵、运行核心银行平台 squad 的高级工程经理——进行 60 分钟的绩效改进计划 (PIP) 对话。过去 9 个月：Marcus 作为具名事件经理处理 3 起生产事件，其中 2 起超过 MAS 报告窗口 2 小时；Marcus 错过了 6 次月度平台评审中的 4 次；他的 squad 中 2 名高级工程师辞职，理由是其管理风格。决策：90 天 PIP、具名支持、可观察的退出触发、清晰的"如未改进则退出"边界。Sarah（集团 HR 业务伙伴）在线上做记录。

A PIP conversation is the most carefully scripted 60 minutes a senior leader runs. The pattern: facts first, gap second, plan third, support fourth, exit trigger last. Never a feeling, never a personality comment. Sarah is on the call because the conversation must survive an external review.

PIP 对话是高级管理者最精心策划的 60 分钟。模式：事实第一、差距第二、计划第三、支持第四、退出触发最后。绝无感觉，绝无人身评论。Sarah 在线是因为对话必须能扛过外部审查。

2. The PIP Conversation — 5 Sections, 60 Minutes

You (CIO): Marcus, thanks for coming. This is a structured performance conversation. Sarah from Group HR is on the call as a witness. Three things I want to cover today: observable gaps, the 90-day plan, and the exit trigger.Marcus，感谢参加。这是一场结构化绩效对话。集团 HR 的 Sarah 作为见证人在线。今天讲三件事：可观察的差距、90 天计划、退出触发。
You (CIO): Observable gaps. Three, with dates. Gap 1: production incident management. INC-2025-0871 (12 May), INC-2025-1102 (18 July), INC-2025-1394 (29 September) — you were the named IM for all three. Two of three breached the MAS 2-hour reporting window by 2 hours each. Gap 2: platform reviews. 4 of 6 monthly reviews missed (April, June, August, September). Gap 3: squad attrition. 2 senior engineers resigned in the last 6 months, both citing management style in their exit interview.可观察的差距。三项，附日期。差距 1：生产事件管理。INC-2025-0871（5 月 12 日）、INC-2025-1102（7 月 18 日）、INC-2025-1394（9 月 29 日）——你是三起的具名 IM。其中 2 起超过 MAS 2 小时报告窗口各 2 小时。差距 2：平台评审。6 次月度评审错过 4 次（4 月、6 月、8 月、9 月）。差距 3：squad 人员流失。过去 6 个月 2 名高级工程师辞职，两人均在离职面谈中提及管理风格。
Marcus: Marcus: I want to push back on the MAS breaches — both were the SOC escalation delay, not me.Marcus：我想就 MAS 违约提出异议——两起都是 SOC 升级延迟，不是我。
You (CIO): Fair point, and Sarah will reflect that in the notes. The MAS breach is shared with SOC, but as the named IM you own the escalation chain end-to-end, including chasing SOC within the 2-hour window. That is the gap. It is not that you caused the breach, it is that you did not own the chase.公平，Sarah 会把这点记入纪要。MAS 违约与 SOC 共同承担，但作为具名 IM，你要对端到端的升级链负责，包括在 2 小时窗口内追 SOC。这就是差距。不是你造成了违约，而是你没有主导追踪。
You (CIO): The 90-day plan. Three observable targets. Target 1: zero MAS reporting window breaches in the next 90 days. Target 2: 5 of 6 monthly platform reviews attended (1 missed allowed). Target 3: zero voluntary resignations in the squad in the next 90 days, and 2 'stay interviews' with the 4 remaining senior engineers by day 60.90 天计划。三个可观察目标。目标 1：未来 90 天零 MAS 报告窗口违约。目标 2：6 次月度平台评审出席 5 次（允许 1 次缺席）。目标 3：未来 90 天 squad 零主动辞职，第 60 天前对剩余 4 名高级工程师做 2 次"留任访谈"。
You (CIO): Named support. Three people, named. Support 1: Priya (Group PMO) will coach you on incident command, bi-weekly 1-hour for 90 days. Support 2: Daniel (Group CTO) will pair-review your platform reviews, the 5 you attend, 30 minutes each. Support 3: Sarah (HR) will co-facilitate the stay interviews, and a 360-feedback round at day 75.具名支持。三人，具名。支持 1：Priya（集团 PMO）辅导事件指挥，90 天内每两周 1 小时。支持 2：Daniel（集团 CTO）配对评审你出席的平台评审，5 次各 30 分钟。支持 3：Sarah（HR）共同主持留任访谈，第 75 天做一轮 360 反馈。
You (CIO): Exit trigger. If at day 90, two of the three targets are not met, we exit. Exit means role re-level to IC6, or alternative role search with 6 months' support, or managed exit with severance per Group policy. Sarah will walk you through the policy options after this meeting.退出触发。如果第 90 天三个目标中有两个未达成，我们退出。退出意味着岗位降级至 IC6，或 6 个月支持下的替代岗位搜索，或按集团政策带补偿的协商离职。Sarah 会在会后向你讲解政策选项。
You (CIO): One ask of you, Marcus. Read the plan, sign it by Wednesday, and let's start Monday week 2. I want this to work — you've been here 14 years and the bank is better for it. But the gaps are real and the targets are real.Marcus，对你一个请求。阅读计划，周三前签字，第 2 周一开始执行。我希望这能成功——你在这里 14 年了，银行因你而更好。但差距是真实的，目标也是真实的。
Marcus: Marcus: Understood, Wei. I'll read it tonight and sign by Wednesday.Marcus：明白，Wei。今晚阅读，周三前签字。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Structured performance conversation This is a structured performance conversation. Sarah from Group HR is on the call as a witness. 这是一场结构化绩效对话。集团 HR 的 Sarah 作为见证人在线 Open with the structure and the witness. The opening line signals this is not a chat — it is a documented event. Sarah's presence is a deal term.
  • Observable gaps, with dates Three observable gaps, with dates. INC-2025-0871, 12 May. 三项可观察的差距，附日期 "Observable" is the precise adjective. Personality traits are not observable; missed reviews and breach dates are. Always pair gap with date.
  • It is not that X, it is that Y It is not that you caused the breach, it is that you did not own the chase. 不是你造成了违约，而是你没有主导追踪 This phrase de-escalates. The PIP is about ownership, not blame. "It is not that X, it is that Y" is the de-escalation template.
  • Observable targets Three observable targets. Zero MAS breaches, 5 of 6 reviews, zero resignations. 三个可观察目标 Targets must be observable. "Improve incident management" is not observable. "Zero MAS breaches in 90 days" is observable.
  • Named support Three people, named. Priya, Daniel, Sarah. 三人，具名 Support is named, not generic. "Coaching will be provided" loses. "Priya will coach bi-weekly for 90 days" wins.
  • Exit trigger, two of three If at day 90, two of the three targets are not met, we exit. 如果第 90 天三个目标中有两个未达成，我们退出 The exit trigger is the most important sentence. "Two of three" is the precise number — not "if you don't improve". The number survives review.
  • I want this to work I want this to work. You've been here 14 years. 我希望这能成功 After the trigger, the human line. Senior leaders do not enjoy PIPs; saying "I want this to work" is the human line that makes the conversation survivable.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • PIP (Performance Improvement Plan) n. 绩效改进计划 A formal 90-day plan to close specific performance gaps.
  • Observable gap n. 可观察的差距 A performance gap that can be evidenced by date, number, or fact.
  • Named IM (Incident Manager) n. 具名事件经理 The person formally accountable for managing an incident.
  • MAS reporting window n. MAS 报告窗口 The maximum time between incident detection and regulator notification.
  • Exit interview n. 离职面谈 A conversation with a departing employee to understand why.
  • Stay interview n. 留任面谈 A conversation with a current employee to understand what keeps them.
  • 360 feedback n. 360 反馈 Anonymous feedback from manager, peers, and reports.
  • Exit trigger n. 退出触发 The specific, observable condition that ends the PIP unsuccessfully.
  • Role re-level to IC6 n. 岗位降级至 IC6 Moving from manager to individual contributor track.
  • Managed exit n. 协商离职 A negotiated departure with severance per policy.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Observable" — the precise adjective

"Observable" means a third party can verify it. Three observable gaps is a phrase that survives HR review. "Three concerns" loses; "three observable gaps with dates" wins. Pair the adjective with the noun.

Observable 意味着第三方可验证。

② "If at day 90, two of three" — the trigger number

A trigger is a number, not a feeling. If at day 90, two of three targets are not met, we exit. The number "two of three" survives appeal. "If you don't improve" loses on appeal. Always name the number and the date.

触发是数字，不是感觉。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Open with structure and witness. "Structured performance conversation" + "Sarah on the call" sets the tone. The opening sentence is the deal term.
  • Observable gaps with dates. Personality is not observable. Missed reviews and breach dates are. Pair the gap with the date and the source.
  • De-escalate with "It is not that X, it is that Y". Marcus's pushback is valid. Acknowledge it, reframe it, move on. The PIP is about ownership, not blame.
  • Three observable targets, not adjectives. "Zero MAS breaches, 5 of 6 reviews, zero resignations" is observable. "Be more proactive" is not.
  • Named support, three people. Support is named, not generic. "Priya, Daniel, Sarah" survives review. "Coaching will be provided" loses on appeal.
  • Exit trigger is the most important sentence. "If at day 90, two of three targets not met, we exit" is the precise trigger. Sarah writes it down.
  • End with the human line. "I want this to work, you've been here 14 years" is the human line. PIPs are professional, but the human line makes them survivable.
  • Sarah is on the call. PIPs without HR are PIPs that don't survive external review. Sarah is not optional.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the PIP plan

Translate the 90-day plan.

"The 90-day plan. Three observable targets. Target 1: zero MAS reporting window breaches in the next 90 days. Target 2: 5 of 6 monthly platform reviews attended (1 missed allowed). Target 3: zero voluntary resignations in the squad in the next 90 days, and 2 'stay interviews' with the 4 remaining senior engineers by day 60. Exit trigger: if at day 90, two of the three targets are not met, we exit."

"90 天计划。三个可观察目标。目标 1：未来 90 天零 MAS 报告窗口违约。目标 2：6 次月度平台评审出席 5 次（允许 1 次缺席）。目标 3：未来 90 天 squad 零主动辞职，第 60 天前对剩余 4 名高级工程师做 2 次"留任访谈"。退出触发：如果第 90 天三个目标中有两个未达成，我们退出。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the PIP mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a PIP conversation? Why?

  1. "Three observable gaps, with dates. INC-2025-0871, 12 May."
  2. "Marcus is a difficult person and the team finds him hard to work with."
  3. "Three observable targets. Zero MAS breaches, 5 of 6 reviews, zero resignations."
  4. "If at day 90, two of three targets are not met, we exit."

② is the credibility-damager. "Difficult person" is personality, not observable. It will be struck from the PIP record on review. ① pairs gaps with dates. ③ names observable targets. ④ names the trigger number. PIPs run on observable facts and trigger numbers, not personality.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute PIP opening

You are the CIO. Senior manager Alex: 4 of 5 quarterly SteerCo updates missed in 12 months, vendor SLA breach on 2 of 3 strategic vendors under his watch, 1 ethics complaint upheld. HR partner on the call. Open the conversation.

"Alex, thanks for coming. This is a structured performance conversation. Sarah from Group HR is on the call as a witness. Three observable gaps with dates. Gap 1: SteerCo updates — 4 of 5 missed in the last 12 months (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4). Gap 2: vendor SLA — 2 of 3 strategic vendors breached SLA, both under Alex's vendor management. Gap 3: 1 ethics complaint, upheld after investigation, Q3. The 90-day plan: Target 1, 4 of 4 SteerCo updates attended next quarter. Target 2, zero vendor SLA breach in 90 days, with monthly SLA review. Target 3, ethics refresher completed and 360 feedback round at day 75. Named support: Priya co-presents the next 2 SteerCos, Daniel pairs the SLA reviews, Sarah facilitates the 360. Exit trigger: if at day 90, two of three not met, we exit. Read the plan, sign by Friday, start Monday."
