Regretful Attrition Conversation — Senior Who No Longer Fits, Dignified Exit

Article 10 · Module 3: Difficult Leadership Conversations · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 Regretful attrition, dignified exit, named role, severance, transition plan

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 16:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You have a 60-minute conversation with Helen, a 19-year veteran and Group Head of Infrastructure, who is technically brilliant but no longer fits the chapter model. Helen's squad NPS is the lowest in the IT function (32 vs function average 58). The recent 360 feedback was honest: 8 of 12 respondents said Helen "does not empower the team". Helen has not been a people-leader problem in the past — she built the platform — but the chapter model requires people leaders who develop others, and Helen does not. The decision: a dignified, structured exit with a 9-month transition, severance per policy, and a named alumni status. Sarah (Group HR) and Daniel (Group CTO, Helen's line manager) are on the call.

周二 16:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你与Helen——一位 19 年老兵、集团基础设施负责人——进行 60 分钟对话。Helen 技术卓越但不再适配 chapter 模型。Helen 的 squad NPS 是 IT 函数中最低的（32 vs 函数平均 58）。最近的 360 反馈很坦率：12 位受访者中 8 位表示 Helen"不赋能团队"。Helen 过去不是人的领导问题——她建了平台——但 chapter 模型要求发展他人的 People Leader，Helen 不擅长。决策：体面、有结构的退出、9 个月过渡、按政策补偿、具名校友身份。Sarah（集团 HR）和Daniel（集团 CTO，Helen 的直线经理）在线。

Regretful attrition is the dignified exit conversation. The pattern: appreciation first, gap second, options third, decision fourth, transition last. Helen is not being fired for performance — she is being released because the role has changed. The language must reflect that.

遗憾离职是体面退出对话。模式：感谢第一、差距第二、选项第三、决策第四、过渡最后。Helen 不是因绩效被解雇——她是被释放，因为角色已变。语言必须反映这一点。

2. The Conversation — 5 Sections, 60 Minutes

You (CIO): Helen, thanks for coming. Daniel and Sarah are on the call. This is a structured conversation, and I want to open with appreciation because that comes first in this room.Helen，感谢参加。Daniel 和 Sarah 在线。这是一场结构化对话，我想先表达感谢，因为在这个房间里感谢排第一。
You (CIO): You joined in 2007 as a senior engineer, you built the Group's first cloud platform in 2014, you led the data centre consolidation in 2019, and you carried the platform through COVID in 2020-2022. The platform would not be where it is without you. That is on the record.你 2007 年加入任高级工程师，2014 年建了集团第一个云平台，2019 年主导了数据中心整合，2020-2022 年在 COVID 期间扛住了平台。没有你，平台不会到今天这个位置。这点已记录在案。
You (CIO): The gap. The chapter model is the operating model now. It requires chapter leads who develop people — coach, delegate, empower. Your 360 in May was honest. 8 of 12 respondents said you do not empower the team. Your squad NPS is 32, the function average is 58. This is not a performance problem, it is a fit problem. The role has changed; the role you took in 2018 is no longer the role of 2026.差距。Chapter 模型是现在的运营模型。它要求 chapter lead 发展人——辅导、授权、赋能。你 5 月的 360 反馈很坦率。12 位受访者中 8 位说你不赋能团队。你的 squad NPS 是 32，函数平均 58。这不是绩效问题，而是适配问题。角色变了；你 2018 年接的岗位不再是 2026 年的岗位。
Helen: Helen: I hear you, Wei. What's the proposal?Helen：我听到了，Wei。提议是什么？
You (CIO): Three options. Option 1: role re-level to Distinguished Engineer (IC7), a senior individual contributor role in the Cloud Platform chapter, focused on architecture and platform innovation, no people leadership. Same base, +12% bonus target. Option 2: alternative role search with 6 months' support, Sarah to lead the search, internal and external. Option 3: managed exit with severance per Group policy, 9 months' base, 6 months' benefits continuation, outplacement support, alumni status.三个选项。选项 1：岗位降级至 Distinguished Engineer (IC7)，云平台 chapter 的高级个人贡献者岗位，聚焦架构和平台创新，无人员领导。基本工资不变，奖金目标 +12%。选项 2：6 个月支持下的替代岗位搜索，Sarah 主导，内外同时。选项 3：按集团政策协商离职补偿，9 个月基本工资、6 个月福利延续、再就业支持、校友身份。
You (CIO): My recommendation, Helen, is Option 1 if you are open to it. You have built platforms; you can keep building. It also gives the team a clean chapter lead transition — Priya is ready, named successor. You and Priya can co-design the handover over the next 90 days. Daniel and I are committed to making Option 1 work for you.Helen，我的推荐是选项 1，前提是你愿意。你建过平台，你可以继续建。这也给团队一个清晰的 chapter lead 过渡——Priya 已就位，具名接班人。你与 Priya 可以在未来 90 天共同设计交接。Daniel 和我承诺让选项 1 对你可行。
Helen: Helen: I need to think about it. Can I have until Friday?Helen：我需要想一想。能否给到周五？
You (CIO): Take until Friday. Daniel and Sarah will give you the three option packs in writing by tomorrow, with the full numbers and the policy references. Whatever you choose, the transition is dignified, and the alumni door is open.可以到周五。Daniel 和 Sarah 明天会书面给你三套选项包，含完整数字和政策依据。无论你选什么，过渡都是体面的，校友之门是开着的。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Appreciation first, on the record Appreciation comes first in this room. That is on the record. 感谢排第一，这点已记录在案 Open with appreciation, name specific contributions, put it on the record. The opening line is the deal term — it sets the tone for the dignity of the conversation.
  • Not a performance problem, a fit problem This is not a performance problem, it is a fit problem. 这不是绩效问题，而是适配问题 "Fit problem" is the precise phrase. "Performance problem" is fired; "fit problem" is the role has changed. Sarah writes this down — it changes the policy path.
  • Three options, named Three options. Option 1: IC7. Option 2: alternative role search. Option 3: managed exit. 三个选项 Always present three options, never one. The CIO recommends, the employee chooses. The recommended option is the dignity-preserving path.
  • Same base, +12% bonus target Same base, +12% bonus target. 基本工资不变，奖金目标 +12% When proposing an IC role to a manager, the comp framing matters. "Same base + bonus uplift" signals respect; "less responsibility, less pay" is a demotion.
  • Named successor, co-design the handover Priya is ready, named successor. You and Priya co-design the handover. 具名接班人，共同设计交接 A named successor and a co-designed handover are the dignity signals. Helen is not pushed out; she is part of the transition design.
  • Take until Friday, the option packs in writing Take until Friday. Option packs in writing by tomorrow, with full numbers and policy references. 给到周五，明天书面选项包，附完整数字和政策依据 Time to decide + written options = a dignified process. Verbal-only options are not a process.
  • Alumni door is open The alumni door is open. 校友之门是开着的 The alumni door is the bank's way of saying: you are still one of us, just no longer in this role. Helen leaves the bank, the bank does not leave her.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Regretful attrition n. 遗憾离职 An exit the bank regrets because the person is good but no longer fits.
  • Chapter model n. Chapter 模型 The operating model that requires people leaders who develop others.
  • Fit problem n. 适配问题 The role has changed; the person is good, the role is no longer the right one.
  • Distinguished Engineer (IC7) n. 杰出工程师 (IC7) A senior individual contributor role for technical leaders.
  • Alternative role search n. 替代岗位搜索 A supported search for a different role, internal or external.
  • Managed exit n. 协商离职 A negotiated departure with severance and outplacement.
  • Severance per Group policy n. 按集团政策补偿 The standard severance package.
  • Outplacement support n. 再就业支持 Career coaching and job search assistance for departing employees.
  • Alumni status n. 校友身份 Ongoing connection to the bank after departure.
  • Named successor n. 具名接班人 The specific person who will take over the role.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Not X, it is Y" — the de-escalation template

Regretful attrition conversations need de-escalation. This is not a performance problem, it is a fit problem. The "not X, it is Y" template moves the conversation from blame to fit, which changes the policy path. Sarah writes it down.

遗憾离职对话需要降级。Not X, it is Y模板将对话从指责转为适配，改变政策路径。

② "Three options" — the option triplet

Always present three options in a regretful attrition. One option is not a choice. Two options is binary. Three options is a process: stay as IC, find another role, leave with dignity. The CIO recommends; the employee chooses.

遗憾离职总是给三个选项。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Appreciation first, on the record. Name the specific contributions, with years and projects. The opening sentence sets the tone.
  • Fit problem, not performance problem. The role has changed, not the person. Sarah writes it down — it changes the policy path and the dignity of the conversation.
  • Three options, named and written. One option is not a choice. Three options with comp details and policy references are a process.
  • Comp framing respects the person. "Same base + bonus uplift" signals respect. "Less responsibility, less pay" is a demotion in disguise.
  • Named successor, co-design the handover. Helen is part of the transition design, not pushed out. The handover is co-designed, not imposed.
  • Time to decide + written options. Friday decision date, written option packs by tomorrow. Verbal-only options are not a process.
  • Alumni door is open. Helen leaves the bank, the bank does not leave her. Alumni status is the bank's dignity commitment.
  • Daniel and Sarah are on the call. The line manager and HR together. The line manager carries the operational message; HR carries the policy.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the three options

Translate the three options.

"Three options. Option 1: role re-level to Distinguished Engineer (IC7), a senior individual contributor role in the Cloud Platform chapter, focused on architecture and platform innovation, no people leadership. Same base, +12% bonus target. Option 2: alternative role search with 6 months' support, Sarah to lead the search, internal and external. Option 3: managed exit with severance per Group policy, 9 months' base, 6 months' benefits continuation, outplacement support, alumni status."

"三个选项。选项 1：岗位降级至杰出工程师 (IC7)，云平台 chapter 的高级个人贡献者岗位，聚焦架构和平台创新，无人员领导。基本工资不变，奖金目标 +12%。选项 2：6 个月支持下的替代岗位搜索，Sarah 主导，内外同时。选项 3：按集团政策协商离职补偿，9 个月基本工资、6 个月福利延续、再就业支持、校友身份。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the regretful attrition mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a regretful attrition conversation? Why?

  1. "Appreciation comes first. You built the platform. That is on the record."
  2. "Helen is not performing, we have to let her go."
  3. "This is not a performance problem, it is a fit problem. The role has changed."
  4. "Three options: IC7, alternative role search, managed exit. Written packs tomorrow."

② is the credibility-damager. "Not performing, let her go" is a performance problem, not a fit problem. The conversation just became a firing. ① opens with appreciation. ③ reframes as fit. ④ presents three written options. Regretful attrition runs on appreciation, fit, and options — not on performance language.

Option 1: IC7 Distinguished Engineer, regional data platform, focus on architecture, same base, +10% bonus. Option 2: alternative role search, 6 months' support, internal and external. Option 3: managed exit, 9 months' base, alumni status. Recommendation: Option 1, named successor Mei, co-design handover. Take until Friday. Written option packs tomorrow."
