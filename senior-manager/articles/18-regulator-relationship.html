Regulator Relationship — HKMA, MAS, PRA — Supervisor Meeting, Supervisory Letter, Remediation

Article 18 · Module 5: Bank Leadership Context · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 Regulator relationship, HKMA, MAS, PRA, supervisory letter, remediation, attestation

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 10:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You are chairing the monthly Regulator Relationship Committee for the next 90 minutes. The agenda: HKMA Q1 supervisory meeting debrief, MAS TRM deep-dive prep, PRA operational resilience attestation status, and remediation of 2 outstanding supervisory letters. The committee is Group CRO (Sarah), Group CISO (David), Group General Counsel (Linda), Group Head of Compliance (Karen), and the 4 regional CIOs. One decision: approve the 90-day remediation plan and the regulator-facing language for the HKMA Q1 letter.

周三 10:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你主持 90 分钟的月度监管关系委员会。议程：HKMA Q1 监管会议复盘、MAS TRM 深度审查准备、PRA 运营韧性合规声明状态、2 项未关闭监管函的修复。委员：集团 CRO（Sarah）、集团 CISO（David）、集团总法律顾问（Linda）、集团合规负责人（Karen）和 4 位区域 CIO。一个决策：批准 90 天修复计划和 HKMA Q1 监管函的对外语言。

The regulator relationship is the single most important external relationship of a CIO. The pattern: supervisor meeting first, supervisory letter second, remediation third, attestation cycle last. Speak the regulator's language: supervisory letter, remediation, attestation, control, evidence pack.

监管关系是 CIO 最重要的外部关系。模式：supervisor 会议第一、监管函第二、修复第三、合规声明周期最后。说监管的语言：supervisory letter、remediation、attestation、control、evidence pack。

2. The Committee — 4 Sections, 90 Minutes

You (CIO): HKMA Q1 supervisory meeting debrief. Met HKMA supervisor Mr. Chan on 14 January, 90 minutes, in HKMA's office. Three asks out of the meeting. Ask 1: cloud exit cost concentration risk register, due 31 March. Ask 2: FIDO2 rollout gap on 28% of privileged accounts, remediation plan by 30 April. Ask 3: cyber resilience attestation with named evidence pack, due 30 June.HKMA Q1 监管会议复盘。1 月 14 日与 HKMA supervisor 陈先生会面，90 分钟，HKMA 办公室。会议提出 3 项要求。要求 1：云退出成本集中度风险登记，3 月 31 日前。要求 2：28% 特权账户 FIDO2 推广缺口，4 月 30 日前提交修复计划。要求 3：网络韧性合规声明附具名证据包，6 月 30 日前。
You (CIO): MAS TRM deep dive, 22 February. MAS supervisor will visit for a full day on the 14 cyber hygiene domains. Pre-read sent 8 February. Named attendees from Group: Sarah (CRO), David (CISO), Wei (CIO), Linda (Legal), Karen (Compliance). Walk-through plan: 30 minutes per domain, 14 domains in 7 hours. Domain leads pre-briefed, evidence pack pre-loaded.MAS TRM 深度审查，2 月 22 日。MAS supervisor 将全天拜访，覆盖 14 个网络卫生域。2 月 8 日已发预读。集团具名参会人：Sarah（CRO）、David（CISO）、Wei（CIO）、Linda（法务）、Karen（合规）。走查计划：每域 30 分钟，14 域共 7 小时。域负责人已预演，证据包已预装。
You (CIO): PRA operational resilience attestation. UK PRA expects attestation by 31 March 2026, under the new operational resilience regime. Important business services mapped: 12, impact tolerances set for each, self-assessment 80% complete, 3 services in remediation, all within tolerance. Pre-read shared with the UK CIO and the UK CRO last week.PRA 运营韧性合规声明。英国 PRA 期望 2026 年 3 月 31 日前完成合规声明，按新运营韧性制度。重要业务服务已映射：12 个，每个设影响容忍度，自评完成 80%，3 个服务在修复中，均在容忍度内。上周已与英国 CIO 和英国 CRO 共享预读。
You (CIO): 2 outstanding supervisory letters. Letter 1: HKMA, 14 October 2025, on the cloud config incident. Original remediation date 31 January 2026. Status: 70% remediated, 2 of 7 control items still in flight, request 30-day extension to 2 March. Letter 2: MAS, 4 November 2025, on the FIDO2 rollout gap on customer service reps. Original remediation date 28 February 2026. Status: on track, evidence pack in preparation, closure confirmed for 28 February.2 项未关闭监管函。函 1：HKMA，2025 年 10 月 14 日，关于云配置事件。原定修复日期 2026 年 1 月 31 日。状态：已修复 70%，7 项控制项中 2 项仍在进行，申请 30 天延期至 3 月 2 日。函 2：MAS，2025 年 11 月 4 日，关于客户服务代表 FIDO2 推广缺口。原定修复日期 2026 年 2 月 28 日。状态：按计划进行，证据包编制中，2 月 28 日确认关闭。
You (CIO): 90-day plan. Three commitments. Commitment 1: HKMA cloud concentration register by 31 March, owner Sarah, evidence pack pre-cleared with Linda. Commitment 2: HKMA FIDO2 remediation plan by 30 April, owner David, named 28% → 8% gap closure in 3 milestones. Commitment 3: HKMA cyber resilience attestation by 30 June, owner David + Sarah, evidence pack from the 14 cyber hygiene domains. One request: the HKMA extension for Letter 1 to 2 March, pre-cleared with Linda on language, to be sent to supervisor by Friday.90 天计划。三项承诺。承诺 1：3 月 31 日前提交 HKMA 云集中度登记，负责人 Sarah，证据包由 Linda 预审。承诺 2：4 月 30 日前提交 HKMA FIDO2 修复计划，负责人 David，具名 28% → 8% 缺口关闭分 3 个里程碑。承诺 3：6 月 30 日前提交 HKMA 网络韧性合规声明，负责人 David + Sarah，证据包来自 14 个网络卫生域。一个请求：HKMA 函 1 延期至 3 月 2 日，语言由 Linda 预审，周五前发出给 supervisor。
Sarah (CRO): Sarah: 90-day plan approved. One condition: all supervisor-facing language pre-cleared with me and Linda before any external commitment. HKMA extension letter drafted by Thursday, sent Friday.Sarah：90 天计划批准。一个条件：所有 supervisor-facing 语言在对外承诺前由我和 Linda 预审。HKMA 延期函周四起草，周五发出。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Three asks out of the meeting Three asks out of the meeting. Ask 1: cloud exit cost register by 31 March. 会议提出 3 项要求。要求 1：3 月 31 日前云退出成本登记 Supervisor meetings always produce named asks with dates. "It went well" loses; "3 asks with due dates" wins.
  • Evidence pack, pre-loaded Domain leads pre-briefed, evidence pack pre-loaded. 域负责人已预演，证据包已预装 "Pre-loaded" is the precise adjective. An evidence pack that is not pre-loaded is a pack that fails the audit. The regulator remembers the pre-load.
  • Important business services, impact tolerances Important business services: 12. Impact tolerances set for each. 重要业务服务 12 个，每个设影响容忍度 PRA language. "Important business services" and "impact tolerances" are the regulator's terms. The CRO and the CIO use them; the regulator recognises them.
  • Original remediation date, request extension Original remediation date 31 January. Request 30-day extension to 2 March. 原定修复日期 1 月 31 日，申请 30 天延期至 3 月 2 日 Pair the original date with the new date. The supervisor reads dates; the extension request must have both.
  • Owner, evidence pack, pre-cleared Owner Sarah, evidence pack pre-cleared with Linda. 负责人 Sarah，证据包由 Linda 预审 Every commitment has three: owner, evidence pack, pre-clearance. "We'll get it done" loses; the three together win.
  • All supervisor-facing language pre-cleared All supervisor-facing language pre-cleared with me and Linda before any external commitment. 所有 supervisor-facing 语言在对外承诺前由我和 Linda 预审 "Supervisor-facing language" is the precise phrase. The CRO and Legal co-own the language; the CIO and the CISO propose, the CRO and Legal pre-clear.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Supervisor meeting n. Supervisor 会议 A scheduled meeting with a regulator supervisor.
  • Supervisory letter n. 监管函 A formal written communication from a regulator.
  • Ask (out of a meeting) n. 要求 A specific request from the regulator, with a date.
  • Remediation n. 修复 The act of fixing a regulatory finding.
  • Original remediation date n. 原定修复日期 The date the regulator first set for closure.
  • Extension request n. 延期申请 A formal request to push the remediation date.
  • Evidence pack n. 证据包 A folder of artefacts (decks, screenshots, logs) that prove controls.
  • Attestation n. 合规声明 A formal declaration that a control is in place.
  • Important business services n. 重要业务服务 The services the regulator considers critical.
  • Impact tolerances n. 影响容忍度 The maximum acceptable disruption to a service.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Three asks, with dates" — the supervisor meeting output

Supervisor meetings always produce three asks, with dates. The committee buys the asks; the supervisor reads the dates. "It went well" loses; "3 asks with due dates" wins.

Supervisor 会议总是产出 三项要求，附日期。

② "Owner, evidence pack, pre-cleared" — the commitment triplet

Every regulator commitment is a triplet: owner, evidence pack, pre-cleared with named role. The triplet is the deal term. The regulator buys the triplet.

每个监管承诺是三元：负责人、证据包、与具名角色预审。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Three asks, with dates. Supervisor meetings produce named asks with due dates. "It went well" loses; "3 asks with dates" wins.
  • Evidence pack, pre-loaded. An evidence pack that is not pre-loaded is a pack that fails the audit. The regulator remembers the pre-load.
  • Use the regulator's language. "Important business services" and "impact tolerances" are PRA terms. The CRO uses them; the regulator recognises them.
  • Pair original date with new date in extensions. The supervisor reads dates. The extension must have both.
  • Owner, evidence pack, pre-cleared. The commitment triplet. The regulator buys the triplet.
  • Supervisor-facing language pre-cleared with CRO + Legal. The CRO and Legal co-own the language. The CIO and CISO propose; the CRO and Legal pre-clear.
  • Letter drafted Thursday, sent Friday. Named dates for the letter workflow. "Soon" loses; "Thursday, Friday" wins.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the 90-day plan

Translate the 90-day plan.

"90-day plan. Three commitments. Commitment 1: HKMA cloud concentration register by 31 March, owner Sarah, evidence pack pre-cleared with Linda. Commitment 2: HKMA FIDO2 remediation plan by 30 April, owner David, named 28% → 8% gap closure in 3 milestones. Commitment 3: HKMA cyber resilience attestation by 30 June, owner David + Sarah, evidence pack from the 14 cyber hygiene domains. One request: the HKMA extension for Letter 1 to 2 March, pre-cleared with Linda on language, to be sent to supervisor by Friday."

"90 天计划。三项承诺。承诺 1：3 月 31 日前提交 HKMA 云集中度登记，负责人 Sarah，证据包由 Linda 预审。承诺 2：4 月 30 日前提交 HKMA FIDO2 修复计划，负责人 David，具名 28% → 8% 缺口关闭分 3 个里程碑。承诺 3：6 月 30 日前提交 HKMA 网络韧性合规声明，负责人 David + Sarah，证据包来自 14 个网络卫生域。一个请求：HKMA 函 1 延期至 3 月 2 日，语言由 Linda 预审，周五前发出给 supervisor。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the regulator mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a regulator committee? Why?

  1. "The regulator meeting was fine."
  2. "Three asks out of the meeting, with dates. Evidence pack pre-loaded."
  3. "Owner Sarah, evidence pack, pre-cleared with Linda."
  4. "All supervisor-facing language pre-cleared with CRO and Legal before any external commitment."

① is the credibility-damager. "The regulator meeting was fine" is impression, not committee output. ② names three asks with dates and pre-loads the evidence. ③ is the commitment triplet. ④ states the pre-clearance rule. Regulator committees run on asks with dates, evidence packs, and pre-clearance — not on impressions.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute regulator debrief

You are the CIO. HKMA supervisor met yesterday, 2 asks: SOC 24/7 by 30 June, MAS attestation refresh by 31 July. Pitch the 90-day plan.

"HKMA debrief. 2 asks out of the meeting. Ask 1: SOC 24/7 by 30 June. Ask 2: MAS attestation refresh by 31 July. 90-day plan. Commitment 1: SOC 24/7 by 30 June, owner David (CISO), 3 milestones (people, process, tooling), evidence pack pre-loaded from the 14 cyber hygiene domains. Commitment 2: MAS attestation refresh by 31 July, owner Sarah (CRO) + David, evidence pack from the prior cycle. All supervisor-facing language pre-cleared with Sarah and Linda before external. HKMA follow-up letter drafted Thursday, sent Friday, with the 30 June and 31 July commitments, owner and evidence pack pre-cleared."
