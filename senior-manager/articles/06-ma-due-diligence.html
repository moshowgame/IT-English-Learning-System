M&A IT Due Diligence — Tech Debt, Regulator Readiness, Integration Cost, Risk Rating

Article 06 · Module 2: Strategic Decisions · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 Tech debt, regulator readiness, integration cost, risk rating, deal go / no-go

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 10:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. The Group M&A Committee meets in 4 hours to decide whether to bid $1.2B for a mid-tier regional bank (Target Bank, 2.4M retail customers, 320 branches across Hong Kong and mainland China). You have spent 3 weeks on the IT due diligence. The findings sit in a 60-page IT DD report; you have 10 minutes to present to the Committee. Present: Group CEO, Group CFO, Group CRO, Head of M&A, Group General Counsel. One decision: IT go / no-go for the bid.

周三 10:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。集团并购委员会4 小时后开会，决定是否以 12 亿美元收购一家区域中型银行（目标银行，香港和中国大陆 240 万零售客户、320 家网点）。你花了 3 周做 IT 尽职调查。结论装在60 页 IT DD 报告里；你有 10 分钟向委员会汇报。参会：集团 CEO、集团 CFO、集团 CRO、并购负责人、集团总法律顾问。一个决策：IT 是否支持竞标。

IT DD is the silent veto in any bank M&A. The pattern: tech debt first, regulator readiness second, integration cost third, risk rating fourth, recommendation last. The Committee will not buy the bank if the IT is a 3-year remediation project.

IT DD 是任何银行并购中的静默否决者。模式：技术债第一、监管就绪第二、集成成本第三、风险评级第四、推荐最后。如果 IT 是 3 年修复项目，委员会不会买。

2. The IT DD Read-out — 5 Sections, 10 Minutes

You (CIO): Tech debt. Target Bank runs 4 core systems: a 22-year-old mainframe deposits, an 11-year-old loans platform, a 7-year-old digital channel (mobile + web), and a 4-year-old data lake. Mainframe is the issue — 12.4M lines of COBOL, 3 staff who can maintain it, no documentation on 38% of modules. End-of-life 2027, no vendor support after that.技术债。目标银行跑 4 个核心系统：22 岁的主机存款、11 岁的贷款平台、7 岁的数字渠道（移动 + Web）、4 岁的数据湖。主机是大问题——1,240 万行 COBOL、3 名维护人员、38% 模块无文档。2027 年生命周期结束，之后无厂商支持。
You (CIO): Regulator readiness. Target Bank has 2 open supervisory letters from HKMA, 1 from CBIRC, and 3 MAS findings on cyber hygiene. None are Tier-1 findings, but 4 are past their original remediation date — that's a 'yellow flag' for the HKMA supervisor.监管就绪。目标银行有 2 封 HKMA 未关闭监管函、1 封 CBIRC、3 项 MAS 网络卫生发现。均非 Tier-1 发现，但 4 项已超过原定修复日期——对 HKMA supervisor 是"黄旗"。
You (CIO): Integration cost. 5-year integration: $340M. Mainframe decommission: $120M. Core banking consolidation onto Group platform: $140M. Cyber uplift to Group baseline: $42M. Data migration: $38M. Integration FTE: 180 over 3 years.集成成本。5 年集成：3.4 亿。主机下线：1.2 亿。核心银行整合到集团平台：1.4 亿。网络提升至集团基线：4,200 万。数据迁移：3,800 万。集成 FTE：3 年 180 个。
Group CRO: CRO: Cyber DD?CRO：网络 DD？
You (CIO): Cyber DD rating: Amber. Target runs 47 unpatched critical CVEs, no MFA on 23% of privileged accounts, no SOC, only log collection. Remediation to Group baseline: 14 months, $42M, must complete before customer data is consolidated.网络 DD 评级：Amber。目标有 47 个未修补关键 CVE，23% 特权账户无 MFA，无 SOC，只有日志采集。提升至集团基线：14 个月、4,200 万，必须在客户数据整合前完成。
You (CIO): Risk rating: Amber overall. Deal is acquirable, but only with conditions. Three conditions. Condition 1: $42M cyber uplift, complete before Day 1 customer migration. Condition 2: 4 open supervisory findings remediated within 12 months of close. Condition 3: mainframe decommission funded, $120M over Years 2-3. Recommendation: bid, with conditions, $1.2B bid price acceptable if conditions are written into the SPA.风险评级：总体 Amber。交易可收购，但有条件。三个条件。条件 1：4,200 万网络提升，在 Day 1 客户迁移前完成。条件 2：4 个未关闭监管发现在交割后 12 个月内修复。条件 3：主机下线资金到位，第 2-3 年 1.2 亿。推荐：在条件下竞标，12 亿报价可接受，前提是条件写入 SPA。
Group CEO: CEO: Conditions accepted in principle. M&A lead to draft the SPA language by Friday, with Wei, Sarah and Daniel as reviewers.CEO：原则接受条件。并购负责人周五前起草 SPA 措辞，Wei、Sarah、Daniel 评审。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Tech debt, end-of-life 22-year-old mainframe, 12.4M lines of COBOL, end-of-life 2027, no vendor support after that. 22 岁主机、1,240 万行 COBOL、2027 年生命周期结束、之后无厂商支持 Pair the age with end-of-life. A 22-year-old mainframe is fine if vendor support extends; a 22-year-old with no support is a deal-breaker.
  • Open supervisory letters, past remediation date 2 open supervisory letters from HKMA, 4 past their original remediation date. 2 封未关闭 HKMA 监管函、4 项超过原定修复日期 "Past their original remediation date" is the precise phrase. The regulator tracks dates; so do you.
  • Integration cost, 5-year 5-year integration: $340M. Mainframe decommission: $120M. 5 年集成：3.4 亿，主机下线：1.2 亿 Always break the integration cost into named workstreams. The CFO approves a cost when each line has a number and a workstream.
  • Risk rating: Amber Cyber DD rating: Amber. 47 unpatched critical CVEs. 网络 DD 评级：Amber，47 个未修补关键 CVE A traffic-light rating (Green / Amber / Red) is the language of M&A committees. The committee buys / declines on the colour, not the pages.
  • Bid with conditions Bid, with conditions. Three conditions written into the SPA. 在条件下竞标，三个条件写入 SPA "Bid with conditions" is the M&A phrase. Conditions are written into the Sale and Purchase Agreement so they survive price negotiations.
  • Day 1 customer migration Complete cyber uplift before Day 1 customer migration. 在 Day 1 客户迁移前完成网络提升 "Day 1" is the moment customer data is moved. Cyber uplift is non-negotiable before that date — never after.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • IT DD (Due Diligence) n. IT 尽职调查 The IT-side investigation before an acquisition.
  • Tech debt n. 技术债 The legacy cost of older systems that slow delivery and risk resilience.
  • End-of-life (EOL) n. 生命周期结束 The date after which a vendor will not support a product.
  • Supervisory letter n. 监管函 A formal written communication from a regulator.
  • Integration cost n. 集成成本 The cost of merging target IT into the acquirer's IT.
  • Mainframe decommission n. 主机下线 The planned shutdown of a mainframe system.
  • Day 1 n. Day 1 The first day after deal close, when the target is legally part of the acquirer.
  • SPA (Sale and Purchase Agreement) n. 买卖协议 The legal contract for the deal.
  • Risk rating: Amber n. 风险评级：Amber A traffic-light rating — proceed with conditions.
  • Privileged account n. 特权账户 An account with administrative access.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Bid, with conditions" — the M&A decision verb

The M&A decision is never a clean yes or no. Bid, with conditions. Declined, regulatory risk too high. Bid, price renegotiated. "Bid / declined" is the verb; "with conditions" is the modifier that survives price renegotiation.

并购决策从来不是干净的是或否。Bid, with conditions。Bid 是动词，with conditions 是修饰语，存活于价格重谈。

② "Past their original remediation date" — the precise past-tense phrase

"Past their date" is the precise phrase. "Overdue" is informal; "behind schedule" is ambiguous. Past their original remediation date is the regulator's own language. The committee writes minutes that survive audit.

Past their original remediation date是监管自己的语言。委员会写的会议纪要要能扛过审计。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Tech debt has an age and an EOL date. "Old mainframe" loses. "22-year-old mainframe, EOL 2027, no vendor support after that" wins.
  • Supervisory letters and findings have dates. Track original remediation date, current status, and the regulator who issued them. The committee reads dates.
  • Integration cost is a workstream, not a number. Break $340M into mainframe decommission, core consolidation, cyber uplift, data migration, FTE. The CFO approves workstreams.
  • Use traffic-light ratings. Green / Amber / Red is the M&A committee language. Avoid prose like "moderate concerns".
  • Bid with conditions, write them into the SPA. A condition that is not in the SPA is a verbal promise. The SPA survives price renegotiation; verbal conditions do not.
  • Day 1 is the milestone, not close. Customer data migration happens at Day 1, not close. Cyber uplift must complete before Day 1, not before close.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the IT DD recommendation

Translate the recommendation sentence.

"Risk rating: Amber overall. Deal is acquirable, but only with conditions. Three conditions. Condition 1: $42M cyber uplift, complete before Day 1 customer migration. Condition 2: 4 open supervisory findings remediated within 12 months of close. Condition 3: mainframe decommission funded, $120M over Years 2-3. Recommendation: bid, with conditions, $1.2B bid price acceptable if conditions are written into the SPA."

"风险评级：总体 Amber。交易可收购，但有条件。三个条件。条件 1：4,200 万网络提升，在 Day 1 客户迁移前完成。条件 2：4 个未关闭监管发现在交割后 12 个月内修复。条件 3：主机下线资金到位，第 2-3 年 1.2 亿。推荐：在条件下竞标，12 亿报价可接受，前提是条件写入 SPA。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the IT DD mistake

Which line would lose credibility in an M&A IT DD read-out? Why?

  1. "22-year-old mainframe, EOL 2027, no vendor support after that."
  2. "The IT is a bit old, but probably fine."
  3. "Cyber DD rating: Amber. 47 unpatched critical CVEs."
  4. "Bid, with conditions, written into the SPA."

② is the credibility-damager. "A bit old, probably fine" is a feeling, not a DD. ① pairs age with EOL. ③ uses a traffic-light rating and a number. ④ makes the conditions legal. M&A committees buy ratings and conditions, not feelings.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute IT DD read-out

You are the CIO. Target is a 18-year-old core banking system, EOL 2028, 9M lines of COBOL, 4 staff. 3 open supervisory letters, 2 past remediation date. Integration: $280M, 150 FTE. Cyber: Red. Recommendation?

"Tech debt: 18-year-old core banking, EOL 2028, 9M lines of COBOL, 4 staff. Regulator readiness: 3 open supervisory letters, 2 past remediation date. Integration: $280M, 150 FTE. Cyber: Red — full Group baseline uplift required, $52M, 18 months. Risk rating: Amber-Red. Recommendation: bid with 4 conditions. Condition 1: $52M cyber uplift complete before Day 1. Condition 2: 2 past-date findings remediated within 9 months of close. Condition 3: core banking decommission funded, $130M Years 2-4. Condition 4: $8M retention package for the 4 COBOL staff through decommission. Bid price acceptable only if all 4 conditions in the SPA."
