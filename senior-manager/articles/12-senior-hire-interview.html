Senior Hire Final Round — Director / MD Level, Competency, Scenario, Fit

Article 12 · Module 3: Difficult Leadership Conversations · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 Senior hire final round, competency, scenario, fit, comp, decision

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 14:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. You are chairing the final-round interview for the Group Head of Cloud Platform (VP) role (Article 08). Two finalists: Candidate A (internal, currently Senior Director at a global tech company, 18 years' experience, deep cloud architecture), Candidate B (external, currently VP Cloud at a major US bank, 14 years' experience, strong regulator-facing). The interview panel: Group CTO (Daniel, line), Group CRO (Sarah, cyber & regulator), Group CHRO (Mark, people & comp). 90 minutes per candidate. One decision per candidate: hire, hold, or decline.

周五 14:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。你主持集团云平台负责人 (VP) 岗位（文章 08）的终面。两位候选人：候选人 A（内部候选人，现任全球科技公司高级总监，18 年经验，云架构深厚），候选人 B（外部候选人，现任美国大银行云 VP，14 年经验，监管应对强）。面试小组：集团 CTO（Daniel，业务线）、集团 CRO（Sarah，网络与监管）、集团 CHRO（Mark，人才与薪酬）。每位候选人 90 分钟。每位一个决策：录用、暂缓或拒绝。

A senior hire final round is the most consequential 180 minutes in a CIO's hiring calendar. The pattern: competency first, scenario second, fit third, comp fourth, decision last. The panel votes, the CIO decides. The CHRO owns the comp, the CRO owns the regulator check, the line manager owns the competency.

高级岗位终面是 CIO 招聘日历上影响最大的 180 分钟。模式：能力第一、场景第二、匹配第三、薪酬第四、决策最后。小组投票，CIO 决策。CHRO 拥有薪酬，CRO 拥有监管检查，直线经理拥有能力评估。

2. The Final Round — 4 Sections, 90 Minutes per Candidate

You (CIO): Thanks for joining, A. Four sections, 90 minutes. Competency, scenario, fit, comp. Daniel will lead competency, I will lead scenario, Sarah will lead fit and regulator check, Mark will lead comp at the end. One decision at the end: hire, hold, or decline.A，感谢参加。四个部分，90 分钟。能力、场景、匹配、薪酬。Daniel 主导能力，我主导场景，Sarah 主导匹配和监管检查，Mark 最后主导薪酬。最后一项决策：录用、暂缓或拒绝。
Daniel (CTO): Competency. Walk me through your largest multi-region cloud migration — scale, timeline, top 3 challenges, your specific ownership.能力。讲讲你做过的最大规模多区域云迁移——规模、时间线、前 3 大挑战、你的具体职责。
Candidate A: A: 2018-2021, $180M programme, 47 workloads to AWS, 18 months. Owned the architecture, vendor management, exec steering. Top 3 challenges: 1) regulator data localisation mid-flight, 2) mainframe decommission sequencing, 3) cloud cost overrun 22% above plan in Year 1. Course-corrected cost with FinOps, 14% above plan by Year 2.A：2018-2021，1.8 亿项目群，47 个工作负载上 AWS，18 个月。负责架构、厂商管理、高层 steering。前 3 大挑战：1) 监管数据本地化中途插入，2) 主机下线排序，3) 第 1 年云成本超支 22%。通过 FinOps 修正，第 2 年超支 14%。
You (CIO): Scenario. Day 1 in the role. HKMA supervisor emails you directly about a cyber finding in the Group cloud — 7 unpatched critical CVEs in production, regulator expects remediation in 14 days. You have no prior relationship with the supervisor. Walk me through your first 72 hours, end to end.场景。入职第 1 天。HKMA supervisor 直接给你发邮件，提到集团云中的一个网络发现——生产环境 7 个未修补关键 CVE，监管期望 14 天内修复。你与 supervisor 没有既有关系。讲讲你前 72 小时，端到端。
Candidate A: A: First 24 hours: read the email carefully, classify the 7 CVEs by exploitability and exposure, build a remediation plan with named owner, target dates, regulatory-aligned. Reply within 4 hours with acknowledgement and a plan-to-reply-by-Friday. First 48 hours: call the supervisor — 30 minutes, structured — facts, plan, named owner, weekly cadence. First 72 hours: deliver the plan, get sign-off, schedule weekly updates until 100% closed.A：前 24 小时：仔细阅读邮件，按可利用性和暴露面给 7 个 CVE 分类，起草带具名负责人、目标日期、与监管对齐的修复计划。4 小时内回复，确认收悉并承诺周五前给详细计划。前 48 小时：给 supervisor 打电话——30 分钟，结构化——事实、计划、具名负责人、每周节奏。前 72 小时：交付计划、获签批、安排每周更新直到 100% 关闭。
Sarah (CRO): Sarah: Fit and regulator check. You will be the public face of our cloud platform to HKMA, MAS, PRA. What is your regulator relationship model?Sarah：匹配和监管检查。你将是云平台面向 HKMA、MAS、PRA 的公开代表。你的监管关系模型是什么？
Candidate A: A: Three principles. 1) Proactive, not reactive — call before the supervisor calls. 2) Single point of accountability — the supervisor has one phone number. 3) Documented cadence — weekly update on open items, monthly on the portfolio, annual attestation cycle aligned with the supervisor's calendar.A：三条原则。1) 主动而非被动——在 supervisor 来电之前打过去。2) 单一问责点——supervisor 有一个电话号码。3) 文档化节奏——未关闭项每周更新、组合每月、年度合规声明周期与 supervisor 日历对齐。
Mark (CHRO): Mark: Comp. Base band VP, $380K-$450K HKD, target bonus 35%. Long-term incentive plan: 3-year vesting, Group standard. Are you in the band?Mark：薪酬。基本工资 VP 段，38 万-45 万港币，目标奖金 35%。长期激励：3 年归属，集团标准。是否在段内？
Candidate A: A: In the band, slightly above current, expected. Decision timeline?A：在段内，略高于现状，符合预期。决策时间线？
You (CIO): Panel discussion offline. Daniel: deep cloud, regulator fit medium, 18 years. Sarah: regulator model strong, fit is good. Mark: in band. My read: A is a strong hire — deep technical, regulator principles aligned, in comp band. Risk: regulator relationship is unproven in HKMA specifically. Mitigation: 90-day buddy with the current Group Head of Cloud, weekly regulator ride-along. Decision: hire A, target start in 90 days, with the 90-day buddy as a named condition.小组线下讨论。Daniel：云深度强、监管匹配中等、18 年。Sarah：监管模型强，匹配良好。Mark：在段内。我的评估：A 是强力录用——技术深、监管原则对齐、薪酬在段内。风险：HKMA 监管关系未经证明。缓解：与现任集团云负责人 90 天 buddy、每周监管陪同。决策：录用 A，目标 90 天入职，90 天 buddy 作为具名条件。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Four sections, four owners Four sections, four owners. Daniel competency, I scenario, Sarah fit, Mark comp. 四个部分，四个负责人 Each section has a named owner. "Panel interview" is vague. "Daniel leads competency, I lead scenario" is a process.
  • Walk me through X — the open-ended opener Walk me through your largest multi-region cloud migration. 讲讲你做过的…… "Walk me through" is the open-ended opener. It lets the candidate structure the story; the panel listens for ownership, scale, and challenges.
  • Day 1 scenario, regulator-facing Day 1 in the role. HKMA supervisor emails you directly. Walk me through your first 72 hours. 入职第 1 天，HKMA supervisor 直接发邮件 The scenario tests composure under regulator pressure. "First 72 hours" is the time window that separates thoughtful from panicked.
  • Three principles, the regulator model Three principles: proactive, single point of accountability, documented cadence. 三条原则：主动、单一问责、文档化节奏 Three principles is the cognitive budget. The candidate who can name three principles is a structured thinker; the one who rambles is not.
  • In the band Are you in the band? 是否在段内？ "In the band" is the CHRO's question. The candidate answers "in the band" or "above the band, gap X". The CHRO owns the band.
  • Risk, mitigation, named condition Risk: regulator relationship unproven in HKMA. Mitigation: 90-day buddy, weekly regulator ride-along. 风险：HKMA 监管关系未经证明。缓解：90 天 buddy、每周监管陪同 Every hire has a risk. The panel buys the risk when the mitigation is named, dated, and owned. "We'll see" loses; "90-day buddy, weekly ride-along" wins.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Final round n. 终面 The last interview round before a hiring decision.
  • Panel n. 面试小组 The group of interviewers (CIO, line, HR, functional).
  • Competency n. 能力 The technical and functional skills of the role.
  • Scenario question n. 场景题 A hypothetical question that tests composure and judgment.
  • Fit n. 匹配 The alignment with culture, values, and the operating model.
  • In the band n. 在段内 Within the approved salary range for the role.
  • Comp band n. 薪酬段 The approved salary range for a level.
  • Long-term incentive plan (LTIP) n. 长期激励 A 3-year vesting share plan.
  • 90-day buddy n. 90 天 buddy A peer mentor assigned for the first 90 days.
  • Regulator ride-along n. 监管陪同 Accompanying the existing regulator-facing leader to a meeting.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Walk me through X" — the open-ended opener

"Walk me through" is the open-ended opener. The candidate structures the answer; the panel listens. Closed questions ("Did you do X?") are follow-ups, not openers.

"Walk me through" 是开放式开场。

② "Risk, mitigation, named condition" — the hire frame

Every hire decision has a risk. The frame is: risk, mitigation, named condition. "Risk: regulator relationship unproven. Mitigation: 90-day buddy, weekly ride-along. Named condition: 90-day buddy." The frame survives panel review.

每个录用决策都有风险。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Four sections, four owners. Each interview section has a named owner. The panel member runs their section; the CIO runs the scenario.
  • "Walk me through" opens; closed questions follow. Open-ended opener tests structure; closed follow-up tests depth. The pattern is opener first, follow-up second.
  • Scenario is regulator-pressure testing. The scenario question is not hypothetical — it is a real pressure test. "First 72 hours" is the time window.
  • Three principles = structured thinker. The candidate who names three principles is a structured thinker. The one who rambles is not M5 material.
  • "In the band" is the CHRO's question. The CHRO owns the band. The CIO does not negotiate comp in the room; the CHRO does.
  • Risk + mitigation = the hire frame. Every hire has a risk. The panel buys the risk when the mitigation is named, dated, and owned.
  • 90-day buddy is the dignity signal. A senior hire with a 90-day buddy, weekly ride-along, and a named condition is a structured start. "We'll see how it goes" is not.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the scenario question

Translate the scenario opener.

"Day 1 in the role. HKMA supervisor emails you directly about a cyber finding in the Group cloud — 7 unpatched critical CVEs in production, regulator expects remediation in 14 days. You have no prior relationship with the supervisor. Walk me through your first 72 hours, end to end."

"入职第 1 天。HKMA supervisor 直接给你发邮件，提到集团云中的一个网络发现——生产环境 7 个未修补关键 CVE，监管期望 14 天内修复。你与 supervisor 没有既有关系。讲讲你前 72 小时，端到端。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the interview mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a senior hire final round? Why?

  1. "Four sections, four owners. Daniel competency, I scenario, Sarah fit, Mark comp."
  2. "We'll just chat and see how it goes."
  3. "Risk: regulator relationship unproven. Mitigation: 90-day buddy, weekly ride-along."
  4. "Decision: hire, with a named 90-day buddy as a condition."

② is the credibility-damager. "Just chat, see how it goes" is not an interview structure. ① names four sections and four owners. ③ frames the risk with mitigation. ④ makes the decision with a named condition. Senior hire final rounds run on structure, scenarios, and named conditions — not on "let's see".

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute scenario question

You are the CIO. Candidate for VP Cloud. Day 1 scenario: Group CFO emails you Friday 18:00, IT cloud capex $14M over the $185M 3-year envelope, needs answer by Monday 09:00. Walk the candidate through what you would probe.

"Day 1 in the role. Friday 18:00, the Group CFO emails you directly: the IT cloud capex is $14M over the $185M 3-year envelope, by Year 2. You have until Monday 09:00 to respond. Walk me through your first 72 hours, end to end. Probe: how do you verify the $14M? What 3 questions do you ask the Finance partner Saturday morning? What trade-off options do you bring to the CFO Monday? How do you avoid a no — what's the recommended path? What do you say to the regulator-priority workloads that are now at risk?"
