IT Org Design & Restructure — Span of Control, Squad vs Chapter, BAU vs Project

Article 08 · Module 2: Strategic Decisions · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Span of control, squad vs chapter, BAU vs project, headcount, named roles

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 11:00 HKT. You are the Group CIO. The Group People Committee meets this afternoon to approve the IT organisation redesign for next year. The redesign responds to three forces: (1) the cloud migration creates a demand for cloud engineers and site reliability engineers (SRE) the current org cannot fill; (2) the regulator trajectory requires 30% more cyber and risk FTE over 2 years; (3) the squad model has matured, but the chapter model has not — engineering excellence is fragmented across 14 squads. The redesign moves IT from 5 layers to 4, from 14 squads to 9 chapters, and adds 2 new senior roles. Present: Group CHRO, Group CFO, Group General Counsel, 3 Regional IT Heads. One decision: approve the new org design and the headcount envelope.

周五 11:00 HKT，你是集团 CIO。集团人员委员会今天下午开会，批准明年的 IT 组织重新设计。重新设计响应三股力量：(1) 云迁移产生对云工程师和站点可靠性工程师 (SRE)的需求，现组织无法满足；(2) 监管轨迹要求2 年内网络和风险 FTE 增加 30%；(3) squad 模型已成熟，但 chapter 模型未成熟——工程卓越在 14 个 squad 间碎片化。重新设计将 IT 从 5 层减为 4 层、14 个 squad 整合为 9 个 chapter、新增 2 个高级岗位。参会：集团 CHRO、集团 CFO、集团总法律顾问、3 位区域 IT 负责人。一个决策：批准新组织设计和 headcount 额度。

Org design is the 5-year people bet. The pattern: drivers first, target operating model second, span of control third, headcount envelope fourth, named senior roles last. The CHRO owns the people, the CIO owns the operating model.

组织设计是5 年人才赌注。模式：驱动因素第一、目标运营模型第二、管理幅度第三、headcount 额度第四、具名高级岗位最后。CHRO 拥有员工，CIO 拥有运营模型。

2. The Redesign — 5 Sections, 60 Minutes

You (CIO): Three drivers. Driver 1: cloud migration creates demand for 24 cloud engineers and 12 SREs — current org has 6 cloud engineers and 4 SREs. Driver 2: regulator trajectory (Article 04) requires 30% more cyber and risk FTE over 2 years, 72 net new roles, 18 named, 54 internal move / upskill. Driver 3: engineering excellence is fragmented across 14 squads, no chapter model to enforce standards.三个驱动因素。驱动 1：云迁移产生 24 名云工程师和 12 名 SRE 需求——现组织有 6 名云工程师和 4 名 SRE。驱动 2：监管轨迹（文章 04）要求 2 年内网络和风险 FTE 增加 30%，净增 72 个岗位，18 个具名外招，54 个内部调动/提升。驱动 3：工程卓越在 14 个 squad 间碎片化，无 chapter 模型执行标准。
You (CIO): Target operating model. From 5 layers to 4. Layer 1: Group CIO. Layer 2: Group CTO, Group CISO, Group CDO, Group Head of Delivery. Layer 3: 9 Chapter Leads (Engineering, Cloud, Data, Cyber, Risk, Apps, Platform, SRE, Quality). Layer 4: 32 Squad Leads. You (CIO): Span of control. Group CIO → 4 directs. Each Group function head → 6-9 chapter leads. Each Chapter Lead → 3-5 squad leads. Total layers: 4 (down from 5). Total report lines reduced by 38%. Decision latency: from 11 days to 5 days (target).管理幅度。集团 CIO → 4 个直接下属。每个集团职能负责人 → 6-9 个 chapter lead。每个 Chapter Lead → 3-5 个 squad lead。总层级：4（从 5 减）。汇报线减少 38%。决策延迟：从 11 天降至 5 天（目标）。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Three drivers Three drivers: cloud migration, regulator trajectory, engineering excellence fragmentation. 三个驱动因素：云迁移、监管轨迹、工程卓越碎片化 An org redesign must have drivers, not a target. "We need to flatten" loses. "Three drivers: cloud, regulator, engineering fragmentation" wins.
  • Target operating model, from 5 to 4 layers From 5 layers to 4. Removed: regional 'solution architecture' layer. 从 5 层减为 4 层，删除区域"解决方案架构"层 Always name the layer that is removed. "Flatten" is vague; "removed: the regional SA layer" is a specific cut.
  • Span of control, decision latency Span of control: 1:6 to 1:9. Decision latency: from 11 days to 5 days (target). 管理幅度 1:6 至 1:9，决策延迟从 11 天降至 5 天（目标） Span of control matters because of decision latency. The committee buys a number-of-days improvement, not a layer count.
  • Headcount envelope, mix +24 cloud engineers, +12 SREs, +18 cyber (named hire), +54 internal move / upskill, -36 chapter consolidation. +24 云工程师、+12 SRE、+18 网络（具名外招）、+54 内部调动/提升、-36 chapter 整合 Headcount mix is the CHRO's question. Always break the net number into named hire, internal move, upskill, and consolidation.
  • No involuntary severance -36 from chapter consolidation, no involuntary severance. "No involuntary severance" is the precise phrase. The CHRO and the unions read the phrase. "Some re-allocation" is ambiguous.
  • BAU vs project, no pool BAU FTE 1,180 (83%), project FTE 312 (17%). No 'pool' of project resources. BAU FTE 1,180（83%），项目 FTE 312（17%），无项目资源池 "No pool" is the chapter-model decision. A pool is a project artefact; chapters own their people end-to-end. The chapter-model talk says "no pool".
  • Effective date Effective date: 1 January next year. 生效日：明年 1 月 1 日 The CHRO needs an effective date. "Q1 next year" is vague. "1 January next year" is a date the payroll system can read.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Span of control n. 管理幅度 The number of direct reports per manager.
  • Target operating model (TOM) n. 目标运营模型 The future-state organisation structure.
  • Squad n. Squad 小组 A cross-functional delivery team (3-9 people).
  • Chapter n. Chapter 章节 A community of practice across squads (same skill, different squads).
  • BAU FTE n. 日常运营 FTE FTE allocated to business-as-usual work.
  • Project FTE n. 项目 FTE FTE allocated to projects.
  • Named hire n. 具名外招 A specific external candidate, not a placeholder.
  • Internal move / upskill n. 内部调动/技能提升 Re-allocation of existing staff.
  • Chapter consolidation n. Chapter 整合 Reducing chapters, removing overlap.
  • Involuntary severance n. 强制裁员 Firing someone for org reasons.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "From X to Y" — the operating model change frame

Org design is always a count: from 5 layers to 4, from 14 squads to 9 chapters, from 11 days to 5 days. The "from X to Y" frame forces a specific before / after. "Flatten the org" is opinion; "from 5 layers to 4" is a change.

组织设计总是数字：从 5 层到 4 层、从 14 squad 到 9 chapter。

② "No involuntary severance" — the precise negative

When proposing a re-org, the precise negative is: no involuntary severance. "Some re-allocation" is ambiguous. "Re-deployment with 6-month transition" is bureaucratic. "No involuntary severance" is the precise negative the CHRO and the unions both understand.

No involuntary severance是精确的否定。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Drivers first, target second. An org redesign is approved because of drivers, not because the CIO wants a flatter org. Cloud + regulator + engineering fragmentation is the answer to "why now".
  • Name the removed layer. "Flatten" is vague. "Removed: regional solution architecture" is a specific cut. The committee needs to see what is gone.
  • Span of control is decision latency. "1:9" is a number on a slide. "Decision latency: 11 days to 5 days" is a benefit. The CHRO buys the benefit, not the ratio.
  • Headcount mix breaks the net number. A "+72" headline is met with "from where?". Mix breaks the number: named hire, internal move, upskill, consolidation.
  • "No involuntary severance" is a deal term. The committee approves a re-org that does not fire people. The phrase is the deal term.
  • BAU vs project, no pool. The chapter model breaks the project pool. Chapters own their people end-to-end. The chapter-model talk says "no pool" three times.
  • Effective date is a payroll date. "1 January next year" lets the CHRO run the payroll change. "Q1 next year" requires a second meeting.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the org design recommendation

Translate the recommendation sentence.

"One decision. Approve the 4-layer, 9-chapter org design. Approve +72 net headcount, $11M annualised cost, covered by productivity. Authorise search for the 2 new VPs (Group Head of Engineering Excellence, Group Head of Cloud Platform), in seat by Q3 next year. Effective date: 1 January next year."

"一个决策。批准 4 层 9 chapter 组织设计。批准净增 72 headcount、年化 1,100 万成本，由生产力覆盖。授权启动 2 个 VP 岗位招聘（工程卓越负责人、云平台负责人），明年 Q3 到位。生效日：明年 1 月 1 日。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the org design mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a People Committee? Why?

  1. "Three drivers: cloud migration, regulator trajectory, engineering fragmentation."
  2. "We need to flatten the org."
  3. "From 5 layers to 4. Removed: regional SA layer."
  4. "No involuntary severance."

② is the credibility-damager. "We need to flatten the org" is opinion. ① names three drivers. ③ names a before / after and a removed layer. ④ is the deal term. People committees buy drivers and deal terms, not opinions.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute org design pitch

You are the CIO. Drivers: 1) AI demands 30 new data engineers, 2) regulator demands +20 cyber, 3) squads duplicating platform work. Current: 1,200 FTE, 6 layers, 12 squads. New: 4 layers, 8 chapters, +50 net. Headcount mix: 30 named AI, 20 named cyber, 50 internal move / upskill, -50 from chapter consolidation. Recommendation?

"Three drivers: AI demand, regulator trajectory, platform work duplication. Target operating model: from 6 layers to 4, from 12 squads to 8 chapters. Removed: the 'platform engineering' duplicate layer — folded into chapters. Span of control: 1:7. Decision latency target: 12 days to 5 days. Headcount envelope: +50 net, current 1,200 to 1,250. Mix: +30 named AI hire, +20 named cyber hire, +50 internal move / upskill, -50 chapter consolidation. No involuntary severance. 1 new senior role: Group Head of AI Engineering, VP, in seat by Q3 next year. One decision: approve 4-layer, 8-chapter design, +50 net headcount, $9M annualised cost covered by AI productivity. Effective 1 April next year."
