Promotion Denial Conversation — Specific Gap, 12-Month Plan, Named Support, Next Review

Article 11 · Module 3: Difficult Leadership Conversations · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Promotion denial, specific gap, 12-month plan, named support, next review date

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 11:00 HKT, end of January. You are the Group CIO. You have a 45-minute promotion denial conversation with Priya, a Senior Engineering Manager (M4) who has been a strong performer for 4 years and applied for Director (M5). The Promotion Committee met yesterday; Priya was not promoted. The reason: Priya is excellent at delivery (her cloud migration programme is on track, on budget, RAG Green), but the Promotion Committee wants to see cross-function influence — evidence that she can lead across the CRO, CFO, and Business MDs — before M5. The committee is not saying no; they are saying not yet. The decision: deliver the news with the specific gap, a 12-month plan, named support, and a clear next review date in February next year. Daniel (Priya's line manager) is on the call.

周三 11:00 HKT，1 月末，你是集团 CIO。你与Priya——一位 4 年来表现强劲的高级工程经理 (M4)、已申请总监 (M5)——进行 45 分钟拒绝晋升对话。晋升委员会昨天开会；Priya 未获晋升。原因：Priya 在交付方面出色（她的云迁移项目群按计划、按预算、RAG Green），但晋升委员会希望看到跨职能影响——她能在 CRO、CFO、业务 MD 间领导的证据——才考虑 M5。委员会不是说不；是说还没到。决策：带着具体差距、12 个月计划、具名支持、明年 2 月明确的下次评审日传达消息。Daniel（Priya 的直线经理）在线。

A promotion denial is the most emotionally charged "no" in a senior leader's calendar. The pattern: acknowledge the application first, name the specific gap second, present the 12-month plan third, name the support fourth, set the next review date last. The next review date is the most important sentence — it converts a "no" into a "not yet".

拒绝晋升是高级管理者日历上情绪最浓的"不"。模式：首先承认申请、第二具名差距、第三呈现 12 个月计划、第四具名支持、最后定下次评审日。下次评审日是最重要的句子——它把"不"变成"还没到"。

2. The Conversation — 5 Sections, 45 Minutes

You (CIO): Priya, thanks for coming. Daniel is on the call. The Promotion Committee met yesterday. I'm going to share the outcome, the specific gap the committee named, and the 12-month plan to close it.Priya，感谢参加。Daniel 在线。晋升委员会昨天开会。我会分享结果，委员会具名的具体差距，以及 12 个月关闭计划。
You (CIO): Outcome. The committee did not promote to M5 this cycle. This is not a no — it is a not yet. Your delivery is strong — cloud migration programme is RAG Green, on plan, on budget. That is on the record.结果。委员会本周期未晋升至 M5。这不是不——是还没到。你的交付很强——云迁移项目群 RAG Green、按计划、按预算。这点已记录在案。
You (CIO): The specific gap the committee named: cross-function influence. M5 is a role that leads across the CRO on regulator, the CFO on capex, and the Business MDs on priorities. The committee wants to see evidence of that in the next 12 months, not in the next 3.委员会具名的具体差距：跨职能影响。M5 是跨 CRO（监管）、CFO（capex）、业务 MD（优先级）领导的角色。委员会希望在未来 12 个月内看到证据，不是未来 3 个月。
Priya: Priya: What does 'cross-function influence' look like in practice?Priya："跨职能影响"在实践中是什么样子？
You (CIO): Three observable behaviours. Behaviour 1: own a SteerCo or programme board with the CRO or CFO as a primary stakeholder, not just the IT PM as chair — at least one in the next 12 months. Behaviour 2: draft a board paper or regulator update yourself, end-to-end, not via the IT PM — at least one. Behaviour 3: present a trade-off decision to the Group ExCo or a regional MD, with a clear recommendation they accept — at least one.三个可观察行为。行为 1：主持一个 SteerCo 或项目群委员会，CRO 或 CFO 作为主要利益相关方，不是仅 IT PM 主持——未来 12 个月至少一次。行为 2：自己端到端起草一份董事会文件或监管更新，不是经 IT PM——至少一份。行为 3：向集团 ExCo 或区域 MD 呈现一个权衡决策，有清晰建议被接受——至少一次。
You (CIO): The 12-month plan. Quarter 1: co-chair the Cloud Resilience SteerCo with the CRO, you and I alternate chair. Quarter 2: own the draft of the Q2 board paper on cloud exit cost, Daniel reviews, I sign off. Quarter 3: lead a trade-off paper to the regional MD on customer-facing platform investment vs run cost, with a recommendation. Quarter 4: Promotion Committee reviews in February next year, with the 12-month evidence pack.12 个月计划。第 1 季度：与 CRO 共同主持云韧性 SteerCo，你和我轮流主持。第 2 季度：主笔 Q2 云退出成本董事会文件，Daniel 评审，我签批。第 3 季度：向区域 MD 主导客户平台投资 vs 运行成本的权衡文件，附建议。第 4 季度：晋升委员会明年 2 月评审，附 12 个月证据包。
You (CIO): Named support. Three people, named. Support 1: I personally coach the SteerCo co-chair — the first one, bi-weekly prep with me for 6 weeks. Support 2: Sarah (HR) reviews the board paper draft, and a 1-hour session on the Group house style. Support 3: Daniel pairs the trade-off paper, 2 sessions of 90 minutes each.具名支持。三人，具名。支持 1：我本人辅导 SteerCo 共同主持——第一次，与我每两周准备 6 周。支持 2：Sarah（HR）评审董事会文件草稿，1 小时集团公文风格课程。支持 3：Daniel 配对权衡文件，2 次各 90 分钟。
You (CIO): Next review date: 1 February next year. Promotion Committee reviews, with the 12-month evidence pack. If the three behaviours are evidenced, the committee will promote to M5. If not, we re-plan, not exit — this is a 12-month plan, not a verdict.下次评审日：明年 2 月 1 日。晋升委员会评审，附 12 个月证据包。如果三个行为有证据，委员会将晋升至 M5。如果没有，我们重新规划，不是退出——这是 12 个月计划，不是判决。
Priya: Priya: Thank you, Wei. I hear the gap. I want the 12 months, and I'll come back to the committee in February next year with the evidence.Priya：谢谢，Wei。我理解差距。我想要这 12 个月，明年 2 月带着证据回到委员会。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Not a no, it is a not yet This is not a no — it is a not yet. 这不是不——是还没到 "Not yet" is the most important phrase. It converts a verdict into a plan. Priya leaves the room with a date, not a denial.
  • Specific gap, named by the committee The specific gap the committee named: cross-function influence. 委员会具名的具体差距 The gap is named by the committee, not invented by the CIO. The phrase "the committee named" is the deal term — Priya cannot argue with a committee.
  • Three observable behaviours Three observable behaviours. Not three goals — three behaviours a third party can see. 三个可观察行为 Behaviours, not goals. "Improve cross-function influence" is a goal. "Own a SteerCo with CRO as primary stakeholder" is a behaviour.
  • Not via the IT PM Draft a board paper yourself, end-to-end, not via the IT PM. 自己端到端起草，不是经 IT PM "Not via X" is the precise negative. "End-to-end" is the precise positive. The two together define ownership.
  • 12-month evidence pack The committee reviews with the 12-month evidence pack. 12 个月证据包 An evidence pack is a folder of artefacts (decks, minutes, board papers) that proves the behaviours. The committee buys evidence, not narrative.
  • Not a verdict, a 12-month plan This is a 12-month plan, not a verdict. 这是 12 个月计划，不是判决 "Plan, not verdict" is the de-escalation template. A verdict is final; a plan has a date. Priya leaves with a date.
  • Next review date Next review date: 1 February next year. 下次评审日：明年 2 月 1 日 The next review date is the most important sentence in the conversation. A "no" without a date is a verdict; a "not yet" with a date is a plan.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Promotion Committee n. 晋升委员会 The cross-function committee that reviews promotion applications.
  • Not yet n. 还没到 A deferred decision with a clear re-review date.
  • Cross-function influence n. 跨职能影响 Leading across functions (CRO, CFO, Business MDs).
  • Observable behaviour n. 可观察行为 A behaviour a third party can evidence.
  • 12-month plan n. 12 个月计划 The plan to close the promotion gap over 12 months.
  • Evidence pack n. 证据包 A folder of artefacts (decks, minutes, board papers) that proves the behaviours.
  • SteerCo co-chair n. SteerCo 共同主持 Sharing the chair role with another leader.
  • Board paper draft n. 董事会文件草稿 The end-to-end draft of a paper for the Board.
  • Trade-off paper n. 权衡文件 A paper that presents a decision with options and a recommendation.
  • Next review date n. 下次评审日 The specific date the committee will re-review.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Not X, it is Y" — the deferral template

Promotion denial is re-framed as deferral. This is not a no, it is a not yet. This is not a verdict, it is a 12-month plan. The "not X, it is Y" template converts a final-feeling decision into a forward-looking one. Sarah writes it down.

拒绝晋升被重新框定为延期。

② "Not via the IT PM" — the precise negative

When a behaviour requires Priya to do something herself, the precise negative is: not via the IT PM. It is the deal term that proves the work is hers. The negative is as important as the positive.

当行为需要 Priya 亲自完成，精确的否定是：不是经 IT PM。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Open with the outcome, then the reframing. "The committee did not promote" + "this is not a no, it is a not yet". The reframe turns a verdict into a plan.
  • Name the specific gap, owned by the committee. "The committee named: cross-function influence" — Priya cannot argue with a committee. The phrase is the deal term.
  • Three observable behaviours, not goals. Behaviours are evidence; goals are wishes. The committee buys evidence.
  • "Not via X" is the precise negative. When a behaviour is end-to-end ownership, the negative ("not via the IT PM") is as important as the positive ("draft the paper yourself").
  • 12-month plan, not verdict. A plan has a date; a verdict is final. Priya leaves with a date.
  • Named support, three people. Support is named, not generic. The CIO personally coaching the first SteerCo is the seniority signal.
  • Next review date is the most important sentence. "1 February next year" is the date. A "no" without a date is a verdict; a "not yet" with a date is a plan.
  • Daniel is on the call. The line manager carries the operational message; the CIO carries the seniority signal. Both voices matter.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the gap and plan

Translate the gap and 12-month plan.

"The specific gap the committee named: cross-function influence. M5 is a role that leads across the CRO on regulator, the CFO on capex, and the Business MDs on priorities. The 12-month plan. Quarter 1: co-chair the Cloud Resilience SteerCo with the CRO. Quarter 2: own the draft of the Q2 board paper on cloud exit cost. Quarter 3: lead a trade-off paper to the regional MD. Next review date: 1 February next year."

"委员会具名的具体差距：跨职能影响。M5 是跨 CRO（监管）、CFO（capex）、业务 MD（优先级）领导的角色。12 个月计划。第 1 季度：与 CRO 共同主持云韧性 SteerCo。第 2 季度：主笔 Q2 云退出成本董事会文件。第 3 季度：向区域 MD 主导权衡文件。下次评审日：明年 2 月 1 日。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the promotion denial mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a promotion denial conversation? Why?

  1. "This is not a no — it is a not yet."
  2. "The committee decided you are not senior enough."
  3. "The specific gap the committee named: cross-function influence."
  4. "Next review date: 1 February next year."

② is the credibility-damager. "Not senior enough" is a personal judgement, not a committee-named gap. Priya will quote it back. ① reframes as deferral. ③ owns the gap by the committee. ④ sets the date. Promotion denials run on committee-owned gaps and dates, not on personal judgement.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute promotion denial opening

You are the CIO. Senior manager Sam applied for M5. The committee named: stakeholder communication under pressure (Sam's last 2 regulator updates required 2 rounds of rework). Open the conversation, present 3 behaviours and a 12-month plan.

"Sam, thanks for coming. Priya is on the call. The Promotion Committee met yesterday. The committee did not promote to M5 this cycle. This is not a no — it is a not yet. Your delivery is strong — on plan, on budget. The specific gap the committee named: stakeholder communication under pressure. The last 2 regulator updates required 2 rounds of rework. Three observable behaviours. Behaviour 1: own a regulator update end-to-end, no rework, in the next 2 quarters. Behaviour 2: pair with me on a SteerCo with the regulator in the room, by Q3. Behaviour 3: draft a board paper with a clear recommendation, by Q4. 12-month plan, quarterly milestones, evidence pack in February next year. Next review date: 1 February next year. Named support: I personally coach the first regulator update, Sarah reviews, Priya pairs the SteerCo."
