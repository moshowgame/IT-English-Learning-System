SteerCo Monthly Deck — RAG, Asks, Decisions, Top 3 Risks

Article 01 · Module 1: SteerCo & Board Communication · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 SteerCo, RAG, milestone, ask, decision, top 3 risks, capex

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 09:00 HKT. You are the IT Director, Wholesale Banking Technology. The monthly Programme SteerCo starts in 60 minutes. 14 stakeholders in the room: the Programme Sponsor (MD, Wholesale Banking), the CFO, the CRO, 4 Business Heads, 2 Risk leads, 3 Group Function heads, and the Programme PM. The deck is 12 slides; the discussion window is 30 minutes. You have to land three things: RAG status across 4 workstreams, 2 decisions needed, 1 ask of the committee. The MD is impatient; the CFO is watching capex; the CRO is watching regulator milestones.

周二 09:00 HKT，你是IT 总监，批发银行技术部。每月一次的项目群 SteerCo 60 分钟后开始。14 位利益相关方在会议室：项目群 Sponsor（批发银行 MD）、CFO、CRO、4 位业务负责人、2 位风险负责人、3 位集团职能负责人、项目群 PM。报告 12 张幻灯片；讨论窗口 30 分钟。你要完成三件事：4 个工作流的 RAG 状态、2 个待决决策、对委员会的 1 个请求。MD 没耐心；CFO 关注 capex；CRO 关注监管里程碑。

A SteerCo deck is a 30-minute conversation compressed into 12 slides. The pattern: RAG first, milestones second, asks third, decisions fourth, risks last. The CFO and CRO read the capex and the regulator lines first; the MD reads the asks; everyone reads the top 3 risks.

SteerCo 报告是压缩到 12 张幻灯片的 30 分钟对话。模式：先 RAG，再里程碑，再请求，再决策，最后风险。CFO 和 CRO 先看 capex 和监管行；MD 看请求；所有人看前 3 大风险。

2. The Deck Walk — 5 Sections

You (IT Director): Slide 2 — RAG summary. 4 workstreams. Reporting Engine: Green — on track, 8 FTE, milestone 3 of 5 complete. Data Quality: Amber — 1 FTE gap, mitigation in flight (Lin moving from KYC next week). Integration: Green — 6 FTE, on plan, milestone 2 of 4 complete. Group Cyber: Amber — 2 FTE gap, recovery via 2 cyber contractors, SOW this Friday.第 2 张——RAG 摘要。4 个工作流。报送引擎：绿——按计划，8 FTE，里程碑 3/5 完成。数据质量：黄——缺 1 FTE，恢复进行中（Lin 下周从 KYC 调入）。集成：绿——6 FTE，按计划，里程碑 2/4 完成。集团 Cyber：黄——缺 2 FTE，通过 2 名 Cyber 外包恢复，SOW 本周五发出。
You (IT Director): Slide 4 — Capex. Plan: $14.8M for the year. Committed: $9.2M (62%). Forecast: $14.6M, $200K under plan. Variance driven by 2 cyber contractor deferral to Q4 (saves $200K). No capex risk to the year-end target.第 4 张——Capex。计划：全年 1,480 万。已承诺：920 万（62%）。预测：1,460 万，比计划少 20 万。差异来自 2 名 Cyber 外包推迟到 Q4（节省 20 万）。年底目标无 capex 风险。
You (IT Director): Slide 5 — Regulator milestones. MAS TRM cyber hygiene attestation: Q3, on track. HKMA Cyber Resilience iCAST: October, on track. PDPA data-breach playbook refresh: Q4, on track. No regulator milestone is at risk.第 5 张——监管里程碑。MAS TRM 网络卫生合规声明：Q3，按计划。HKMA 网络韧性 iCAST：10 月，按计划。PDPA 数据泄露预案刷新：Q4，按计划。无监管里程碑有风险。
You (IT Director): Slide 7 — Asks. One ask: sign off the 2 cyber contractor SOWs by Friday — total $320K for 9 months. Without the SOWs signed, the Group Cyber gap stays open and the MAS milestone is at risk.第 7 张——请求。一个请求：周五前签批 2 名 Cyber 外包 SOW——9 个月总计 32 万。SOW 不签，集团 Cyber 缺口保持开放，MAS 里程碑有风险。
You (IT Director): Slide 8 — Decisions needed. Decision 1: confirm the M4 code-freeze date of 28 July. If the committee wants to pull forward, we have 5 dev-days of buffer; otherwise the buffer protects the regulator date. Decision 2: approve the Phase 2 scope (FX rate display, push notification v2) for Q1 next year — $620K capex, 4 FTE for 6 months.第 8 张——待决决策。决策 1：确认 M4 code-freeze 日期 7 月 28 日。如果委员会想提前，我们有 5 个开发日缓冲；否则缓冲保护监管日期。决策 2：批准 Q2 范围（FX 汇率显示、推送 v2）纳入明年 Q1——62 万 capex，6 个月 4 FTE。
You (IT Director): Slide 10 — Top 3 risks. Risk 1: Group Cyber FTE gap — Medium, mitigation in flight. Risk 2: HKMA iCAST date slip if any P1 incident diverts the team — Medium, contingency plan in place. Risk 3: Q4 vendor renewal at 8% above plan — Low, Group Procurement has the negotiation.第 10 张——前 3 大风险。风险 1：集团 Cyber FTE 缺口——中，缓解进行中。风险 2：任何 P1 事件抽调团队导致 HKMA iCAST 日期推迟——中，应急计划就位。风险 3：Q4 厂商续约比计划高 8%——低，集团采购负责谈判。
MD (Sponsor): MD: Both decisions approved. SOW sign-off in the room, by Friday. Good deck, Wei.MD：两个决策都批准。SOW 签批在会议室完成，周五前。报告不错，Wei。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • RAG first, milestones second, asks third, decisions fourth, risks last RAG, milestones, asks, decisions, risks — in that order. RAG、里程碑、请求、决策、风险——按顺序 The order of the deck is the order the committee reads. RAG answers "where are we?"; milestones answer "what did we do?"; asks answer "what do we need?"; decisions answer "what does the committee choose?"; risks answer "what could go wrong?".
  • Plan / Committed / Forecast / Variance Plan $14.8M, committed $9.2M, forecast $14.6M, variance $200K under plan. 计划 1,480 万，已承诺 920 万，预测 1,460 万，差异比计划少 20 万 CFO language. Plan is the budget. Committed is the contracted. Forecast is the year-end projection. Variance is the difference. Always name all four.
  • No regulator milestone is at risk No regulator milestone is at risk. 无监管里程碑有风险 The line that makes the CRO and the MD exhale. "No regulator milestone at risk" is the headline; the detail comes in the next slide.
  • One ask, with consequence Without the SOWs signed, the Group Cyber gap stays open and the MAS milestone is at risk. SOW 不签，集团 Cyber 缺口保持开放，MAS 里程碑有风险 An ask without a consequence is a wish. The ask + the consequence is the decision the committee has to make.
  • If you want to pull forward, here is the trade If the committee wants to pull forward, we have 5 dev-days of buffer; otherwise the buffer protects the regulator date. 如果委员会想提前，我们有 5 个开发日缓冲；否则缓冲保护监管日期 Decisions always come with a trade. The committee chooses with eyes open. The IT Director names the trade; the committee makes the call.
  • Top 3 risks, rated, with mitigation Risk 1: Group Cyber FTE gap — Medium, mitigation in flight. 风险 1：集团 Cyber FTE 缺口——中，缓解进行中 Top 3, not top 10. Each risk has a rating (High/Medium/Low) and a mitigation. Without a mitigation, the risk is a worry, not a managed risk.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • SteerCo (Steering Committee) n. 指导委员会 The governance body that owns the programme and makes decisions.
  • Sponsor n. Sponsor The senior executive who owns the business case and the budget.
  • RAG (Red / Amber / Green) n. 红黄绿 The standard status indicator; Green = on track, Amber = at risk, Red = slipped/blocked.
  • Workstream n. 工作流 A sub-programme within the larger programme (e.g., Reporting Engine, Data Quality).
  • Milestone n. 里程碑 A named, dated deliverable in the plan.
  • Capex (Capital Expenditure) n. 资本支出 Money spent on long-term assets, depreciated over years.
  • Opex (Operating Expenditure) n. 运营支出 Money spent on day-to-day operations, expensed in the year.
  • Variance n. 差异 The difference between plan and forecast.
  • Ask n. 请求 A specific decision or resource the committee needs to provide.
  • Decision needed n. 待决决策 A choice only the committee can make, with the trade named.
  • Top 3 risks n. 前 3 大风险 The risks the committee needs to know, with rating and mitigation.
  • Buffer n. 缓冲 Spare capacity (dev-days, FTE, capex) that absorbs variance.

5. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 5 sections, fixed order. RAG, capex, regulator milestones, asks, decisions, top 3 risks. The committee reads the structure, not the prose.
  • RAG first. The committee wants to know where they are in 30 seconds. RAG is the headline; the detail is the backup.
  • Capex: plan, committed, forecast, variance. The CFO's four-line read. Always name all four, in that order.
  • "No regulator milestone at risk" is the line that closes the regulator chapter. The CRO and the MD exhale. Detail comes in the next slide.
  • One ask, with consequence. An ask without a consequence is a wish. The ask + the consequence is the decision the committee has to make.
  • Decisions come with a trade. "If you want to pull forward, here is the buffer" is the structure. The committee chooses with eyes open.
  • Top 3 risks, not top 10. Each risk rated, each risk with a mitigation. Without a mitigation, the risk is a worry, not a managed risk.

Exercise 2 — Spot the SteerCo deck mistake

Which line would damage the SteerCo deck the most? Why?

  1. "RAG: Reporting Engine Green, Data Quality Amber, Integration Green, Group Cyber Amber. Capex: plan $14.8M, committed $9.2M, forecast $14.6M, variance $200K under. Regulator: no milestone at risk. Ask: sign off 2 cyber SOWs by Friday."
  2. "Things are going well, mostly. Some areas are challenges but we are managing them. We will get there. Thank you."
  3. "Decisions needed: confirm M4 code-freeze date 28 July. Approve Phase 2 scope for Q1 next year — $620K capex, 4 FTE for 6 months."
  4. "Top 3 risks: Cyber FTE gap Medium mitigation in flight; iCAST slip Medium contingency in place; Q4 vendor renewal Low Group Procurement owns it."

② is the credibility-damager. "Things are going well, mostly" replaces the 5 sections with generalities. The CFO, the CRO, and the MD all walk out of the room with no decision, no ask, and no risk. ① leads with RAG, names capex with all four numbers, says no regulator milestone at risk, and names the ask. ③ names the two decisions. ④ names the top 3 risks with rating and mitigation. ② turns a 30-minute SteerCo into a 3-hour follow-up email chain.
