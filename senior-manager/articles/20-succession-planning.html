Succession Planning for IT Leadership — 9-Box, Ready Now / 1-2 Years, Named Successor, Risk

Article 20 · Module 5: Bank Leadership Context · SM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 9-box, ready now, ready in 1-2 years, named successor, retention risk, development plan

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 10:00 HKT, mid-November. You are the Group CIO. You are chairing the annual IT Succession Planning Review with the Group CHRO. The agenda: 9-box placement of the 18 senior IT leaders (M4 and above), readiness (ready now / ready in 1-2 years / ready in 3-5 years), named successors for the 4 most critical roles, retention risk for the top 5 high-potentials, and the development plan for the 3 in the "ready in 1-2 years" box. One decision: approve the 9-box, the named successors, the retention packages, and the development plan.

周五 10:00 HKT，11 月中旬，你是集团 CIO。你与集团 CHRO 主持年度IT 继任规划评审。议程：18 名高级 IT 领导者（M4 及以上）的 9-box 定位、就绪度（现在可就任 / 1-2 年可就任 / 3-5 年可就任）、4 个最关键岗位的具名继任者、前 5 名高潜的保留风险、"1-2 年可就任"框中 3 人的发展计划。一个决策：批准 9-box、具名继任者、保留包、发展计划。

Succession planning is the 5-year people bet of any CIO. The pattern: 9-box first, readiness second, named successors third, retention risk fourth, development plan last. Speak the CHRO's language: 9-box, ready now, named successor, retention risk, development plan.

继任规划是任何 CIO 的5 年人才赌注。模式：9-box 第一、就绪度第二、具名继任者第三、保留风险第四、发展计划最后。说 CHRO 的语言：9-box、ready now、named successor、retention risk、development plan。

2. The Review — 4 Sections, 90 Minutes

Box 3 (high performance, moderate potential — core players): 5 leaders. 3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 9-box placement, by box Box 1 (high performance, high potential — stars): 5 leaders. 框 1（高绩效、高潜力——明星）5 名 9-box is always named by box, with counts. "We have good people" loses; "Box 1 has 5, Box 2 has 4" wins. The CHRO buys the counts.
  • Ready now, 1-2 years, 3-5 years 2 ready now, 1 ready in 1-2 years. 2 名现在可就任，1 名 1-2 年可就任 The three readiness buckets are the CHRO's language. "She can do it" loses; "ready in 1-2 years" wins. Pair the candidate with the bucket.
  • Named successor, plus backup Daniel (incumbent, plus Marcus as 1-2 year backup). Daniel（现任，加 Marcus 作为 1-2 年备份） "Named successor plus backup" is the deal term. The CHRO buys the pair; a single name is not a plan.
  • Retention risk, with package Marcus retention: restricted shares $480K, cash $120K, named role. Marcus 保留：限制性股票 48 万、现金 12 万、具名岗位 "Retention risk with package" is the precise frame. Risk rating + named package + role. The CHRO and the employee both see the deal.
  • Stretch assignment, with support Stretch assignment as ASP Regional CIO for 12 months, bi-weekly ExCo prep with me, 1 external coach for 6 months. 12 个月 ASP 区域 CIO 拉伸任务，与我双周 ExCo 准备，1 名外部教练 6 个月 "Stretch assignment" is the precise phrase. Pair the stretch with named support (mentor, coach, conference). A stretch without support is a stretch, not a development plan.
  • Quarterly update, measurable progress Quarterly update with measurable progress against the development plan, at our monthly 1:1. 每季度更新附发展计划的可衡量进展，在我月度 1:1 上 Quarterly is the cadence. Measurable progress is the metric. The CHRO closes with a cadence, not a feeling.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • 9-box n. 9-box A 3x3 grid placing leaders on performance and potential axes.
  • Ready now n. 现在可就任 The candidate could step into the role within 6 months.
  • Ready in 1-2 years n. 1-2 年可就任 The candidate could step into the role within 1-2 years with development.
  • Ready in 3-5 years n. 3-5 年可就任 The candidate could step into the role within 3-5 years.
  • Named successor n. 具名继任者 A specific person named as the successor for a role.
  • Backup (1-2 year) n. 1-2 年备份 A second person named as the next successor if the first is not available.
  • Retention package n. 保留包 A compensation package to retain a high-potential.
  • Restricted shares n. 限制性股票 Shares that vest over a defined period.
  • Stretch assignment n. 拉伸任务 A temporary role that tests a candidate at the next level.
  • Measurable progress n. 可衡量进展 Concrete metrics against a development plan.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "9-box by box, with counts" — the placement sentence

9-box placement is always by box, with counts. "We have good people" loses; "Box 1 has 5, Box 2 has 4" wins. The CHRO buys the counts.

9-box 定位总是 按框，附数字。

② "Named successor plus backup" — the pair frame

Succession is a pair: named successor plus backup. A single name is not a plan. The CHRO buys the pair; the regulator recognises the pair.

继任是成对：具名继任者加备份。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 9-box by box, with counts. "We have good people" loses; "Box 1 has 5, Box 2 has 4" wins. The CHRO buys the counts.
  • Three readiness buckets, paired with candidate. "She can do it" loses; "ready in 1-2 years" wins. Pair the candidate with the bucket.
  • Named successor plus backup. A single name is not a plan. The CHRO buys the pair.
  • Retention risk with named package. Risk rating + package + role. The CHRO and the employee both see the deal.
  • Stretch assignment with named support. A stretch without support is a stretch, not a development plan. Pair the stretch with mentor, coach, conference.
  • Quarterly update, measurable progress. Quarterly is the cadence. Measurable progress is the metric. The CHRO closes with a cadence.
  • HR owns the agreement, not the CIO. Marcus retention: HR drafts the restricted shares agreement. The CHRO signs; the CIO proposes.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the named successors

Translate the named successors.

"Named successors for the 4 critical roles. Role 1: Group CIO — Priya (ready now, 18 years, ex-Group CTO track). Role 2: Group CTO — Daniel (incumbent, plus Marcus as 1-2 year backup). Role 3: Group CISO — David (incumbent, plus Sarah from Group Cyber as 1-2 year backup). Role 4: ASP Regional CIO — Mei (ready now, ex-HK CIO). Each named successor has a development plan, a retention package, and a backup."

"4 个关键岗位的具名继任者。岗位 1：集团 CIO——Priya（现在可就任，18 年，原集团 CTO 轨道）。岗位 2：集团 CTO——Daniel（现任，加 Marcus 作为 1-2 年备份）。岗位 3：集团 CISO——David（现任，加集团网络的 Sarah 作为 1-2 年备份）。岗位 4：ASP 区域 CIO——Mei（现在可就任，原香港 CIO）。每位具名继任者都有发展计划、保留包和备份。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the succession mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a CHRO succession review? Why?

  1. "We have a good pipeline."
  2. "9-box by box with counts. Box 1 has 5, Box 2 has 4. 7 ready now, 8 in 1-2 years."
  3. "Named successor plus backup. Daniel incumbent, Marcus 1-2 year backup."
  4. "Stretch assignment with named support, quarterly update, measurable progress."

① is the credibility-damager. "Good pipeline" is impression, not 9-box. ② is counts by box. ③ is the pair. ④ is stretch with support. Succession reviews run on counts, pairs, and named support — not on impressions.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute 9-box review

You are the CIO. 12 M4-M5 leaders. 3 ready now for Group Head of Engineering, 2 ready in 1-2 years, 1 high-potential retention risk (1 external offer). Pitch the 9-box summary.

"9-box placement of 12 M4-M5 leaders. Box 1 (stars): 4. Box 2 (rising): 3. Box 3 (core players): 3. Box 4-9: 2 across the remaining boxes, 0 on PIP. Readiness for Group Head of Engineering: 3 ready now, 2 ready in 1-2 years. Named successor: Lina (ready now, 14 years, ex-Group Architecture). Backup: Robert (ready in 1-2 years, current chapter lead). Retention risk: Robert — 1 external offer last month, medium-high risk. Retention package: restricted shares $320K vesting over 3 years, cash $80K, named backup role. Development plan for Robert: stretch as acting Group Head of Engineering for 4 weeks during Lina's leave, 1 external conference, bi-weekly coaching with me. Quarterly update with measurable progress at our monthly 1:1."
