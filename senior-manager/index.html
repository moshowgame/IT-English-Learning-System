Welcome, Senior IT Leader / IT 高级管理者你好

As a Senior IT Manager (MD, Director, Senior VP) in a foreign bank, you sit at the intersection of strategy, governance, leadership, and cross-function alignment. You chair SteerCos, present to the Board Risk Committee, own the IT strategy for the region, lead senior hiring and performance conversations, align with the CFO on capex, the CRO on cyber, the COO on operations, and the Business MDs on priorities. You also face the regulator (HKMA, MAS, PRA) and represent IT in Group off-sites. This section focuses on the spoken English scenarios you actually face — from a 60-second board update to telling a 20-year veteran they are not getting the promotion.

面向外企银行 IT 高级管理者 (MD / Director / 总监)，本节聚焦你日常真正遇到的口语场景——从 60 秒董事会简报到告诉一位 20 年老兵他们没有得到晋升。每篇文章包含真实场景、英文对话、中文解释、关键句型与练习。

  • ✅ Run a SteerCo, board update, QBR with confidence: RAG / NPV / capex / headcount / regulator milestone

How to Use This Section / 学习建议

💡 Daily Practice Tip

Senior IT leaders live in SteerCos, board updates, leadership conversations, and regulator interactions. Pick one article per day and try to paraphrase the dialogue from memory — that is the muscle you use when the Board Risk Committee chair asks you a question you did not prepare for.

⚠️ For Real Foreign-Bank Work

Senior leadership vocabulary (delegated authority, supervisory letter, ring-fencing, succession, NPV, RAG) carries regulator and Group weight. Always validate with Group HR, Group Legal, and Group Compliance before any external commitment. Group Standards override local practice. Regulator milestones (HKMA, MAS, FCA) are sacred — never trade them for scope or schedule.
