Scope Cut Pushback — When IT Says No to a Business Request

Article 14 · Module 4: Difficult Business Conversations · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Accept, escalate, named trade, customer impact, decision

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 15:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The IT PM, Sara, has formally proposed to cut FR-07 cross-sell engine from Phase 1 to keep the November go-live. You are the business sponsor. FR-07 is the $8M Year 1 revenue item, contractually tied to the November product launch. The MD (Karen) is in your office. Sara and the CIO (Wei) are on the call. Sara's argument: capacity is 6 FTE for 9 weeks, 8 items don't fit, FR-07 adds 4 FTE for 6 weeks. You have 45 minutes to push back, escalate, or accept with a named trade.

周二 15:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。IT PM Sara 正式建议从第 1 阶段裁掉 FR-07 交叉销售引擎以保住 11 月上线。你是业务发起方。FR-07 是第 1 年 800 万美元收入项，合同绑 11 月产品发布。MD（Karen）在你办公室。Sara 和 CIO（Wei）电话上。Sara 的论点：容量 6 FTE 9 周，8 个项放不下，FR-07 加 4 FTE 6 周。你有 45 分钟提出异议、升级或接受附具名权衡。

Scope cut pushback is the mirror of the ad-hoc request. The PM says no to a business item; the sponsor must accept, escalate, or counter. The pattern: ask the customer impact, ask the trade, name the decision rule, decide.

范围裁剪异议是临时需求的镜像。PM 对业务项说不；发起方必须接受、升级或反提案。模式：问客户影响、问权衡、给定决策规则、决定。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sara (IT PM): Sara: Joyce, we have to cut FR-07 cross-sell from Phase 1. Capacity is 6 FTE for 9 weeks, the 8 items don't fit, FR-07 adds 4 FTE for 6 weeks. If we keep it, 2 other items slip, and the November milestone moves to 15 December.Sara：Joyce，我们必须从第 1 阶段裁掉 FR-07 交叉销售。容量 6 FTE 9 周，8 个项放不下，FR-07 加 4 FTE 6 周。如果保留，另外 2 个项推迟，11 月里程碑挪到 12 月 15 日。
You (Business Sponsor): I hear the capacity argument. Let me name the business consequence first.我听到容量论点了。让我先列业务后果。
You: FR-07 is contractually tied to the November product launch. 9 of 10 top HNW clients have signed commitment letters referencing the cross-sell engine. If we defer FR-07, the launch slips, 9 letters become void, and we lose $8M Year 1 forecast.FR-07 合同绑 11 月产品发布。前 10 大高净值客户 9 个已签承诺函提到交叉销售引擎。推迟 FR-07，发布推迟，9 份函失效，损失第 1 年 800 万预测。
Sara: Sara: I understand. If we keep FR-07, 2 items slip. What are they?Sara：我理解。如果保留 FR-07，2 个项推迟。是哪 2 个？
You: The PM knows better than I do — the 2 lowest regulator or revenue items. What are they?PM 比我知道得更清楚——监管或收入最低的 2 个项。是哪 2 个？
Sara: Sara: FR-11 reporting dashboard and FR-14 HNW journey. Both Could-tier, no regulator tie, no contract. Defer them to Phase 2 with named commit dates.Sara：FR-11 报表仪表盘和 FR-14 高净值旅程。两个都是 Could 级别，无监管绑定，无合同。推迟到第 2 阶段，附具名承诺日期。
You: Customer impact of deferring FR-11 and FR-14?推迟 FR-11 和 FR-14 的客户影响？
Sara: Sara: FR-11 — manual report for 3 months, BA team pulls weekly, no customer impact. FR-14 — 240 HNW clients use the manual workaround with their RM, no system, but a known friction point.Sara：FR-11——3 个月人工报表，BA 团队每周拉数，无客户影响。FR-14——240 个高净值客户和客户经理走人工方案，无系统，但有已知摩擦点。
You: Decision rule for me: contractually committed + revenue-tied stays in Phase 1, Could-tier and no customer impact can defer to Phase 2. FR-07 wins. Defer FR-11 and FR-14.我的决策规则：合同承诺 + 收入绑定留在第 1 阶段，Could 级别且无客户影响可推迟到第 2 阶段。FR-07 赢。推迟 FR-11 和 FR-14。
Karen (MD): Karen: Agreed. What's the Phase 2 commit for FR-11 and FR-14?Karen：同意。FR-11 和 FR-14 的第 2 阶段承诺是什么？
Sara: Sara: FR-11 by 28 February, FR-14 by 31 March. Both with named FTE in the Q1 plan.Sara：FR-11 2 月 28 日，FR-14 3 月 31 日。两个都在 Q1 计划里有具名 FTE。
Karen: Karen: Accepted. Joyce, send me the cut plan by EOD. BRD v3.5 by Friday with the deferral terms.Karen：接受。Joyce，EOD 前把裁剪计划发我。周五前 BRD v3.5 含推迟条款。
You: Done. Wei, are we aligned?好。Wei，我们对齐了吗？
Wei (CIO): Wei: Aligned. Sara, lock the FTE plan against the new list, and confirm the Phase 2 dates with the workstream leads by Friday.Wei：对齐。Sara，根据新清单锁定 FTE 计划，周五前和 2 个工作流负责人确认第 2 阶段日期。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Hear the argument, then name consequence I hear the capacity argument. Let me name the business consequence first. 我听到容量论点了。让我先列业务后果 Always acknowledge the IT argument first. Then name the consequence. The structure shows you understand both sides before pushing back.
  • Contractually tied, with count FR-07 is contractually tied to the November product launch. 9 of 10 top HNW clients have signed commitment letters. FR-07 合同绑 11 月产品发布。前 10 大高净值客户 9 个已签承诺函 Contractual commitment is the strongest argument. Always name the count (9 of 10) and the artefact (commitment letters). The PM and CIO cannot argue against signed letters.
  • What are the 2 lowest items? If we keep FR-07, 2 items slip. What are they? 如果保留 FR-07，2 个项推迟。是哪 2 个？ Always ask: if we keep X, what slips? The PM names the 2 lowest items. You trade FR-07 for the 2 lowest, not the 2 critical-path.
  • Customer impact, per deferred item FR-11: manual report for 3 months, no customer impact. FR-14: 240 HNW on manual workaround, known friction point. FR-11：3 个月人工报表，无客户影响。FR-14：240 个高净值走人工方案，已知摩擦点 Each deferred item has a customer impact. State it. The MD sees which items have zero customer impact (safe to defer) and which have friction (defer with caveat).
  • Decision rule, with named tiers Decision rule: contractually committed + revenue-tied stays in Phase 1. Could-tier and no customer impact can defer. 决策规则：合同承诺 + 收入绑定留在第 1 阶段。Could 级别且无客户影响可推迟 State the decision rule before naming the decision. The rule is the principle; the decision is the application. The MD buys the rule, then confirms the decision.
  • Are we aligned? Wei, are we aligned? Wei，我们对齐了吗？ Always close with an alignment check to the CIO. The CIO confirms; the room is aligned. The next step (lock FTE, confirm dates) is then unambiguous.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Scope cut pushback n. 范围裁剪异议 The business sponsor's response when IT proposes to cut a business item.
  • Contractually tied n. 合同绑定 A business item backed by a signed client commitment, contract, or revenue forecast.
  • Decision rule n. 决策规则 The principle that guides which items stay and which defer (e.g., contractually committed stays).
  • Could-tier n. Could 级别 A MoSCoW tier for nice-to-have items, no regulator tie, no contract.
  • Manual workaround n. 人工方案 A non-system way to deliver the customer outcome while the system is built later.
  • Known friction point n. 已知摩擦点 A customer experience issue that the manual workaround causes, but does not block the customer.
  • Phase 2 commit date n. 第 2 阶段承诺日期 The named date by which a deferred item will be delivered, with named FTE.
  • Are we aligned? phr. 我们对齐了吗？ The closing alignment check with the CIO. The room confirms; the next step is unambiguous.
  • Cut plan n. 裁剪计划 The list of items kept in Phase 1, items deferred to Phase 2, with named dates and FTE.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Decision rule: [principle 1] + [principle 2]" — the rule-setting sentence

The decision rule is one sentence with two principles: Decision rule: [principle 1] + [principle 2]. The grammar is label + colon + two principles joined by plus. The MD sees the rule before the application.

决策规则是一句话两个原则：决策规则：【原则 1】+【原则 2】。

② "FR-N: [impact], [workaround]" — the deferred item sentence

A deferred item has a label and an impact: FR-N: [impact], [workaround]. The grammar is label + colon + impact + workaround. The MD reads the deferred list in 30 seconds.

推迟项有标签和影响：FR-N：【影响】、【回退】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Acknowledge the IT argument first. "I hear the capacity argument" is 4 words that buy you credibility. The PM feels heard before you push back.
  • Contractual commitment is the strongest argument. Name the count (9 of 10) and the artefact (commitment letters). The PM cannot argue against signed letters.
  • Always ask: if we keep X, what slips? The PM names the 2 lowest. You trade FR-07 for the 2 lowest, not the 2 critical-path.
  • State the decision rule before the decision. "Contractually committed stays; Could-tier defers" is the rule. "FR-07 wins" is the application. The MD buys the rule, then confirms.
  • Customer impact per deferred item. "Manual report, no customer impact" is safe to defer. "Manual workaround, friction point" is defer with caveat.
  • Close with an alignment check. "Wei, are we aligned?" The CIO confirms; the room is aligned. The next step is then unambiguous.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Build the decision rule

You are the business sponsor. IT proposes to cut FR-12 (revenue-tied, $4M, contractually committed to a top client). Write the decision rule and the decision in the pattern "Decision rule: [principle]. FR-N wins."

Exercise 2 — Name the 2 lowest items

IT says keeping FR-12 will slip 2 items. The candidate items are FR-04 (regulator-tied), FR-07 (revenue-tied, $8M), FR-11 (Could-tier, no customer impact), FR-14 (Could-tier, friction point). Which 2 should slip? Justify with the decision rule.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing decision rule

A sponsor says: "I want to keep FR-12 because it's important to me." What is missing? Rewrite the sentence with a decision rule and a contractual artefact.
