Asking for Status — "Where Is My Project?"

Article 02 · Module 1: Daily Business Touchpoints · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟢 Beginner 🎯 Polite ask, escalation, board-ready answer, named ask

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 11:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. In 90 minutes you have a 1:1 with your MD, Karen, who will ask you about the Digital Onboarding 2.0 programme. You have not heard from IT in 5 days. You need a board-ready answer: where the programme is, when it lands, what the risk is, what you need. You call your IT PM, Sara, and ask for status — politely, with a clear ask.

周四 11:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。90 分钟后你要和你的 MD Karen 1:1，她会问数字开户 2.0项目群。你已经 5 天没收到 IT 的消息了。你需要一个董事能听的答案：项目群在哪、什么时候落地、风险是什么、需要什么。你打给 IT PM Sara 问状态——礼貌、有明确需要。

Asking for status is the most common business-IT touchpoint. The trick is to ask in a way that forces a board-ready answer — RAG, milestone date, top risk, ask — in under 5 minutes. If you only ask "how is it going", you will get a feeling, not a number.

询问状态是最常见的业务-IT 对接。诀窍是问得能逼出董事能听的答案——RAG、里程碑日期、首要风险、需要——5 分钟内。如果你只问"怎么样"，你会得到感觉，不是数字。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Business Sponsor): Sara, hi. I have a 1:1 with Karen at 12:30 and I need a board-ready update on Onboarding 2.0. Can you give me 5 minutes?Sara 你好。我 12:30 要和 Karen 1:1，需要一个董事能听的开户 2.0 更新。你能给我 5 分钟吗？
Sara (IT PM): Sara: Sure. What's the format Karen wants?Sara：好。Karen 想要什么格式？
You: RAG, top 3 milestones and dates, top risk, top ask of me. 30 seconds each.RAG、前 3 个里程碑和日期、首要风险、对我的首要需要。每项 30 秒。
Sara: Sara: Amber overall. Identity verification, FIDO2, and journey sign-off are the 3 critical-path items. All three are at risk of slipping UAT entry on 28 July by 1 to 2 weeks.Sara：整体黄。身份核验、FIDO2、旅程签批是 3 个关键路径项。三项都有把 7 月 28 日 UAT 进入推迟 1 到 2 周的风险。
You: Top risk?首要风险？
Sara: Sara: HKMA eKYC threshold letter — no response since 12 July. If we don't get the threshold by 31 July, UAT slips 2 weeks and the MAS milestone shifts to 15 September.Sara：HKMA eKYC 阈值函——7 月 12 日以来没有回复。如果 7 月 31 日前拿不到阈值，UAT 推迟 2 周，MAS 里程碑推迟到 9 月 15 日。
You: Ask of me?对我有什么需要？
Sara: Sara: Three things. One — nudge Daniel Tang at HKMA by Monday, he's your regulatory contact. Two — sign off the high-net-worth journey today, UI build is blocked. Three — escalate FIDO2 to Wei, Group Cyber timeline is now a programme risk, not a workstream risk.Sara：三件。一——周一前推动 Daniel Tang 在 HKMA，他是你的监管联络人。二——今天签批高净值旅程，UI 构建卡住了。三——把 FIDO2 升级到 Wei，集团 Cyber 时间线现在是项目群风险，不是工作流风险。
You: Done, done, and done by 14:00 today. Send me the 1-pager in this format — RAG, 3 milestones with dates, top risk, 3 asks — by 12:00. I'll walk Karen through it.三件都做，今天 14:00 前。12:00 前按这个格式发我 1 页纸——RAG、3 个里程碑附日期、首要风险、3 个需要。我拿给 Karen 看。
Sara: Sara: Done.Sara：好。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Board-ready update I need a board-ready update on Onboarding 2.0. 我需要一个董事能听的更新 "Board-ready" sets the bar. The PM must answer in numbers, dates, named risks, named asks. Anything less is not board-ready.
  • Format: 4 boxes, 30 seconds each RAG, top 3 milestones and dates, top risk, top ask of me. 30 seconds each. RAG、前 3 个里程碑和日期、首要风险、对我的首要需要。每项 30 秒 A board-ready answer is 4 boxes. Set the format upfront. Without a format, the PM talks for 10 minutes and you walk out with nothing.
  • At risk of slipping by N weeks At risk of slipping UAT entry on 28 July by 1 to 2 weeks. 有把 7 月 28 日 UAT 进入推迟 1 到 2 周的风险 Always state the slip in weeks, with the date. "It might slip" loses. "1 to 2 weeks, UAT entry 28 July" wins.
  • Programme risk, not workstream risk FIDO2 is now a programme risk, not a workstream risk. FIDO2 现在是项目群风险，不是工作流风险 "Programme risk" triggers SteerCo attention. "Workstream risk" stays inside IT. The PM telling you it's a programme risk is a flag to escalate to the MD.
  • Send me the 1-pager in this format Send me the 1-pager in this format by 12:00. 12:00 前按这个格式发我 1 页纸 Always ask for a 1-pager in the format you specified. Email it to yourself so you can walk Karen through it on one page.
  • Done, done, and done by 14:00 Done, done, and done by 14:00 today. 三件都做，今天 14:00 前 Always commit with a time when you take an ask. "I'll get to it" loses. "By 14:00 today" wins. The PM holds you to it.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Board-ready adj. 董事能听的 Format that an MD or ExCo can read in 60 seconds: numbers, dates, named risks, named asks.
  • 1-pager n. 一页纸 A single-page summary, the standard format for senior updates.
  • Format n. 格式 The structure of the update (RAG, milestones, risks, asks).
  • Programme risk n. 项目群风险 A risk at the programme level that triggers SteerCo or ExCo attention.
  • Workstream risk n. 工作流风险 A risk inside a single workstream, managed by the PM and the workstream lead.
  • Slip n. 推迟 A delay to a milestone, always stated in weeks or days with the new date.
  • Critical path n. 关键路径 The list of items that must close to make the next milestone.
  • Escalate v. 升级 To raise an issue to a higher authority (MD, CIO, ExCo).
  • Format upfront phr. 提前定格式 Setting the structure of the answer before asking, so the answer is in the right shape.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Board-ready" — the bar-setting word

"Board-ready" is a bar-setting word. It tells the PM that vague feelings do not qualify. Numbers, dates, and named risks qualify. The phrase forces the PM to translate their internal status into a format the MD will accept.

董事能听是定标词。它告诉 PM 模糊感觉不够格。

② "At risk of slipping by N weeks" — the slip pattern

Every slip is stated with a duration and a date: at risk of slipping UAT entry on 28 July by 1 to 2 weeks. The verb slip takes the milestone as object and the duration as adverbial. "It might be late" loses; the slip pattern wins.

每次推迟都附时长和日期：有把 7 月 28 日 UAT 进入推迟 1 到 2 周的风险。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Set the format upfront. "RAG, milestones, risk, ask — 30 seconds each" is 5 seconds to say. Without it, the PM talks for 10 minutes and you walk out with nothing.
  • Ask for a 1-pager, in the format. One page, in the box structure. Email it to yourself so you can forward to Karen.
  • State slips in weeks, with the date. "It might be late" loses. "1 to 2 weeks, UAT entry 28 July" wins.
  • Ask "programme risk or workstream risk?" Programme risk = SteerCo. Workstream risk = inside IT. The distinction is the trigger for escalation.
  • Commit with a time when you take an ask. "By 14:00 today" is a promise the PM holds you to. "I'll look into it" is a promise to forget.
  • Do not use status asks as venting sessions. The PM is not your therapist. The ask is a working session, 5 minutes, board-ready answer.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Set the format

You need a status update on the "KYC Refresh" programme before your 14:00 SteerCo prep call. Write the first 3 sentences you would say to the IT PM, including the format.

Exercise 2 — State a slip in weeks + date

The PM tells you "we might be late on the GDPR milestone." Rewrite this in the slip pattern (date + duration) for an MD update.

Exercise 3 — Distinguish programme risk from workstream risk

For each item, say whether it is a programme risk or a workstream risk, and what the escalation should be:

  1. UI designer is off sick for 1 week.
  2. Group Cyber cannot sign off FIDO2 in time, blocking 4 workstreams.
  3. One BA made a typo in a Jira ticket.
