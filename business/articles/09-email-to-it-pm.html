Email to IT PM — Request, Follow-up, Escalation, Sign-off

Article 09 · Module 3: Written Business Communication · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Subject, ask, deadline, escalation, named next step

1. Scenario 场景背景

You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. In 30 minutes you have 4 emails to write to your IT PM, Sara. The 4 situations: (1) a new ad-hoc request — a high-net-worth client demo on 30 August needs the new onboarding flow; (2) a follow-up on the UAT closure report that is 2 days late; (3) an escalation to Sara and Sara's boss on the FIDO2 timeline slip; (4) a sign-off confirmation on BRD v3.4. Each email must be under 150 words, with a clear subject, a clear ask, a clear deadline, and a clear next step.

你是客户开户业务负责人。30 分钟内你要给 IT PM Sara 写 4 封邮件。4 个场景：(1) 新临时需求——8 月 30 日高净值客户演示需要新开户流程；(2) UAT 关闭报告延迟 2 天的跟进；(3) FIDO2 时间线推迟升级到 Sara 和 Sara 的老板；(4) BRD v3.4 签批确认。每封邮件必须150 字以内，附明确主题、明确需要、明确截止、明确下一步。

An email from the business to IT follows a 5-part structure: Subject with action + project, Context in 1 sentence, Ask in 1 sentence, Deadline with date, Next step with owner. Anything more than 200 words is a meeting, not an email.

业务给 IT 的邮件遵循 5 部分结构：主题含动作+项目、1 句背景、1 句需要、附日期截止、下一步具名责任人。超过 200 字就是会议，不是邮件。

2. The 4 Emails 4 封邮件

Email 1 — Ad-hoc Request 临时需求

To: Sara Chen (IT PM) Cc: Priya (BA) Subject: Ad-hoc request — HNW client demo 30 August needs new onboarding flow
Sara,

One of our top-10 HNW clients is visiting HK on 30 August and wants a live demo of the new Digital Onboarding 2.0 flow.

Ask: Can the UAT environment support a 60-minute live demo with realistic HNW test data (joint account, FIDO2, eKYC) by 28 August EOD?

Deadline: Confirmation by Wednesday 27 August, 12:00, so I can brief the client.

Next step: If yes, please send the UAT environment URL and test data plan by Friday 29 August, 17:00.

Thanks,
JoyceSara，

我们前 10 大高净值客户之一 8 月 30 日来香港，想现场演示新的数字开户 2.0 流程。

需要：UAT 环境能否在 8 月 28 日 EOD 前支持 60 分钟现场演示，附真实高净值测试数据（共同账户、FIDO2、eKYC）？

截止：8 月 27 日周三 12:00 前确认，我好向客户汇报。

下一步：如可以，请在 8 月 29 日周五 17:00 前把 UAT 环境 URL 和测试数据方案发我。

谢谢，
Joyce

Email 2 — Follow-up 跟进

To: Sara Chen (IT PM) Cc: Anna (UAT lead) Subject: Follow-up — UAT closure report due today, not received
Sara,

The UAT closure report for Digital Onboarding 2.0 was due today EOD. I have not received it.

Ask: Please send the closure report by 21:00 tonight, or let me know the exact time it will arrive if there is a slip.

Deadline: 21:00 tonight, 15 August.

Context: I have Karen's co-sign window open until 22:00. If the report arrives after 21:00, I cannot get her signature before Monday, and the regulator pack is incomplete.

Thanks,
JoyceSara，

数字开户 2.0 的 UAT 关闭报告今天 EOD 截止。我还没收到。

需要：请在今晚 21:00 前发关闭报告；如有推迟，请告知具体到达时间。

截止：今晚 21:00，8 月 15 日。

背景：Karen 的联签窗口开放到 22:00。报告 21:00 后到，我周一前拿不到她的签字，监管包不完整。

谢谢，
Joyce

Email 3 — Escalation 升级

To: Sara Chen (IT PM); Wei Lin (CIO) Cc: Karen (MD); Steve (Group Cyber) Subject: Escalation — FIDO2 timeline now a programme risk, not a workstream risk
Sara, Wei,

The FIDO2 risk assessment sign-off by Group Cyber has slipped 3 weeks, from 5 August to 26 August. The slip is now on the critical path for Digital Onboarding 2.0 UAT entry (28 August) and the HKMA C-RAF submission (25 August).

Ask: Wei, can Group Cyber commit to 22 August (3 days earlier) with a named FTE escalation, or do we need to add contractor capacity?

Deadline: Decision by Wednesday 20 August, 17:00, so I can update Karen and the SteerCo.

Next step: I will hold the FIDO2 item on the SteerCo risk log until I have a named date and named FTE.

JoyceSara、Wei，

集团 Cyber 的 FIDO2 风险评估签批已推迟 3 周，从 8 月 5 日到 8 月 26 日。这个推迟现在在数字开户 2.0 UAT 进入（8 月 28 日）和 HKMA C-RAF 提交（8 月 25 日）的关键路径上。

需要：Wei，集团 Cyber 能否在 8 月 22 日（提前 3 天）承诺，附具名 FTE 升级？或者我们需要增加外包容量？

截止：8 月 20 日周三 17:00 前决定，我好更新 Karen 和 SteerCo。

下一步：在我拿到具名日期和具名 FTE 之前，我会把 FIDO2 项留在 SteerCo 风险日志上。

Joyce

Email 4 — Sign-off Confirmation 签批确认

To: Sara Chen (IT PM) Cc: Priya (BA); Karen (MD) Subject: BRD v3.4 sign-off confirmed — effective immediately
Sara,

Confirming BRD v3.4 sign-off for Digital Onboarding 2.0.

Effective: As of today, 14 August, 16:30 HKT.

Conditions met:
1. AC-4 added to FR-04 (joint-account failure handling) — confirmed in the v3.4 doc.
2. Out-of-scope list final (6 items, including video-based identity verification).

Next steps:
1. Priya to lock the v3.4 in Confluence today, 17:00.
2. Sara to issue the updated FTE plan against the v3.4 by Monday 18 August, 10:00.

Karen co-signs by Monday 18 August, 12:00.

JoyceSara，

确认数字开户 2.0 的 BRD v3.4 签批。

生效：今天 8 月 14 日 16:30 HKT 起。

已满足条件：
1. FR-04 新增 AC-4（共同账户失败处理）——v3.4 文档中已确认。
2. 超出范围清单终版（6 个项，含视频身份核验）。

下一步：
1. Priya 今天 17:00 前在 Confluence 锁定 v3.4。
2. Sara 8 月 18 日周一 10:00 前根据 v3.4 发出更新后的 FTE 计划。

Karen 8 月 18 日周一 12:00 前联签。

Joyce

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Subject with action + project Subject: Ad-hoc request — HNW client demo 30 August needs new onboarding flow 主题：临时需求——8 月 30 日高净值客户演示需要新开户流程 Subject line carries 3 things: the action (request / follow-up / escalation / sign-off), the project, and the date or event. One line, scannable in the inbox.
  • Ask in 1 sentence Ask: Can the UAT environment support a 60-minute live demo with realistic HNW test data by 28 August EOD? 需要：UAT 环境能否在 8 月 28 日 EOD 前支持 60 分钟现场演示，附真实高净值测试数据？ The ask is one sentence: the verb (can you / please send / please confirm) and the specific outcome. One ask per email.
  • Deadline with date Deadline: Confirmation by Wednesday 27 August, 12:00. 截止：8 月 27 日周三 12:00 前确认 Always name the date and the time. "ASAP" loses. "27 August 12:00" wins. The PM can hold you to it.
  • Next step with owner Next step: If yes, please send the UAT environment URL and test data plan by Friday 29 August, 17:00. 下一步：如可以，请在 8 月 29 日周五 17:00 前把 UAT 环境 URL 和测试数据方案发我 Close the email with the next step, owned by a named person, with a date. The PM walks out of the inbox knowing the next action.
  • Cc with intent Cc: Anna (UAT lead) — so the UAT lead sees the ask, not just the PM. 抄送：Anna（UAT 负责人）——让 UAT 负责人看到需要，不只是 PM Cc is a signal, not a courtesy. Cc the person who can do the work, the boss who needs visibility, or the regulator-side stakeholder. Always have a reason for each Cc.
  • Under 150 words, no essay Each email is under 150 words, with a clear subject, ask, deadline, next step. 每封邮件 150 字以内，附明确主题、需要、截止、下一步 Business-to-IT email is not an essay. 150 words max. If you need more, you need a meeting, not an email.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Subject line n. 主题行 The first thing the PM reads. Carries action + project + date.
  • Ask n. 需要 The single, named, dated action the email requests.
  • Deadline n. 截止 The specific date and time by which the PM must respond.
  • Next step n. 下一步 The action after the email: who does what by when.
  • Cc with intent n. 有意图的抄送 Each Cc has a reason (visibility, can-do, regulator-side).
  • Escalation email n. 升级邮件 An email to the PM and the PM's boss, with a named decision and deadline.
  • Sign-off confirmation n. 签批确认 The email that locks a BRD or closure report, with conditions met and next steps.
  • Effective n. 生效 The named date and time a sign-off takes effect (e.g., "as of 14 August, 16:30 HKT").

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Subject: [Action] — [Project] [date or event]" — the subject line

The subject line is a fixed pattern: Subject: [Action] — [Project] [date or event]. The grammar is colon + label + em-dash + project + event. One line. The PM can triage the inbox by subject alone.

主题行是固定模式：主题：[动作]——[项目] [日期或事件]。

② "Ask: [verb] [specific outcome] by [date]" — the ask line

The ask is one sentence: Ask: [verb] [specific outcome] by [date]. The verb is a request verb (can you / please send / please confirm). The specific outcome is a noun phrase (URL, plan, sign-off). The date is a named day and time.

需要是一句话：需要：[动词] [具体结果] 截止 [日期]。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Subject line carries 3 things: action, project, date. "Subject: Follow-up — UAT closure report due today, not received" is a triage-ready subject.
  • One ask per email. Multiple asks = multiple emails. The PM can hold one deadline, not three.
  • Always name the date and time. "ASAP" loses. "27 August 12:00" wins. The PM can plan against it.
  • Cc with intent, not courtesy. Each Cc has a reason: can-do, visibility, regulator-side. No courtesy Cc.
  • Under 150 words. Email is not an essay. If you need more, schedule a meeting.
  • Escalation email goes to PM and PM's boss. Cc the boss, name the decision, name the deadline. The boss will see the ask.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Write 3 subject lines

You have 3 emails to write: (1) request for a status update, (2) follow-up on a missed deadline, (3) sign-off confirmation. Write a subject line for each in the pattern "Subject: [Action] — [Project] [date or event]".

Exercise 2 — Write the ask line

You need the IT PM to send you a BRD v3.5 by Thursday 17:00. Write the ask line in the pattern "Ask: [verb] [specific outcome] by [date]".

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing deadline

An email says: "Please send the closure report ASAP." What is missing? Rewrite the line with a named date and time.
