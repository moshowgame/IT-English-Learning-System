MVP Cut Conversation — Phase 1 vs Phase 2, Customer Impact, Roadmap

Article 07 · Module 2: Requirements & Prioritisation · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 MVP, customer impact, roadmap, regret analysis, named commit

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 10:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. Two weeks before UAT entry, the IT PM Sara calls: capacity has dropped by 1.5 FTE (a contractor pulled out, a BA on parental leave). The MoSCoW from 4 weeks ago no longer fits. You need an MVP cut conversation with your MD, Karen, in 90 minutes. 3 items are at risk: FR-07 cross-sell engine ($8M forecast), FR-11 reporting dashboard, FR-14 HNW journey. The choice: cut 2 of 3 from Phase 1, defer to Phase 2 with a named commit date.

周五 10:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。UAT 进入前两周，IT PM Sara 来电：容量下降 1.5 FTE（一个外包退出、一个 BA 休产假）。4 周前的 MoSCoW 不再适用。90 分钟后你需要和 MD Karen 进行MVP 裁剪对话。3 个项有风险：FR-07 交叉销售引擎（800 万预测）、FR-11 报表仪表盘、FR-14 高净值旅程。选择：从第 1 阶段裁掉 3 选 2，推迟到第 2 阶段，附具名承诺日期。

The MVP cut is the most political conversation in a programme. The sponsor and the MD fight over what stays in Phase 1; the PM and Tech Lead fight over capacity. The right pattern: name the 3 items, name the cut, name the customer impact, name the Phase 2 commit date.

MVP 裁剪是项目群里最具政治性的对话。业务方和 MD 争什么留在第 1 阶段；PM 和 Tech Lead 争容量。正确模式：列出 3 项、给出裁剪决定、给出客户影响、给出第 2 阶段承诺日期。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sara (IT PM): Sara: Joyce, capacity dropped 1.5 FTE — contractor pulled out, BA on parental leave. We have 6 FTE for the next 9 weeks. The 8-item Phase 1 list from 4 weeks ago doesn't fit.Sara：Joyce，容量下降 1.5 FTE——外包退出、BA 休产假。未来 9 周我们只有 6 FTE。4 周前 8 项的第 1 阶段清单放不下。
You (Business Sponsor): What's the cut?裁剪方案是什么？
Sara: Sara: 3 items at risk. FR-07 cross-sell engine, FR-11 reporting dashboard, FR-14 HNW journey. We can keep 2 in Phase 1, defer 1 to Phase 2. Must stays as Must — that's the regulator baseline.Sara：3 个项有风险。FR-07 交叉销售引擎、FR-11 报表仪表盘、FR-14 高净值旅程。第 1 阶段留 2 个，1 个推迟到第 2 阶段。Must 保持 Must——那是监管基线。
You: What's the customer impact for each if deferred?每个推迟的客户影响是什么？
Sara: Sara: FR-07 cross-sell — no cross-sell at onboarding for Q4, $8M forecast moves to Q1 next year, customer experience is unchanged. FR-11 reporting dashboard — manual report for 3 months, BA team pulls data weekly, no customer impact. FR-14 HNW journey — 240 HNW clients use the manual workaround, relationship manager handles, no system, but a known friction point.Sara：FR-07 交叉销售——Q4 开户时无交叉销售，800 万预测挪到明年 Q1，客户体验不变。FR-11 报表仪表盘——3 个月人工报表，BA 团队每周拉数，无客户影响。FR-14 高净值旅程——240 个高净值客户用人工方案，客户经理处理，无系统，但是已知的摩擦点。
You: Regret analysis. Which deferral will we most regret 6 months from now?遗憾分析。6 个月后我们最遗憾推迟的是哪个？
Sara: Sara: FR-07 cross-sell. $8M slips to Q1, but more importantly, the cross-sell engine has a 3-month payback once built, and the longer we wait, the more we lose in Q1.Sara：FR-07 交叉销售。800 万挪到 Q1，但更重要的是，交叉销售引擎建成后有 3 个月回收期，越拖越在 Q1 损失越多。
You: My call. Defer FR-11 reporting dashboard and FR-14 HNW journey to Phase 2. Keep FR-07 cross-sell in Phase 1. Reason: $8M forecast, 3-month payback, no customer impact from deferral of the other two.我的决定。FR-11 报表仪表盘和 FR-14 高净值旅程推迟到第 2 阶段。FR-07 交叉销售保留在第 1 阶段。理由：800 万预测、3 个月回收期，另外两个推迟无客户影响。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Customer impact, per item What's the customer impact for each if deferred? 每个推迟的客户影响是什么？ Customer impact is the MD's question, not yours. Always have a per-item impact: customer experience, manual workaround, segment affected, NPS risk.
  • Regret analysis Which deferral will we most regret 6 months from now? 6 个月后我们最遗憾推迟的是哪个？ Regret analysis is the senior move. Forward-looking, 6-12 months out, with $ impact. The MD buys the regret, not the deferral list.
  • My call: keep X, defer Y, Z Defer FR-11 and FR-14. Keep FR-07 in Phase 1. 推迟 FR-11 和 FR-14，FR-07 保留在第 1 阶段 State the call in 1 sentence: what is kept, what is deferred. The MD can challenge the call, not the items.
  • Phase 2 commit date, named FR-11 by 28 February, FR-14 by 31 March. Both with named FTE in the Q1 plan. FR-11 2 月 28 日、FR-14 3 月 31 日，两个都在 Q1 计划里有具名 FTE A deferral is not a deferral without a named commit date and named FTE. "We'll do it later" loses; "by 28 February, 2 FTE named" wins.
  • Phase 1 / Phase 2 commit, with counts Phase 1 commit (7). Phase 2 commit (2). 第 1 阶段承诺（7），第 2 阶段承诺（2） Always close with the count of items in each phase. The MD can verify "we have 7 in Phase 1, 2 deferred with dates" in 5 seconds.
  • Lock in BRD vN.M Lock this in the BRD v3.5 by Monday. 周一前在 BRD v3.5 锁定 Always name the BRD version that will hold the cut. v3.5 is a verifiable artefact. The PM can confirm by Monday.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • MVP (Minimum Viable Product) n. 最小可行产品 The smallest set of features that delivers the Phase 1 outcome.
  • Cut n. 裁剪 Removing an item from Phase 1 and deferring to Phase 2.
  • Customer impact n. 客户影响 The effect of a deferral on customer experience, NPS, segment, manual workaround.
  • Regret analysis n. 遗憾分析 Forward-looking 6-12 month view: which deferral will hurt most.
  • Payback period n. 回收期 The time it takes for the benefit to equal the cost (3 months for cross-sell engine).
  • Manual workaround n. 人工方案 A non-system way to deliver the customer outcome while the system is built later.
  • Named FTE n. 具名 FTE A specific person assigned by name, not a placeholder.
  • Phase 2 commit date n. 第 2 阶段承诺日期 The named date by which a deferred item will be delivered, with named FTE.
  • Lock in BRD vN.M phr. 在 BRD vN.M 锁定 Recording the cut in the BRD version, signed by sponsor and MD.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Customer impact for each if deferred" — the conditional pattern

Customer impact is always asked in the conditional: if deferred, what's the impact? The grammar is if [past participle], [impact]. One sentence per item. The MD can read the impact list in 30 seconds.

客户影响总是用条件句问：若推迟，影响是什么？。

② "Phase 1 commit (N). Phase 2 commit (N)." — the count sentence

The cut ends with two counts: Phase 1 commit (N), Phase 2 commit (N). The grammar is parallel Phase N commit (Number), repeated. The MD can verify the totals in 5 seconds.

裁剪以两个数字结束：第 1 阶段承诺（N）、第 2 阶段承诺（N）。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always have a per-item customer impact. "It's a cut" is not an answer. "FR-11: manual report for 3 months, no customer impact" is.
  • Run the regret analysis forward, 6-12 months. "Which deferral will we most regret?" is the senior question. Pair it with $ and payback.
  • State the call in 1 sentence. "Defer X and Y, keep Z, because $8M and 3-month payback" is a call. "We need to think" is not.
  • Every deferral needs a named commit date and named FTE. "By 28 February, 2 FTE named" is a deferral with a plan. "Later" is a wish.
  • Close with the counts. Phase 1 commit (N), Phase 2 commit (N). The MD verifies in 5 seconds.
  • Lock the cut in a named BRD version. v3.5 is a verifiable artefact. The PM confirms by Monday.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Customer impact for 3 items

You must cut 2 of 3 items: A (revenue-tied, $5M, no customer impact if deferred), B (revenue-tied, $2M, manual workaround works), C (nice-to-have, 0 revenue, but high NPS impact). Write the customer impact sentence for each in the pattern "Item: impact, workaround, segment".

Exercise 2 — Regret analysis

You must defer 1 of 2 items. Item X has 3-month payback if built, $4M forecast. Item Y has 18-month payback, $1M forecast. Which will you most regret deferring in 6 months? Justify with payback and $.

Exercise 3 — Phase 2 commit date

You defer a dashboard to Phase 2. Write the Phase 2 commit sentence with a named date, named FTE, and named owner.
