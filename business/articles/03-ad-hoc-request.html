Raising an Ad-hoc Request — "Can You Also..."

Article 03 · Module 1: Daily Business Touchpoints · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Scope check, capacity, trade, prioritisation, named ask

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 16:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. Your MD has just come from a Wealth Management town hall where 12 high-net-worth clients asked for video-based identity verification — a feature the original Digital Onboarding 2.0 BRD explicitly excluded as "out of scope". The MD wants it for the November product launch. You have 30 minutes to frame this as an ad-hoc request to your IT PM, Sara, with a clear scope, capacity check, and trade.

周三 16:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。你的 MD 刚从一场财富管理客户大会回来，12 名高净值客户要求视频身份核验——这是原始数字开户 2.0 BRD明确列为"超出范围"的功能。MD 想要在 11 月产品发布时加上。你有 30 分钟把它框定为对 IT PM Sara 的临时需求，附明确范围、容量检查、权衡。

An ad-hoc request is a scope change in disguise. The right pattern is: state the request, ask the scope impact, ask the capacity impact, propose a trade. The wrong pattern is "can you also..." with no follow-up.

临时需求是伪装的范围变更。正确模式：陈述需求、问范围影响、问容量影响、提出权衡。错误模式是"你能不能顺便……"然后没有下文。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Business Sponsor): Sara, I have an ad-hoc request from the business side. Before you say yes or no, let's walk through scope, capacity, and trade.Sara，业务方有个临时需求。在你说行或不行之前，我们过一下范围、容量、权衡。
Sara (IT PM): Sara: OK. What's the ask?Sara：好。需求是什么？
You: Video-based identity verification for the high-net-worth segment. Twelve HNW clients asked for it at the WM town hall yesterday. MD wants it for the November product launch.高净值客户的视频身份核验。12 名高净值客户昨天在 WM 客户大会提出。MD 想要 11 月产品发布时上线。
Sara: Sara: That was explicitly out of scope in the BRD. What's the scope impact?Sara：那在 BRD 里明确是超出范围的。范围影响是什么？
You: I want to ask you. What does it add — 4 weeks, 8 weeks? Which workstream, which vendor?我想问你。它加多少——4 周、8 周？哪个工作流、哪个厂商？
Sara: Sara: Identity verification workstream, new vendor, Onfido video. 6 to 8 weeks build, plus 2 weeks Group Cyber risk assessment, plus 2 weeks UAT, plus MAS notification because it's a new customer-facing biometric. Total: 10 to 12 weeks, and we land on 15 December, not 15 November.Sara：身份核验工作流，新厂商 Onfido 视频。6 到 8 周构建，加 2 周集团 Cyber 风险评估，加 2 周 UAT，加 MAS 报备因为是新的客户人脸生物识别。总计：10 到 12 周，落地 12 月 15 日，不是 11 月 15 日。
You: Capacity impact?容量影响？
Sara: Sara: 3 FTE for 12 weeks — 1 BA, 1 engineer, 1 UAT lead. That delays 2 other items on the critical path: the cross-sell engine and the audit log retention module.Sara：3 FTE 12 周——1 BA、1 工程师、1 UAT 负责人。这会推迟关键路径上另外 2 个项：交叉销售引擎和审计日志保留模块。
You: Trade options?权衡方案？
Sara: Sara: Three. Option A — approve, slip 2 items, MAS notification, 15 December. Option B — approve, hire 2 contractors, $360K, 15 December, no slip on the 2 items. Option C — defer to Phase 2 (Q2 next year), and we ship a manual workaround in November: 2 relationship managers do the video call and confirm ID, no system change.Sara：三个。方案 A——批准、推迟 2 个项、MAS 报备、12 月 15 日。方案 B——批准、招 2 名外包、36 万美元、12 月 15 日，2 个项不推迟。方案 C——推迟到第 2 阶段（明年 Q2），11 月上线人工方案：2 名客户经理视频通话并人工确认身份证，无系统变更。
You: I take it to the MD tomorrow with 3 options. My recommendation will be Option C — manual workaround in November, video build in Q2, because we keep the 2 critical-path items and we don't slip the MAS milestone. What do you need from me by when?我明天把 3 个方案拿给 MD。我的建议是方案 C——11 月人工方案，Q2 上视频，因为保住 2 个关键路径项，且不推迟 MAS 里程碑。你什么时候需要我做什么？
Sara: Sara: MD decision by Friday EOD, so I can lock the FTE plan on Monday. Send me the MD's name and the meeting time.Sara：周五 EOD 前 MD 决策，我周一能锁定 FTE 计划。把 MD 的名字和会议时间发我。
You: Done. Decision by Friday EOD.好。周五 EOD 前决策。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Scope, capacity, trade Let's walk through scope, capacity, and trade. 我们过一下范围、容量、权衡 The 3-box frame for any ad-hoc request. Without it, "can you also..." becomes an undocumented scope change. With it, the request has a shape the PM can answer.
  • What's the scope impact? What does it add — 4 weeks, 8 weeks? Which workstream, which vendor? 它加多少——4 周、8 周？哪个工作流、哪个厂商？ Scope is in weeks, workstream, and vendor. Without these 3, "scope impact" is a feeling, not an answer.
  • Capacity impact Capacity impact? 容量影响？ Capacity is in FTE and weeks. Always ask "how many FTE for how long". 3 FTE for 12 weeks is the precise answer; "we'll need people" is not.
  • Trade options, A / B / C Three trade options. Option A, Option B, Option C. 三个权衡方案。方案 A、方案 B、方案 C Always present 3 options: cheap, fast, deferred. The MD chooses with eyes open. One option is a diktat; three is a decision.
  • Manual workaround Manual workaround in November: 2 relationship managers do the video call and confirm ID. 11 月人工方案：2 名客户经理视频通话并人工确认身份证 A manual workaround is the "ship now, build later" option. It trades system risk for relationship-manager time. The MD can choose this if customer experience matters more than system scale.
  • What do you need from me by when? What do you need from me by when? 你什么时候需要我做什么？ Always close with a named, dated ask. The PM walks out knowing what date the next gate is (MD decision by Friday EOD).

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Ad-hoc request n. 临时需求 A request that was not in the original BRD or scope.
  • Scope impact n. 范围影响 The change in timeline, workstream, and vendor triggered by the request.
  • Capacity impact n. 容量影响 The FTE and weeks needed to deliver the request.
  • Trade options n. 权衡方案 A set of 2 or 3 ways to handle a request (cheap, fast, deferred).
  • Manual workaround n. 人工方案 A non-system way to deliver the customer outcome while the system is built later.
  • Phase 2 n. 第 2 阶段 A later release of the same programme, often Q+1 or Q+2.
  • MAS notification n. MAS 报备 A formal notice to the regulator for a new customer-facing biometric or material change.
  • Out of scope n. 超出范围 Explicitly excluded in the BRD; an in-scope change requires a formal scope change.
  • Lock the FTE plan phr. 锁定 FTE 计划 Confirming the named resources and start dates.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Scope, capacity, trade" — the 3-box frame

The 3-box frame is the most efficient way to handle an ad-hoc request. Scope answers "how much more work". Capacity answers "how many people for how long". Trade answers "what are the options". One frame, three answers, one decision.

范围、容量、权衡是处理临时需求最高效的方式。

② "Option A / Option B / Option C" — the three-option frame

Three options is the cognitive budget for an MD. A = approve and slip. B = approve and hire. C = defer and workaround. The MD picks one. Two options is a yes/no; three is a decision.

三个方案是 MD 的认知预算。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always frame an ad-hoc request as a scope change. "Can you also..." is a hidden scope change. Surface it openly with scope, capacity, trade.
  • Present 3 options, not 1. A is approve-and-slip, B is approve-and-hire, C is defer-and-workaround. The MD picks one.
  • Scope is in weeks, workstream, vendor. "It might be 4 to 8 weeks, identity verification workstream, Onfido video" is an answer. "It'll take some time" is not.
  • Capacity is in FTE and weeks. 3 FTE for 12 weeks is a precise answer. "We'll need people" is not.
  • Always include a manual workaround option. It is the cheapest path to a customer outcome and it preserves the original critical path.
  • Close with a named, dated ask. "MD decision by Friday EOD" is a date. The PM walks out knowing the next gate.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Apply the 3-box frame

A retail banking head asks IT to add a "voice biometrics" feature to the mobile app next month. Apply the 3-box frame (scope, capacity, trade) and write the first 4 sentences you would say to the IT PM.

Exercise 2 — Build 3 trade options

The PM says the request adds 8 weeks and 4 FTE. Build 3 trade options (approve-and-slip, approve-and-hire, defer-and-workaround) and write a 1-line description for each.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing ask

A PM says: "We can do it, but we'll need to delay the audit log module." What is the missing ask? What would you say to the PM to make the ask explicit?
