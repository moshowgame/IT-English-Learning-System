Business Case for Investment — NPV, Payback, Customer Impact, Alternatives

Article 11 · Module 3: Written Business Communication · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 NPV, payback, customer impact, regulator, alternatives, ownership

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 14:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. You have a 60-minute slot with the Group Investment Committee (CFO, CRO, COO, your MD Karen) to defend the Digital Onboarding 2.0 business case for the next phase. Phase 2 capex is $24M over 18 months, focused on: (1) video-based identity verification, (2) voice biometrics, (3) corporate onboarding, (4) cross-border onboarding. You have a 12-slide deck. The CFO buys NPV. The CRO buys regulator. The COO buys operational risk. Karen buys customer impact.

周二 14:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。你有 60 分钟和集团投资委员会（CFO、CRO、COO、你的 MD Karen）一起为数字开户 2.0下阶段商业案例辩护。第 2 阶段 capex 2,400 万美元，18 个月，聚焦：(1) 视频身份核验、(2) 声纹生物识别、(3) 企业开户、(4) 跨境开户。你有 12 张幻灯片。CFO 买 NPV；CRO 买监管；COO 买运营风险；Karen 买客户影响。

The business case is the most political document in the bank's planning cycle. The pattern: problem, options, recommended option, NPV, payback, customer impact, regulator impact, alternatives, ownership. The CFO signs when NPV is positive and payback is short. The CRO signs when regulator impact is clear. The MD signs when customer impact is named.

商业案例是银行规划周期里最具政治性的文档。模式：问题、方案、推荐方案、NPV、回收期、客户影响、监管影响、替代方案、所有权。

2. The 12-Slide Deck 12 张幻灯片

Slide 1 — Problem 问题

Onboarding today: 4 days for retail, 7 days for HNW, 14 days for corporate, 21 days for cross-border. Customer NPS: 32 (industry average: 48). Manual rework rate: 18%. Top-10 HNW clients have flagged onboarding friction as the #1 reason for considering a competitor.今天开户：零售 4 天、高净值 7 天、企业 14 天、跨境 21 天。客户 NPS 32（行业平均 48）。人工返工率 18%。前 10 大高净值客户已把开户摩擦列为考虑转投他行的首要原因。

Slide 2 — Three Options 三个方案

Option A — Do nothing. Status quo, friction stays, NPS stays at 32, top-10 HNW at risk ($40M AUM churn risk in Year 1).
Option B — Digital Onboarding 2.0 Phase 2 (recommended). Video, voice, corporate, cross-border. $24M capex, 18 months, NPV $42M, payback 22 months.
Option C — Buy a third-party platform. Vendor SaaS, $9M annual subscription, 3-year commitment, less control on customer data and regulator audit trail.方案 A——不作为。维持现状，摩擦不变，NPS 保持 32，前 10 大高净值有风险（第 1 年 4,000 万 AUM 流失风险）。
方案 B——数字开户 2.0 第 2 阶段（推荐）。视频、声纹、企业、跨境。2,400 万 capex，18 个月，NPV 4,200 万，回收期 22 个月。
方案 C——购买第三方平台。厂商 SaaS，每年 900 万订阅，3 年承诺，客户数据和监管审计轨迹控制较弱。

Slide 3 — NPV and Payback (CFO language)

NPV: $42M over 5 years, discount rate 9%.
Payback: 22 months blended.
IRR: 28%.
Sensitivity: NPV positive across all 3 scenarios (base, downside, regulator-tight).
Sensitivity break-even: $14M capex overrun, or $8M revenue downside, or 6-month delay — NPV goes to zero, not negative.NPV：5 年 4,200 万，折现率 9%。
回收期：混合 22 个月。
IRR：28%。
敏感性：3 个情景（基线、下行、监管收紧）下 NPV 均为正。
敏感性盈亏平衡：capex 超支 1,400 万，或收入下行 800 万，或延迟 6 个月——NPV 归零而非为负。

Slide 4 — Customer Impact (MD language)

Onboarding time: 4 days → 4 hours retail, 7 days → 1 day HNW, 14 days → 2 days corporate, 21 days → 3 days cross-border.
Customer NPS: 32 → 56 (industry-leading).
Manual rework rate: 18% → 4%.
Top-10 HNW retention: secured, 9 of 10 signed commitment letters.
AUM growth: $80M Year 1, $240M Year 3.开户时间：零售 4 天 → 4 小时，高净值 7 天 → 1 天，企业 14 天 → 2 天，跨境 21 天 → 3 天。
客户 NPS：32 → 56（行业领先）。
人工返工率：18% → 4%。
前 10 大高净值客户保留：已确保，10 个中 9 个签承诺函。
AUM 增长：第 1 年 8,000 万，第 3 年 2.4 亿。

Slide 5 — Regulator Impact (CRO language)

MAS TRM: 14 cyber hygiene items, full closure.
HKMA C-RAF: Tier-1 attestation, year 2 of 3-year plan.
PDPO: customer consent management, full alignment.
MAS notification: biometric features, completed within 30 days of launch.
Open items: zero Tier-1 findings, zero supervisory letters.MAS TRM：14 项网络卫生，完整关闭。
HKMA C-RAF：Tier-1 合规声明，3 年计划第 2 年。
PDPO：客户同意管理，完全对齐。
MAS 报备：生物识别功能，上线后 30 天内完成。
未关闭项：零 Tier-1 发现，零监管函。

Slide 6 — Operational Risk (COO language)

BAU impact: 38% reduction in manual onboarding hours, 12 FTEs redeployed to higher-value advisory work.
System availability: 99.95% target, multi-region active-active.
Cyber: FIDO2 across all customers, vendor on the panel, no new vendor.
Vendor lock-in: low, 3 vendor on panel, 18-month switching cost.BAU 影响：人工开户工时减少 38%，12 个 FTE 重新部署到高价值顾问工作。
系统可用性：99.95% 目标，多区域 active-active。
网络安全：所有客户 FIDO2，厂商在 panel 上，无新厂商。
厂商锁定：低，panel 上 3 个厂商，18 个月切换成本。

Slide 7 — Alternatives 已考虑

Outsourced onboarding: BPO vendor, $14M over 3 years, no IP retention, regulator risk on data residency.
Build with internal team: 28 months, $32M capex, slower time-to-market, $8M NPV negative.
Defer to Year 3: lose 18 months of AUM growth ($40M), regulator trajectory slips, HNW retention at risk.外包开户：BPO 厂商，3 年 1,400 万，无 IP 保留，数据驻留有监管风险。
内部团队自建：28 个月，3,200 万 capex，上市时间更慢，NPV 负 800 万。
推迟到第 3 年：损失 18 个月 AUM 增长（4,000 万），监管轨迹推迟，高净值保留有风险。

Slide 8 — One Decision 一个决策

Approve $24M capex for Digital Onboarding 2.0 Phase 2, 18 months, NPV $42M, payback 22 months.
Approve $9M opex per year for 3 years for vendor subscriptions and Group Cyber FTE.
Authorise contract negotiation with 2 named vendors, target execution by 31 December.批准 2,400 万 capex 用于数字开户 2.0 第 2 阶段，18 个月，NPV 4,200 万，回收期 22 个月。
批准 3 年每年 900 万 opex 用于厂商订阅和集团 Cyber FTE。授权与 2 家具名厂商进行合同谈判，目标 12 月 31 日签署。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Three options, A / B / C, with NPV and payback Option A — Do nothing. Option B — Recommended. Option C — Buy SaaS. 方案 A——不作为。方案 B——推荐。方案 C——购买 SaaS Always present 3 options: do nothing, build, buy. The CFO needs the comparison. "We should do it" loses; "3 options with NPV for each" wins.
  • NPV, payback, IRR NPV $42M, payback 22 months, IRR 28%. NPV 4,200 万，回收期 22 个月，IRR 28% The CFO's three numbers. NPV is over 5 years with discount rate. Payback in months. IRR in percent. Always all three.
  • Sensitivity, with break-even Sensitivity break-even: $14M capex overrun, or $8M revenue downside, or 6-month delay — NPV goes to zero. 敏感性盈亏平衡：capex 超支 1,400 万，或收入下行 800 万，或延迟 6 个月——NPV 归零 The CFO's stress test. Always name the break-even point, with the unit. "It's robust" loses; "NPV positive across 3 scenarios, breaks at $14M overrun" wins.
  • Customer impact, in NPS and AUM NPS 32 → 56. AUM growth $80M Year 1, $240M Year 3. NPS 32 → 56。AUM 增长第 1 年 8,000 万、第 3 年 2.4 亿 The MD's two numbers. NPS is a measure of customer satisfaction. AUM is the $ consequence. The MD signs when both are named.
  • Regulator impact, with named items MAS TRM 14 items full closure. HKMA C-RAF Tier-1 attestation. Zero open Tier-1 findings. MAS TRM 14 项完整关闭。HKMA C-RAF Tier-1 合规声明。零未关闭 Tier-1 发现 The CRO's language. Always name the regulator item, the closure status, and the open items. The CRO signs when the list is clear.
  • Operational risk, in BAU and FTE 38% reduction in manual onboarding hours, 12 FTEs redeployed. 人工开户工时减少 38%，12 个 FTE 重新部署 The COO's language. BAU impact in hours and FTEs. The COO signs when the redeployment plan is named.
  • One decision, with 3 actions Approve $24M capex. Approve $9M annual opex for 3 years. Authorise contract negotiation with 2 named vendors. 批准 2,400 万 capex。批准 3 年每年 900 万 opex。授权与 2 家具名厂商谈判 The decision is one slide, with 2-3 numbered actions: capex, opex, contract authority. The committee signs the actions.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • NPV (Net Present Value) n. 净现值 The total $ benefit of a project over its life, discounted to today.
  • Payback period n. 回收期 The time it takes for the benefit to equal the cost.
  • IRR (Internal Rate of Return) n. 内部收益率 The discount rate that makes NPV zero. Higher IRR = better project.
  • Sensitivity / break-even n. 敏感性 / 盈亏平衡 The point at which NPV goes to zero: capex overrun, revenue downside, or delay.
  • Capex / Opex n. 资本支出 / 运营支出 Capital expenditure (one-time) vs operating expenditure (annual).
  • BAU impact n. BAU 影响 The change in business-as-usual operations: hours, FTEs, redeployment.
  • AUM (Assets Under Management) n. 管理资产 The $ value of customer assets the bank manages.
  • NPS (Net Promoter Score) n. 净推荐值 A measure of customer satisfaction (0-100).
  • Vendor lock-in n. 厂商锁定 The cost and risk of being tied to a single vendor.
  • Authorise contract negotiation phr. 授权合同谈判 The committee's authority for the CIO to sign a vendor contract.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "NPV $42M, payback 22 months, IRR 28%" — the CFO's three numbers

The CFO's three numbers are stated in parallel: NPV $X, payback N months, IRR N%. The grammar is Category Number Unit, repeated three times. The CFO reads the three numbers in 5 seconds.

CFO 的三个数字平行陈述：NPV X 美元、回收期 N 个月、IRR N%。

② "Sensitivity break-even: $14M capex overrun — NPV goes to zero" — the break-even sentence

The break-even sentence has a fixed structure: Sensitivity break-even: [trigger] — NPV goes to zero, not negative. The grammar is break-even: trigger, outcome. One sentence. The CFO buys the trigger and the outcome.

盈亏平衡句子有固定结构：盈亏平衡：【触发】——NPV 归零而非为负。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 3 options, with NPV for each. A (do nothing), B (build, recommended), C (buy SaaS). The CFO needs the comparison.
  • NPV, payback, IRR — three numbers, every time. The CFO's three numbers. Always all three. The CFO reads them in 5 seconds.
  • Sensitivity with break-even. Name the trigger (capex overrun, revenue downside, delay) and the outcome (NPV goes to zero). The CFO buys the trigger.
  • Customer impact in NPS and AUM. The MD's two numbers. NPS is satisfaction; AUM is $. The MD signs when both are named.
  • Regulator impact in named items. MAS, HKMA, PDPO — name the regulator, the closure status, the open items. The CRO signs when the list is clear.
  • Operational risk in BAU and FTE. The COO's language. Hours saved, FTEs redeployed. The COO signs when the redeployment is named.
  • One decision, with 2-3 numbered actions. Capex, opex, contract authority. The committee signs the actions, not the strategy.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Write the CFO's three numbers

You have a 3-year project: $12M capex, $4M annual benefit, $1M annual opex, 9% discount rate. Calculate NPV (rough), payback in months, and IRR (rough). State all three in the parallel pattern.

Exercise 2 — Build 3 options

You are proposing a $6M investment in a customer service AI assistant. Build 3 options (do nothing, build, buy SaaS) with NPV, payback, and IRR for each.

Exercise 3 — Sensitivity break-even

Your project has $18M NPV, $24M capex. Write the break-even sentence for a $6M capex overrun.
