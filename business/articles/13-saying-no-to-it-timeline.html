Saying No to an IT Timeline — Customer Event, Regulator Date, Counter-Proposal

Article 13 · Module 4: Difficult Business Conversations · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Customer event, regulator date, revenue impact, counter-proposal, named alternative

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 09:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The IT PM, Sara, called Friday: the Digital Onboarding 2.0 go-live has slipped from 18 August to 8 September (3 weeks) because of a vendor issue at Onfido. You have a HSBC Group customer event on 15 September where 30 of your top HNW clients are flying in for a product showcase. The CIO (Wei) and your MD (Karen) are on the call. The 18 August date was already committed in writing to the top 10 HNW clients. Saying yes to 8 September means losing the customer event and 9 signed commitment letters. You have 60 minutes to push back with a counter-proposal.

周一 09:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。IT PM Sara 周五来电：数字开户 2.0上线从8 月 18 日推迟到 9 月 8 日（3 周），因为 Onfido 厂商问题。你有一个9 月 15 日汇丰集团客户活动，30 名顶级高净值客户飞来参加产品展示。CIO（Wei）和你的 MD（Karen）都在电话上。8 月 18 日已书面承诺给前 10 大高净值客户。同意 9 月 8 日意味着失去客户活动和 9 份已签承诺函。你有 60 分钟提出反提案。

Saying no to IT is the hardest sponsor conversation. The pattern: acknowledge the slip, name the business consequence with $ and customer impact, present 2-3 counter-proposals, let the IT side choose. The goal is not to win; the goal is to find a path that protects the customer commitment.

对 IT 说"不"是最难的发起方对话。模式：承认推迟、列出业务后果附 $ 和客户影响、提出 2-3 个反提案、让 IT 选。目标不是赢；目标是找到保护客户承诺的路径。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sara (IT PM): Sara: Joyce, the 18 August go-live is at risk. Onfido eKYC vendor has a 3-week delivery issue, their identity verification component is delayed. Go-live moves to 8 September, 3 weeks slip, no other path.Sara：Joyce，8 月 18 日上线有风险。Onfido eKYC 厂商有 3 周交付问题，他们的身份核验组件延期。上线挪到 9 月 8 日，3 周推迟，没有其他路径。
You (Business Sponsor): I hear the vendor issue. Let me name the business consequence first, then we look at counter-proposals.我听到厂商问题了。让我先列业务后果，然后我们看反提案。
You: Consequence 1: 9 signed commitment letters from the top-10 HNW clients are tied to 18 August. 3-week slip means 4 clients will reconsider, $14M AUM at risk.后果 1：前 10 大高净值客户的 9 份已签承诺函绑 8 月 18 日。3 周推迟意味着 4 个客户会重新考虑，1,400 万 AUM 有风险。
You: Consequence 2: HSBC Group customer event on 15 September, 30 HNW clients flying in. If we cannot demo the new flow, the event loses its anchor, and the MD-level relationship hits a low point.后果 2：9 月 15 日汇丰集团客户活动，30 名高净值客户飞来。如果我们不能演示新流程，活动失去锚点，MD 级关系会跌到低点。
You: Consequence 3: November product launch is tied to the new flow. 3-week slip cascades to the launch, so Q4 revenue ($4M forecast) moves to Q1 next year, and the year-end number is short by $4M.后果 3：11 月产品发布绑新流程。3 周推迟级联到发布，Q4 收入（400 万预测）挪到明年 Q1，年终数字差 400 万。
You: Total business consequence: $14M AUM at risk, $4M revenue slip, reputational hit on the 15 September event.业务后果总计：1,400 万 AUM 有风险，400 万收入推迟，9 月 15 日活动声誉影响。
Wei (CIO): Wei: I hear the consequences. What's the counter-proposal?Wei：我听到后果了。反提案是什么？
You: Three counter-proposals, in order of preference.三个反提案，按优先级。
You: Counter-proposal 1 (preferred): split the go-live. Phase 1a on 18 August with everything except the eKYC re-verification step (defer that 3 weeks). Customer journey works, manual fallback for the 8% non-HK ID cases (already in the BRD v3.4 as a documented fallback). Onfido re-verification rolls out 8 September as Phase 1b.反提案 1（首选）：拆分上线。第 1a 阶段 8 月 18 日，除 eKYC 复验步骤外全部（那一步推迟 3 周）。客户旅程可用，8% 非香港身份证走人工回退（BRD v3.4 已记录为文档化回退）。Onfido 复验 9 月 8 日作为第 1b 阶段推出。
You: Counter-proposal 2: swap Onfido for the second vendor on the panel, ID.me, who can deliver in 2 weeks. Cost: $400K extra for the swap, plus 2 weeks of re-testing. Go-live 1 September instead of 18 August, still misses the 15 September event by 2 weeks.反提案 2：把 Onfido 换成 panel 上第二家厂商 ID.me，他们 2 周能交付。成本：换厂商多花 40 万，加 2 周重测。上线 9 月 1 日而不是 8 月 18 日，仍然错过 9 月 15 日活动 2 周。
You: Counter-proposal 3 (last resort): accept 8 September, cancel or downsize the 15 September event to a webinar, and personally call the 4 at-risk HNW clients this week to manage the relationship.反提案 3（最后手段）：接受 9 月 8 日，取消或缩小 9 月 15 日活动为线上 webinar，本周亲自打给 4 个有风险的高净值客户管理关系。
Wei: Wei: Counter-proposal 1 is interesting. Sara, technical feasibility of the split?Wei：反提案 1 有意思。Sara，拆分的技术可行性？
Sara: Sara: Feasible. The eKYC re-verification is a stand-alone service, we can deploy the rest of the platform on 18 August, and the re-verification step on 8 September as a config change, no re-test of the full platform. 1 week of re-test for the eKYC step only.Sara：可行。eKYC 复验是独立服务，我们可以 8 月 18 日部署平台其余部分，9 月 8 日把复验步骤作为配置变更部署，无需对整个平台重测。只需对 eKYC 步骤重测 1 周。
Wei: Wei: Counter-proposal 1 approved. Sara, BRD v3.5 documents the split, and a separate UAT entry for the eKYC step on 1 September. Karen, you sign v3.5 by Friday.Wei：反提案 1 批准。Sara，BRD v3.5 记录拆分，eKYC 步骤的 UAT 进入独立安排在 9 月 1 日。Karen，周五前你签 v3.5。
Karen (MD): Karen: Approved. Joyce, send me the customer event update by EOD — new flow is live, demo confirmed.Karen：批准。Joyce，EOD 前把客户活动更新发我——新流程上线，演示确认。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Acknowledge the slip, then consequences I hear the vendor issue. Let me name the business consequence first, then we look at counter-proposals. 我听到厂商问题了。让我先列业务后果，然后我们看反提案 Always acknowledge the IT issue first. Then name the business consequence. The structure shows you understand both sides before pushing back.
  • Consequence with $ and customer Consequence 1: 9 commitment letters tied to 18 August. 3-week slip means 4 clients will reconsider, $14M AUM at risk. 后果 1：9 份承诺函绑 8 月 18 日。3 周推迟意味着 4 个客户会重新考虑，1,400 万 AUM 有风险 Each consequence has two: a customer-side fact and a $ number. "Customers will be upset" loses. "4 clients will reconsider, $14M AUM at risk" wins.
  • Total business consequence Total business consequence: $14M AUM at risk, $4M revenue slip, reputational hit. 业务后果总计：1,400 万 AUM 有风险、400 万收入推迟、声誉影响 Always close the consequence section with a total. The CIO and the MD see the sum before the counter-proposals. The total frames the trade.
  • Three counter-proposals, in order Three counter-proposals, in order of preference. 三个反提案，按优先级 Always present 3 counter-proposals, in order: preferred, second-best, last-resort. The CIO chooses from 3, not from 1.
  • Counter-proposal 1, with cost and trade Counter-proposal 1: split the go-live. Phase 1a on 18 August, Phase 1b on 8 September. 反提案 1：拆分上线。第 1a 阶段 8 月 18 日、第 1b 阶段 9 月 8 日 The preferred counter-proposal. Always name the split, the dates, the cost, the trade. The CIO sees the full picture.
  • Counter-proposal 3, last resort Counter-proposal 3 (last resort): accept 8 September, cancel the customer event. 反提案 3（最后手段）：接受 9 月 8 日，取消客户活动 Always name the last-resort option. It tells the CIO you are not bluffing — you have a real plan for the worst case. The CIO respects the preparedness.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Counter-proposal n. 反提案 A business-side alternative to the IT-proposed slip. Always 2-3 options.
  • Split the go-live phr. 拆分上线 Phase 1a on the original date, Phase 1b on the slip date. The customer-facing parts ship first.
  • Manual fallback (documented) n. 人工回退（已记录） A non-system way to handle a customer, documented in the BRD as the in-scope behaviour.
  • Customer event n. 客户活动 A bank-hosted showcase (often with travel) for top clients. Anchored on a product demo.
  • Commitment letter n. 承诺函 A signed letter from a top client committing to a product launch date.
  • AUM at risk n. AUM 风险 The $ value of client assets that may move to a competitor if the date slips.
  • Revenue slip n. 收入推迟 The forecast $ that moves to a later quarter because of the date slip.
  • Reputational hit n. 声誉影响 The loss of trust with the client, the MD, or the regulator caused by the slip.
  • Swap vendor phr. 更换厂商 Replacing vendor A with vendor B (e.g., Onfido with ID.me) to recover the timeline.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Consequence N: [customer fact]. [Consequence], $[number] at risk." — the consequence sentence

A consequence is one sentence with two clauses: [customer fact]. [Consequence], $[number] at risk. The grammar is two clauses separated by a period, with a $ number in the second clause. The CIO reads the customer + $ pair in 5 seconds.

后果是一句话两个从句：【客户事实】。【后果】，【数字】美元有风险。

② "Counter-proposal N (preferred/second-best/last-resort): [option]" — the counter-proposal label

A counter-proposal has a label: Counter-proposal N (preferred / second-best / last-resort): [option]. The grammar is label + parenthetical ranking + colon + option. The CIO sees the rank before the detail.

反提案有标签：反提案 N（首选/次选/最后手段）：【方案】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Acknowledge the IT issue first, then the consequence. "I hear the vendor issue" is 3 words that buy you 10 minutes of credibility. The CIO feels heard before you push back.
  • Each consequence has a customer fact and a $ number. "Customers will be upset" loses. "4 clients will reconsider, $14M AUM at risk" wins.
  • Close the consequence section with a total. $14M AUM + $4M revenue + reputational hit. The CIO sees the sum before the counter-proposals.
  • 3 counter-proposals, in order of preference. Preferred, second-best, last-resort. The CIO chooses from 3, not from 1.
  • Name the cost and trade for each. "Split the go-live" is a slogan. "Phase 1a on 18 August, Phase 1b on 8 September, 1 week of re-test, no extra cost" is a counter-proposal.
  • Last resort is real, not bluff. "Accept 8 September, cancel the event, call the 4 clients" is a real plan. The CIO respects the preparedness.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Name 3 consequences with $ and customer

The IT PM says a new mobile feature will slip from 1 September to 15 October. The business consequence is: (1) 12 HNW clients have a contract tied to 1 September, (2) the Q3 revenue forecast drops by $2M, (3) a competitor launches in October. Write 3 consequence sentences, each with customer + $.

Exercise 2 — Build 3 counter-proposals

For the slip above, build 3 counter-proposals: (1) split the launch with MVP, (2) reduce scope to fit 1 September, (3) accept the slip with a customer comms plan. Name the cost and trade for each.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing $ number

A consequence says: "The slip will upset our top clients and hurt the relationship." What is missing? Rewrite the sentence with a customer count and a $ number.
