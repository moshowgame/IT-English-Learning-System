Status Update to Business Team — Sponsor to Business, Not Sponsor to IT

Article 10 · Module 3: Written Business Communication · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟢 Beginner 🎯 Progress, business value, risk, ask, next milestone, business language

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 17:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. You need to send a weekly status update to your business team (8 people: onboarding managers, relationship managers, contact centre lead, branch ops lead) and copy your MD, Karen. The update is about the Digital Onboarding 2.0 programme. The team does not speak IT. The update must be in business language: customer impact, revenue, dates, what the team needs to do, what you need from them.

周五 17:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。你需要给业务团队（8 人：开户经理、客户经理、联络中心负责人、网点运营负责人）发每周状态更新，并抄送 MD Karen。更新是关于数字开户 2.0项目群。团队不会说 IT 话。更新必须用业务语言：客户影响、收入、日期、团队需要做什么、你需要他们做什么。

A status update from sponsor to business is the opposite of a status update from sponsor to IT. IT wants RAG, milestone, blocker, ask. Business wants progress, customer impact, what changes for me, what I need to do. Translate, do not copy.

业务方给业务团队的状态更新和给 IT 的状态更新相反。IT 要 RAG、里程碑、卡点、需要。业务要进展、客户影响、对我的变化、我需要做什么。翻译，不要复制。

2. The Email 邮件

To: Onboarding Team DL; Branch Ops DL; Contact Centre Lead Cc: Karen (MD) Subject: Digital Onboarding 2.0 — weekly update, week 31
Team,

Weekly update on Digital Onboarding 2.0. Plain-English version below; the IT detail is in the appendix for those who want it.

1. Where we are.
UAT finishes on Friday 15 August. We will sign off the system on Friday and go live on Monday 18 August, 09:00 HKT. Customer journeys from Tuesday 19 August.

2. What this means for you.
· Onboarding managers: prepare to brief the relationship managers on the new flow from Monday 18 August.
· Branch ops: the new customer journey replaces the old paper-based process for retail and HNW onboarding from 19 August. Paper-based onboarding stops for these segments on that date.
· Contact centre: 4 short FAQs will be circulated by Monday 18 August, 17:00, for handling inbound customer questions about the new flow.

3. Customer impact.
For 8% of applications (non-HK ID), the new flow falls back to manual onboarding with a 4-day SLA. The customer experience for these cases is unchanged from today's process.
For 5% of HNW clients who use Safari on Mac, they will see a banner asking them to use Chrome or Edge for the strongest authentication.

4. Risk and what we are doing.
Group Cyber sign-off on the FIDO2 risk assessment has slipped 3 weeks. This is now on the regulator's radar. We have an escalation in flight and a target of 22 August for a named FTE and a named date. If we miss it, the next regulator slot is 30 days out, and we lose the 25 August HKMA submission.

5. What I need from you.
· Onboarding managers: by Wednesday 13 August, confirm you have briefed the 4 RMs handling the top-10 HNW clients on the new flow.
· Branch ops: by Friday 15 August, confirm the 12 branches are ready for the new paperless process.
· Contact centre: by Monday 18 August, 09:00, confirm the 12 agents handling onboarding calls have read the FAQ.

6. Next milestone.
Go-live: Monday 18 August, 09:00 HKT. First customer journey: Tuesday 19 August. First NPS check: Friday 22 August.

Any questions, ping me on Teams or call me directly.

Joyce各位，

数字开户 2.0 每周更新。通俗版见下；IT 细节在附录，需要的同事可看。

1. 我们在哪。
UAT 8 月 15 日周五结束。我们将在周五签批系统，8 月 18 日周一 09:00 HKT 上线。客户旅程从 8 月 19 日周二开始。

2. 对你们意味着什么。
· 开户经理：从 8 月 18 日周一起准备向客户经理介绍新流程。
· 网点运营：8 月 19 日起，新客户旅程取代零售和高净值开户的旧纸质流程。这些客群的纸质开户当日停止。
· 联络中心：8 月 18 日周一 17:00 前会分发 4 个简版 FAQ，用于接听客户关于新流程的来电。

3. 客户影响。
8% 申请（非香港身份证）走新流程会回退到人工开户，4 天 SLA。这些情况的客户体验与今天流程一致。
5% 用 Safari on Mac 的高净值客户会看到横幅，建议他们用 Chrome 或 Edge 以获得最强身份验证。

4. 风险和我们的应对。
集团 Cyber 的 FIDO2 风险评估签批推迟了 3 周。这已在监管视野。我们正在升级，目标 8 月 22 日拿到具名 FTE 和具名日期。如果错过，下一个监管空位要 30 天后，我们将失去 8 月 25 日 HKMA 提交。

5. 我需要你们做什么。
· 开户经理：8 月 13 日周三前确认已向前 10 大高净值客户的 4 名客户经理介绍新流程。
· 网点运营：8 月 15 日周五前确认 12 家网点为新无纸化流程就绪。
· 联络中心：8 月 18 日周一 09:00 前确认 12 名开户电话坐席已阅读 FAQ。

6. 下一个里程碑。
上线：8 月 18 日周一 09:00 HKT。首个客户旅程：8 月 19 日周二。首次 NPS 检查：8 月 22 日周五。

任何问题，Teams 找我或直接打电话。

Joyce

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Plain-English version, IT in appendix Plain-English version below; the IT detail is in the appendix. 通俗版见下；IT 细节在附录 Always offer two versions: business language on top, IT detail in appendix. The team reads the top; the IT-curious read the appendix. Never force business to read IT.
  • What this means for you What this means for you. 对你们意味着什么 The single most important sentence. Translates the IT status into action for the reader. Each role (onboarding manager, branch ops, contact centre) gets a line.
  • Customer impact, in customer language For 8% of applications, the new flow falls back to manual onboarding with a 4-day SLA. The customer experience is unchanged. 8% 申请走新流程会回退到人工开户，4 天 SLA。客户体验与今天一致 Customer impact is in customer-experience language, not control language. "Manual onboarding, 4-day SLA, experience unchanged" is the right sentence. "The eKYC timeout defect" is not.
  • Risk and what we are doing Risk and what we are doing. 风险和我们的应对 Always pair risk with action. "We have a risk" loses. "We have a risk, here's the escalation, here's the target" wins. The team sees the business is in control.
  • What I need from you, with date By Wednesday 13 August, confirm you have briefed the 4 RMs on the new flow. 8 月 13 日周三前确认已向 4 名客户经理介绍新流程 The ask is always per-role, with a date. "Get ready" loses. "Onboarding managers: by Wednesday 13 August, confirm briefings" wins.
  • Next milestone, customer-facing Go-live: Monday 18 August, 09:00. First customer journey: Tuesday 19 August. First NPS check: Friday 22 August. 上线：8 月 18 日周一 09:00。首个客户旅程：8 月 19 日周二。首次 NPS 检查：8 月 22 日周五 Milestones are in customer-facing language, not IT-internal. "First customer journey" and "first NPS check" are the dates the team will remember.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Plain-English version n. 通俗版 The status update written in business language, not IT jargon.
  • IT detail in appendix n. IT 细节在附录 The IT-only version (RAG, milestone, blocker, ask) for the IT-curious reader.
  • What this means for you phr. 对你们意味着什么 The single most important sentence in a business status update.
  • Customer impact n. 客户影响 The effect of a change on the customer experience, in customer language.
  • Risk and what we are doing phr. 风险和我们的应对 Always pair risk with action. The team sees the business in control.
  • What I need from you, with date phr. 我需要你们做什么，附日期 The per-role ask, with a named date.
  • Next milestone, customer-facing phr. 下一个里程碑，面向客户 Milestones in customer language, not IT-internal.
  • First NPS check n. 首次 NPS 检查 The first customer satisfaction measurement after go-live.
  • Briefing the RMs phr. 介绍给客户经理 The act of preparing the relationship managers for the new flow.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Plain-English version below; IT in appendix" — the two-version structure

A status update to business has two versions in one email: plain-English on top, IT in appendix. The grammar is two clauses separated by a semicolon. The top is for the team; the appendix is for the IT-curious. Never force business to read IT.

给业务的状态更新在一封邮件里有两个版本：通俗版在上，IT 在附录。

② "What this means for you" — the role-by-role translation

Translation is per role: Onboarding managers: prepare to brief. Branch ops: paperless from 19 August. Contact centre: FAQs by Monday. The grammar is Role: action. One sentence per role. Each reader sees their own line.

翻译按角色：开户经理：准备介绍。网点运营：19 日起无纸化。联络中心：周一分发 FAQ。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Translate, do not copy. The IT status update is in RAG and milestone. The business version is in customer impact and per-role action.
  • Two versions in one email. Plain-English on top, IT detail in appendix. The team reads the top; the IT-curious read the appendix.
  • "What this means for you" is the single most important sentence. One line per role. Each reader sees their own action.
  • Customer impact in customer language. "Manual onboarding, 4-day SLA, experience unchanged" wins. "eKYC timeout defect" loses.
  • Always pair risk with action. "We have a risk" loses. "We have a risk, here's the escalation, here's the target" wins.
  • Milestones in customer-facing language. "First customer journey" and "first NPS check" are the dates the team remembers. "UAT closure" is not.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the IT status

An IT status update says: "RAG Amber. FIDO2 risk assessment slipped 3 weeks. UAT entry 28 August at risk." Translate this into a business version with "where we are", "what this means for you", and "risk and what we are doing".

Exercise 2 — Write the "what this means for you" section

You are launching a new mobile onboarding feature on 25 September. Write the "what this means for you" section for 3 roles: branch ops, contact centre, onboarding managers.

Exercise 3 — Spot the IT jargon

A draft status update says: "The FIDO2 risk assessment by Group Cyber is now a programme risk. The eKYC timeout defect requires BRD v3.4 documentation." Rewrite both sentences in business language.
