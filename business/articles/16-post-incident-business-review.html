Post-Incident Business Review — Customer Impact, Root Cause, Prevention

Article 16 · Module 4: Difficult Business Conversations · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Customer impact, root cause, business decision, prevention, named owner

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 10:00 HKT, 5 days after a P1 incident. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The Digital Onboarding 2.0 platform went down for 4 hours on the morning of 23 August, blocking all new customer onboarding across HK and SG. The IT root cause review (RCA) is done. You now chair the post-incident business review. Present: Karen (your MD), Sara (IT PM), Daniel (Tech Lead), Steve (Group Cyber), Priya (BA), and Mark (Contact Centre Lead, customer-facing). You have 90 minutes to align on customer impact, root cause, business decisions, and prevention.

周二 10:00 HKT，P1 事件后 5 天。你是客户开户业务负责人。数字开户 2.0平台 8 月 23 日上午宕机 4 小时，阻断香港和新加坡所有新客户开户。IT 根因分析（RCA）已完成。你现在主持事件后业务复盘。参会有：Karen（你的 MD）、Sara（IT PM）、Daniel（Tech Lead）、Steve（集团 Cyber）、Priya（BA）、Mark（联络中心负责人，客户侧）。你有 90 分钟对齐客户影响、根因、业务决定、预防。

The post-incident business review is the second-most important meeting after the SteerCo. The pattern: customer impact first, root cause second, business decisions third, prevention fourth. The MD buys the customer impact, not the technical detail.

事件后业务复盘是仅次于 SteerCo 的第二重要会议。模式：客户影响第一、根因第二、业务决定第三、预防第四。MD 买客户影响，不是技术细节。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Business Sponsor): 90 minutes, 4 sections. Customer impact, root cause, business decisions, prevention. Mark, customer impact first.90 分钟，4 部分。客户影响、根因、业务决定、预防。Mark，先讲客户影响。
Mark (Contact Centre Lead): Mark: Total customer impact over the 4-hour window. 1,847 onboarding attempts across HK and SG. 0 successful, all blocked at the eKYC step. Complaints: 312 inbound calls, 89 chat complaints, 14 social media posts (3 viral). NPS impact: survey went from 56 to 41 in 24 hours. AUM impact: estimated $6M in onboarding attempts abandoned, 1,200 customers who said they would not retry.Mark：4 小时窗口的客户影响总计。香港和新加坡共 1,847 次开户尝试。0 成功，全部卡在 eKYC 步骤。投诉：312 通呼入电话，89 个在线投诉，14 条社交媒体帖（3 条病毒式）。NPS 影响：24 小时内调研从 56 跌到 41。AUM 影响：估计 600 万开户尝试放弃，1,200 个客户表示不会重试。
You: $6M AUM, NPS 56 → 41, 1,200 customers will not retry. Sara, root cause.600 万 AUM，NPS 56 → 41，1,200 个客户不会重试。Sara，根因。
Sara (IT PM): Sara: Root cause: Onfido eKYC vendor's authentication service returned a 503 error at 08:12 HKT, and our platform did not have a circuit breaker to fail over to the backup vendor. Detection at 08:23 (11-minute delay), vendor failover engaged at 08:35, full recovery at 12:14 (4 hours total). Contributing factors: the backup vendor was last tested 14 months ago, and the alerting on the 503 error went to a low-priority Slack channel, not the on-call PagerDuty.Sara：根因：Onfido eKYC 厂商的身份验证服务在 8:12 HKT 返回 503 错误，我们的平台没有熔断器切换到备份厂商。8:23 检测到（延迟 11 分钟），8:35 厂商故障切换启动，12:14 完整恢复（共 4 小时）。促成因素：备份厂商上次测试是 14 个月前，503 错误的告警发到低优先级 Slack 频道，不是 on-call 的 PagerDuty。
You: 3 business decisions needed.需要 3 个业务决定。
You: Decision 1: Customer recovery. 1,200 customers who said they will not retry — proactive call from Mark's team, this week, named owners by 30 August.决定 1：客户挽回。1,200 个表示不会重试的客户——Mark 团队本周主动联系，8 月 30 日前具名负责人。
You: Decision 2: Compensation. $50 voucher to each of the 1,847 affected customers, $92K total cost, owner Karen's team, sent by 6 September.决定 2：补偿。给 1,847 个受影响客户每人 50 美元代金券，总计 9.2 万美元，Karen 团队负责，9 月 6 日前发出。
You: Decision 3: Regulator notification. HKMA incident notification within 72 hours of the event (we have 24 hours left), drafted by Group Compliance, signed by the CIO, sent by EOD today.决定 3：监管报备。事件后 72 小时内 HKMA 事件报备（还剩 24 小时），集团合规起草，CIO 签发，今天 EOD 前发出。
You: Prevention. 5 actions, with named owners and dates.预防。5 个动作，附具名负责人和日期。
You: Action 1: Circuit breaker — add a circuit breaker pattern to the eKYC service, owner Daniel, by 30 September, $80K.动作 1：熔断器——给 eKYC 服务加熔断器模式，负责人 Daniel，9 月 30 日前，8 万美元。
You: Action 2: Backup vendor test — test the backup vendor failover every quarter, owner Daniel, first test 30 September, recurring quarterly.动作 2：备份厂商测试——每季度测试备份厂商故障切换，负责人 Daniel，首次测试 9 月 30 日，每季度一次。
You: Action 3: Alerting — move 503 alerts to PagerDuty on-call rotation, owner Steve (Cyber), by 6 September.动作 3：告警——把 503 告警移到 PagerDuty on-call 轮值，负责人 Steve（Cyber），9 月 6 日前。
You: Action 4: Detection time — reduce detection time from 11 minutes to 3 minutes, owner Daniel, by 30 September.动作 4：检测时间——把检测时间从 11 分钟降到 3 分钟，负责人 Daniel，9 月 30 日前。
You: Action 5: Customer comms — real-time status page during incidents, owner Mark, live from 13 September, auto-update every 15 minutes during a P1.动作 5：客户沟通——事件期间实时状态页，负责人 Mark，9 月 13 日上线，P1 期间每 15 分钟自动更新。
Karen (MD): Karen: 5 actions approved. Joyce, send me the 1-pager — customer impact, 3 decisions, 5 actions, named owners, named dates — by EOD. I'll co-sign with you for the regulator submission.Karen：5 个动作批准。Joyce，EOD 前把 1 页纸发我——客户影响、3 个决定、5 个动作、具名负责人、具名日期——我和你联签监管报备。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Customer impact first, in numbers 1,847 attempts, 0 success, 312 calls, 89 chats, 14 social posts (3 viral), NPS 56 → 41, $6M AUM abandoned. 1,847 次尝试，0 成功，312 通电话，89 个在线投诉，14 条社媒（3 条病毒式），NPS 56 → 41，600 万 AUM 放弃 Customer impact is always first, always in numbers. The MD buys the numbers, not the technical detail. 5 metrics in 30 seconds.
  • Root cause, with contributing factors Root cause: no circuit breaker. Contributing factors: backup vendor not tested in 14 months, alerting to low-priority Slack. 根因：无熔断器。促成因素：备份厂商 14 个月未测试，告警发到低优先级 Slack Root cause is the single thing that, if fixed, would have prevented the incident. Contributing factors are the surrounding issues. The PM names both.
  • Business decisions, with named owners and dates Decision 1: customer recovery. 1,200 customers. Proactive call. Owner Mark's team. By 30 August. 决定 1：客户挽回。1,200 个客户。主动联系。负责人 Mark 团队。8 月 30 日前 Each decision has 5 elements: what, who is affected, the action, the named owner, the named date. The MD buys the 5 elements.
  • Regulator notification, with deadline HKMA incident notification within 72 hours (we have 24 hours left). Drafted by Group Compliance, signed by CIO, sent by EOD today. HKMA 事件报备 72 小时内（还剩 24 小时）。集团合规起草，CIO 签发，今天 EOD 前发出 Regulator notification has 4 elements: regulator, deadline, drafter, signer. The MD buys the 4 elements. Late notification is a Tier-1 finding.
  • Prevention actions, with owner, date, $ Action 1: circuit breaker. Owner Daniel. By 30 September. $80K. 动作 1：熔断器。负责人 Daniel。9 月 30 日前。8 万美元 Each prevention action has 4 elements: what, owner, date, $ where relevant. The MD buys the 4 elements. Without $ or date, the action is a wish.
  • Co-sign the regulator submission I'll co-sign with you for the regulator submission. 我和你联签监管报备 For P1 incidents, sponsor and MD co-sign the regulator submission. The co-sign is the bank's evidence that the business owns the response.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Post-incident business review n. 事件后业务复盘 The business-led review after a P1 incident, focused on customer impact and decisions.
  • Root cause (RCA) n. 根因 The single thing that, if fixed, would have prevented the incident.
  • Contributing factor n. 促成因素 A surrounding issue that made the incident worse (e.g., untested backup, wrong alert channel).
  • Circuit breaker n. 熔断器 A pattern that automatically fails over to a backup service when the primary returns errors.
  • Customer recovery n. 客户挽回 Proactive outreach to affected customers after an incident.
  • Compensation n. 补偿 A $ or service credit given to affected customers (e.g., $50 voucher).
  • Regulator notification n. 监管报备 A formal notice to the regulator after a material incident, within a regulator-defined window.
  • Detection time n. 检测时间 The time from incident start to alert, ideally under 3 minutes.
  • Status page n. 状态页 A public-facing page showing real-time system status during an incident.
  • Co-sign the regulator submission phr. 联签监管报备 The sponsor and MD jointly sign the regulator notification.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "1,847 attempts, 0 success, 312 calls" — the impact count list

Customer impact is a parallel list of counts: 1,847 attempts, 0 success, 312 calls, 89 chats, 14 social posts. The grammar is Number Category, repeated. The MD reads the list in 30 seconds.

客户影响是平行的数字列表：1,847 次尝试、0 成功、312 通电话、89 个在线投诉、14 条社媒。

② "Action N: [what]. Owner [name]. By [date]. $[amount]." — the action sentence

Each prevention action has 4 elements: Action N: [what]. Owner [name]. By [date]. $[amount]. The grammar is 4 short sentences. The MD reads the action list in 30 seconds.

每个预防动作有 4 要素：动作 N：【内容】。负责人【姓名】。【日期】前。【金额】美元。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Customer impact first, always in numbers. 5 metrics in 30 seconds. The MD buys the numbers, not the technical detail.
  • Root cause + contributing factors. Root cause is the single fix. Contributing factors are the surrounding issues. Both go on the slide.
  • Business decisions: 5 elements each. What, who is affected, the action, the named owner, the named date. The MD buys the 5 elements.
  • Regulator notification: 4 elements. Regulator, deadline, drafter, signer. Late notification is a Tier-1 finding.
  • Prevention actions: 4 elements each. What, owner, date, $ where relevant. Without $ or date, the action is a wish.
  • Co-sign the regulator submission. Sponsor and MD co-sign. The co-sign is the bank's evidence that the business owns the response.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Write the customer impact list

A 2-hour P1 incident blocked 800 transactions, caused 120 complaints, NPS dropped 10 points, $2M in abandoned transactions. Write the impact list in 5 numbers, in the parallel-count pattern.

Exercise 2 — Build the prevention actions

Root cause: missing database failover. Contributing factors: backup not tested in 12 months, alerting to email. Build 4 prevention actions, each with what, owner, date, $.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing $ or date

A prevention action says: "Add a circuit breaker." What is missing? Rewrite the action with owner, date, and $.
