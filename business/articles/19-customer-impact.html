Customer Impact Conversation — NPS, Complaint, Churn, Segment, Comms

Article 19 · Module 5: Bank Business Context · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 NPS, complaint, churn, segment, communication, named owner

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 14:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The Group Chief Customer Officer, Linda, is on a call with you and your MD, Karen. A piece in the South China Morning Post yesterday named HSBC HK as one of three banks with the slowest onboarding in the industry (average 4.2 days, vs industry 2.1 days). NPS has dropped 6 points in the last quarter. The board wants a customer impact plan by Monday. You have 60 minutes to walk Linda through the customer data and commit to a named plan.

周五 14:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。集团首席客户官Linda和你、你的 MD Karen 电话会议。昨天南华早报一篇文章把汇丰香港列为行业开户最慢的三家银行之一（平均 4.2 天，行业 2.1 天）。上个季度 NPS 跌了 6 分。董事会要周一前拿到客户影响计划。你有 60 分钟给 Linda 走查客户数据并承诺具名计划。

The customer impact conversation is the CCO's exam. The pattern: customer data (NPS, complaints, churn), segment cut, root cause, plan with named owner and date. The CCO buys the customer data and the named plan, not the strategy.

客户影响对话是CCO 的考试。模式：客户数据（NPS、投诉、流失）、客群切分、根因、具名负责人和日期的计划。CCO 买客户数据和具名计划，不是战略。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Linda (Group CCO): Linda: Joyce, customer data first. NPS, complaints, churn, by segment.Linda：Joyce，先讲客户数据。NPS、投诉、流失，按客群切分。
You (Business Sponsor): Customer data, last 90 days.客户数据，过去 90 天。
You: NPS: overall 56 → 50 in 90 days, dropped 6 points. By segment: retail 58 → 54, HNW 62 → 56, corporate 48 → 42 (biggest drop), cross-border 54 → 50.NPS：整体 90 天从 56 → 50，跌 6 分。按客群：零售 58 → 54，高净值 62 → 56，企业 48 → 42（跌幅最大），跨境 54 → 50。
You: Complaints: 312 inbound calls in 90 days, up 28% from prior quarter. Top 3 reasons: onboarding time (38%), document re-submission (22%), status visibility (18%).投诉：90 天 312 通呼入电话，比上季度增加 28%。前 3 原因：开户时间（38%）、文件重复提交（22%）、状态可见性（18%）。
You: Churn: 3.2% of corporate onboarding customers did not proceed after submission, up from 1.4% prior quarter. That's 47 corporate customers, $14M AUM walked away. Retail churn unchanged at 0.8%.流失：3.2% 企业开户客户提交后未继续，比上季度 1.4% 上升。47 个企业客户，1,400 万 AUM 流失。零售流失未变 0.8%。
Linda: Linda: Corporate is bleeding. What's the root cause?Linda：企业在流血。根因是什么？
You: Corporate onboarding time today is 14 days, vs retail 4 days and HNW 7 days. Root cause: the corporate flow uses 3 different legacy systems, with manual re-keying at each handoff, and no status visibility for the relationship manager or the customer.企业开户今天 14 天，零售 4 天、高净值 7 天。根因：企业流程用 3 个不同的遗留系统，每次交接都人工重输，客户经理和客户都看不到状态。
Linda: Linda: What's the plan?Linda：计划是什么？
You: Customer impact plan, 4 actions, named owners and dates.客户影响计划，4 个动作，具名负责人和日期。
You: Action 1: Real-time status page for corporate onboarding. Owner Mark (Contact Centre Lead) + Priya (BA). Live 30 September. Customer sees status at every step; RM sees the same view.动作 1：企业开户实时状态页。负责人 Mark（联络中心负责人）+ Priya（BA）。9 月 30 日上线。客户每步看到状态；客户经理看到同样视图。
You: Action 2: Single document upload, no re-keying across systems. Owner Sara (IT PM) + Daniel (Tech Lead). Live 30 November. Customer uploads once; system propagates to all 3 legacy systems.动作 2：单次文件上传，系统间不重输。负责人 Sara（IT PM）+ Daniel（Tech Lead）。11 月 30 日上线。客户上传一次；系统传播到所有 3 个遗留系统。
You: Action 3: Proactive call-back for any onboarding stalled more than 48 hours. Owner Mark. Live 15 September. Reduces complaint volume 22% based on the HNW pilot.动作 3：任何开户停滞超过 48 小时主动回呼。负责人 Mark。9 月 15 日上线。基于高净值试点，投诉量减少 22%。
You: Action 4: Corporate NPS recovery target. From 42 to 52 by 31 March next year. Owner me, monthly update to Linda and Karen.动作 4：企业 NPS 恢复目标。从 42 到 52，明年 3 月 31 日前。负责人我，每月向 Linda 和 Karen 更新。
Linda: Linda: 4 actions approved. Joyce, customer impact 1-pager to the board by Monday — customer data, 4 actions, named owners, named dates, NPS recovery target.Linda：4 个动作批准。Joyce，周一前把客户影响 1 页纸发董事会——客户数据、4 个动作、具名负责人、具名日期、NPS 恢复目标。
You: Done. Linda, Karen, monthly joint review of NPS, complaints, churn, first one 30 days from now.好。Linda、Karen，每月联合评审 NPS、投诉、流失，第一次 30 天后。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • NPS by segment, before and after NPS: retail 58 → 54, HNW 62 → 56, corporate 48 → 42, cross-border 54 → 50. NPS：零售 58 → 54、高净值 62 → 56、企业 48 → 42、跨境 54 → 50 NPS is always by segment, with before and after. The CCO sees which segment is bleeding. "NPS dropped" loses; "corporate 48 → 42" wins.
  • Complaints, with top reasons 312 inbound calls, up 28%. Top 3 reasons: onboarding time 38%, document re-submission 22%, status visibility 18%. 312 通呼入电话，增加 28%。前 3 原因：开户时间 38%、文件重复提交 22%、状态可见性 18% Complaints are always with the top reasons, in %. The CCO sees the dominant friction. 312 calls alone is a count; 312 with 3 reasons is a story.
  • Churn, with $ 3.2% of corporate customers churned, 47 customers, $14M AUM walked away. 3.2% 企业客户流失，47 个客户，1,400 万 AUM 流失 Churn is always with %. + count + $. The CCO sees the $ consequence. "Customers left" loses; "47 customers, $14M AUM" wins.
  • Root cause, named Root cause: 3 legacy systems, manual re-keying at each handoff, no status visibility. 根因：3 个遗留系统、每次交接都人工重输、无状态可见性 The root cause is the single thing that, if fixed, would prevent the bleeding. Named in 1 sentence. The CCO buys the fix, not the cause alone.
  • Action: what, owner, date, outcome Action 1: real-time status page. Owner Mark + Priya. Live 30 September. Customer sees status at every step. 动作 1：实时状态页。负责人 Mark + Priya。9 月 30 日上线。客户每步看到状态 Each action has 4 elements: what, named owner, named date, customer outcome. The CCO buys the 4 elements.
  • NPS recovery target, with date Corporate NPS recovery: 42 to 52 by 31 March next year. Owner me, monthly update to Linda and Karen. 企业 NPS 恢复：42 到 52，明年 3 月 31 日前。负责人我，每月向 Linda 和 Karen 更新 Always close with a recovery target: NPS from X to Y by named date. The CCO signs when the target is named. Cadence named.
  • Monthly joint review Monthly joint review of NPS, complaints, churn, first one 30 days from now. 每月联合评审 NPS、投诉、流失，第一次 30 天后 Always close with the cadence. Monthly joint review, with named first date. The CCO signs when the cadence is named.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • NPS (Net Promoter Score) n. 净推荐值 A measure of customer satisfaction (0-100). Drop of 6+ points is a red flag.
  • Complaint, with reason n. 投诉，附原因 A customer complaint always paired with the dominant reason (onboarding time, re-submission, visibility).
  • Churn n. 流失 A customer who started a process and did not complete. Always with %, count, and $.
  • Segment cut n. 客群切分 The breakdown of a metric by customer segment (retail, HNW, corporate, cross-border).
  • Status page n. 状态页 A real-time view of onboarding progress visible to customer and RM.
  • Single document upload n. 单次文件上传 Customer uploads once; system propagates. Eliminates re-keying across legacy systems.
  • Proactive call-back n. 主动回呼 A call from the bank when an onboarding is stalled more than 48 hours.
  • NPS recovery target n. NPS 恢复目标 A named NPS target (e.g., 42 → 52) with a named date and owner.
  • Monthly joint review n. 每月联合评审 The cadence: monthly, joint with CCO and MD, named metrics, named first date.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Segment: before → after" — the segment cut

A segment cut is stated in parallel: Segment: before → after. The grammar is Category: Number → Number, repeated per segment. The CCO reads the segment cut in 30 seconds.

客群切分平行陈述：客群：前 → 后。

② "Action N: [what]. Owner [name]. [Date]. [Outcome]." — the action sentence

Each action has 4 sentences: Action N: [what]. Owner [name]. [Date]. [Outcome]. The grammar is 4 short sentences, one per element. The CCO reads the action in 30 seconds.

每个动作有 4 句：动作 N：【内容】。负责人【姓名】。【日期】。【结果】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • NPS by segment, before and after. "NPS dropped" loses; "corporate 48 → 42" wins. The CCO sees which segment is bleeding.
  • Complaints with top reasons, in %. 312 calls alone is a count; 312 with 3 reasons is a story. The CCO buys the reason.
  • Churn with % + count + $. "Customers left" loses; "47 customers, $14M AUM" wins. The CCO sees the $ consequence.
  • Root cause in 1 sentence. The single thing that, if fixed, would prevent the bleeding. The CCO buys the fix.
  • Action: what, owner, date, outcome. 4 elements per action. The CCO signs the 4 elements.
  • Always close with the recovery target and the cadence. NPS from X to Y by named date. Monthly joint review. The CCO signs when both are named.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Build the segment cut

NPS dropped 8 points overall. Build the segment cut for retail (-4), HNW (-6), corporate (-12), cross-border (-5) in the "Segment: before → after" pattern.

Exercise 2 — Write 3 actions

Root cause: 3 legacy systems, manual re-keying, no status visibility. Build 3 actions, each with what, owner, date, outcome.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing $

A churn sentence says: "3.2% of corporate customers churned, 47 customers walked away." What is missing? Rewrite with the AUM $ lost.
