Escalation: IT and Business Disagree — Group Arbitration

Article 15 · Module 4: Difficult Business Conversations · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🔴 Expert 🎯 Escalation, arbitration paper, Group MD, named decision, named next step

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 14:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The IT-business disagreement on Digital Onboarding 2.0 has lasted 3 weeks. IT (CIO Wei) wants to defer the cross-sell engine (FR-07) to keep the November milestone. Business (you and MD Karen) want to keep FR-07 because of the $8M revenue and 9 signed commitment letters. Three weekly SteerCo rounds have not resolved it. It is time to escalate to Group MD David for arbitration. You have 60 minutes to write and present the escalation paper.

周三 14:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。IT 业务对数字开户 2.0的分歧已持续 3 周。IT（CIO Wei）想推迟交叉销售引擎（FR-07）以保住 11 月里程碑。业务（你和 MD Karen）想保留 FR-07，因为 800 万收入和 9 份已签承诺函。3 轮每周 SteerCo 都没解决。是时候升级到集团 MD David仲裁。你有 60 分钟写并呈现升级文件。

Escalation to Group is the nuclear option. Both sides lose face; the Group MD picks. The pattern: present both positions equally, name the decision rule, name the precedent (or lack of), recommend a path, ask for a named decision and date.

升级到集团是核选项。双方失面子；集团 MD 选。模式：平等呈现双方立场、列出决策规则、列出先例（或无）、推荐路径、要求具名决策和日期。

2. The Escalation Paper 升级文件

Section 1 — Issue 议题

Digital Onboarding 2.0 — Phase 1 scope: keep FR-07 cross-sell engine (revenue-tied, $8M Year 1, 9 commitment letters) or defer to Phase 2 (commit 28 February)?数字开户 2.0 第 1 阶段范围：保留 FR-07 交叉销售引擎（收入绑定、第 1 年 800 万、9 份承诺函）还是推迟到第 2 阶段（承诺 2 月 28 日）？

Section 2 — IT Position IT 立场

Capacity: 6 FTE for 9 weeks (vendor pulled out, BA on parental leave).
Keeping FR-07 slips 2 items to Phase 2: FR-11 reporting dashboard, FR-14 HNW journey.
Slip risk: November milestone moves to 15 December, regulator trajectory at risk.
Recommendation: defer FR-07, keep November milestone, $8M moves to Q1 next year.容量：6 FTE 9 周（外包退出、BA 休产假）。
保留 FR-07 推迟 2 个项到第 2 阶段：FR-11 报表仪表盘、FR-14 高净值旅程。
推迟风险：11 月里程碑挪到 12 月 15 日，监管轨迹有风险。
建议：推迟 FR-07，保住 11 月里程碑，800 万挪到明年 Q1。

Section 3 — Business Position 业务立场

FR-07 contractually tied to November product launch. 9 of 10 top HNW clients have signed commitment letters referencing the cross-sell engine. Deferral voids the letters, 4 clients likely to reconsider, $8M Year 1 forecast at risk.
Manual workaround for the 2 deferred items (FR-11, FR-14) is acceptable: FR-11 manual report, no customer impact, FR-14 240 HNW on RM-managed workflow, known friction but no client loss.
Recommendation: keep FR-07, defer FR-11 and FR-14, name the November milestone as 30 November (2 weeks contingency, no regulator slip).FR-07 合同绑 11 月产品发布。前 10 大高净值客户 9 个已签承诺函提到交叉销售引擎。推迟使承诺函失效，4 个客户可能重新考虑，第 1 年 800 万预测有风险。
推迟的 2 个项（FR-11、FR-14）人工方案可接受：FR-11 人工报表，无客户影响，FR-14 240 个高净值走客户经理管理工作流，已知摩擦但无客户流失。
建议：保留 FR-07，推迟 FR-11 和 FR-14，11 月里程碑命名为 11 月 30 日（2 周缓冲，监管不推迟）。

Section 4 — Decision Rule 决策规则

Rule: contractually committed + revenue-tied items stay in Phase 1; Could-tier and no customer impact defer to Phase 2; regulator milestones are sacred, never traded for revenue or schedule.规则：合同承诺 + 收入绑定项留在第 1 阶段；Could 级别且无客户影响推迟到第 2 阶段；监管里程碑神圣，从不与收入或时间表交易。

Section 5 — Precedent 先例

2024 KYC Refresh programme: similar situation, Group MD Jane ruled contractually committed items stay, Could-tier deferred, regulator milestone kept. Same rule applies here.2024 KYC 刷新项目群：类似情况，集团 MD Jane 裁定合同承诺项保留，Could 级别推迟，监管里程碑保住。同样规则适用于此。

Section 6 — Recommendation 建议

Keep FR-07 cross-sell engine in Phase 1.
Defer FR-11 reporting dashboard to Phase 2, commit 28 February.
Defer FR-14 HNW journey to Phase 2, commit 31 March.
Move November milestone to 30 November (2 weeks contingency, no regulator slip).
Add 1 contractor for 6 weeks ($180K) to manage the contingency, named FTE, named start date.保留 FR-07 交叉销售引擎在第 1 阶段。
FR-11 报表仪表盘推迟到第 2 阶段，承诺 2 月 28 日。
FR-14 高净值旅程推迟到第 2 阶段，承诺 3 月 31 日。
11 月里程碑挪到 11 月 30 日（2 周缓冲，监管不推迟）。
增加 1 名外包 6 周（18 万美元）管理缓冲，具名 FTE，具名开始日期。

Section 7 — One Decision Needed 需要的一个决策

Approve the recommendation above.
Authorise 1 contractor ($180K) and the 30 November milestone.
By Friday 18:00, so the PM can lock the FTE plan on Monday.批准上述建议。
授权 1 名外包（18 万美元）和 11 月 30 日里程碑。
周五 18:00 前，我好让 PM 周一锁定 FTE 计划。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Both positions, equal weight IT position: defer FR-07, keep November. Business position: keep FR-07, defer 2 lower items. IT 立场：推迟 FR-07，保住 11 月。业务立场：保留 FR-07，推迟 2 个低优先级项 Present both positions with equal weight. The Group MD decides; the paper's job is to show the trade, not to take a side before the meeting.
  • Decision rule, with named tiers Rule: contractually committed + revenue-tied stays; Could-tier defers; regulator milestones are sacred. 规则：合同承诺 + 收入绑定留下；Could 级别推迟；监管里程碑神圣 The decision rule is the principle. The Group MD applies the rule. Without the rule, the Group MD has to invent one — and the outcome is unpredictable.
  • Precedent, named 2024 KYC Refresh: Group MD Jane ruled contractually committed items stay, Could-tier deferred, regulator milestone kept. 2024 KYC 刷新：集团 MD Jane 裁定合同承诺项保留，Could 级别推迟，监管里程碑保住 Precedent is the strongest argument. A named past decision by a named Group MD carries weight. The Group MD will likely follow their own precedent.
  • Recommendation, with 4 actions Keep FR-07. Defer FR-11 by 28 Feb. Defer FR-14 by 31 Mar. Move milestone to 30 Nov. Add 1 contractor, $180K. 保留 FR-07。FR-11 推迟到 2 月 28 日。FR-14 推迟到 3 月 31 日。里程碑挪到 11 月 30 日。增加 1 名外包，18 万 The recommendation is 4-5 numbered actions, each with a date and a $ where relevant. The Group MD signs the actions, not the strategy.
  • One decision, with deadline Approve the recommendation. By Friday 18:00, so the PM can lock the FTE plan on Monday. 批准建议。周五 18:00 前，我好让 PM 周一锁定 FTE 计划 The decision is one sentence, with a deadline and a "so that" clause. The Group MD knows the consequence of delay (the PM cannot lock FTE).
  • Regulator milestones are sacred Regulator milestones are sacred, never traded for revenue or schedule. 监管里程碑神圣，从不与收入或时间表交易 The single most important sentence in any escalation paper. The Group MD signs when this rule is on the page. The CRO co-signs.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Escalation paper n. 升级文件 A 1-2 page document presented to Group MD when IT and Business cannot align.
  • Arbitration n. 仲裁 The Group MD's role in picking between IT and Business when they cannot align.
  • Both positions, equal weight phr. 双方立场，平等权重 The paper presents IT and Business views without taking a side until the recommendation.
  • Decision rule n. 决策规则 The principle that guides the decision (e.g., contractually committed stays).
  • Precedent n. 先例 A named past decision by a named Group MD in a similar situation.
  • Regulator milestones are sacred phr. 监管里程碑神圣 The bank's cardinal rule: regulator dates are never traded for revenue or schedule.
  • So that clause n. 以便从句 The reason for the deadline: "by Friday 18:00, so that the PM can lock the FTE plan on Monday".
  • Group MD arbitration n. 集团 MD 仲裁 The Group MD's final call on a cross-function disagreement, recorded in writing.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "IT position: [X]. Business position: [Y]." — the parallel pair

The two positions are stated in parallel: IT position: [X]. Business position: [Y]. The grammar is Side: position., two times. The Group MD reads the pair in 5 seconds.

双方立场平行陈述：IT 立场：【X】。业务立场：【Y】。

② "By [date], so that [consequence]" — the deadline with reason

The deadline is always paired with a consequence: by [date], so that [consequence]. The grammar is by [date] + comma + so that [consequence]. The Group MD knows why the date matters.

截止总是附后果：【日期】前，以便【后果】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Present both positions with equal weight. The paper's job is to show the trade, not to take a side before the meeting.
  • Always state the decision rule. Without the rule, the Group MD invents one — and the outcome is unpredictable.
  • Cite precedent by name. A named past decision by a named Group MD carries weight. The current Group MD will follow their own precedent.
  • "Regulator milestones are sacred" is the cardinal sentence. The Group MD signs when this rule is on the page. The CRO co-signs.
  • Recommendation is 4-5 numbered actions. Each with a date and a $ where relevant. The Group MD signs the actions, not the strategy.
  • Deadline always with a "so that" clause. "By Friday 18:00, so that the PM can lock the FTE plan on Monday." The Group MD knows why the date matters.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — State both positions

IT wants to defer FR-12 (revenue-tied, $4M, contractually committed to a top client). Business wants to keep FR-12. Write the IT position and the Business position in two parallel sentences.

Exercise 2 — Cite a precedent

You are escalating to Group MD. The situation is "contractually committed vs slip risk". Name a precedent from your bank's history (real or plausible) that supports the contractually-committed-stays rule.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing "so that" clause

A deadline says: "Decision by Friday 18:00." What is missing? Rewrite the sentence with a "so that" clause that names the consequence of delay.
