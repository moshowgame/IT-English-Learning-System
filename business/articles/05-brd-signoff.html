BRD Sign-off — Acceptance Criteria, Edge Cases, Conditional Approval

Article 05 · Module 2: Requirements & Prioritisation · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Acceptance criteria, edge cases, conditional sign-off, out-of-scope list

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 14:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The BRD v3.2 for Digital Onboarding 2.0 has been circulated for 5 working days. Priya (BA) and Sara (IT PM) are in your office for the sign-off discussion. The BRD has 14 functional requirements, 8 non-functional, and 6 explicit out-of-scope items. Your job: walk through acceptance criteria line by line, surface edge cases, and either sign off or sign off with conditions. You have 60 minutes.

周二 14:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。数字开户 2.0的BRD v3.2已分发 5 个工作日。Priya（BA）和 Sara（IT PM）在你办公室进行签批讨论。BRD 有 14 个功能需求、8 个非功能需求、6 个明确的超出范围项。你的工作：逐条过验收标准、暴露边界情况、签批或有条件签批。你有 60 分钟。

BRD sign-off is the contract between business and IT. Everything in the BRD is in scope; everything not in it is out of scope. The sign-off locks both sides. After sign-off, scope changes require a formal process.

BRD 签批是业务和 IT 之间的合同。BRD 里的都在范围内；不在的都不在范围内。签批锁定双方。签批后，范围变更需要正式流程。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Business Sponsor): 60-minute walkthrough. Goal: sign off BRD v3.2 today, with conditions if needed. Priya, walk me through the 14 functional requirements and their acceptance criteria.60 分钟过一遍。目标：今天签批 BRD v3.2，必要时附条件。Priya，给我过 14 个功能需求和验收标准。
Priya (BA): Priya: 14 functional requirements grouped into 4 capabilities. Identity verification — 3 requirements. Customer journey — 5 requirements. Audit and compliance — 3 requirements. Integration — 3 requirements. Each requirement has 2 to 4 acceptance criteria.Priya：14 个功能需求分为 4 个能力。身份核验——3 个需求。客户旅程——5 个需求。审计与合规——3 个需求。集成——3 个需求。每个需求有 2 到 4 个验收标准。
You: Stop at FR-04, customer journey, joint account. What's the acceptance criteria for the joint-account flow?停在 FR-04，客户旅程，共同账户。共同账户流程的验收标准是什么？
Priya: Priya: AC-1: both parties complete eKYC independently. AC-2: relationship manager reviews and signs off. AC-3: account is opened in 4 hours.Priya：AC-1：双方独立完成 eKYC。AC-2：客户经理审核签批。AC-3：账户在 4 小时内开立。
You: Edge case. What if one party fails eKYC and the other passes? What's the system behaviour?边界情况。如果一方 eKYC 失败、另一方通过，系统行为是什么？
Priya: Priya: The BRD is silent on that. Sara, what's the design?Priya：BRD 没说这个。Sara，设计是什么？
Sara: Sara: The current design blocks the joint account until both pass. The relationship manager can manually override with a 4-eyes approval.Sara：当前设计是双方都通过才能开共同账户。客户经理可以通过 4-eyes 审批手工放行。
You: Add AC-4: failure handling — if one party fails eKYC, the system blocks the joint account; relationship manager can override with 4-eyes approval; override is logged in the audit trail. Priya, update the BRD and re-circulate by EOD tomorrow.加 AC-4：失败处理——一方 eKYC 失败，系统阻止共同账户；客户经理可 4-eyes 审批放行；放行记入审计日志。Priya，更新 BRD，明天 EOD 前重新分发。
You: Next, out-of-scope list. What's in and what's out?下一个，超出范围清单。什么在内、什么在外？
Priya: Priya: 6 items out of scope. Video-based identity verification, voice biometrics, joint account with non-HK ID, corporate onboarding, offshore onboarding, and credit card onboarding. All flagged for Phase 2.Priya：6 个超出范围项。视频身份核验、声纹生物识别、非香港身份证共同账户、企业开户、离岸开户、信用卡开户。全部标记为第 2 阶段。
You: Confirm: video-based identity verification is out of scope for Phase 1. We will not deliver it. And if the MD asks for it, it's an ad-hoc scope change with cost and timeline impact.确认：视频身份核验在第 1 阶段超出范围。我们不会交付。如果 MD 要求，那就是有成本和时间线影响的临时范围变更。
Sara: Sara: Confirmed.Sara：确认。
You: My decision. Conditional sign-off. Condition 1: Priya updates BRD v3.3 with AC-4 for FR-04 and re-circulates by EOD Wednesday. Condition 2: out-of-scope list is final, no additions without formal scope change. Sign-off effective when v3.3 is in the repo.我的决定。有条件签批。条件 1：Priya 更新 BRD v3.3 加入 FR-04 的 AC-4，周三 EOD 前重新分发。条件 2：超出范围清单是终版，未经正式范围变更不增加。v3.3 入库后签批生效。
Priya: Priya: Confirmed. v3.3 by EOD Wednesday.Priya：确认。周三 EOD 前 v3.3。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Walk me through, with criteria Walk me through the 14 functional requirements and their acceptance criteria. 给我过 14 个功能需求和验收标准 Always ask for requirements + acceptance criteria together. A requirement without an acceptance criterion is a wish, not a contract.
  • Edge case, system behaviour What if one party fails eKYC? What's the system behaviour? 如果一方 eKYC 失败，系统行为是什么？ Edge cases are how the system behaves when input is bad. The 3 most common: failure, partial, exception. The PM must answer for each.
  • Add AC-N, with named behaviour Add AC-4: failure handling — if one party fails eKYC, the system blocks; relationship manager can override with 4-eyes; override is logged. 加 AC-4：失败处理——一方 eKYC 失败，系统阻止；客户经理可 4-eyes 放行；放行记入审计 Edge case handling becomes a new AC, numbered, with the system behaviour described in one sentence. The new AC goes into the BRD as v3.3.
  • Out of scope, final, no additions Out-of-scope list is final, no additions without formal scope change. 超出范围清单是终版，未经正式范围变更不增加 "Final" is the lock. After sign-off, the out-of-scope list is closed. New items require a formal scope change with cost and timeline impact. The MD buys the closure.
  • Conditional sign-off, effective when vN.M in repo Conditional sign-off. Sign-off effective when v3.3 is in the repo. 有条件签批，v3.3 入库后签批生效 A conditional BRD sign-off always has a "effective when" clause. Without it, the sign-off is ambiguous. "v3.3 in the repo" is a verifiable event.
  • 4-eyes approval Relationship manager can override with 4-eyes approval. 客户经理可 4-eyes 审批放行 4-eyes is a control term: two people must approve. Always used for overrides and exceptions. The audit log captures both names.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • BRD (Business Requirements Document) n. 业务需求文档 The contract between business and IT for what will be built.
  • Acceptance criteria (AC) n. 验收标准 The testable conditions that define "done" for a requirement.
  • Edge case n. 边界情况 A scenario where the input is partial, bad, or unexpected. Must be defined for each requirement.
  • Out of scope n. 超出范围 Explicitly excluded in the BRD. New items require a formal scope change.
  • Scope change n. 范围变更 A formal addition to the BRD with cost, timeline, and FTE impact.
  • 4-eyes approval n. 4-eyes 审批 A control requiring two people to approve an action; logged in the audit trail.
  • Override n. 放行 A manual action that bypasses the system default. Always requires 4-eyes and audit logging.
  • Audit trail n. 审计日志 The system log of who did what, when. Required for regulator evidence.
  • Sign-off effective when phr. 签批生效条件 The verifiable event that triggers the sign-off to take effect (e.g., v3.3 in the repo).
  • Repo n. 文档库 The Confluence or SharePoint location where the document of record lives.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "AC-N: failure handling — if X, system Y" — the AC pattern

An acceptance criterion has a fixed structure: AC-N: [name] — if [condition], [system behaviour]. The grammar is conditional (if) plus imperative (system does X). One sentence per AC, numbered, with a name. The MD can read the AC list in 30 seconds.

验收标准有固定结构：AC-N：[名称]——若【条件】、【系统行为】。

② "Out-of-scope list is final, no additions without..." — the closure clause

The closure clause uses final (the lock) and no X without Y (the process). "We won't add things casually" loses; "the list is final, no additions without a formal scope change" wins. The MD buys the closure.

关闭条款使用 final（锁定）和 no X without Y（流程）。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always sign off conditionally if there are open items. Unconditional sign-off on a v3.2 with a known gap is reckless. "Sign-off effective when v3.3 is in the repo" is professional.
  • Walk the BRD by FR, with ACs. Functional requirement, then acceptance criteria, then edge case. One FR at a time.
  • Surface edge cases for every FR. The 3 most common: failure, partial, exception. The PM must answer for each.
  • Edge case handling becomes a new AC, numbered. AC-4: failure handling — if X, system Y. One sentence per AC.
  • The out-of-scope list is a lock, not a wish list. "Final" is the word. New items require a formal scope change with cost and timeline impact.
  • Always name the "effective when" event. v3.3 in the repo is verifiable. "When Priya's done" is not.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Write an AC for an edge case

Requirement: "Customer can reset password via OTP." Edge case: customer enters wrong OTP 3 times. Write AC-2 (the failure handling) in the pattern "AC-N: name — if X, system Y."

Exercise 2 — Spot the missing acceptance criterion

A requirement says "Customer can update address." The BRD has 2 ACs. What is the most likely missing AC? Write it in the AC pattern.

Exercise 3 — Write a conditional sign-off

You find 2 edge cases during the BRD walkthrough. Write a conditional sign-off with 2 conditions, an effective-when event, and a slip date.
