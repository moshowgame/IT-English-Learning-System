Daily Standup with IT PM — From the Business Side

Article 01 · Module 1: Daily Business Touchpoints · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟢 Beginner 🎯 RAG, blocker, milestone, ask, unblock

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 09:30 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding at HSBC HK, the business sponsor for the Digital Onboarding 2.0 programme. You have 10 minutes before your 10:00 Leadership Team meeting. Your IT PM, Sara, calls for the daily standup. You are the customer of this programme; she runs the delivery. The standup must be crisp: where are we, what is the blocker, what do you need from me.

周二 09:30 HKT，你是汇丰香港客户开户业务负责人，也是数字开户 2.0项目群的业务发起方。10:00 领导力团队会议前你有 10 分钟。你的 IT PM Sara 打电话来开每日站会。你是项目群的客户，她负责交付。站会要简练：我们在哪、卡点是什么、你需要我做什么。

The standup from the business side is the reverse of the IT standup: you ask, they answer. Three questions: RAG, blocker, ask. Time budget: 10 minutes. Anything longer means scope or governance failure on either side.

业务方的站会和 IT 站会是反向的：你问、他们答。三个问题：RAG、卡点、需要。时间预算 10 分钟。超过 10 分钟意味着双方的范围或治理失败。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sara (IT PM): Sara: Hi Joyce, daily standup, 10 minutes. Give me the milestone status first — onboarding 2.0.Sara：Joyce 你好，每日站会，10 分钟。先给我里程碑状态——开户 2.0。
You (Business Sponsor): Walk me through RAG by workstream. Identity verification?按工作流给我 RAG。身份核验？
Sara: Sara: Amber. HKMA on the eKYC threshold is still pending, 2-week delay risk, but the team has a workaround in flight.Sara：黄。HKMA 关于 eKYC 阈值的口径还没定，有 2 周延迟风险，但团队已有应对方案在推进。
You: What's the blocker?卡点是什么？
Sara: Sara: Three things. One — HKMA clarification on eKYC threshold, no response to our letter from 12 July. Two — Group Cyber needs 2 more weeks to sign off the FIDO2 risk assessment, they're short-staffed. Three — your team hasn't signed off the journey redesign for the high-net-worth segment, which is blocking UI build.Sara：三件事。一——HKMA 关于 eKYC 阈值的口径，我们 7 月 12 日发函还没回复。二——集团 Cyber 需要再加 2 周签批 FIDO2 风险评估，他们人少。三——你的团队还没签批高净值客户的旅程重设计，卡住了 UI 构建。
You: I'll sign off the high-net-worth journey today by 14:00. What's the ask for the other two?高净值旅程我今天 14:00 前签批。另外两件需要我做什么？
Sara: Sara: HKMA — can you ask your regulatory contact, Daniel Tang, to nudge the supervisor? FIDO2 — can you escalate to your CIO, Wei, that the Group Cyber timeline is now a programme risk?Sara：HKMA——你能不能让你的监管联络人 Daniel Tang 推动一下主管？FIDO2——你能不能把这个集团 Cyber 时间线升级到你的 CIO Wei，作为项目群风险？
You: Yes to both. I'll call Daniel after the LT meeting and email Wei by 11:00. What's the next milestone and the date?两件都做。LT 会议后我打给 Daniel，11:00 前邮件给 Wei。下一个里程碑和日期？
Sara: Sara: UAT entry — Monday 28 July. Today + 3 weeks. Critical path: eKYC threshold + FIDO2 sign-off + your journey sign-off. If all three close by Friday, UAT enters on time.Sara：UAT 进入——7 月 28 日周一。今天 + 3 周。关键路径：eKYC 阈值 + FIDO2 签批 + 你的旅程签批。三件都在周五前关闭，UAT 按时进入。
You: Got it. Anything else?收到。还有别的吗？
Sara: Sara: No. Same time tomorrow.Sara：没有。明天同一时间。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Walk me through RAG by workstream Walk me through RAG by workstream. 按工作流给我 RAG Always ask RAG by workstream, not by project. Workstream = the unit of accountability (Identity Verification, FIDO2, UI, etc.). The PM reports against the workstream.
  • What's the blocker? What's the blocker? 卡点是什么？ One question. Forces the PM to name the single thing in the way. "Three things" is fine if they are all true blockers; otherwise "the blocker" is one.
  • What's the ask for me? What's the ask for the other two? 需要我做什么？ Always ask "the ask" of the business. The PM must end the standup with a named, dated, single ask. "We need help" is not an ask.
  • Next milestone and the date What's the next milestone and the date? 下一个里程碑和日期？ Always close the standup with the next milestone + date. The business owner walks out knowing what date the next gate is.
  • Critical path, named items Critical path: eKYC threshold + FIDO2 sign-off + journey sign-off. 关键路径：eKYC 阈值 + FIDO2 签批 + 旅程签批 The critical path is a list of named items. The sponsor walks out knowing which 3 things must close to make the next milestone.
  • Yes to both / I'll do it by... Yes to both. I'll call Daniel after the LT meeting and email Wei by 11:00. 两件都做。LT 会议后打给 Daniel，11:00 前邮件给 Wei Always commit in the standup with a time. "I'll look into it" loses. "By 11:00" wins. The PM can hold you to it tomorrow.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • RAG (Red / Amber / Green) n. 红黄绿状态 The health of a workstream or project. Red = off track, Amber = at risk, Green = on track.
  • Workstream n. 工作流 A sub-delivery unit inside a programme (Identity Verification, FIDO2, UI build).
  • Blocker n. 卡点 The single thing preventing progress.
  • Ask n. 需要 A named, dated request from one party to another.
  • Critical path n. 关键路径 The list of items that must close to make the next milestone.
  • Milestone n. 里程碑 A specific date-gated deliverable (UAT entry, Go-Live, regulator sign-off).
  • UAT (User Acceptance Testing) n. UAT 验收测试 The business-led test phase before Go-Live.
  • Workaround n. 应对方案 A temporary fix while the permanent solution is in flight.
  • FIDO2 n. FIDO2 强身份验证 Phishing-resistant authentication standard.
  • eKYC threshold n. eKYC 阈值 The limit above which enhanced identity checks apply.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "What's the ask" — the sponsor's most useful question

The phrase what's the ask for me is the single most useful sentence in a business standup. It forces the PM to convert a vague need ("we need help with the regulator") into a named, dated action ("nudge Daniel Tang by Friday"). The sponsor walks out with a clear to-do, not a feeling.

需要我做什么是业务方站会里最有用的句子。它迫使 PM 把模糊需要转换为具名、有日期的动作。

② "Critical path: A + B + C" — the named-items pattern

Critical path is always a list of named items, joined by plus signs. "eKYC + FIDO2 + journey" is a list a sponsor can hold in their head. "The critical path is complicated" is not.

关键路径总是具名项的列表，用加号连接。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Time-box the standup at 10 minutes. Anything longer means scope or governance failure. Set a timer if you have to.
  • Ask RAG by workstream, not by project. "How's the project" is unanswerable. "Identity verification: RAG?" is.
  • End with a named, dated ask. "Can you help with HKMA" loses. "Call Daniel Tang by Friday" wins.
  • Commit in the standup with a time. "I'll look into it" is a promise to forget. "By 11:00" is a promise to deliver.
  • Close with the next milestone + date. UAT entry 28 July. The sponsor walks out knowing the next gate.
  • Standup is not a status update for the whole programme. It is a 1:1 escalation channel. Use it to clear blockers, not to hear the whole report.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the asks

Translate these 3 asks from the standup into clear, dated actions:

  1. HKMA — can you ask your regulatory contact, Daniel Tang, to nudge the supervisor?
  2. FIDO2 — can you escalate to your CIO, Wei, that the Group Cyber timeline is now a programme risk?
  3. Your team hasn't signed off the journey redesign for the high-net-worth segment.

Exercise 2 — Rewrite the standup opener

The standup opened with "Walk me through RAG by workstream." Rewrite the opener for a different programme, "Customer Service 2.0", with 3 workstreams (Voice Bot, Agent Desktop, Reporting). Use the same structure.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing ask

A PM says: "We're amber on UI, the designer is off sick, and we have a few risks in Cyber." What is the missing ask? Rewrite the PM's update so it includes a named, dated ask.
