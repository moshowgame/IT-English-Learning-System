UAT Business Review — Defect Severity, Sign-off, Go-Live Readiness

Article 04 · Module 1: Daily Business Touchpoints · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Severity 1/2/3, sign-off, conditional approval, regulator evidence

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 14:00 HKT, end of UAT week 2. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding, sitting in the UAT exit review for the Digital Onboarding 2.0 programme. Present: Sara (IT PM), Daniel (Tech Lead), Priya (BA), and Karen (your MD, joining remotely). UAT has 47 test cases. 41 passed, 4 are Sev-2 defects, 2 are Sev-3 defects. Go-Live is targeted for Monday 18 August. You have 60 minutes to make a sign-off call. The MD is watching for regulator evidence: HKMA, MAS.

周五 14:00 HKT，UAT 第 2 周结束。你是客户开户业务负责人，坐在数字开户 2.0项目群的 UAT 退场评审会上。参会有：Sara（IT PM）、Daniel（Tech Lead）、Priya（BA）、Karen（你的 MD，远程）。UAT 共 47 个测试用例。41 个通过，4 个 Sev-2 缺陷，2 个 Sev-3 缺陷。Go-Live 目标8 月 18 日周一。你有 60 分钟做签批决定。MD 关注监管证据：HKMA、MAS。

UAT sign-off is the business owner's most consequential decision. The pattern: defect list by severity, regulator evidence, conditional approval with named dates, named go/no-go criteria. A sign-off without conditions is reckless; a sign-off with conditions is professional.

UAT 签批是业务方最重要的决定。模式：按严重度列缺陷清单、监管证据、带具名日期的有条件批准、具名 go/no-go 标准。无条件签批是鲁莽；有条件签批是专业。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Business Sponsor): Walk me through the defect list by severity. How many Sev-1, Sev-2, Sev-3?按严重度给我过一下缺陷清单。Sev-1、Sev-2、Sev-3 各多少？
Sara (IT PM): Sara: Zero Sev-1. 4 Sev-2 — eKYC timeout for non-HK ID, audit log missing timestamp, journey broken for joint accounts, FIDO2 fallback path. 2 Sev-3 — typo on confirmation screen, slow report load.Sara：零 Sev-1。4 个 Sev-2——非香港身份证 eKYC 超时、审计日志缺时间戳、共同账户旅程中断、FIDO2 回退路径。2 个 Sev-3——确认屏打字错、报表加载慢。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Defect list by severity Walk me through the defect list by severity. 按严重度给我过一下缺陷清单 Always start with severity. Sev-1 blocks go-live. Sev-2 blocks go-live unless explicitly accepted. Sev-3 is a backlog item. The MD buys the severity counts, not the defect names.
  • Fix plan, who by when Fix plan for the 4 Sev-2? Who, by when? 修复计划？谁、什么时候？ Every Sev-2 must have a named owner and a named date. "We'll fix it" loses. "Daniel, by Tuesday EOD" wins.
  • Retest, closure report Retest by Anna, Wednesday and Thursday. Closure report by Friday EOD. You sign the closure report. Anna 周三周四重测，周五 EOD 前关闭报告，你签 The retest-then-closure-report pattern is the only sign-off path. Without a closure report, there is no sign-off. The business owner signs the closure report, not the IT PM.
  • Regulator evidence, named items HKMA — eKYC threshold letter, C-RAF, FIDO2 risk assessment, 14 cyber hygiene. HKMA——eKYC 阈值函、C-RAF、FIDO2 风险评估、14 项网络卫生 Regulator evidence is always a list of named items. The MD buys the list. Anything missing is a sign-off blocker.
  • Conditional sign-off, with 3 conditions Conditional sign-off today. 3 conditions for go-live on 18 August. 今天有条件签批。8 月 18 日 go-live 的 3 个条件 A sign-off is always conditional or unconditional. Conditional is professional; unconditional is reckless. Always state the conditions in numbered form.
  • If any condition fails, slip with named date If any condition fails, we go to 25 August. 任一条件未达成，推迟到 8 月 25 日 Always state the slip date in the conditional sign-off. "We'll let you know" loses. "We go to 25 August" wins. The MD buys the slip date.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Sev-1 / Sev-2 / Sev-3 n. 严重度 1/2/3 Defect severity. Sev-1 = blocks go-live (system down, data loss). Sev-2 = blocks go-live unless accepted (broken journey, missing audit). Sev-3 = backlog (typo, slow load).
  • Closure report n. 关闭报告 The document that says all defects are fixed, retested, and signed off by the business owner.
  • Conditional sign-off n. 有条件签批 A sign-off that lists 2-4 conditions that must close before go-live.
  • Retest n. 重测 The second pass of UAT after defects are fixed, to confirm the fix works and did not break anything else.
  • Regulator evidence / regulator pack n. 监管证据 / 监管包 The set of named documents the regulator expects to see (C-RAF, TRM, MAS notification, 14 cyber hygiene).
  • Go-live n. 上线 The date the system goes into production.
  • Slip v. 推迟 To move a go-live date, always with a new date and a reason.
  • Co-sign v. 联签 Two people signing the same document (sponsor + MD).
  • Fallback path n. 回退路径 The alternative flow when the primary flow fails (e.g., FIDO2 fallback to OTP).

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Walk me through the defect list by severity" — the framing sentence

The phrase by severity forces the PM to group the defects into Sev-1, Sev-2, Sev-3 buckets before listing names. The MD can then read the count of Sev-1 (the only go-live blocker) in 5 seconds. Listing by name is unparseable; listing by severity is a sign-off-ready report.

按严重度迫使 PM 先把缺陷分到严重度桶里再列名字。

② "If any condition fails, we go to 25 August" — the conditional slip

A conditional sign-off has a built-in slip date. If any condition fails, we go to [date]. The grammar is conditional (if) plus named slip (date). "If something goes wrong, we'll see" is not a sign-off; the slip date is.

有条件签批内置推迟日期。任一条件未达成，推迟至【日期】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Start the review with severity, not names. Sev-1 / Sev-2 / Sev-3 first, names second. The MD buys the severity counts.
  • Every Sev-2 needs a named owner and a named date. "We'll fix it" is not a fix plan. "Daniel, Tuesday EOD" is.
  • Retest-then-closure-report is the only sign-off path. No closure report, no sign-off. The business owner signs the closure, not the IT PM.
  • Regulator evidence is a named list, not a folder. "C-RAF, FIDO2 risk assessment, 14 cyber hygiene" is a list. "The regulator pack" is a folder. The MD buys the list.
  • Always sign off conditionally. Unconditional sign-off is reckless. 2-4 numbered conditions are professional.
  • Always state the slip date in the conditional. "If any condition fails, we go to 25 August" is a slip date. The MD buys the date.
  • Co-sign with the MD for material sign-offs. Sponsor signs first, MD co-signs. The MD's signature is the regulator's evidence that the business owns the outcome.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Write a conditional sign-off

UAT has 0 Sev-1, 3 Sev-2, 5 Sev-3. Go-live is 25 September. Write a conditional sign-off with 3 numbered conditions and a slip date.

Exercise 2 — Build the retest-then-closure report plan

For the 3 Sev-2 above, write the retest plan (who, when) and the closure report (who signs, by when). Use the pattern from the dialogue.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing severity

A PM says: "We have a few bugs, mostly minor, but the eKYC one is a problem." What is missing? Rewrite the PM's update so it has clear severity counts.
