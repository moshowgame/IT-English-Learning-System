KPI Dashboard Discussion — Revenue, AUM, Customer, Digital Adoption, NPS, Cost-Income

Article 20 · Module 5: Bank Business Context · Business English

⏱ 7 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Revenue, AUM, customer, digital adoption, NPS, cost-income, ownership

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 14:00 HKT. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. You chair the monthly KPI dashboard review for the customer onboarding business. Present: Karen (your MD), Mark (Contact Centre Lead), Priya (BA), and Peter (FP&A partner from CFO's office). The dashboard has 12 KPIs across 5 categories. You have 60 minutes to walk the 12 KPIs, flag the reds, name the recovery owner, and commit to next-month targets.

周四 14:00 HKT，你是客户开户业务负责人。你主持客户开户业务的每月KPI 仪表盘评审。参会有：Karen（你的 MD）、Mark（联络中心负责人）、Priya（BA）、Peter（CFO 办公室的 FP&A 合伙人）。仪表盘有 5 类 12 个 KPI。你有 60 分钟走查 12 个 KPI，标记红色，具名恢复负责人，承诺下月目标。

The KPI dashboard is the monthly exam of the business. The pattern: 5 categories, 12 KPIs, RAG, target vs actual, named recovery owner. The MD buys the recovery, not the number.

KPI 仪表盘是业务的每月考试。模式：5 类、12 个 KPI、RAG、目标 vs 实际、具名恢复负责人。MD 买恢复，不是数字。

4. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 5 categories, 12 KPIs 5 categories, 12 KPIs. 5 类，12 个 KPI Always open with the count. The MD sees the scope in 5 seconds. 5 categories and 12 KPIs is a cognitive budget; more is unmanageable.
  • Target vs actual, RAG Target $14M, actual $11.2M, 80% of plan, RAG Amber. 目标 1,400 万，实际 1,120 万，80% 完成，RAG 黄 Each KPI has 4 elements: target, actual, % of plan, RAG. The MD reads the 4 elements in 5 seconds. "It's behind" loses; "80% of plan, Amber" wins.
  • Recovery owner, target, date Recovery owner: Mark, target 30% by 30 November. 恢复负责人：Mark，目标 11 月 30 日前 30% Each Amber has 3 elements: named recovery owner, named target, named date. The MD signs the 3 elements. Without them, Amber is a complaint, not a recovery.
  • Summary: G / A / R with counts 5 Green, 6 Amber, 1 Red. 5 个绿、6 个黄、1 个红 Always close the summary with the counts. The MD sees the spread in 5 seconds. 12 with 5/6/1 is the cognitive budget.
  • Red recovery: top reasons + 3 actions + target Top 3 reasons: onboarding time 38%, re-submission 22%, visibility 18%. Action 1, 2, 3 with dates. Target: 12 to 10 by 30 November. 前 3 原因：开户时间 38%、重提交 22%、可见性 18%。动作 1、2、3 附日期。目标：12 到 10，11 月 30 日前 A Red has 3 elements: top reasons (with %), 3 actions (with dates), and a named target with date. The MD signs the Red when all 3 are named.
  • Next review cadence Next review 30 October, same time. 下次评审 10 月 30 日，同一时间 Always close with the next review date. The cadence is monthly. The MD holds the date.

5. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • KPI (Key Performance Indicator) n. 关键绩效指标 A named metric with a target, actual, and owner.
  • Dashboard n. 仪表盘 The monthly view of all KPIs, grouped by category, with RAG.
  • Target vs actual n. 目标 vs 实际 The 4 elements: target, actual, % of plan, RAG.
  • RAG (Red / Amber / Green) n. RAG 红黄绿 The health of a KPI. Red = off plan, Amber = at risk, Green = on plan.
  • Recovery owner n. 恢复负责人 The named person who owns the recovery for an Amber or Red.
  • Net new AUM n. 净新增 AUM The $ value of new client assets minus outflows, year-to-date.
  • Cross-sell attach rate n. 交叉销售附加率 The % of onboardings where a second product is sold.
  • Digital onboarding share n. 数字开户占比 The % of onboardings completed through the digital channel (vs branch or paper).
  • Cost-income ratio n. 成本收入比 The cost of running the onboarding function divided by the revenue it generates.
  • FP&A partner n. FP&A 合伙人 The CFO's finance partner assigned to the business for monthly KPI consolidation.

6. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Target X, actual Y, Z% of plan, RAG Amber" — the 4-element KPI sentence

Each KPI is one sentence with 4 elements: Target X, actual Y, Z% of plan, RAG [color]. The grammar is 4 short clauses, comma-separated. The MD reads the 4 elements in 5 seconds.

每个 KPI 一句话 4 要素：目标 X，实际 Y，Z% 完成，RAG【色】。

② "5 Green, 6 Amber, 1 Red" — the parallel count summary

The summary uses parallel structure: N Green, N Amber, N Red. The grammar is Number Color, repeated three times. The MD sees the spread in 5 seconds.

总结使用平行结构：N 绿、N 黄、N 红。

7. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 5 categories, 12 KPIs — the cognitive budget. More is unmanageable. The MD can hold 12 KPIs in their head; more means dashboard theatre.
  • Each KPI: target, actual, % of plan, RAG. 4 elements. "It's behind" loses; "80% of plan, Amber" wins.
  • Amber always has a recovery owner, target, date. 3 elements. Without them, Amber is a complaint, not a recovery.
  • Red always has top reasons + 3 actions + target. The MD signs the Red when all 3 are named. The FP&A partner is the financial conscience.
  • Close with G / A / R counts. 5 Green, 6 Amber, 1 Red. The MD sees the spread in 5 seconds.
  • Always close with the next review date. Monthly cadence. The MD holds the date.
