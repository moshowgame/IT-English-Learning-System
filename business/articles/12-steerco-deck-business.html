SteerCo Deck (Business View) — Progress, Value, Risk, Decision, Next

Article 12 · Module 3: Written Business Communication · Business English

⏱ 6 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Progress, business value, risk, decision, next milestone, business language

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 10:00 HKT, the day before SteerCo. You are the Head of Customer Onboarding. The SteerCo is chaired by your MD, Karen, and includes the CIO (Wei), the CFO, the CRO, and the COO. You have 10 minutes on the agenda to present the business view of the Digital Onboarding 2.0 programme. The IT PM, Sara, presents the delivery view before you. Your slides must complement hers, not duplicate them. Focus: progress in business terms, business value delivered, business risk, the one decision you need.

周四 10:00 HKT，SteerCo 前一天。你是客户开户业务负责人。SteerCo 由你的 MD Karen 主持，成员有 CIO（Wei）、CFO、CRO、COO。你在议程上有 10 分钟呈现数字开户 2.0的业务视角。IT PM Sara 在你之前呈现交付视角。你的幻灯片必须补充她的，不重复。聚焦：业务语言下的进展、交付的业务价值、业务风险、你需要的一个决策。

The SteerCo deck from the business side is the mirror of the IT delivery deck. IT shows RAG, milestone, FTE. Business shows customer adoption, NPS movement, revenue realised, regulator milestone status, the one decision. The two decks together tell the full story.

SteerCo 业务方幻灯片是 IT 交付幻灯片的镜像。IT 呈现 RAG、里程碑、FTE；业务呈现客户采用、NPS 变化、已实现收入、监管里程碑状态、一个决策。两份幻灯片一起讲完整个故事。

2. The 5-Slide Business Deck 5 张业务幻灯片

Slide 1 — Progress (business terms) 进展（业务语言）

Programme on track for go-live Monday 18 August, 09:00 HKT.
UAT closes Friday 15 August, 17:00.
Customer journeys from Tuesday 19 August.
Top-10 HNW clients briefed, 9 of 10 commitment letters signed.
Branch readiness: 11 of 12 branches confirmed, last one by Friday.项目群按计划 8 月 18 日周一 09:00 HKT 上线。
UAT 8 月 15 日周五 17:00 关闭。
客户旅程从 8 月 19 日周二开始。
前 10 大高净值客户已通知，10 个中 9 个签承诺函。
网点就绪：12 家网点中 11 家确认，最后一家周五前。

Slide 2 — Business Value Delivered 已交付的业务价值

Onboarding time: 4 days → 4 hours retail (pilot result, 200 customers).
NPS pilot: 32 → 54 in the 200-customer pilot.
Manual rework rate: 18% → 5% in pilot.
Revenue: $0 in pilot, $4M forecast Q4, $14M full-year forecast.
Cost saved: $1.2M annual from 12 FTE redeployment.开户时间：零售 4 天 → 4 小时（试点结果，200 个客户）。
NPS 试点：32 → 54，200 客户试点。
人工返工率：试点 18% → 5%。
收入：试点 0，Q4 预测 400 万，全年预测 1,400 万。
节省成本：12 FTE 重新部署年省 120 万。

Slide 3 — Business Risk 业务风险

Risk 1: FIDO2 risk assessment slip, 3 weeks. Customer impact: 5% of HNW on Safari get a UX banner, no control failure. Mitigation: escalation to Group Cyber CIO, target 22 August.
Risk 2: eKYC timeout for non-HK ID, 8% of applications. Customer impact: manual fallback, 4-day SLA, experience unchanged from today. Mitigation: BRD v3.4 documents the fallback, Priya EOD today.
Risk 3: regulator slot 30 days out if we miss 25 August HKMA. Customer impact: $4M revenue slip to Q1 next year, top-10 HNW confidence hit. Mitigation: no slip; conditions on the go-live call are the mitigation.风险 1：FIDO2 风险评估推迟 3 周。客户影响：5% Safari 高净值看到 UX 横幅，无控制失效。应对：升级到集团 Cyber CIO，目标 8 月 22 日。
风险 2：非香港身份证 eKYC 超时，8% 申请。客户影响：人工回退，4 天 SLA，体验与今天一致。应对：BRD v3.4 记录回退，Priya 今天 EOD 前。
风险 3：如果错过 8 月 25 日 HKMA，下一个监管空位要 30 天后。客户影响：400 万收入推迟到明年 Q1，前 10 大高净值信心受挫。应对：不推迟；上线决定的 3 个条件就是应对。

Slide 4 — One Decision Needed 需要的一个决策

Decision: Approve go-live on Monday 18 August, 09:00 HKT, with 3 named conditions:
Condition 1: All 4 Sev-2 closed and retest-passed by Thursday 14 August EOD.
Condition 2: Group Cyber final sign-off on FIDO2 retest by Friday 15 August EOD.
Condition 3: MAS notification acknowledgement received by 12 August.
If any condition fails, slip to 25 August, brief MD and regulator in writing.决策：批准 8 月 18 日周一 09:00 HKT 上线，附 3 个具名条件：
条件 1：4 个 Sev-2 全部关闭并在 8 月 14 日周四 EOD 前重测通过。
条件 2：集团 Cyber 在 8 月 15 日周五 EOD 前对 FIDO2 重测做最终签批。
条件 3：MAS 报备确认回执 8 月 12 日前收到。
任一条件未达成，推迟到 8 月 25 日，书面告知 MD 和监管。

Slide 5 — Next Milestone 下一个里程碑

Go-live: Monday 18 August, 09:00 HKT.
First customer journey: Tuesday 19 August.
First NPS check: Friday 22 August.
First AUM measurement: 30 September.
Closure report (Karen + Joyce): Monday 18 August, 12:00.上线：8 月 18 日周一 09:00 HKT。
首个客户旅程：8 月 19 日周二。
首次 NPS 检查：8 月 22 日周五。
首次 AUM 测量：9 月 30 日。
关闭报告（Karen + Joyce）：8 月 18 日周一 12:00。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Progress in business terms Onboarding time 4 days → 4 hours retail (pilot result, 200 customers). 开户时间零售 4 天 → 4 小时（试点结果，200 个客户） Progress in business is customer-facing: time saved, NPS, revenue, cost saved. The IT version (RAG, milestone, FTE) is the PM's slide. Your slide is the business outcome.
  • Business value delivered, with pilot number NPS pilot 32 → 54 in the 200-customer pilot. NPS 试点 32 → 54，200 客户试点 Always pair the result with the pilot size. "NPS went up" loses. "NPS 32 → 54 in a 200-customer pilot" wins. The SteerCo buys the pilot number.
  • Business risk, with customer impact Risk: FIDO2 slip. Customer impact: 5% HNW on Safari see UX banner, no control failure. 风险：FIDO2 推迟。客户影响：5% Safari 高净值看到 UX 横幅，无控制失效 Business risk is always named with customer impact. The IT version is "Sev-2 defect". The business version is "5% of customers see a banner". Same fact, different language.
  • One decision, with 3 conditions Approve go-live on 18 August with 3 named conditions. If any condition fails, slip to 25 August. 批准 8 月 18 日上线，附 3 个具名条件。任一条件未达成，推迟到 8 月 25 日 The decision is one slide, with 3 numbered conditions and a slip date. The SteerCo signs the conditions, not the strategy.
  • Next milestone, customer-facing First customer journey 19 August. First NPS check 22 August. 首个客户旅程 8 月 19 日。首次 NPS 检查 8 月 22 日 Milestones in business language, not IT-internal. The MD remembers "first NPS check", not "closure report". The customer-facing dates are the ones the SteerCo will hold you to.
  • Co-sign the closure Closure report (Karen + Joyce): Monday 18 August, 12:00. 关闭报告（Karen + Joyce）：8 月 18 日周一 12:00 For a material go-live, the sponsor and the MD co-sign the closure report. The names are named, the date is named. The SteerCo sees the ownership.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Business view n. 业务视角 The sponsor's view of the programme: customer outcomes, business value, business risk, the decision.
  • Delivery view n. 交付视角 The PM's view: RAG, milestone, FTE, blocker, ask. The two views together tell the full story.
  • Pilot result, with size n. 试点结果，附规模 The customer outcome from a small pilot, with the customer count named.
  • Revenue realised n. 已实现收入 The $ that has actually landed, not the forecast.
  • AUM measurement n. AUM 测量 The first measurement of assets under management after launch.
  • NPS check n. NPS 检查 A customer satisfaction measurement, usually weekly for the first month.
  • Closure report (co-sign) n. 关闭报告（联签） The document signed by sponsor and MD confirming the go-live decision.
  • Next milestone, customer-facing phr. 下一个里程碑，面向客户 Milestones the business team will remember and the SteerCo will hold you to.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Onboarding time 4 days → 4 hours retail (pilot result, 200 customers)" — the before-after pattern

The before-after pattern: [Metric] [before] → [after] [segment] (pilot result, [size]). One sentence per metric. The grammar is arrow + parenthetical for the pilot. The SteerCo reads the result in 5 seconds.

前后对比模式：【指标】【前】→【后】【客群】（试点结果，【规模】）。

② "Risk: [name]. Customer impact: [X]. Mitigation: [Y]." — the risk triplet

A business risk has three lines: Risk: [name]. Customer impact: [X]. Mitigation: [Y]. The grammar is Category: detail., three times. The SteerCo reads the triplet in 5 seconds.

业务风险有三行：风险：【名称】。客户影响：【X】。应对：【Y】。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Business view complements delivery view, never duplicates. IT shows RAG; business shows customer outcome. Same programme, two slides.
  • Progress in business terms. Customer-facing numbers: time saved, NPS, revenue, cost saved. The MD buys the outcome.
  • Always pair the result with the pilot size. "NPS went up" loses. "NPS 32 → 54 in a 200-customer pilot" wins.
  • Business risk in customer impact. The IT version is "Sev-2 defect". The business version is "5% of customers see a banner". Same fact, different language.
  • One decision, with named conditions. 3 numbered conditions + slip date. The SteerCo signs the conditions, not the strategy.
  • Milestones in customer-facing language. "First NPS check" wins. "UAT closure" loses. The dates the SteerCo will hold you to are the customer-facing ones.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the IT slide

An IT slide says: "RAG Amber. FIDO2 slipped 3 weeks. Sev-2 defect 1, eKYC timeout for non-HK ID, 8% of applications." Translate this into a business-view slide with risk + customer impact + mitigation.

Exercise 2 — Build the business value slide

You have a 200-customer pilot. Onboarding time 4 days → 4 hours. NPS 32 → 54. Manual rework 18% → 5%. Revenue $0 in pilot, $4M forecast Q4. Build the business value slide with 3-4 metrics in the before-after pattern.

Exercise 3 — Spot the missing pilot size

A slide says: "NPS improved from 32 to 54 in the pilot." What is missing? Rewrite the sentence with the pilot size and the segment.
