Welcome, Business Analyst / BA 你好

As a BA at HSBC, you sit at the centre of every requirement — translating business needs into clear user stories, walking stakeholders through Jiras, running UAT, and aligning Tech Leads, developers, and business sponsors across the UK, HK, mainland China, and India. This section focuses on the spoken English scenarios you actually face — from requirements elicitation interviews to last-minute compliance sign-off.

面向汇丰BA，本节聚焦你日常真正遇到的口语场景，从需求访谈到Story编写，从 UAT管理到与senior stakeholders谈判。每篇文章都包含真实场景、英文对话、中文解释、关键句型与练习。

What you'll learn / 你将学到

  • ✅ Elicit requirements with confidence: walk me through / can you give me an example / what would success look like
  • ✅ Write sharp user stories with clear acceptance criteria
  • ✅ Run a defect triage, walkthrough demo, and UAT cycle in spoken English
  • ✅ Push back on scope creep and last-minute requests politely
  • ✅ Use HSBC-specific banking, KYC, AML, and regulatory terminology correctly

How to Use This Section / 学习建议

💡 Daily Practice Tip

Pick one article per day. Read the dialogue aloud twice, then try to paraphrase the key phrases from memory. After a week, you'll have the core patterns for that module locked in.

⚠️ For Real HSBC Work

Always confirm regulatory terms (HKMA, MAS, FCA, GDPR, PCI-DSS) with your local Compliance and Risk teams. The terms here reflect common usage in HK / UK / mainland China — your region may have local variants.
