Jira / Confluence Story Description

Article 12 · Module 4: Documentation & User Stories · BA English

⏱ 9 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Writing a self-explanatory story that any dev can pick up cold

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is 2:00 PM HKT. Akash, a developer in Bangalore, opens Jira ticket WM-2410 on the RBWM Onboarding squad — a brand-new story you shaped this morning. The Business PO in London is on annual leave for the next two days, and Akash needs to start the story today to keep the sprint on track. He messages you on Teams: "Wei, I opened WM-2410. Could you walk me through the intent and edge cases? The description is a bit thin."

下午 2 点香港时间。班加罗尔的开发 Akash 打开了你在 RBWM 开户 squad 早上刚拆好的 Jira WM-2410。伦敦的业务 PO 正在休年假，未来两天联系不上。Akash 为了 Sprint 进度需要今天 启动，他 Teams 上找你："Wei，WM-2410 我开了，能不能讲下需求意图和边界场景？描述里写得不太清楚。"

This is the moment a BA earns the title. A great story description lets any developer in any region pick it up cold — with the AC, context, scope, dependencies, and edge cases already on the ticket. No IM ping required, no walking over to your desk, no waiting for a meeting. The ticket itself tells the story.

这就是体现 BA 价值的关键时刻。一份优秀的 Story 描述让任何地区的开发都能冷启动—— AC、上下文、范围、依赖、边界场景全在工单里，不用 IM 问你，不用找你当面聊，不用等会议，工单本身就 把故事讲清楚。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Akash (Dev): Hey Wei, quick one — I just opened WM-2410 on the Onboarding squad. The description's a bit thin; could you help me pick it up cold?Wei 问个快问题——我刚打开开户 squad 的 WM-2410，描述写得有点单薄，能帮我冷启动吗？
You (BA): Sure, give me five minutes and I'll beef up the description on Confluence and Jira. The PO is on leave, so I want to make sure the ticket stands on its own.好，给我 5 分钟，我把 Confluence 和 Jira 上的描述都补全。PO 在休假，所以我想让工单独立可读。
You (BA): OK, so the intent: when a HK retail client upgrades from Premier to Jade, we need to re-run CDD on the source-of-wealth evidence and trigger a fresh EDD review if the customer's net worth crosses the HKD 8 million threshold.好，需求意图是这样的：当香港零售客户从 Premier 升级到 Jade 时，我们要对资金来源证据重新跑一次 CDD，如果客户净资产超过 800 万港币门槛就触发新一轮 EDD 审核。
You (BA): I've linked the BRD section in the description — RBWM-CDD-2025 §4.2. The AC is scoped to HK for this sprint; UK and Singapore are explicitly out of scope.我在描述里链了 BRD 章节——RBWM-CDD-2025 §4.2。AC 范围是仅限香港，英国和新加坡明确排除。
You (BA): There are three edge cases you need to know: joint accounts, PEP hits, and re-application within 90 days. I've listed them in the comments with the expected behavior — please read those first before you start.有 3 个边界场景你必须知道：联名账户、PEP 命中、90 天内重新申请。我在评论里列了预期行为，开始前先看评论。
Akash (Dev): Got it. One dependency I'm worried about — the sanctions screening service. Is that in flight?明白。我担心一个依赖——制裁名单筛查服务，它现在在做吗？
You (BA): Yes — the sanctions screening API is owned by the Compliance tribe and is on track for end of sprint. I've logged it as a dependency in the ticket's Issue Links. If it slips, we have a fallback path documented in §6 of the BRD.在做，制裁名单筛查 API 由合规 tribe 负责，sprint 末可以交付。我在工单的 Issue Links 里登记了依赖。如果延期，BRD §6 里有备案路径。
Akash (Dev): Perfect. Last thing — who do I ping if I find a gap in the AC?完美。最后一个问题——我发现 AC 有漏洞时该 @ 谁？
You (BA): Raise it on the ticket as a comment and assign it to me — I'll triage with the PO when she's back. Don't sit on it; the BA review is part of our Definition of Ready.在工单上加评论并指给我，PO 回来后我会跟她一起 triage。别憋着，BA 评审是我们 DoR 的一部分。
Akash (Dev): Brilliant. I'm good to start — thanks for the heads-up.太棒了，可以开工了，谢谢提醒。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Cold Start Can a dev pick it up cold? 开发能否冷启动 (零上下文也能开工) "Pick up cold" = start work with zero prior context. The gold standard for story quality.
  • Beef Up I'll beef up the description. 我把描述补完整 "Beef up" = add depth and detail. Casual BA shorthand for "make it complete".
  • Self-Contained The ticket should stand on its own. 工单应该独立可读 "Stand on its own" = no IM pings, no tribal knowledge required. A Definition of Ready pillar.
  • Scope The AC is scoped to HK only. AC 范围仅限香港 Always name the in-scope region / segment. Scoped to = bounded by.
  • Out of Scope UK and SG are explicitly out of scope. 英国和新加坡明确排除 Stating what's out of scope prevents scope creep later. HSBC BAs must write this.
  • Edge Case Three edge cases you need to know… 有 3 个边界场景你必须知道 Edge cases belong in the description (not hidden in someone's head). Joint accounts, PEP hits, timeouts.
  • Dependency I've logged it as a dependency in Issue Links. 我在 Issue Links 里登记了依赖 Use Jira's Issue Links field, not comments. Comments get buried; links stay visible.
  • Fallback We have a fallback path in §6 of the BRD. BRD §6 里有备案路径 "Fallback path" = Plan B if a dependency slips. BAs document this in the BRD, not in chat.
  • Gap Raise it on the ticket if you find a gap in the AC. 如果发现 AC 有漏洞，在工单上提 "Gap" = something missing or ambiguous. Always log gaps on the ticket, never in DM.
  • DoR BA review is part of our Definition of Ready. BA 评审是我们 DoR 的一部分 DoR = Definition of Ready. The checklist a story must pass before it can be pulled into a sprint.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Story description n. Story 描述 The "why" and "what" of a story. Distinct from AC (the "done" check).
  • Pick up cold phr. 冷启动 Start work with no verbal context. Litmus test for story quality.
  • Issue Links n. Jira 关联项 A Jira field that links related tickets (blocks, depends on, relates to).
  • BRD (Business Requirements Document) n. 业务需求文档 The long-form business requirements, version-controlled on Confluence.
  • DoR (Definition of Ready) n. 就绪定义 The squad's checklist a story must pass before sprint entry.
  • DoD (Definition of Done) n. 完成定义 The squad's checklist a story must pass before "done".
  • CDD (Customer Due Diligence) n. 客户尽职调查 KYC's lighter layer. Required for all customers at onboarding.
  • EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence) n. 强化尽职调查 A deeper review for high-risk customers, PEPs, and high-value accounts.
  • PEP (Politically Exposed Person) n. 政治敏感人物 A customer who holds a prominent public function. Triggers EDD.
  • Source of wealth n. 资金来源 How a customer accumulated their assets. Must be evidenced for Premier / Jade.
  • Sanctions screening n. 制裁名单筛查 Checking customers against OFAC, EU, UN, HMT, HKMA lists.
  • Edge case n. 边界场景 An unusual or boundary condition (joint account, PEP hit, re-application window).

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "I've logged it as a dependency" — present perfect for state, not action

The present perfect (have logged, have raised, have linked) is the BA's best friend in a story description conversation. It signals the work is done and the state is now correct. Compare: "I'm logging it" (in progress, not safe) vs "I've logged it" (auditable, done). Use past simple (I logged it yesterday at 3 PM) only when the timing is itself the news.

现在完成时 (have logged / have raised / have linked) 表示"已完成，状态已就绪"。BA 在工单沟通里最常用。

② Passive "is scoped to" / "is owned by" — defining the boundary, not blaming a person

"The AC is scoped to HK" and "The API is owned by the Compliance tribe" use the passive voice to focus on scope and accountability, not on who did something. This is the politeness default in BA documentation — it keeps the description neutral, factual, and free of personal pronouns that can age badly in a comment thread.

被动语态强调"范围"和"归属"而非"谁做的"。BA 文档中保持中立、客观，不写人名。

③ Imperative + "please" — the softest ask for compliance actions

"Read those first before you start" and "Raise it on the ticket as a comment" use the imperative for clear, action-oriented instructions — but HSBC BAs soften it with please or with "I'd recommend" framing. The combo is: imperative for clarity, please for relationship. Avoid the bare imperative in cross-region Teams messages.

祈使句给出清晰指令，加上 please 保持关系友好。跨地区 Teams 沟通时不要用裸祈使句。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • "Pick up cold" is a real HSBC test. Tech Leads at HSBC often ask: "Can a Bangalore dev pick this up cold next sprint?" If the answer is no, the story doesn't pass Definition of Ready. Build the habit of writing for someone who has never seen the project.
  • Confluence is the source of truth; Jira is the work tracker. The BRD lives on Confluence (versioned, auditable, accessible to Risk and Compliance). Jira holds the AC, the status, and the comments. Never put business rationale only in Jira — it will be lost when the ticket closes.
  • Always cite the BRD section number. "RBWM-CDD-2025 §4.2" is gold. Auditors and Risk reviewers love it. The story description should always link to the section, not just the document.
  • Stating "out of scope" protects the squad. When the UK business asks "but can we also have this in London?" in two weeks, you can point to the original ticket: "Out of scope for this story — see WM-2410 description." It's a polite shield.
  • Use the Issue Links field, not comments. Comments scroll away; Issue Links are queryable in Jira dashboards. A BA who logs dependencies properly gets a 3-second dependency graph in JQL.
  • Tag the right squad, not the right person. @onboarding-squad outlives @akash-sharma. When Akash rotates, the @-mention will still route to the right team.
  • Document edge cases in the description, not in your head. PEP hits, joint accounts, expired IDs — these often live as "tribal knowledge" with the original PO. The day the PO leaves, the story breaks. Write it down.
  • Time zones, not personal schedules. When the PO is on leave and Akash is starting now, don't say "the PO is asleep" — say "the PO is on annual leave; coverage is in the handover doc linked on Confluence." Time zones are facts; people's schedules are personal.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the BA explanation

Translate the following message into natural Chinese, paying attention to the BA's "self-contained" framing.

"Quick context on WM-2410. The intent: when a HK retail client upgrades from Premier to Jade, we re-run CDD on source-of-wealth evidence and trigger EDD if net worth crosses the HKD 8M threshold. AC is scoped to HK only; UK and SG are explicitly out of scope this sprint. Three edge cases are listed in the comments — joint accounts, PEP hits, and re-application within 90 days. The sanctions screening API is a dependency, owned by the Compliance tribe, and I've logged it in Issue Links. Pick it up cold and ping me if you find a gap."

"WM-2410 快速同步一下背景。需求意图：香港零售客户从 Premier 升级到 Jade 时，重新跑一次 CDD 验证资金来源，净资产超过 800 万港币就触发 EDD。AC 范围仅限香港，英国和新加坡本 Sprint 明确排除。3 个边界场景在评论里——联名账户、PEP 命中、90 天内重新申请。制裁名单筛查 API 是一个依赖，由合规 tribe 负责，已经在 Issue Links 里登记。可以冷启动，发现漏洞随时 @ 我。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the well-shaped story description

Which of these Jira descriptions is most likely to pass Definition of Ready? Why?

  1. "As a HK retail customer, I want to upgrade to Jade so I get more benefits. AC: TBD."
  2. "Premier→Jade upgrade flow. Re-run CDD on source of wealth, trigger EDD above HKD 8M. HK only. See BRD RBWM-CDD-2025 §4.2 for full intent. Out of scope: UK, SG. Dependencies: SAN-301 (sanctions API). Edge cases: joint accounts, PEP hits, re-app within 90 days — see comments."
  3. "Need to fix the Jade upgrade. Please ask Wei if anything is unclear."
  4. "Implement Jade upgrade. Should be similar to Premier upgrade. Dev: Akash."

② passes DoR — it has intent, scope, BRD reference, dependencies, and edge cases. ① has "TBD" AC (fails). ③ is tribal knowledge ("ask Wei") and a single point of failure. ④ is vague and skips scope/edge cases. A good description lets any dev in any region pick up cold — ② is the only one that does.

Exercise 3 — Your turn

You're a BA on the GPB KYC squad. Akash just opened GPB-1188 for a SWIFT MT202 cover payment screening enhancement. The PO is in Singapore, on a half-day today. The story is linked to the HKMA sanctions circular. You need to (a) write a Confluence paragraph describing the intent, scope, and edge cases, and (b) write a Teams message to Akash telling him to pick it up cold. Use at least 4 patterns from the Key Phrases section.

Confluence paragraph: "GPB-1188 enhances the SWIFT MT202 cover payment screening to align with HKMA's latest sanctions circular (2025-04). Intent: any MT202 outgoing cover payment must pass enhanced sanctions screening on both ordering and beneficiary BICs. Scope: GPB HK only this sprint. Out of scope: GPB UK, GPB SG. Dependencies: SAN-301 sanctions API (owned by Compliance tribe). Edge cases: (1) cover payments where the ordering BIC is a correspondent, (2) MT202 with a related-party beneficiary, (3) payment chain longer than 3 hops. See BRD GPB-AML-2025 §3.4 for full rationale."

Teams message to Akash: "Hey Akash — just beefed up GPB-1188 on Confluence and Jira. The ticket stands on its own now: intent, scope, edge cases, and the SAN-301 dependency. AC is scoped to GPB HK only. Please read the edge case comments first before you start. Raise any gap in the AC as a ticket comment and assign it to me — I'll triage with the SG PO when she's online. Good to start cold."
