Stakeholder Mapping & RACI

Article 10 · Module 2: Discovery & Workshops · BA English

⏱ 9 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Sponsors, gatekeepers, RACI, and the right conversations

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday afternoon, 2 PM HKT. You're a BA on the GPB (Global Private Banking) Source-of-Wealth squad. The programme is about to enter UAT in three weeks, and you have 14 named stakeholders spread across HK, Shanghai, Singapore, London, and Bangalore. Last week, a story got stuck for four days because nobody knew who was Accountable for signing off the source-of-wealth evidence policy.

周五下午 2 点，香港时间。你是 GPB（全球私人银行）资金来源 squad 的 BA。项目 3 周后进入 UAT，你有 14 个明确的干系人，分布在香港、上海、新加坡、伦敦、 班加罗尔。上周一个 Story 卡了 4 天，因为没人知道资金来源证据政策由谁 Accountable 签字。

Your Scrum Master (Sarah, HK) has asked you to run a 90-minute stakeholder-mapping and RACI workshop with the core squad. The output will be a one-page RACI matrix in Confluence, signed off by the Programme Sponsor, and used to triage every open story from now on.

你的 Scrum Master（Sarah，香港）请你主持一场 90 分钟的干系人地图和 RACI 工作坊， 核心 squad 参加。产出是 Confluence 上一张一页纸的 RACI 矩阵，由项目 Sponsor 签字，并 作为今后每条开放 Story 的分流依据。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (BA): Right, so the goal today is one page in Confluence — sponsor, gatekeepers, and a full RACI for the top 8 deliverables. Sarah, can you walk us through who you think is Accountable for source-of-wealth evidence?好，今天的目标是 Confluence 上一页纸——Sponsor、Gatekeeper，以及 8 个主要交付物的完整 RACI。Sarah，你先说说你认为资金来源证据的 Accountable 是谁？
Sarah (SM): My read: Accountable is the Group Head of Financial Crime Compliance, but the Responsible person day-to-day is our Compliance BA Carmen in Shanghai.我的看法：Accountable 是集团反洗钱合规负责人，但日常 Responsible 是上海合规 BA Carmen。
You (BA): Good. Just to flag — Accountable is always exactly one person. Multiple A's is the most common RACI mistake. Who is the single A for that policy?好。提一下——Accountable 永远只会有一个人，多个 A 是 RACI 最常见的错误。这条政策的单个 A 是谁？
Sarah (SM): OK, single A — let's call it Margaret Yu, Group Head of FCC, based in HK. Carmen is R, I agree.好，单个 A——就叫 Margaret Yu，集团 FCC 负责人，在香港。Carmen 是 R，我同意。
You (BA): Noted. Now the gatekeepers. Gatekeepers are the people who can block progress if we don't get their input. I'm thinking the HKMA contact, the GPB COO, and the UK Risk Lead — agree?收到。现在看 Gatekeeper。Gatekeeper 是如果我们不找他就会阻塞进展的人。我想的是 HKMA 联系人、GPB COO、英国 Risk Lead——同意吗？

You (BA): Good shout, Priya — adding her in. Quick one for the team: for the UAT execution row, who's the single A? Is it me, or is it the QA Lead?好建议，Priya——加进去。问一下团队：UAT 执行这一行，单个 A 是谁？是我还是 QA Lead？
Sandeep (QA): I'd say it's the QA Lead, but you're Consulted for the test scenarios — and the business PO is Informed at minimum.我说应该是 QA Lead，但你是 Consulted（参与测试场景），业务 PO 至少是 Informed。
You (BA): OK, so QA = A, BA = C, PO = I. What about the CAB rule change — who's A there? The Head of GPB Operations?好，UAT 这行：QA = A，BA = C，PO = I。那 CAB 规则变更呢？谁是 A？GPB 运营负责人？
Priya (PO): That has to be Margaret as well — it's a financial-crime policy, not a tech change. If we put the COO as A there, we'll get a different answer every region.那也必须是 Margaret——这是反洗钱政策，不是技术变更。如果把 COO 放上去，每个地区都会给出不同答案。
You (BA): Wise call. Time check — 20 minutes left for the last two rows. Let's do the CAB sign-off and the eCAB case management rows. I'll capture in Confluence live.明智。时间提示——还剩 20 分钟做最后两行：CAB 签字和 eCAB 案件管理。我会实时录入 Confluence。
You (BA): Final ask — can we get Margaret to e-sign the matrix in eCAB by Monday EOD? Then we publish v1.0 and circulate to all 14 stakeholders.最后问一下——能让 Margaret 周一 EOD 前在 eCAB 里电子签字这个矩阵吗？然后我们发 v1.0，通知所有 14 个干系人。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open The goal today is one page in Confluence. 今天的目标是 Confluence 上一页纸 A 90-minute workshop with a one-page output is the right size. State it upfront.
  • Walk Can you walk us through who you think is Accountable? 你说说你认为 Accountable 是谁 Ask one person to seed the conversation. Avoids blank-page paralysis.
  • RACI rule Accountable is always exactly one person. Accountable 永远只会有一个人 The cardinal RACI rule. State it explicitly. Multiple A's = no accountability = stuck stories.
  • Gatekeeper Gatekeepers are the people who can block progress if we don't get their input. Gatekeeper 是如果不找他们就会阻塞进展的人 A gatekeeper is defined by blocking power, not by seniority. The junior who owns the data feed can be a gatekeeper.
  • Single A Single A — let's call it Margaret Yu. 单个 A——就叫 Margaret Yu Always name the A with a real person + a real role. A is not a team, a region, or a function.
  • Consulted BA is Consulted for the test scenarios. BA 在测试场景上是 Consulted "C" = two-way communication, give-and-receive input. Reserve for stakeholders whose input changes the deliverable.
  • Informed The business PO is Informed at minimum. 业务 PO 至少是 Informed "I" = one-way, push-only updates. Use liberally — keeps people on side without overloading them.
  • Wise call Wise call. If we put the COO as A, we'll get a different answer every region. 明智。如果把 COO 放上去，每个地区会给出不同答案 Reinforces good thinking. Use "wise call" or "good shout" sparingly — they mean more.
  • Capture I'll capture in Confluence live. 我会实时录入 Confluence Always say "live". Signals that nothing is being lost in transcription.
  • Circulate Then we publish v1.0 and circulate to all 14 stakeholders. 然后我们发 v1.0，通知所有 14 个干系人 "Circulate" is the verb for senior distribution. Includes "I" stakeholders who should be kept in the loop.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • RACI n. RACI 矩阵 Responsible / Accountable / Consulted / Informed.
  • Accountable (A) n. 最终责任人 The single person who owns the outcome and the sign-off.
  • Responsible (R) n. 执行人 The person who does the work. Multiple R's are allowed.
  • Consulted (C) n. 咨询对象 Two-way input — give-and-receive before the decision.
  • Informed (I) n. 知会对象 One-way update — kept in the loop, no input required.
  • Gatekeeper n. 守门人 A stakeholder whose input is required to unblock progress.
  • Sponsor n. 项目 Sponsor The senior owner who funds and champions the programme.
  • DPO (Data Protection Officer) n. 数据保护官 Owner of GDPR / PDPO compliance — true PII gatekeeper.
  • FCC (Financial Crime Compliance) n. 反洗钱合规 Group function owning AML, sanctions, KYC.
  • PDPO n. 个人资料条例 Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance.
  • GDPR n. 通用数据保护条例 EU General Data Protection Regulation.
  • Stakeholder map n. 干系人地图 A 2x2 (power vs interest) or RACI view of who is involved.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Single A" / "Single Accountable" — the noun-as-modifier pattern

Single A — let's call it Margaret Yu uses single + letter as a shorthand for "the single Accountable person". This is industry-standard RACI shorthand and saves a lot of words. Use it in workshops, not in the matrix document itself.

Single A 是 RACI 圈内的简写，会议里可以直接用，正式文档里要写全称 Accountable。

② "If we put X as A, we'll get Y" — conditional risk framing

If we put the COO as A there, we'll get a different answer every region. uses if + present, will + base (first conditional) to predict a future problem. It's the standard pattern for surfacing risk in a workshop. Combine it with a specific, concrete consequence ("a different answer every region") for max impact.

If + 一般现在时, will + 动词原形 用于预测未来问题，搭配具体后果效果最强。

③ "At minimum" — the soft-floor adverb

The business PO is Informed at minimum uses the adverbial phrase at minimum to signal "I = at least; could be C if circumstances change". A subtle way to leave room for future escalation of stakeholder involvement.

at minimum 用于表达"至少是这个层级，未来可能升级"，给后续调整留余地。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • "A" is sacred. Never put two. At HSBC, multiple A's in a RACI row is treated as no A at all. As BA, you enforce the rule in the workshop. If two people argue, escalate to the sponsor and let the sponsor pick — do not split.
  • Gatekeepers are defined by blocking power, not seniority. A junior engineer who owns the data feed in Bangalore can be a real gatekeeper. A Group MD who never opens email is not. Map blocking power, not org chart height.
  • GDPR / PDPO gatekeeper is non-negotiable. If your story touches PII (which most onboarding stories do), the Group DPO is a gatekeeper. The DPO lives in the UK or HK; book a 30-min slot in their calendar at programme kick-off, not at UAT.
  • Sponsor is the one who funds the pain. The Programme Sponsor at HSBC is typically a Director or MD level. They sign the v1.0 RACI matrix and own escalations. Find them in the first 2 weeks or you'll be blocked for months.
  • Cross-region RACI = region names in C and I. With stakeholders in HK, Shanghai, Singapore, London, Bangalore, your matrix rows should make regional involvement explicit. "GPB Operations (HK + UK)" beats "GPB Operations".
  • "I'll capture live" earns trust in cross-region workshops. When the UK Risk Lead can't be in the room and dials in from a noisy home office, live Confluence capture is the only way to keep them on side. Send the doc link in the Teams chat before the meeting starts.
  • Always end with a published v1.0 and a circulation list. A RACI in someone's head is not a RACI. Force the v1.0 publication in the workshop, with sponsor sign-off and a named circulation list. Otherwise the matrix is just a slide deck.

Exercise 2 — Spot the RACI mistake

Which of these RACI assignments is wrong? Why?

  1. For the UAT execution row: QA Lead = A, BA = C, Business PO = I.
  2. For the source-of-wealth evidence policy row: Group Head of FCC (HK) = A, Compliance BA (Shanghai) = R, Group DPO (UK) = C, COO GPB (HK + UK) = I.
  3. For the UAT execution row: QA Lead = A, BA = A, Tech Lead = A.
  4. For the CAB sign-off row: Group Head of FCC = A, Group DPO = C, BA = R.

③ is wrong: it has three A's, which means no A. Reframe: pick one A (typically the QA Lead) and demote the others to R (e.g., BA = R for test scenario review, Tech Lead = R for automation harness). Multiple A's is the most common RACI mistake and is the leading cause of stuck stories.

Exercise 3 — Your turn

You're a BA on the CMB Trade Finance squad. You have 3 open stories that are blocked because nobody knows the RACI. The 3 stories are: (a) update the Letters of Credit AC for UCP 600, (b) ship the SWIFT MT798 migration to production, (c) handle trade-AML hits above $1M. Stakeholders: Compliance BA in Shanghai, Head of Trade Finance in HK, Group FCC in London, Tech Lead in Bangalore, QA Lead in Singapore. For each story, propose a single A and the R/C/I for each stakeholder. Justify in 1 sentence why that A makes sense. Use at least 3 patterns from the Key Phrases section.

Story (a) — UCP 600 AC update: A = Head of Trade Finance (HK), R = Compliance BA (Shanghai), C = Group FCC (London), I = Tech Lead, QA Lead. Justification: the UCP 600 rule is a Trade Finance product policy, not a financial-crime policy — Head of Trade Finance is the right single A.

Story (b) — MT798 migration to production: A = Tech Lead (Bangalore), R = QA Lead (Singapore), C = BA, Compliance BA, I = Head of Trade Finance, Group FCC. Justification: this is a production release, so the Tech Lead who owns the deployment is the right A; the BA and Compliance are Consulted because the release touches regulated functionality.

Story (c) — Trade-AML hits above $1M: A = Group FCC (London), R = Compliance BA (Shanghai), C = BA, Head of Trade Finance (HK), I = Tech Lead, QA Lead. Justification: trade-AML is a financial-crime policy — putting the Head of Trade Finance as A here would mean a different answer in every corridor, so the single A has to be Group FCC.

Workshop phrasing you might use: "Right, so for story (a) — if we put Group FCC as A, the Tech Lead will be blocked for two weeks waiting on UK sign-off. Single A is Head of Trade Finance, with Group FCC as C. Wise call, Priya. Time check — 10 minutes left for the other two. I'll capture live in Confluence and circulate the v1.0 matrix by 5 PM HKT."
