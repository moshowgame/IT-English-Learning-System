Banking Terminology for BA

Article 19 · Module 5: HSBC Banking Context · BA English

⏱ 11 min read 🔴 Advanced 🏦 KYC, AML, CDD, EDD, sanctions, credit risk, trade finance vocabulary

1. Scenario 场景背景

You have just joined the GPB (Global Private Banking) Onboarding tribe as a BA in Hong Kong. Your first PI planning is in two days. To prepare, you sit in on a requirements elicitation workshop for the "High-Risk Jurisdiction Review" epic with the private bankers in Singapore and the regional Financial Crime Compliance (FCC) lead. The bankers drop acronyms continuously: KYC, CDD, EDD, AML, PEP, sanctions, TBML, beneficial ownership, source of wealth (SoW), source of funds (SoF), risk rating, periodic review, exit.

你刚加入香港 GPB（全球私人银行） 开户 tribe 担任 BA。两次 PI 规划会后就是需求获取工作坊。 为了准备，你旁听了与 新加坡私行家 和区域 金融犯罪合规（FCC） 负责人关于 "高风险司法管辖区审查" Epic 的需求工作坊。银行家们不停地抛缩写： KYC、CDD、EDD、AML、PEP、制裁、TBML、实益所有人、财富来源（SoW）、资金来源（SoF）、 风险评级、定期复核、退出（exit）。

As a BA, you don't need to be a compliance expert — but you must understand the vocabulary well enough to write sharp AC. Misunderstanding periodic review vs event-driven review will lead to stories that don't meet MAS TRM. This article is your banking-English survival kit.

作为 BA，你不需要成为合规专家——但你必须理解这些词汇到能写出清晰 AC 的程度。 分不清 定期复核 和 事件触发复核 会导致 Story 无法满足 MAS TRM。这篇文章就是你的银行英语生存包。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Vikram (FCC Lead, Singapore): Welcome, Wei. Quick context for the epic — under MAS TRM, any client onboarded from a high-risk jurisdiction must have Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), which is over and above the standard CDD. Specifically, we need source of wealth (SoW) and source of funds (SoF) verified to a deeper level.Wei，欢迎。先简单说背景——根据 MAS TRM，凡是高风险司法管辖区的客户开户必须做强化尽职调查（EDD），比标准 CDD 更严格。具体来说，财富来源（SoW）和资金来源（SoF）必须做更深入的核实。
Wei (You, BA): Got it. Just to be clear — SoW is how the client built their overall wealth over time (e.g. inheritance, business sale, salary), and SoF is where the money for this specific transaction is coming from. Is that right?明白。确认一下——SoW 是客户整体财富的来源（例如：继承、出售企业、薪资），SoF 是本次交易资金的具体来源。对吗？
Vikram (FCC Lead, Singapore): Spot on. Now, the kicker — if the client is a PEP (Politically Exposed Person), or has adverse media, the system should trigger the EDD workflow automatically, not rely on the banker to remember.完全正确。现在重点来了——如果客户是 PEP（政治敏感人物），或者有负面媒体记录，系统应该自动触发 EDD 流程，而不是依赖客户经理记得。
Mei (Private Banker, HK): Real-world question — what about the existing book? We've got about 200 high-risk-jurisdiction clients onboarded pre-OneScreening. Are we bringing them in scope for the periodic review, or just new ones?实际业务问题——存量客户怎么办？我们大约有 200 个在 OneScreening 上线前开户的高风险司法管辖区客户。他们在定期复核的范围内，还是只针对新客户？
Vikram (FCC Lead, Singapore): Good question. Per Group Standard, all existing high-risk clients need a periodic refresh at least every 12 months. However, event-driven reviews trump periodic ones — for example, if a client appears on a new sanctions list (OFAC, UN, EU, HMT), that triggers an immediate review, regardless of where they are in the cycle.好问题。根据集团标准，所有存量高风险客户至少每 12 个月要做一次定期刷新。但是，事件触发复核优先于定期复核——例如，客户上了新的制裁名单（OFAC、UN、EU、HMT），会立即触发审查，无论他在周期内的什么阶段。
Wei (You, BA): Quick check on the exit scenario — if a client fails EDD, is it a soft exit (relationship ended) or a hard exit (relationship ended and a SAR/STR filed)?快速确认一下"退出"场景——如果客户 EDD 不通过，是软退出（终止关系）还是硬退出（终止关系并提交 SAR/STR 可疑活动报告）？
Vikram (FCC Lead, Singapore): It depends. If the red flag is mitigated — say, the SoW was unclear but the client provided a satisfactory explanation — it's a soft exit. But if there's suspected money laundering or terrorist financing, we file a SAR (Suspicious Activity Report) — in HK that's a STR (Suspicious Transaction Report) to the JFIU (Joint Financial Intelligence Unit) — and exit hard.看情况。如果红旗被缓解了——比如，SoW 当时不清楚，但客户提供了合理解释——就软退出。但如果涉嫌洗钱或恐怖融资，我们提交 SAR（可疑活动报告）——在香港是向 JFIU（联合财富情报组）提交 STR（可疑交易报告）——然后硬退出。
Mei (Private Banker, HK): And for trade finance clients — TBML (Trade-Based Money Laundering) is a separate workstream, right? It falls under the GLCM OneTrade squad, not us.那贸易融资客户呢——TBML（基于贸易的洗钱）是另一条工作流对吧？归 GLCM 的 OneTrade squad 管，不归我们。
Vikram (FCC Lead, Singapore): Correct. Trade-Based Money Laundering is a separate risk theme — it covers things like over- and under-invoicing, phantom shipments, and shell-company trading. That'll be a separate BA on the GLCM side.正确。基于贸易的洗钱是单独的风险主题——包括高价低价开票、虚假运输、壳公司贸易等。这块归 GLCM 那边单独的 BA 负责。
Wei (You, BA): Perfect, that's really helpful. To summarise what I've heard: (1) EDD is auto-triggered for high-risk jurisdictions, PEPs, and adverse media; (2) periodic refresh is annual, with event-driven reviews overriding; (3) SoW/SoF are explicit AC items with documentation; (4) exit can be soft or hard, with SAR/STR for the hard path. Have I missed anything?太好了，很有帮助。我来总结一下我听到的：(1) 高风险司法管辖区、PEP、负面媒体自动触发 EDD；(2) 定期刷新是年度，事件触发复核优先；(3) SoW/SoF 是显式 AC 项，需要文档支持；(4) 退出分软和硬，硬路径要提交 SAR/STR。我有没有漏掉的？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Clarify Just to be clear — SoW is how the client built their overall wealth… 确认一下——SoW 是客户整体财富的来源 "Just to be clear" is the BA's best friend. Use it before rephrasing something technical — it confirms understanding without slowing the room.
  • Trigger The system should trigger the EDD workflow automatically. 系统应自动触发 EDD 流程 "Trigger" = cause to start. Compliance loves the word. Always specify the trigger condition and the action.
  • In scope Are we bringing them in scope for the periodic review? 把他们纳入定期复核范围吗？ "In scope" / "out of scope" = the regulator's mental model. Default to clarity on who's covered.
  • Over and above EDD is over and above the standard CDD. EDD 在标准 CDD 之上 "Over and above" = additional to. Critical for compliance — clarifies that EDD doesn't replace CDD, it adds to it.
  • Periodic vs event-driven Event-driven reviews trump periodic ones. 事件触发复核优先于定期复核 Always specify which "review" you're talking about. Mixing periodic and event-driven in a story is a common BA bug.
  • Red flag If the red flag is mitigated… 如果红旗被缓解 "Red flag" = a suspicious indicator. "Mitigated" = addressed / explained away. The two words together define an exit decision.
  • File We file a SAR / an STR. 我们提交 SAR/STR "File" = formally submit. SAR in the US/UK; STR in HK/SG. The verb is the same; the noun is jurisdiction-specific.
  • Soft / hard exit Soft exit (relationship ended) vs hard exit (relationship ended AND SAR filed). 软退出 / 硬退出 A shorthand the FCC team uses constantly. Capture both in your AC — they have very different regulatory consequences.
  • Risk theme TBML is a separate risk theme. TBML 是独立的风险主题 "Risk theme" = a logical grouping of related risks, often mapped to a single squad. Use it to set boundaries in scope discussions.
  • Summarise To summarise what I've heard… 总结一下我听到的 End a discovery session with a "what I've heard" recap. Surprises the room — and gives Compliance the chance to correct you on the spot.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • KYC (Know Your Customer) n. 了解你的客户 The overarching process of verifying a client's identity, risk profile, and source of wealth.
  • CDD (Customer Due Diligence) n. 客户尽职调查 Standard KYC checks: ID, address, beneficial ownership. The default for all clients.
  • EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence) n. 强化尽职调查 Deeper checks for high-risk clients: SoW, SoF, source verification, senior approval.
  • PEP (Politically Exposed Person) n. 政治敏感人物 An individual holding a prominent public function. Triggers EDD automatically.
  • Adverse media n. 负面媒体 Negative news about a client (sanctions, fraud, criminal charges). Triggers EDD automatically.
  • SoW (Source of Wealth) n. 财富来源 How the client accumulated their overall wealth. Required for EDD.
  • SoF (Source of Funds) n. 资金来源 Where the money for a specific transaction originated. Required for EDD.
  • Beneficial ownership n. 实益所有人 The natural person(s) who ultimately own or control the entity. Required for CDD.
  • Sanctions list n. 制裁名单 A government-issued list of prohibited parties. OFAC (US), UN, EU, HMT (UK) are the major ones.
  • Sanctions screening n. 制裁名单筛查 The process of matching clients and transactions against sanctions lists. Run at onboarding and on every payment.
  • TBML (Trade-Based Money Laundering) n. 基于贸易的洗钱 Money laundering through fake or manipulated trade transactions. A separate workstream in GLCM.
  • SAR / STR n. 可疑活动/交易报告 SAR (US/UK) and STR (HK/SG) are the local names for the same filing: a report of suspected criminal activity to the regulator.
  • JFIU (Joint Financial Intelligence Unit) n. 联合财富情报组 The HK Police unit that receives STRs. A filing triggers a criminal investigation, not an internal review.
  • Trade finance n. 贸易融资 HSBC's product set: Letter of Credit (LC), SBLC, collections, guarantees. Settled via SWIFT MT103 / MT202.
  • Credit risk n. 信用风险 The risk that a borrower fails to repay. Scored via internal PD / LGD / EAD models.
  • Risk rating n. 风险评级 The classification of a client (low / medium / high) that determines the KYC cycle and EDD triggers.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Per Group Standard" / "Per MAS TRM" / "Per HKMA SPM" — the "per" prepositional phrase

In compliance English, per + standard name is the standard way to attribute a rule. Compare: According to MAS (informal) vs Per MAS TRM Guidelines (formal). Always pair the regulator with the specific standard. Per Group Standard is HSBC's internal equivalent.

per + 标准名 是合规英语里归因规则的标准方式，总是指明具体的标准。

② "If X, then Y" vs "If X, the system should Y" — active vs passive trigger language

When writing AC for compliance, use active trigger language: If a client is a PEP, the system should trigger EDD automatically. Avoid the passive EDD should be triggered — it loses the system as the agent and makes testing harder.

合规 AC 用主动语态："系统应触发"，而不是"应被触发"——更利于测试。

③ "It depends" / "It varies" / "Per jurisdiction" — the hedging shorthand

When a rule differs by region, the standard hedge is it depends + per jurisdiction. It depends. In HK we file an STR; in the US we file a SAR. This signals to the room that you've handled cross-region work before.

规则因地区不同时的标准对冲：it depends / per jurisdiction——立即让会议室知道你有跨地区经验。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Speak the regulator's local language. In HK it's STR + JFIU; in Singapore it's STR + STRO; in the US it's SAR + FinCEN; in the UK it's SAR + NCA. Mismatching these is a tell that you don't know the jurisdiction. Always confirm the local filing regime in the first meeting.
  • SoW ≠ SoF. Don't conflate them. Wealth of clients built over 30 years (SoW) is very different from the source of $5M wire (SoF). A common BA mistake is to write one AC for both. They have different evidence requirements.
  • "Red flag" is a regulatory term of art. In compliance, a red flag isn't a code smell — it's a defined category in the Group AML Policy. Reference the specific red flag (e.g. "red flag 4.2.1 — unusual wire pattern"), not just "a red flag".
  • PEP screening is global, not local. A PEP in Brazil triggers EDD in Singapore. Don't assume local residency limits the screening scope. HSBC's PEP list is global.
  • Sanctions screening is on every payment, not just onboarding. A common squad mistake is to scope sanctions screening only at onboarding. Real-time screening on every outbound payment is the regulatory baseline.
  • TBML is its own beast. If you're on a GPB / RBWM squad, you probably don't own TBML — that's GLCM. But you do need to know the term to avoid scope confusion in cross-squad conversations.
  • When in doubt, cite the policy document. "Per Group AML Policy section 4.2" beats "I think this is a high-risk client" every time. Compliance officers will quote section numbers back at you — match their precision.
  • Don't translate "exit" loosely. In Chinese BA conversations, "退出" can mean anything from "close the account" to "fire the client". Always specify the exit type — soft, hard, with SAR, without SAR — in writing.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the requirements workshop

Translate the following BA summary into natural Chinese. Pay attention to the SoW/SoF distinction and the SAR/STR terminology.

"To summarise what I've heard: (1) EDD is auto-triggered for high-risk jurisdictions, PEPs, and adverse media; (2) periodic refresh is annual, with event-driven reviews overriding; (3) SoW and SoF are explicit AC items with documentation; (4) exit can be soft (relationship ended) or hard (relationship ended AND SAR/STR filed). One thing to flag — TBML is a separate workstream under GLCM OneTrade, not in our scope. Have I missed anything?"

"我来总结一下我听到的：(1) 高风险司法管辖区、PEP、负面媒体自动触发 EDD；(2) 定期刷新是年度，事件触发复核优先；(3) SoW 和 SoF 是显式 AC 项，需要文档支持；(4) 退出分软（终止关系）和硬（终止关系并提交 SAR/STR）。提一个事项——TBML 是 GLCM OneTrade 的独立工作流，不在我们的范围内。我有没有漏掉的？"

Exercise 2 — Spot the BA's terminology mistake

Which of these BA statements contains a banking-vocabulary error that a senior BA would catch?

  1. "SoW and SoF are the same — both are about where the client's money came from."
  2. "A PEP automatically triggers EDD under MAS TRM."
  3. "Sanctions screening is run at onboarding, not on every payment."
  4. "A and C are both wrong."

① conflates SoW (overall wealth) with SoF (specific transaction). ③ says screening is "not on every payment" — opposite of the real-time payment screening baseline. ④ is the correct answer: both ① and ③ are mistakes. A senior BA would catch both at the AC definition stage. ② is correct.

You're running a 30-min requirements elicitation call with a regional FCC lead. They need a new story to capture source of wealth for clients onboarded under the pre-2022 standard who never had SoW recorded. Write a 4-6 sentence BA summary email that (a) names the trigger condition, (b) names the AC items, (c) flags a risk, (d) proposes a UAT approach. Use at least 3 patterns from the Key Phrases section.

"Hi Vikram, thanks for the session today. To summarise what I've heard: we need a new story under the 'Legacy KYC Remediation' epic to capture source of wealth for all pre-2022 clients where the SoW field is null. One risk to flag — if the legacy data has gaps in the beneficial ownership field, this story will surface them; that may need a follow-on. For UAT, I'd propose three personas: (a) a clean legacy client, (b) a legacy client with adverse media, (c) a legacy PEP. I'll have the draft story and AC on the Jira ticket by Thursday. Could I ask you to confirm the legal basis wording for the consent ledger before I freeze the AC?"
