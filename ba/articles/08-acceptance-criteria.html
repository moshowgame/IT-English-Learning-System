Acceptance Criteria Definition

Article 08 · Module 2: Discovery & Workshops · BA English

⏱ 9 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Given/When/Then, edge cases, unambiguous AC

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday morning, 10 AM HKT. The CMB (Commercial Banking) sanctions screening squad — a BA (you, Wei, in HK), a Tech Lead (Hannah, in the UK), a QA (Sandeep, in Bangalore), and a Compliance Business Analyst (Carmen, in Shanghai) — is sitting in a refinement session. The story on the table is SCN-4410: Real-time sanctions hit handling for cross-border payments.

周三上午 10 点，香港时间。CMB（工商金融）制裁名单筛查 squad——包括香港的 BA 你（Wei）、 英国的 Tech Lead Hannah、班加罗尔的 QA Sandeep，以及上海的合规业务分析师 Carmen——在开 Grooming 会。桌上的 Story 是 SCN-4410: 跨境支付的实时制裁名单命中处理。

The business PO has written the AC, but it's vague: "the system should handle sanctions hits appropriately". Your job as BA is to turn it into a Given/When/Then list, surface edge cases (PEP, false positives, dual-jurisdiction rules under OFAC + HKMA), and make it testable before the story goes into sprint planning tomorrow.

业务方 PO 写的 AC 很模糊："系统应适当处理制裁名单命中"。BA 你的任务是在 Story 进 Sprint Planning 之前，把它变成 Given/When/Then 列表，暴露边界情况 （PEP、误报、OFAC + HKMA 双司法管辖），并让 AC 可测。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (BA): OK, so let's read through Carmen's AC draft line by line and stress-test it.好，我们一行一行看 Carmen 写的 AC 草稿，逐一压测。
Carmen (Compliance BA): Sure. "The system should handle sanctions hits appropriately." That's the only line I have.好。"系统应适当处理制裁名单命中"——这是我目前唯一一行。
You (BA): Right, "appropriately" isn't testable. What does "handle" mean? Block? Hold for review? Let through with a flag? We need to pick a behaviour and pin it down."适当地"不可测。"处理"是什么意思？拦截？挂起待审？放行但加标记？我们必须选定一种行为并写死。
Sandeep (QA): Same problem from my side. Given/When/Then, please — I want to write a test the same day this story is picked up.我这边也是同一个问题。麻烦用 Given/When/Then——我希望 Story 一开工我就能写测试。
You (BA): Agreed. Carmen, walk me through what should happen on a hard hit — say, a 100% match on the OFAC SDN list for a USD payment to a counterparty in Hong Kong.同意。Carmen，你讲讲硬命中（hard hit）应该怎么处理——比如一笔 USD 付款，对方在香港，OFAC SDN 名单 100% 命中。
Carmen (Compliance BA): Hard hit means block and escalate to Level 2 Compliance. The payment is held in the eCAB queue within 60 seconds. The relationship manager gets a OneWealth notification.硬命中就是拦截并升级到二级合规。60 秒内挂到 eCAB 队列，客户经理在 OneWealth 收到通知。
You (BA): Got it. And on a soft hit? A name match but wrong date of birth or nationality — what's the policy there?明白。软命中（soft hit）呢？名字匹配但出生日期或国籍不对——政策是？
Carmen (Compliance BA): Soft hit goes into a manual review queue — the payment is not blocked but held. SLA is 4 business hours for L1 review.软命中进人工审核队列——不拦截但挂起。一级审核 SLA 是 4 个工作小时。
Hannah (Tech Lead): Quick one — does the SLA clock start at the screening event or at the payment submission? The architecture differs.问一下——SLA 时钟从筛查事件开始算还是从付款提交开始算？架构上不一样。
You (BA): Good catch. Let's lock it as the screening event for now and call it out as an open assumption for the architecture review on Friday.好问题。我们先按筛查事件开始算，作为开放假设留到周五架构评审再确认。
You (BA): And PEP hits? If the counterparty is a PEP, do we still treat it as a soft hit, or does it escalate regardless?PEP 命中呢？如果对方是 PEP，是按软命中处理，还是无论命中强度都升级？
Carmen (Compliance BA): PEP — regardless of fuzzy-match score — always goes to manual review with a 2-hour SLA. That's a Group Compliance rule, not negotiable.PEP——无论模糊匹配分数多少——都进人工审核，SLA 2 小时。这是集团合规的硬规定，不能谈。
You (BA): OK, so we have three ACs now: hard hit (block + escalate, 60s), soft hit (manual review, 4h), PEP (manual review, 2h). I'll draft the Given/When/Then for each and share in Confluence by EOD.好，现在我们有 3 条 AC：硬命中（拦截+升级，60 秒）、软命中（人工审核，4 小时）、PEP（人工审核，2 小时）。我今天 EOD 前在 Confluence 写好每条的 Given/When/Then。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open Let's read through the AC draft line by line. 我们一行一行看 AC 草稿 Forces a literal walkthrough. "Line by line" is the antidote to vague AC.
  • Vague "Appropriately" isn't testable. "适当地"不可测 The two words to ban from AC: "appropriately" and "as needed". Replace with a number or a decision.
  • Define What does "handle" mean? "处理"是什么意思 When a verb is ambiguous, force the stakeholder to pick one of: block, hold, allow, escalate, notify.
  • Walk Walk me through what should happen on a hard hit. 你讲讲硬命中时应该怎么处理 "Walk me through" + a specific scenario is the best elicitation line for turning policy into AC.
  • Edge case And on a soft hit? 软命中呢？ Always ask for the second case after the first. Hard / soft / PEP / timeout / partial match — surface them all.
  • SLA SLA is 4 business hours for L1 review. 一级审核 SLA 是 4 个工作小时 SLAs go in AC as numbers, not adjectives. "Within 4 business hours" beats "quickly".
  • Assumption Let's lock it as the screening event for now and call it out as an open assumption. 我们先按……来，作为开放假设 When the team can't agree, capture as an assumption + owner + due date. Don't kill the workshop.
  • Rule That's a Group Compliance rule, not negotiable. 这是集团合规的硬规定，不能谈 Use "not negotiable" only for genuine hard rules. If you over-use it, people stop trusting you.
  • Lock We have three ACs now. 现在我们锁了 3 条 AC Locking = "we won't re-debate this today". A workshop productivity device.
  • Draft I'll draft the Given/When/Then and share in Confluence by EOD. 我今天 EOD 前在 Confluence 写好 Given/When/Then Always end with a concrete next step + a channel + a deadline. Avoids the "who's writing it?" stall.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Acceptance Criteria (AC) n. 验收标准 The bullet list of conditions that define "done" for a story.
  • Given/When/Then n. Gherkin 三段式 The standard AC syntax: precondition, action, expected outcome.
  • Hard hit n. 硬命中 A 100% match against the sanctions list — block-and-escalate.
  • Soft hit n. 软命中 A fuzzy / partial match — manual review.
  • PEP (Politically Exposed Person) n. 政治公众人物 A high-risk customer profile that always triggers EDD.
  • OFAC SDN list n. OFAC 制裁名单 The US Office of Foreign Assets Control sanctions database.
  • HKMA n. 香港金管局 Hong Kong Monetary Authority — local regulator.
  • Sanctions screening n. 制裁名单筛查 Real-time check of all parties against global watchlists.
  • Edge case n. 边界情况 A scenario at the boundary of normal — e.g., timeout, partial match, dual jurisdiction.
  • eCAB n. 电子合规审批 HSBC's electronic Compliance Approval Booking system.
  • Group Compliance n. 集团合规 The global compliance function — its rules override local policy.
  • Open assumption n. 开放假设 A working decision captured in the AC, awaiting validation.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "What does X mean?" — the disambiguation question

When a stakeholder uses a vague verb (handle, process, manage), your best response is "What does X mean in this context?" The question is grammatically simple — auxiliary does + subject + base verb — but politically powerful. It signals "I want to capture your intent precisely" without saying "your AC is bad".

面对"处理/管理/应对"这种模糊动词，反问 What does X mean? 比直接说"AC 不好"更得体。

② "Regardless of X" — the override clause

PEP — regardless of fuzzy-match score — always goes to manual review uses regardless of + noun to express a rule that overrides other rules. It pattern-matches perfectly to "regardless of" lines in policy docs. Use it when one factor dominates all others.

Regardless of + 名词 表达"无论……都……"，对应合规文档中常见的"压倒性条款"措辞。

③ "Within X seconds / hours / business days" — the SLA pattern

SLAs in AC are written as "within + duration": within 60 seconds, within 4 business hours, within 2 business days. Avoid "quickly" / "as soon as possible" / "promptly". The within form is measurable and ties cleanly to a Jira test assertion.

SLA 在 AC 里用 within + 时间 表达，避免 "quickly"、"as soon as possible" 这类不可测措辞。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • "Appropriately" is the enemy of testability. At HSBC, the QA chapter will reject a story if any AC line contains "appropriately", "as needed", or "TBD". As BA, you catch these before the story hits refinement — it saves a full round of ping-pong.
  • Use Group Compliance wording verbatim. If a Group Compliance rule says "regardless of fuzzy-match score", put that exact phrase in the AC. Re-paraphrasing it ("always escalate PEPs") opens a debate you can't win. Verbatim = defensible.
  • SLAs in HSBC always reference a clock start. "Within 60 seconds" means nothing without saying of what event. Capture the clock start (screening event, payment submission, alert generation) in the AC line — never leave it implicit.
  • Edge cases for sanctions: always ask the "what if the list changes mid-flight?" question. If the SDN list updates while a payment is in the queue, the AC must say what happens. Hannah and the Tech Lead will thank you for surfacing it.
  • Cross-region refinement etiquette. With Hannah in the UK, Carmen in Shanghai, and Sandeep in Bangalore, expect Sandeep to want a clear test script, Carmen to want policy words verbatim, and Hannah to want edge cases. Address all three concerns in the same refinement — don't play them sequentially.
  • "Not negotiable" is a Group Compliance phrase. BAs at HSBC are trained to use it only when the underlying rule is published at Group level. Over-using it for your own preferences erodes its power. If in doubt, say "this is the published policy — let's confirm with Group Compliance".
  • Always capture the AC in Confluence, not just Jira. Jira AC is the source of truth, but the discussion of why each AC exists belongs in a Confluence page. Carmen will look there when she audits the story six months later.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the compliance AC discussion

Translate the following compliance AC line into natural Chinese, keeping the testability focus.

"Right, 'appropriately' isn't testable. What does 'handle' mean? Block, hold, escalate, or let through with a flag? We need to pick one and pin it down. Same problem from the QA side — Given/When/Then, please. Carmen, walk me through what should happen on a hard hit: a 100% match on the OFAC SDN list for a USD payment to a counterparty in Hong Kong. SLA is 60 seconds; the clock starts at the screening event, not the payment submission."

"好，'适当地'不可测。'处理'到底是拦截、挂起、升级，还是放行加标记？我们必须选一个并写死。QA 那边也是同样的问题——麻烦用 Given/When/Then。Carmen，你讲讲硬命中应该怎么处理：OFAC SDN 名单 100% 命中，付款币种 USD，对方在香港。SLA 是 60 秒，时钟从筛查事件开始算，不是从付款提交开始算。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the weak AC

Which of these AC lines is the strongest and most testable? Why?

  1. "The system should handle sanctions hits appropriately and notify the relationship manager."
  2. "Given a payment triggers a 100% OFAC SDN match, when the screening event completes, then the payment is blocked and an eCAB case is created within 60 seconds."
  3. "The system should be fast and user-friendly."
  4. "Soft hits are reviewed quickly by Compliance."

② is the strongest. It has a specific precondition (100% OFAC match), a clear trigger (screening event), a defined outcome (block + eCAB case), and a measurable SLA (within 60 seconds). ① uses "appropriately" — vague. ③ is a non-functional claim dressed as a functional AC. ④ uses "quickly" — replace with a number. Reframe: Given a soft hit, when L1 review is requested, then the review is completed within 4 business hours.

Exercise 3 — Your turn

You're a BA on the GPB onboarding squad. The Compliance team has given you a one-line AC for a new feature: "High-risk customer profiles should trigger enhanced due diligence." Rewrite this as 3 Given/When/Then ACs covering (a) a PEP hit, (b) a high-jurisdiction-risk country, and (c) a customer with adverse media. Include a specific SLA for each. Use at least 3 patterns from the Key Phrases section.

AC 1 (PEP): Given a new GPB prospect matches a PEP list with a score above 80, when the CDD event is recorded, then an EDD case is opened in eCAB within 2 business hours and assigned to a Level 2 Compliance officer.

AC 2 (Jurisdiction): Given a prospect's country of residence is on the Group High-Risk Jurisdiction list, when the onboarding record is created, then the system flags the record for EDD and blocks account opening until Compliance clearance is recorded.

AC 3 (Adverse media): Given a prospect has 3 or more adverse-media hits in the last 12 months, when the screening refresh runs, then the RM is notified in OneWealth and an EDD review is queued within 4 business hours.

Workshop phrasing you might use: "'Trigger EDD' isn't testable on its own. What does trigger mean — automatic case, RM notification, or both? Let's pin each path down. Carmen, walk me through the PEP path first. Regardless of fuzzy-match score, is it 2 hours or 4 hours? We need to lock the SLA before refinement."
