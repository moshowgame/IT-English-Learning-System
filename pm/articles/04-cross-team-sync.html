Cross-Team Sync — Aligning Dependencies Across Squads

Article 04 · Module 1: Daily PM Communication · PM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Sequencing, handoffs, soft dependencies, cross-team asks, no-blame language

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 11:00 HKT. You are running a 45-minute cross-team sync between your project (Real-Time Payments Modernisation) and two dependent squads: the Core Banking squad and the Group Platform / Shared Services squad. On the call: Wei (Tech Lead, Core Banking, Shanghai), Rohan (PM, Group Platform, Bangalore), Yuki (Architect, Tokyo), and Catherine (PMO, London).

周三 11:00 香港时间，你在主持你的项目（实时支付现代化）和两个依赖 squad 之间的 45 分钟跨团队同步会：核心银行squad 和集团平台 / 共享服务squad。参会有：Wei（核心银行 Tech Lead，上海）、Rohan（集团平台 PM，班加罗尔）、Yuki（架构师，东京），以及 Catherine（PMO，伦敦）。

Your job in this meeting is to sequence the dependencies, lock the handoff dates, and unblock anything that crosses team boundaries. You don't command the other squads — you negotiate with them. The language is collaborative, not directive.

你在这个会议中的职责是排列依赖顺序、锁定交接日期、解除任何跨团队边界的阻塞。你不指挥其他 squad——你协商。语言是协作型，不是指令型。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Catherine, can you capture actions as we go?感谢各位。今天同步的目标：锁定我的项目和两个依赖 squad 之间的交接日期，并排列 API gateway 工作的顺序。30 分钟会，然后 15 分钟风险。Catherine，能随时记 action 吗？
You (PM): Rohan, first ask from my side: we need the mutual TLS certificate rotation policy from the Platform team by 12th, otherwise our API gateway hardening slips. Is 12th still realistic?Rohan，我方第一个 ask：我们需要 Platform 团队 12 日前提供双向 TLS 证书轮换策略，否则我们的API gateway 加固会推迟。12 日还现实吗？
Rohan (Platform PM): 12th is tight. We can do 15th if we defer the certificate pinning change. What's the impact on your side?12 日紧。如果推迟证书 pinning 变更，我们可以 15 日做。对你们影响是什么？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Goal Goal of today's sync: lock the handoff dates. 今天同步的目标：锁定交接日期 Always open a cross-team sync with the goal. Cross-team meetings drift without one.
  • First ask First ask from my side: we need X by date. 我方第一个 ask：我们需要在某日前拿到 X Numbering your asks shows you've thought about priority. Other PMs do the same.
  • Realistic? Is 12th still realistic? 12 日还现实吗？ "Realistic?" is a polite way to ask "can you really do it?" without sounding confrontational.
  • Impact What's the impact on your side? 对你们影响是什么？ Always ask the other side for impact. They may know something you don't.
  • Counter-proposal Counter-proposal: can you stagger the delivery? 反建议：能否分阶段交付？ Counter-proposal is PM gold. Don't just say no — propose an alternative that works for both.
  • Stagger Give us the policy doc on 12th and the pinning change on 15th. 12 日给策略文档，15 日给 pinning 变更 Staggered delivery is a common compromise. It keeps both sides moving without one side blocking the other.
  • Workshop Workshop Friday 14:00 to close the open questions. 周五 14:00 工作坊关闭开放问题 When async isn't enough, propose a workshop. Workshops close open questions faster than Jira comments.
  • For decision Add it to the SteerCo decisions page as 'for decision'. 加到 SteerCo 决策页作为'for decision' When you can't decide in the meeting, escalate cleanly. Tag the page, attach a one-pager.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Cross-team sync n. 跨团队同步会 A meeting to align two or more teams on shared dependencies.
  • Handoff n. 交接 The point where one team delivers an artefact to another team.
  • Sequencing n. 排序 Deciding the order in which dependent tasks are executed.
  • Soft dependency n. 软依赖 A dependency that isn't blocking today but will become blocking if unresolved.
  • Hard dependency n. 硬依赖 A dependency that is blocking work today.
  • Counter-proposal n. 反建议 An alternative offer made in response to a request that can't be met as asked.
  • Staggered delivery n. 分阶段交付 Delivering in multiple tranches instead of all at once.
  • Buffer n. 缓冲 Time built into the plan to absorb slips.
  • Contingency n. 应急储备 Backup time or budget held in reserve.
  • Idempotency n. 幂等性 The property that a duplicate request produces the same result as a single one.
  • Reconciliation n. 对账 The process of matching transactions across systems.
  • One-pager n. 一页摘要 A single-page summary attached to a SteerCo decision ask.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Is 12th still realistic?" — the polite probe

Cross-team PMs never say "you missed the deadline" or "you can't do it". They probe: Is 12th still realistic? Are you comfortable with that date? This gives the other PM room to revise without losing face.

跨团队 PM 永远不说"你错过了截止日"或"你做不了"。他们用Is 12th still realistic?来探测，给对方 PM 留出修改空间而不失面子。

② "Counter-proposal" — propose, don't just refuse

When the other side can't meet your ask, propose an alternative. Counter-proposal: can you stagger the delivery? The word "counter-proposal" itself signals collaboration — you're trying to find a way, not fight.

当对方无法满足你的 ask 时，提出一个替代方案。"Counter-proposal"这个词本身就传达了协作的信号。

③ "We lose 3 days of buffer" — quantify the impact, not the feeling

Always translate a slip into a number that the other PM will recognise: days of buffer / contingency / sprint scope / regulator date. "It's tight" is your feeling. "3 days of buffer" is a fact.

永远把延期翻译成对方 PM 能识别的数字：days of buffer / contingency / sprint scope / regulator date。"紧"是感受，"3 天缓冲"是事实。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Open with the goal, always. Cross-team meetings drift without a stated goal. The goal is the agenda.
  • Number your asks. "First ask from my side" tells the other PM you've thought about priority. They will number theirs back.
  • Probe with questions, not statements. "Is 12th still realistic?" beats "You can't make 12th". Same information, different tone.
  • Counter-proposal is the secret weapon. If you only ever say no, you become the PM nobody wants on the call. Counter-proposals keep the relationship.
  • Capture actions in the meeting, not after. "Catherine, please send the invite now" beats "I'll follow up offline". The action is captured before the room empties.
  • Quantify the trade. "We lose 3 days of buffer" is a trade the other PM can evaluate. "It's tight" is not.
  • Don't escalate in the room. Cross-team meetings are for negotiation, not escalation. Take the unresolved to SteerCo, with a one-pager.
  • PMO is your friend. Catherine captures minutes so the actions don't get lost. Use her — and acknowledge her in the room.
  • Regulator dates are the final backstop. If a cross-team debate is going in circles, anchor back to the regulator date. That usually focuses the mind.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the cross-team sync

Translate the following cross-team sync excerpt into natural Chinese.

"Goal of today's sync: lock the handoff dates between my project and the Core Banking squad, and sequence the data migration work. First ask from my side: we need the customer master schema v2 by 5th. Wei, is 5th still realistic? Wei: 5th is tight, we can do 8th. Counter-proposal: can you stagger — give us the schema on 5th and the migration mapping on 8th? That keeps us on plan. Wei: that works. I'll confirm with the data lead this afternoon and circle back by EOD. Action: workshop Wednesday 14:00 HKT, Wei / my BA / data lead. Goal: schema and mapping final by Friday EOD."

"今天同步的目标：锁定我的项目和核心银行 squad 之间的交接日期，并排列数据迁移工作。我方第一个 ask：5 日前需要客户主数据 schema v2。Wei，5 日还现实吗？Wei：5 日紧，可以 8 日。反建议：能否分阶段——5 日给 schema，8 日给迁移映射？这样我们按计划走。Wei：可以，下午和数据负责人确认，今日 EOD 前回复。Action: 周三 14:00 香港时间工作坊，Wei / 我们 BA / 数据负责人。目标：周五 EOD 前 schema 和映射定稿。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the cross-team mistake

Which line would damage the cross-team relationship? Why?

  1. "Is 12th still realistic?"
  2. "We need this by 12th, no exceptions."
  3. "Counter-proposal: can you stagger the delivery?"
  4. "What's the impact on your side?"

② is the relationship-damager. "No exceptions" is a hard command that leaves the other PM no room to negotiate. ① probes politely. ③ offers an alternative. ④ asks for impact. Cross-team PMs negotiate; they don't dictate.

Exercise 3 — Run a 5-minute cross-team check-in

You are the PM. The other PM is asking you for a deliverable by 5th that you can only do by 12th. Use: a goal, an impact statement, a counter-proposal, and an action. Write the script.

"Goal of my update: align on the data export format. From your side, you need the export by 5th. From my side, the data team has two upstream dependencies and the realistic date is 12th. Impact if we shift to 12th: you lose 1 week of testing window. Counter-proposal: we can give you a partial export on 5th (top 80% of accounts) and the full export on 12th. That keeps your integration work moving. Action: my data lead to send the partial export schema by 3rd EOD; your integration lead to confirm the 80/20 split works. Anything else?"
