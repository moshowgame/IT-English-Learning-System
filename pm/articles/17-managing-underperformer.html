Managing an Underperformer — Observable Facts, Not Judgement

Article 17 · Module 4: Difficult Conversations · PM English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 PIP language, observable facts, support plan, performance review, escalation path

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 10:00 HKT. You are the PM. One of the senior developers on the squad, Hiro, has been consistently missing sprint commitments for the past 6 weeks. Code quality is dropping — the Tech Lead has flagged 3 critical defects in his last 4 PRs. The other developers are starting to complain about pair-programming sessions. You've had two informal 1:1s with him; the behaviour hasn't changed. Today is a formal performance discussion.

周三 10:00 香港时间，你是 PM。Squad 中一名高级开发 Hiro，过去 6 周持续未完成 Sprint 承诺。代码质量在下降——Tech Lead 标记了他最近 4 个 PR 中的 3 个严重缺陷。其他开发开始抱怨结对编程。你和他开过两次非正式 1:1；行为没变。今天是正式绩效讨论。

Managing an underperformer is the PM's most uncomfortable conversation. The instinct is to avoid, soften, or hope it improves. Don't. The professional pattern is: observable facts, impact, support plan, performance plan, consequences. HR must be involved. The conversation must be documented.

管理低绩效是 PM 最不舒服的对话。本能是回避、软化或希望好转。不要。专业模式是：可观察事实、影响、支持计划、绩效计划、后果。HR 必须参与。谈话必须记录。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (PM): Hiro, thanks for making time. I've asked Priya from HR to join us as a witness. Goal of today's conversation: walk through observable facts on the past 6 weeks, agree a support plan, and put a performance plan in place.Hiro，感谢抽时间。我请了 HR 的 Priya 参加做见证人。今天谈话的目标：过过去 6 周的可观察事实，达成支持计划，并启动绩效计划。
You (PM): Let me walk the facts. Sprint commitments: over the last 6 sprints, you closed 4 of 18 committed stories. Code quality: the Tech Lead flagged 3 critical defects in your last 4 PRs. Pair-programming: two squad members have asked for reduced pairing time with you. These are observable facts in Jira and the PR review log, not personal opinions.让我过事实。Sprint 承诺：过去 6 个 Sprint，你关闭了 18 个承诺故事中的 4 个。代码质量：Tech Lead 标记了你最近 4 个 PR 中的 3 个严重缺陷。结对编程：两名 squad 成员要求减少与你的结对时间。这些是 Jira 和 PR review 日志中的可观察事实，不是个人意见。
Hiro: I understand the data. I've been going through a difficult time at home and I haven't managed my capacity well.我理解这些数据。我家里遇到了一些困难，没有管理好我的容量。
You (PM): I'm sorry to hear that. I want to support you. Two questions. One: is there anything we can put in place to help — EAP, reduced hours, time off? Two: are you able to commit to a recovery plan in the next 4 weeks?听到这些我很难过。我想支持你。两个问题。一：有没有我们可以提供帮助的事——EAP、缩短工时、休假？二：你能在未来 4 周内承诺恢复计划吗？
Hiro: EAP would help. I'll reach out this week. Yes, I can commit to the recovery plan.EAP 会帮助。我本周联系。是的，我能承诺恢复计划。
You (PM): Good. Support plan: One — EAP referral by Friday. Two — weekly 1:1 with me for 4 weeks to track progress. Three — no new scope for the next 4 weeks; you focus on closing 6 stories you can finish cleanly.好。支持计划：一——周五前 EAP 转介。二——未来 4 周和我每周 1:1跟踪进展。三——未来 4 周不加新范围；你专注于干净地关闭 6 个故事。
You (PM): Performance plan. Measurable targets over the next 4 weeks. One: close 6 stories, all with zero critical defects in PR review. Two: pair-program with at least 2 squad members per week. Three: attend all standups and sprint ceremonies. Reviewed weekly by me and the Tech Lead.绩效计划。未来 4 周的可度量目标。一：关闭 6 个故事，所有PR review 零严重缺陷。二：每周与至少 2 名 squad 成员结对。三：参加所有站会和 Sprint 仪式。每周由我和 Tech Lead review。
You (PM): Consequences. If you meet the targets at 4 weeks, we close the performance plan and return to normal cadence. If you don't meet the targets, we escalate to a formal PIP under HR policy, which has further consequences up to and including role change.后果。如果你达成目标在 4 周后，我们关闭绩效计划，恢复正常节奏。如果未达成目标，我们升级到 HR 政策下的正式 PIP，会有进一步后果，包括直到岗位变更。
Hiro: I understand. I'm committed to the plan.我理解。我承诺执行计划。
You (PM): Priya, anything to add from HR?Priya，HR 有补充吗？
Priya (HR): Two things. One: this conversation is documented in the HR system as a performance improvement discussion. Two: Hiro, you have the right to bring a colleague or union rep to future meetings. Wei will send you the support plan and performance plan in writing by EOD today.两件事。一：这次谈话在HR 系统中记录为绩效改进讨论。二：Hiro，你有权带同事或工会代表参加未来的会议。Wei 今日 EOD 前书面发出支持计划和绩效计划。
You (PM): Thank you, Hiro. Next 1:1 is Monday.谢谢你，Hiro。下次 1:1 周一。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Witness I've asked Priya from HR to join us as a witness. 我请了 HR 的 Priya 参加做见证人 Always have HR as a witness. The conversation is documented. Memory is fallible.
  • Observable facts Observable facts in Jira and the PR review log, not personal opinions. Jira 和 PR review 日志中的可观察事实，不是个人意见 "Observable facts" is HR language. It signals: the data is the data, not me vs you.
  • Impact Two squad members have asked for reduced pairing time with you. 两名 squad 成员要求减少与你的结对时间 Always include the impact on the team. Performance is not just numbers — it's also team morale.
  • Support I want to support you. Anything we can put in place to help? 我想支持你。有没有我们可以提供帮助的事？ Lead with support. PMs are not just enforcers — they also help. EAP, time off, training.
  • Measurable target Measurable targets over the next 4 weeks. 未来 4 周的可度量目标 "Measurable" is the operative word. A target without a number is an opinion.
  • Zero critical defects All with zero critical defects in PR review. 所有 PR review 零严重缺陷 "Zero" is a powerful word. It sets the bar at the level the team needs.
  • PIP Escalate to a formal PIP under HR policy. 升级到 HR 政策下的正式 PIP PIP (Performance Improvement Plan) is HR vocabulary. Use it; the consequences are formal.
  • Documented This conversation is documented in the HR system. 这次谈话在 HR 系统中记录 "Documented" is the protection for both sides. Memory fades; the system doesn't.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Underperformer n. 低绩效员工 A team member who consistently misses agreed targets.
  • Observable facts n. 可观察事实 Data that is verifiable in systems (Jira, PR logs, attendance), not opinions.
  • Support plan n. 支持计划 Actions the manager takes to help the employee (EAP, training, time off).
  • Performance plan n. 绩效计划 Measurable targets over a defined period, reviewed weekly.
  • PIP (Performance Improvement Plan) n. 绩效改进计划 A formal HR process with consequences up to and including role change.
  • EAP (Employee Assistance Programme) n. 员工援助计划 Confidential counselling and support service.
  • Witness n. 见证人 An HR partner or another manager who attends to document the conversation.
  • Critical defect n. 严重缺陷 A bug that breaks core functionality or has a security impact.
  • PR review log n. PR 评审日志 The record of pull-request comments and approvals.
  • Sprint commitment n. Sprint 承诺 The stories a team member has agreed to deliver in a sprint.
  • Cadence n. 节奏 The normal rhythm of work (standups, ceremonies, 1:1s).
  • Role change n. 岗位变更 A formal change in responsibilities, often the consequence of an unsuccessful PIP.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Observable facts in Jira and the PR review log" — name the source

When stating underperformance, always name the source. Observable facts in Jira and the PR review log. The source is what makes the fact verifiable. "I think he's underperforming" loses; "4 of 18 stories in Jira" wins.

陈述低绩效时，总是指明来源。Jira 和 PR review 日志中的可观察事实。

② "I want to support you" — lead with support, not enforcement

Open the support section with I want to support you, not You need to improve. The PM's job is to help first, enforce second. The team member who feels supported performs better than the one who feels attacked.

支持部分以 I want to support you 开头，而不是 You need to improve。

③ "Up to and including role change" — name the maximum consequence

Always name the maximum consequence. Up to and including role change. The team member needs to know the ceiling. Without it, the performance plan looks informal; with it, it's a real plan with real stakes.

始终点名最大后果。直到并包括岗位变更。 员工需要知道上限。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always have HR as a witness. The conversation is documented. Memory is fallible. The HR partner is your protection and the employee's.
  • Lead with observable facts, not opinions. "4 of 18 stories in Jira" beats "he's not delivering". The data is the data.
  • Lead with support, not enforcement. "I want to support you" comes before "you need to improve". The team member who feels supported performs better.
  • Measurable targets, every time. A target without a number is an opinion. "Close 6 stories with zero critical defects" is a target.
  • Name the consequence ceiling. "Up to and including role change" sets the maximum. The team member knows the stakes.
  • Document everything. The support plan and performance plan must be in writing. HR files a copy. Both sides sign.
  • Weekly 1:1 during the plan. Don't wait 4 weeks to find out if it's working. Weekly 1:1 with the PM catches the early signals.
  • Don't argue the personal story. If the team member has personal challenges, acknowledge and refer to EAP. Don't argue the cause in the performance discussion.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the performance discussion

Translate the performance discussion excerpt into natural Chinese.

"Hiro, thanks for making time. I've asked Priya from HR to join us as a witness. Goal: walk observable facts on the past 6 weeks, agree a support plan, and put a performance plan in place. Let me walk the facts. Sprint commitments: over the last 6 sprints, you closed 5 of 20 committed stories. Code quality: the Tech Lead flagged 4 critical defects in your last 5 PRs. Pair-programming: three squad members have asked for reduced pairing time with you. These are observable facts in Jira and the PR review log, not personal opinions. I'm sorry to hear that. I want to support you. Two questions: is there anything we can put in place to help — EAP, reduced hours, time off? Are you able to commit to a recovery plan in the next 4 weeks? Support plan: EAP referral by Friday; weekly 1:1 with me for 4 weeks; no new scope for the next 4 weeks; you focus on closing 5 stories you can finish cleanly. Performance plan: measurable targets over the next 4 weeks — close 5 stories with zero critical defects; pair-program with at least 2 squad members per week; attend all standups and sprint ceremonies. Consequences: if you meet the targets, we close the plan. If not, we escalate to a formal PIP, which has further consequences up to and including role change."

"Hiro，感谢抽时间。我请了 HR 的 Priya 做见证人。目标：过过去 6 周的可观察事实，达成支持计划，并启动绩效计划。让我过事实。Sprint 承诺：过去 6 个 Sprint，你关闭了 20 个承诺故事中的 5 个。代码质量：Tech Lead 标记了你最近 5 个 PR 中的 4 个严重缺陷。结对编程：三名 squad 成员要求减少与你的结对时间。这些是 Jira 和 PR review 日志中的可观察事实，不是个人意见。听到这些我很难过。我想支持你。两个问题：有没有我们可以帮助的事——EAP、缩短工时、休假？你能在未来 4 周承诺恢复计划吗？支持计划：周五前 EAP 转介；未来 4 周和我每周 1:1；未来 4 周不加新范围；你专注于干净地关闭 5 个故事。绩效计划：未来 4 周的可度量目标——关闭 5 个故事，PR review 零严重缺陷；每周与至少 2 名 squad 成员结对；参加所有站会和 Sprint 仪式。后果：达成目标，关闭计划。未达成，升级到正式 PIP，包括直到岗位变更。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the performance-discussion mistake

Which line would damage the conversation most? Why?

  1. "Observable facts in Jira and the PR review log, not personal opinions."
  2. "Hiro is lazy and doesn't care about the project."
  3. "I want to support you. Anything we can put in place to help?"
  4. "Measurable targets over the next 4 weeks. If you meet them, we close the plan."

② is the conversation-damager. "Lazy" and "doesn't care" are character judgements, not observable facts. They will be disputed, the conversation derails, and HR cannot defend them. ①, ③, ④ are all HR-defensible. Performance discussions run on observable facts and support, not character.

Exercise 3 — Run a 30-minute performance discussion

You are the PM. A squad member has missed 7 of 15 sprint commitments and has 3 critical defects. Build a 30-minute script: (1) witness from HR, (2) observable facts with sources, (3) support plan with EAP, (4) performance plan with 3 measurable targets, (5) consequences up to PIP / role change.

"Hiro, thanks for making time. Priya from HR is here as a witness. Goal: walk observable facts, agree a support plan, and put a performance plan in place. Sprint commitments: 7 of 15 committed stories closed. Code quality: 3 critical defects in last 4 PRs. Pair-programming: 2 squad members have asked for reduced pairing. Observable facts in Jira and PR review log, not opinions. I want to support you. Two questions: anything to help — EAP, time off, training? Can you commit to a 4-week recovery plan? Support plan: EAP referral by Friday; weekly 1:1 for 4 weeks; no new scope for 4 weeks; focus on 4 stories to close cleanly. Performance plan: 4 measurable targets over 4 weeks — close 4 stories with zero critical defects; pair-program with 2 squad members per week; attend all ceremonies; meet weekly with me and Tech Lead. Consequences: meet targets, close the plan. Don't meet, escalate to formal PIP, up to and including role change. Priya, anything from HR? Hiro, do you commit to the plan? Next 1:1 Monday."
