Resource & Capacity Planning — Closing the Demand / Supply Gap

Article 10 · Module 2: Governance & Steering · PM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 FTE, named resources, contractors, capacity vs demand, recovery options

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 15:00 HKT. You are running a 45-minute resource planning workshop for the next two quarters of the Regulatory Reporting Modernisation programme. Present: Priya (Group PMO), Steve (Group Cyber resourcing lead), Daniel (Tech Lead, Engineering), and Mark (Workstream Lead, Data Quality). The goal: lock the named resources, expose any FTE gap, and propose recovery options.

周二 15:00 香港时间，你在主持监管报送现代化项目群下两个季度的 45 分钟资源规划工作坊。参会有：Priya（集团 PMO）、Steve（集团 Cyber 资源负责人）、Daniel（工程 Tech Lead）、Mark（数据质量工作流负责人）。目标：锁定具名资源、暴露 FTE 缺口、提出恢复方案。

Resource planning is a number game: you have a demand (work to do) and a supply (people available). The PM's job is to close the gap — by adding, swapping, deferring, or contracting. The language is precise: FTE, named resource, contractor, gap, recovery option.

资源规划是数字游戏：你有需求（要做的工作）和供给（可用的人）。PM 的职责是关闭缺口——通过增加、替换、推迟或外包。语言要精确：FTE, named resource, contractor, gap, recovery option。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (PM): Thanks all. Goal of today's workshop: lock the named resources for Q3 and Q4, expose any FTE gap, and agree recovery options for the gaps we can't close.感谢各位。今天工作坊的目标：锁定Q3 和 Q4 的具名资源，暴露FTE 缺口，并对无法关闭的缺口达成恢复方案。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Demand Reporting Engine needs 8 FTE for 6 months. 报送引擎需要 6 个月 8 个 FTE Always pair FTE count with a duration. "8 FTE" alone is meaningless without a time window.
  • Supply Supply confirmed: 7 FTE, 1 short of demand. 已确认供给：7 FTE，比需求少 1 个 State supply against demand. The gap is the difference, not a feeling.
  • Gap Total gap: 3 FTE across the programme. 总缺口：项目群 3 个 FTE Always name the gap in numbers, not adjectives. "Tight" is not a gap. "3 FTE" is.
  • Recovery option Two recovery options for Cyber. Cyber 有两个恢复方案 Always present options. Don't just say "we need more people". Show how to close the gap.
  • Trade-off Defer 2 pen tests, slipping MAS by 6 weeks — not recommended. 推迟 2 个渗透测试，MAS 推迟 6 周——不推荐 Always attach the trade-off to an option. The committee chooses with eyes open.
  • Internal move Any internal move we can make? 有内部调动可以做吗？ Internal moves are cheaper than contractors. Always ask the workstream lead first.
  • Pre-cleared Already pre-cleared with HR. 已和 HR 预审 "Pre-cleared" is HR language. It means the contract is ready to issue, no further approvals.
  • SOW Issue the contractor SOW by Friday. 周五前发出外包 SOW SOW (Statement of Work) is the contract for a contractor. Always state when it will be issued.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) n. 全职等效 One full-time person, or multiple people totalling one.
  • Named resource n. 具名资源 A specific person assigned by name, not a placeholder.
  • Demand n. 需求 The work to be done, expressed in FTE and duration.
  • Supply n. 供给 The people available, expressed in FTE and duration.
  • Gap n. 缺口 The difference between demand and supply.
  • Recovery option n. 恢复方案 A way to close the gap (add, swap, defer, contract).
  • Contractor / Contract uplift n. 外包 / 合同增补 An external person brought in under a contract.
  • Internal move n. 内部调动 Moving a person from one team to another within the bank.
  • Panel n. 供应商 panel A pre-approved list of vendors for fast engagement.
  • SOW (Statement of Work) n. 工作说明书 The contract for a contractor's work.
  • Pre-cleared n. 预审 Already approved by HR or procurement, ready to issue.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "8 FTE for 6 months" — pair count with duration

An FTE count without a duration is meaningless. 8 FTE for 6 months is 4 person-years. 8 FTE is a number on a slide. Always pair count with duration.

FTE 数字没有时长没有意义。8 FTE for 6 months 是 4 人年。8 FTE 是 slide 上的数字。

② "Option A / Option B" — the two-option frame

When presenting recovery options, present two — one preferred, one trade-off. Option A: contract uplift. Option B: defer pen tests. The committee chooses. You recommend. Never present one option — that's not a choice.

呈现恢复方案时，给两个——一个推荐，一个 trade-off。Option A: 合同增补。Option B: 推迟渗透测试。

③ "Already pre-cleared" — past participle as status flag

Pre-cleared, signed off, approved, in flight, blocked — past participles are status flags. Already pre-cleared with HR tells the committee the contractor is one signature away from starting. Saves meeting time.

Pre-cleared, signed off, approved, in flight, blocked——过去分词是状态标志。Already pre-cleared with HR告诉委员会外包只差一个签字就能启动。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always pair FTE with duration. "8 FTE" is a number. "8 FTE for 6 months" is a resource. The duration is what makes it comparable across workstreams.
  • State the gap, not the feeling. "We're tight on resources" loses. "We have a 3-FTE gap across the programme" wins.
  • Present two options. Option A (preferred) and Option B (trade-off). The committee chooses. Never present one option.
  • Internal moves beat contractors. Internal moves are cheaper, faster, and carry less risk. Always ask workstream leads about internal moves before contracting.
  • Name the cost on every option. "$320K for 9 months" beats "we can get contractors". Costs let the committee choose with eyes open.
  • Pre-cleared saves meeting time. If a contractor is pre-cleared with HR, say so. The committee approves faster when the path is clear.
  • Anchor trade-offs to regulator dates. "Defer pen tests, MAS slips 6 weeks — not recommended" is the trade-off. The regulator date is the final backstop.
  • Recovery options are about closing the gap, not complaining. Always leave the workshop with named actions, owners, and dates for the chosen recovery.

Exercise 1 — Translate the resource planning workshop

Translate the following resource workshop into natural Chinese.

"Demand by workstream: Cross-Border Payments needs 6 FTE for 8 months; FX Engine needs 4 FTE for 6 months; Compliance needs 3 FTE for 12 months. Total demand: 13 FTE, average 8.7 over the year. Supply confirmed: Cross-Border Payments 5 FTE, FX Engine 4 FTE, Compliance 1 FTE — 2 short. Total gap: 3 FTE. Two recovery options for Compliance. Option A: 2 contract uplifts from the existing panel, cost $280K for 12 months. Option B: defer 2 of 4 reg reports to Q1 next year, slipping the HKMA milestone by 8 weeks — not recommended. Steve: Option A works. I'll engage the panel this week. Total recovery cost: $280K. Action: Steve to engage Compliance panel by Friday, Mark to issue SOW by next Wednesday. Anything else?"

"按工作流的需求：跨境支付需要 8 个月 6 个 FTE；FX 引擎需要 6 个月 4 个 FTE；合规需要 12 个月 3 个 FTE。总需求：13 个 FTE，年均 8.7。已确认供给：跨境支付 5 FTE，FX 引擎 4 FTE，合规 1 FTE——少 2 个。总缺口：3 个 FTE。合规有两个恢复方案。方案 A：从现有 panel 增补 2 个合同，12 个月 28 万美元。方案 B：4 个监管报告中推迟 2 个到明年 Q1，HKMA 里程碑推迟 8 周——不推荐。Steve：方案 A 可行。本周启动 panel。总恢复成本：28 万美元。Action：Steve 周五前启动合规 panel，Mark 下周三前发出 SOW。还有别的吗？"

Exercise 2 — Spot the resource mistake

Which line would lose credibility in a resource workshop? Why?

  1. "Total gap: 3 FTE across the programme."
  2. "We need more people, this is unsustainable."
  3. "Option A: contract uplift, $320K. Option B: defer 2 pen tests, MAS slips 6 weeks."
  4. "Total recovery cost: $500K, three contractors."

② is the credibility-damager. "We need more people" is a complaint, not a resource plan. ① states the gap in numbers. ③ presents two options with trade-offs. ④ names the cost. Resource workshops run on numbers and options, not feelings.
