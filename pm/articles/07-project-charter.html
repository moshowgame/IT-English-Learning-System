Project Charter & Scope Sign-off — Framing What Is In and Out

Article 07 · Module 2: Governance & Steering · PM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Objectives, in/out of scope, success criteria, MoSCoW, sign-off language

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 10:00 HKT. You are the newly-appointed PM for the Real-Time Cross-Border Payments project. The kick-off workshop has just produced a draft charter. You are now walking the charter through the Sponsor and key stakeholders for sign-off. In the room: Margaret (Sponsor, MD of Payments Technology), Steven (Head of Operations, Payments, Hong Kong), Raj (Head of Compliance, UK), and Karen (PMO, London).

周一 10:00 香港时间，你是 实时跨境支付项目新任命的 PM。Kick-off 工作坊刚产出章程草案。现在你正向 Sponsor 和关键 stakeholder 走章程的签字流程。在场的有：Margaret（Sponsor，支付技术部 MD）、Steven（支付运营负责人，香港）、Raj（合规负责人，英国）、Karen（PMO，伦敦）。

A charter is a boundary document: it defines what's in, what's out, who decides, what success looks like, and how scope changes are handled. Your job in this meeting is to get the sponsor to sign — which means making the trade-offs explicit, not hiding them.

章程是边界文档：定义什么在内、什么在外、谁决策、什么是成功、范围变更如何处理。你在这个会议的职责是让 sponsor 签字——意味着让 trade-off 显式化，而不是隐藏。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open Goal of today's session: walk the draft charter and close the open points. 今天会议的目标：过章程草案，关闭开放点 Open with the goal. Charter workshops drift without a goal.
  • In scope In scope: 14 corridors, 4 currencies, STP rate target 95%. 范围内：14 条 corridor，4 个币种，STP 率目标 95% "In scope" must be specific. Corridors, currencies, percentages — not "global payments".
  • Out of scope Out of scope: FX optimisation, sanctions screening rework, retail customer onboarding. 范围外：FX 优化、制裁筛查重构、零售客户开户 Saying "out of scope" is what protects the project from scope creep later. Write it down.
  • Flag I want to flag — Operations had asked for FX optimisation. 我想提一下——运营之前要求把 FX 优化放进来 "Flag" is the polite word. Stakeholders use it to surface conflicts without creating them.
  • Explicit Reconciliation breaks — I want that explicit. 对账差错——我希望明确写出来 "Explicit" means "in writing, with a number". MDs always want the implicit made explicit.
  • Success criteria Five criteria, each with a measurable target and an owner. 五项标准，每项有可度量目标和 owner Success without a number is opinion. Always pair the criterion with a number and an owner.
  • Change control Any scope change over $100K or impacting a regulator milestone goes to SteerCo. 任何超过 10 万美元或影响监管里程碑的变更提 SteerCo "Change control" is the rule that prevents scope creep. Always state the threshold and the destination.
  • Sign-off I'll sign the charter by Friday once Karen circulates the updated version. Karen 发出更新版后，我周五前签 Sign-off is always conditional. The sponsor signs after the document is updated. Don't push for an in-room signature.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Project Charter n. 项目章程 The boundary document that defines objectives, scope, success criteria, governance.
  • Scope n. 范围 The set of features, corridors, capabilities the project will deliver.
  • In scope / Out of scope n. 范围内 / 范围外 Explicit lists of what the project will and will not do.
  • Success criteria n. 成功标准 Measurable conditions that define project success.
  • Measurable target n. 可度量目标 A success criterion expressed as a number, percentage, or date.
  • Owner n. 负责人 The single person accountable for a criterion or action.
  • Scope change control n. 范围变更控制 The governance process for adding, removing, or changing scope.
  • Threshold n. 阈值 The dollar or impact level at which a change must be escalated.
  • Sign-off n. 签字 Formal approval from a named authority, usually documented in writing.
  • STP (Straight-Through Processing) n. 直通处理率 The percentage of transactions completed without manual intervention.
  • Phase 2 n. 二期 The follow-on project that delivers deferred scope.
  • Budget envelope n. 预算包 A reserved amount of money for a specific future purpose.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "In scope / Out of scope" — the parallel structure

Always present the lists in parallel: In scope: A, B, C. Out of scope: X, Y, Z. The parallel structure makes the boundary visible and prevents arguments later. If a stakeholder asks "is D in scope?", the answer is in the list.

始终平行列出：In scope: A, B, C. Out of scope: X, Y, Z. 平行结构让边界可见。

② "Each with a measurable target and an owner" — the success-criteria pair

Success criteria always come as a pair: target + owner. A criterion without a number is opinion. A criterion without an owner is drift. Train yourself to always emit both.

成功标准总是成对出现：target + owner。没有数字的标准是意见，没有 owner 的标准是漂移。

③ "Once Karen circulates" — the conditional sign-off

Sponsor sign-off is almost always conditional. Once Karen circulates the updated version, I'll sign. The conditional structure acknowledges that the document is still moving, and lets the sponsor sign without re-discussion. Don't push for an in-room signature.

Sponsor 签字几乎都是有条件的。Once Karen circulates the updated version, I'll sign. 条件结构承认文档仍在变动。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • A charter is a boundary document, not a wish list. Its job is to say "no" as much as "yes". If everything is in scope, nothing is in scope.
  • "Out of scope" is the project saver. Without it, every new ask becomes "is this in scope?" arguments for the next 12 months. Write it down.
  • Make the implicit explicit. MDs always want success criteria written as numbers. "Reduce breaks" loses; "reduce breaks from 3.2% to 0.64%" wins.
  • Phase 2 is a valid answer. When a stakeholder asks for something you can't fit in Phase 1, propose Phase 2 with a budget envelope. It's a "yes" that protects the plan.
  • Change control prevents scope creep. Set the threshold in dollars and the destination in governance. "Any change over $100K or impacting a regulator milestone goes to SteerCo."
  • Conditional sign-off is professional. Don't push for a signature in the room. Let the sponsor sign after the document is updated. The conditional sentence preserves both the relationship and the document.
  • Every action has an owner and a due date. The charter workshop ends with action count: "Four actions: Karen by Wednesday, Margaret by Friday, Raj tomorrow, Steven tomorrow."
  • Stakeholders who "flag" are saving you. A stakeholder who flags a conflict in the workshop is a friend. Thank them, capture it, and resolve it on the spot.

Exercise 2 — Spot the charter mistake

Which charter statement is most risky? Why?

  • "Improve the payment experience for our customers."
  • "Reduce reconciliation breaks from 3.2% to 0.64% by Q4 next year, owned by Steven."
  • "Deliver real-time payments across key corridors."
  • "Support the bank's digital transformation agenda."

    • ①, ③, and ④ are risky — they have no numbers, no owners, no dates. ② has all three. A charter is a contract. Vague objectives in a charter become vague deliverables in 12 months. Always write a number, an owner, and a date.

In scope: 4 list providers, 14 sanctions regimes, 1 unified workflow. Out of scope: transaction monitoring, KYC refresh, name-matching ML model. Section 3 — success criteria. False-positive rate ≤ 3%, owner Mei. Screening latency p95 ≤ 300ms, owner Daniel. 99.9% availability, owner Operations. Zero regulator findings, owner Compliance. Section 4 — change control.
