Banking PM Terminology — Speak the Group PMO's Language

Article 19 · Module 5: Banking Context · PM English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 BAU vs project, Capex/Opex, RAG, RAID, P50/P80, MOC, Gating, NPV, IRR

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is 09:30 GMT. You are the PM of the Core Banking Modernisation programme, with a budget of USD 12M and three workstreams. You are walking into a Group PMO checkpoint with Margaret, the Group Portfolio Director (London), and Daniel, the Group Finance Business Partner. They will ask you questions in Group Standards language — BAU vs project, Capex/Opex split, RAG, RAID, P50/P80 forecast, gating, MOC, NPV. You must answer in the same language or risk your project being de-prioritised at portfolio review.

上午 9:30 格林威治时间，你是 核心银行现代化项目的 PM，预算 1200 万美元，三个工作流。你要走进 Group PMO 检查点，与集团 Portfolio 总监 Margaret（伦敦）和集团 Finance 业务伙伴 Daniel 对话。他们会用 集团标准语言提问——BAU vs 项目、Capex/Opex 拆分、RAG、RAID、P50/P80 预测、Gating、MOC、NPV。你必须用同样语言回答，否则项目可能在组合评审中被降级。

Banking PMs do not live in Agile / sprint language only. They also live in Group PMO language — financial vocabulary, governance vocabulary, and risk vocabulary. This article gives you the dictionary and shows it used in a real Group PMO checkpoint.

银行 PM 不仅仅活在 Agile / Sprint语言里，更活在 Group PMO语言里——财务词汇、治理词汇、风险词汇。本文给你词典，并展示真实 Group PMO 检查点中的用法。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Margaret (Group PMO): Wei, walk me through the programme. What's the current RAG status, and where are you against plan?Wei，给我过一下项目。当前 RAG 是什么？和计划比进度如何？
You (PM): Sure, Margaret. Overall RAG is amber. We are on plan in workstream 1 (data migration), on plan in workstream 2 (channel integration), and amber in workstream 3 (regulatory reporting) — two milestones slipped by 3 weeks against the HKMA remediation plan. Root cause: the MAS TRM evidence pack took longer than scoped. Mitigation in place.好的 Margaret。整体 RAG 琥珀色。工作流 1 按计划（数据迁移），工作流 2 按计划（渠道集成），工作流 3 琥珀色（监管报告）——两个里程碑比 HKMA 整改计划晚 3 周。根因：MAS TRM 证据包比范围耗时更久。缓解措施已就位。
Daniel (Finance BP): And the financial position? Capex versus Opex split, and the P50/P80 forecast to gate 4?财务情况呢？Capex 对 Opex 拆分，以及到 Gate 4 的 P50/P80 预测？
You (PM): Daniel, the original budget was USD 12M — 70% Capex, 30% Opex. P50 forecast to gate 4 is USD 12.4M — 3% over plan, mostly from the MAS TRM work. P80 forecast is USD 13.1M — 9% over plan. Variance drivers: the third-party risk assessment cost an extra USD 280k, and the vendor SOW change added USD 110k. Both approved by SteerCo last month.Daniel，原预算 1200 万美元——70% Capex，30% Opex。P50 预测到 Gate 4 是 1240 万——比计划高 3%，主要来自 MAS TRM 工作。P80 预测 1310 万——比计划高 9%。差异驱动：三方风险评估额外 28 万美元，厂商 SOW 变更增加 11 万美元。两项上月 SteerCo 已批准。
Daniel (Finance BP): NPV and IRR are still inside the original case?NPV 和 IRR 还在原案范围内吗？
You (PM): Yes. NPV remains USD 38M at the Group hurdle rate of 9%. IRR is 14% — 300 bps above the 11% portfolio benchmark. Both inside the original case. Even at P80 cost, the NPV is still USD 35M, so the case is robust.是。NPV 在集团 9% 门槛利率下仍是 3800 万美元。IRR 14%——比 11% 组合基准高 300 bps。两项都在原案范围内。即使按 P80 成本，NPV 仍有 3500 万美元，所以方案是稳健的。
Margaret (Group PMO): What about the RAID log? Top three risks, please.RAID log 呢？前三大风险。
You (PM): Top three risks, ranked by exposure. R1: HKMA remediation milestone slips by 4 weeks — probability medium, impact high. Mitigation: pull forward the UAT cycle, escalate to Risk SteerCo. R2: vendor dependency on the data migration tooling — single point of failure, contract renewal Q4. Mitigation: identify a backup vendor, build a 2-week exit plan. R3: resource attrition in workstream 3 — two BA contractors end in October. Mitigation: extend one, replace one, locked in by 30 Sep.前三大风险，按敞口排序。R1：HKMA 整改里程碑推迟 4 周——概率中，影响高。缓解：前移 UAT 周期，升级到 Risk SteerCo。R2：数据迁移工具的厂商依赖——单点故障，合同 Q4 续签。缓解：识别备用厂商，建 2 周退出计划。R3：工作流 3 资源流失——两个 BA 合同工 10 月到期。缓解：续一个、换一个，9 月 30 日前锁定。
Margaret (Group PMO): Last question — gating. Are you still on track for Gate 4 on 28 Feb? And is there an MOC pending?最后一个问题——gating。2 月 28 日 Gate 4 还能按计划吗？有没有 MOC 待批？
You (PM): Gate 4 is amber. We're forecast 2 weeks late — new date 14 March. That triggers an MOC — Management of Change request to the Gate 4 date. I've drafted it, SteerCo endorses next Tuesday, Group PMO reviews on 20 March. No scope change, no budget change, only the date. All in the pack.Gate 4 琥珀色。预测推迟 2 周——新日期 3 月 14 日。这会触发 MOC——针对 Gate 4 日期的管理变更请求。我已起草，SteerCo 下周二背书，Group PMO 3 月 20 日评审。无范围变更、无预算变更，只改日期。全在材料里。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • RAG Overall RAG is amber. 整体 RAG 琥珀色 Red / Amber / Green. Group PMO lives in traffic-light language. Always lead with your overall RAG.
  • Variance driver Variance drivers: third-party risk assessment added USD 280k. 差异驱动：三方风险评估增加 28 万美元 Always name the variance drivers. "Over plan" is not enough. Group Finance wants the named reason.
  • P50 / P80 P50 forecast is USD 12.4M, P80 is USD 13.1M. P50 预测 1240 万，P80 预测 1310 万 P50 = 50% probability of coming in at or below. P80 = 80%. Group PMO always asks for both.
  • NPV / IRR NPV remains USD 38M at the 9% hurdle rate; IRR is 14%. NPV 9% 门槛下 3800 万；IRR 14% NPV = Net Present Value. IRR = Internal Rate of Return. Always quote at the Group hurdle rate.
  • Ranked by exposure Top three risks, ranked by exposure. 前三大风险，按敞口排序 Always rank by exposure (probability × impact), not by chronology. Group PMO wants the worst first.
  • Single point of failure Vendor dependency is a single point of failure. 厂商依赖是单点故障 SPOF is the worst kind of risk. Always name a SPOF explicitly and propose a backup.
  • Gating Gate 4 is amber, forecast 14 March. Gate 4 琥珀色，预测 3 月 14 日 Gates are formal stage approvals. Missing a gate is a major event; an MOC may be needed.
  • MOC That triggers an MOC — Management of Change to the Gate 4 date. 这会触发 MOC——针对 Gate 4 日期的管理变更 MOC = Management of Change. Use it when a committed date, scope, or budget moves. MOC ≠ informal fix.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • BAU (Business as Usual) n. 日常运营 Ongoing operational work — funded by Opex, not Capex.
  • Project n. 项目 Time-bound, funded work to deliver a defined outcome — funded by Capex or Opex.
  • Capex (Capital Expenditure) n. 资本支出 Spend on assets that deliver value over multiple years.
  • Opex (Operational Expenditure) n. 运营支出 Spend on running the business day-to-day.
  • RAG status n. 红黄绿状态 Red / Amber / Green overall project health.
  • RAID log n. 风险日志 Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies — one consolidated log.
  • P50 / P80 n. 50%/80% 置信预测 P50 = 50% confidence forecast; P80 = 80% confidence.
  • NPV (Net Present Value) n. 净现值 The present value of future cash flows, discounted at the hurdle rate.
  • IRR (Internal Rate of Return) n. 内部收益率 The discount rate that makes NPV = 0.
  • Hurdle rate n. 门槛利率 Group's minimum acceptable rate of return on investment.
  • Gating / Gate review n. 阶段门评审 Formal approval to move from one project stage to the next.
  • MOC (Management of Change) n. 管理变更 Formal change request to a committed date, scope, or budget.
  • Variance driver n. 差异驱动 The named reason a forecast differs from plan.
  • SPOF (Single Point of Failure) n. 单点故障 A dependency that, if it fails, brings the whole workstream down.
  • SOW (Statement of Work) n. 工作说明书 The contracted scope of vendor work.
  • SteerCo (Steering Committee) n. 指导委员会 Senior forum that endorses major decisions and escalations.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "RAG is amber" — state the headline first

Open every Group PMO checkpoint with the RAG. Overall RAG is amber. Then break it down by workstream. The RAG is the single thing the Group PMO remembers; the workstream detail is for the pack.

每次 Group PMO 检查点都用 RAG 开头。整体 RAG 琥珀色。然后按工作流拆解。RAG 是 Group PMO 唯一记住的；工作流细节在材料里。

② "P50 is X, P80 is Y" — quote both numbers, always

Group PMO always asks for both P50 and P80. P50 is USD 12.4M, P80 is USD 13.1M. Quoting only one signals you don't understand probabilistic forecasting.

Group PMO 总要 P50 和 P80 两个数。P50 是 1240 万，P80 是 1310 万。只报一个说明你不懂概率预测。

③ "Ranked by exposure" — order by impact, not chronology

Always rank risks by exposure (probability × impact), not by date order. Top three risks, ranked by exposure. Group PMO wants the worst first; the order is the message.

风险总按敞口（概率 × 影响）排序，不是按日期。前三大风险，按敞口排序。Group PMO 要最坏先看，顺序本身就是信息。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Speak Group PMO's language at Group PMO. RAG, RAID, P50/P80, NPV, IRR, MOC, gating — these are the words. Use them, or your project reads as "outsourced Agile".
  • Always lead with the overall RAG. Group PMO doesn't have time to read your pack first. Lead with the headline.
  • Variance drivers must be named. "Over plan" is not a driver. The MAS TRM evidence pack took longer than scoped is a driver. Name the reason.
  • Quote both P50 and P80. P50 alone is over-confident. P80 alone is over-cautious. Both together is professional.
  • NPV / IRR at the Group hurdle rate. Always quote at the Group rate, not at a project-internal rate. The Group rate is the only one that matters.
  • MOC for any committed-date move. Don't slip a date quietly. Raise an MOC. The MOC is your friend — it documents the slip and the mitigation.
  • SPOFs are the worst risks. Always name a single point of failure explicitly. Group PMO respects a PM who calls out a SPOF.
  • RAID log is one log, not four. Many PMs keep separate lists. Group PMO wants one consolidated log with the same columns.
  • Bring the pack, but speak the headlines. The pack supports; the spoken headline is what Group PMO remembers.

Exercise 2 — Spot the Group-PMO language mistake

Which line would weaken the project at Group PMO the most? Why?

  1. "Overall RAG is amber. We're 3% over plan at P50."
  2. "We slipped the milestone because the team was busy."
  3. "Top three risks, ranked by exposure. R1 is the HKMA remediation milestone, probability medium, impact high."
  4. "The MOC is drafted, SteerCo endorses next Tuesday, Group PMO reviews 20 March."

② is the project-weaker. "The team was busy" is not a variance driver — it's a hand-wave. Group PMO wants the named reason: the MAS TRM evidence pack took longer than scoped. ① leads with RAG and P50. ③ ranks by exposure. ④ names the MOC path. ② damages the case because it doesn't name a driver.

Exercise 3 — Run a 10-minute Group PMO checkpoint

You are the PM of a 3-workstream regulatory programme, budget USD 8M, 60% Capex, 40% Opex. Workstream 1 green, workstream 2 amber (resource attrition), workstream 3 red (vendor delay). Build a 10-minute script: (1) overall RAG, (2) workstream detail, (3) financial position, (4) top 3 risks ranked by exposure, (5) gating and MOC.

P50 to Gate 3 is USD 8.2M, P80 is USD 8.7M. Variance drivers: vendor SOW change 110k, resource backfill 90k. NPV remains USD 22M at 9% hurdle, IRR 13%. Top three risks, ranked by exposure. R1: workstream 3 vendor delay, probability high, impact high — mitigation: dual-track the SOW, exit plan in place. R2: workstream 2 attrition, probability medium, impact medium — mitigation: replace + extend. R3: HKMA remediation milestone, probability medium, impact high — mitigation: pull forward UAT, escalate to Risk SteerCo.
