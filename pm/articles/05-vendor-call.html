Vendor / Third-Party Call — Holding a Vendor Accountable

Article 05 · Module 1: Daily PM Communication · PM English

⏱ 9 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 SLA language, escalation paths, contract references, polite firmness

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 16:00 HKT. You are running a 30-minute vendor progress call with AcmeTech, the third-party provider of the sanctions screening engine. They have missed two consecutive weekly milestones. The contract carries a Service Level Agreement (SLA) with credits for missed deliveries. On the call: Sam (Vendor Engagement Manager, AcmeTech, London), Hina (Vendor Technical Lead, AcmeTech, Bangalore), and from your side Daniel (your Tech Lead, London) and Mei (your Compliance Lead, Hong Kong).

周四 16:00 香港时间，你在主持和 AcmeTech（制裁名单筛查引擎的第三方厂商）的 30 分钟厂商进度会。他们连续两周错过里程碑。合同里有 SLA（服务水平协议）和错过交付的 credit 条款。参会有：Sam（AcmeTech 厂商对接经理，伦敦）、Hina（AcmeTech 厂商技术负责人，班加罗尔），你这边的 Daniel（你的 Tech Lead，伦敦）和 Mei（你的合规负责人，香港）。

Your job is to hold the vendor accountable, get a recovery plan, and protect your regulator date. The tone is firm but professional. The bank is the customer — but AcmeTech is also a long-term partner. Both matter.

你的职责是让厂商负责、拿到恢复计划、保护监管日期。语气坚定但专业。银行是客户——但 AcmeTech 也是长期合作伙伴。两者都重要。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (PM): Thanks for joining, Sam, Hina. Goal of today's call: walk the two missed milestones, agree a recovery plan, and protect our 14th OFAC milestone. 30 minutes, hard stop.感谢参加，Sam，Hina。今天会议的目标：过两个错过的里程碑，达成恢复计划，并保护我们 14 号的 OFAC 里程碑。30 分钟，到点停。
You (PM): Sam, looking at the dashboard: M3 build was due 28th, delivered 4 days late. M4 SIT entry was due Monday, still not delivered. Both are now in breach of the SLA in the master agreement, section 4.2. I'd like to walk through the root cause first, then the recovery plan.Sam，看 dashboard：M3 构建应 28 日交付，晚了 4 天。M4 SIT 进入应周一交付，仍未交付。两项都违反了主协议 SLA 第 4.2 节。想先过根因，再过恢复计划。
Sam (Vendor): Understood. Root cause is twofold: a key engineer left in late August, and the API schema change took longer than estimated. We've backfilled the role and added a second dev.明白。根因有两点：8 月底一名核心工程师离职，API schema 变更比预期时间长。我们已补齐该岗位，并增加一名开发。
You (PM): Acknowledged. Hina, what's the recovery plan for M4? We need it no later than Friday 18:00 HKT for our M5 UAT to start on Monday.收到。Hina，M4 的恢复计划是什么？我们需要不晚于周五 18:00 香港时间拿到，让我们的 M5 UAT 周一启动。
Hina (Vendor TL): Confirmed — M4 will land Friday 18:00 HKT. The second dev is already on the work. Mei, can you join a 30-minute hand-off call tomorrow afternoon to walk the API changes?确认——M4 周五 18:00 香港时间交付。第二位开发已经在做。Mei，明天下午能参加 30 分钟交接会过 API 变更吗？
You (PM): Mei?Mei？
Mei: Yes, tomorrow 15:00 HKT works.可以，明天下午 15:00 香港时间。
You (PM): Good. Two things I need to flag formally. First, under SLA section 4.2, we will be applying service credits for both missed milestones. Sam, you'll receive the credit note from procurement. Second, if M4 misses Friday 18:00 HKT, we will escalate to the executive sponsor at AcmeTech — that's a contractual escalation path, not personal.好。两件事我要正式提一下。第一，根据SLA 4.2 节，我们将对两个错过的里程碑应用 service credits。Sam，你会收到采购的 credit note。第二，如果 M4 错过周五 18:00 香港时间，我们将升级到 AcmeTech 的执行 Sponsor——这是合同里的升级路径，不是针对个人。
Sam: Understood. We will hit Friday 18:00 HKT.明白。我们一定赶上周五 18:00 香港时间。
You (PM): Good. Actions: Hina to deliver M4 by Friday 18:00 HKT; Mei and Hina hand-off call tomorrow 15:00; Sam to acknowledge SLA credit note by EOD Friday. Anything else?好。Action：Hina 周五 18:00 香港时间前交付 M4；Mei 和 Hina 明天 15:00 交接会；Sam 周五 EOD 前确认 SLA credit note。还有别的吗？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Goal Goal of today's call: walk the missed milestones and agree a recovery plan. 今天会议的目标：过错过的里程碑，达成恢复计划 Open with a goal. Vendor calls drift without one. Hard stop time keeps it tight.
  • In breach Both are now in breach of the SLA, section 4.2. 两项都违反了 SLA 第 4.2 节 "In breach" is contract language. Cite the section. It signals you have read the contract.
  • Root cause Root cause is twofold: a key engineer left, and the API change took longer. 根因有两点：核心工程师离职，API 变更比预期长 Force the vendor to articulate root cause, not excuses. Acceptable reasons have an action.
  • Recovery plan What's the recovery plan for M4? M4 的恢复计划是什么？ "Recovery plan" is contract vocabulary. The vendor should be ready to present one before you ask.
  • No later than We need it no later than Friday 18:00 HKT. 我们需要不晚于周五 18:00 香港时间 "No later than" is a hard contract term. The vendor can't say "we'll try".
  • Service credits We will be applying service credits for both missed milestones. 我们将对两个错过的里程碑应用 service credits Service credits are a contractual right, not a punishment. Use them as the language of accountability.
  • Contractual That's a contractual escalation path, not personal. 这是合同里的升级路径，不是针对个人 "Contractual" de-escalates the relationship. You're invoking a clause, not attacking a person.
  • Acknowledged Sam to acknowledge SLA credit note by EOD Friday. Sam 周五 EOD 前确认 SLA credit note "Acknowledge" is a soft word but a hard action. It commits the vendor in writing.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Vendor n. 厂商 A third-party company providing services or products under a contract.
  • SLA (Service Level Agreement) n. 服务水平协议 Contract clause defining service standards and remedies.
  • Service credits n. 服务积分 Financial compensation owed by the vendor for missing SLA targets.
  • Master agreement n. 主协议 The umbrella contract between the bank and the vendor.
  • Section reference n. 条款引用 Citing a specific clause in a contract (e.g., "section 4.2").
  • Breach n. 违反 Failure to meet a contractual obligation.
  • Recovery plan n. 恢复计划 The vendor's plan to bring a slipped deliverable back on track.
  • Hand-off n. 交接 The transfer of an artefact or knowledge from one party to another.
  • Contractual escalation path n. 合同升级路径 The formal steps defined in the contract for raising issues.
  • Backfill n. 补员 Replacing a person who has left the team.
  • Executive sponsor n. 执行 Sponsor The senior executive accountable for the vendor relationship.
  • Credit note n. 信用票据 A formal document from the vendor acknowledging the credit owed.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "In breach of the SLA, section 4.2" — cite the contract

Vendor accountability is a contract conversation. Always cite the specific clause. In breach of the SLA, section 4.2. This signals you have read the contract — and that the vendor cannot argue.

厂商负责是合同对话。始终引用具体条款。In breach of the SLA, section 4.2.

② "We will be applying service credits" — passive + future continuous

Use the passive will be applying to depersonalise the action. "We will deduct credits" sounds like you; "we will be applying credits" sounds like the contract. Same money, different tone.

用被动will be applying让动作去人格化。"We will deduct credits"听起来像你；"we will be applying credits"听起来像合同。

③ "Contractual, not personal" — the de-escalation sentence

When you must escalate, the word contractual de-escalates the relationship. "This is a contractual escalation path" means "the contract says so, not me". Same action, but the vendor can't take it personally.

当必须升级时，contractual一词能让关系降温。"This is a contractual escalation path"意思是"合同说的，不是我"。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • The bank is the customer. The vendor is the partner. Hold both. Firm on deliverables, respectful in tone. The relationship outlives the project.
  • Cite the contract, not your feelings. "In breach of section 4.2" is harder to argue than "we're disappointed".
  • "No later than" is a hard contract term. Use it. It commits the vendor to a specific date and time, not "soon".
  • Service credits are a right, not a punishment. Apply them as the contract requires. Refusing to apply them signals weakness and sets a bad precedent.
  • Always close with actions, owners, dates. Vendor calls that end with "we'll get back to you" are wasted. End with: who, what, by when.
  • Document the call. Send minutes within 24 hours. The vendor can't argue with a written record.
  • Don't make it personal. "That's a contractual escalation path, not personal" is the sentence that saves relationships.
  • Know your own escalation chain too. If M4 slips again, where does it go? AcmeTech's exec sponsor, your procurement, your SteerCo. Have the path ready.
  • Regulator-tied dates are the final backstop. If the vendor doesn't deliver by Friday, you cannot hit the regulator date. That's the line — be ready to escalate.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the vendor call

Translate the following vendor call excerpt into natural Chinese.

"Sam, looking at the dashboard: M3 was due 28th, delivered 5 days late. M4 SIT entry was due Monday, still not delivered. Both are in breach of the SLA, section 4.2. I'd like to walk the root cause first, then the recovery plan. Sam: Root cause is a key engineer on paternity leave and a schema change took longer than estimated. Hina, what's the recovery plan for M4? Hina: M4 will land Wednesday 18:00 HKT. The second dev is already on the work. Two things I need to flag formally. First, under SLA section 4.2, we will be applying service credits for both missed milestones. Second, if M4 misses Wednesday 18:00, we will escalate to the executive sponsor at AcmeTech — that's a contractual escalation path, not personal. Acknowledged. Actions: Hina to deliver M4 by Wednesday 18:00 HKT, Sam to acknowledge SLA credit note by EOD Friday."

"Sam，看 dashboard：M3 应 28 日交付，晚了 5 天。M4 SIT 进入应周一交付，仍未交付。两项都违反 SLA 第 4.2 节。想先过根因，再过恢复计划。Sam：根因是一名核心工程师在陪产假，schema 变更比预期长。Hina，M4 恢复计划是什么？Hina：M4 周三 18:00 香港时间交付。第二位开发已经在做。两件事我要正式提一下。第一，根据 SLA 第 4.2 节，我们对两个错过的里程碑应用 service credits。第二，如果 M4 错过周三 18:00，我们升级到 AcmeTech 的执行 Sponsor——这是合同里的升级路径，不是针对个人。明白。Action：Hina 周三 18:00 香港时间前交付 M4，Sam 周五 EOD 前确认 SLA credit note。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the tone mistake

Which line damages the relationship the most? Why?

  1. "In breach of SLA section 4.2."
  2. "You guys keep missing the dates — this is unacceptable."
  3. "We will be applying service credits under section 4.2."
  4. "That's a contractual escalation path, not personal."

② is the tone-damager. "You guys" is accusatory; "unacceptable" is emotional. ① and ③ cite the contract. ④ de-escalates. Hold vendors accountable in the language of the contract, not feelings.

Exercise 3 — Run a vendor escalation

You are the PM. The vendor has missed M3 by 5 days. Write a 60-second script that: (1) cites the SLA clause, (2) asks for root cause, (3) asks for a recovery plan, (4) flags service credits, (5) flags the contractual escalation path if M4 slips, (6) closes with named actions.

"Sam, M3 was due 28th, delivered 5 days late. That is in breach of SLA section 4.2 of the master agreement. Two questions. First: what is the root cause? Second: what is the recovery plan for M4? We need M4 no later than Friday 18:00 HKT or we cannot start our M5 UAT. Two things to flag formally. First, under section 4.2, we will be applying service credits for the M3 slip — you'll receive the credit note from procurement. Second, if M4 misses Friday 18:00 HKT, we will escalate to your executive sponsor per the contractual escalation path. That's not personal — that's the contract. Actions: root cause and recovery plan by EOD today, M4 delivery by Friday 18:00 HKT, your written acknowledgement of the credit note by EOD Friday. Anything else?"
