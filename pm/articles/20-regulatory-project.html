Regulatory Project Communication — HKMA, MAS, FCA, GDPR

Article 20 · Module 5: Banking Context · PM English

⏱ 10 min read 🔴 Must-Read for Bank PMs 🎯 Regulator demos, remediation plans, attestation language, evidence packs, supervisory letters

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is 14:00 HKT. You are the PM of the HKMA Resolution Planning programme — a Tier 1 regulatory commitment with a binding milestone of 30 September. You are on a Regulator Readiness Call with Karen, the Head of Regulatory Compliance (HK), and David, the Group Legal Director. The HKMA has just sent a supervisory letter asking for three remediation actions by the milestone, plus a demonstration of the new resolution playbook to the Joint Supervisory Team on 15 September. You have 60 minutes to walk the readiness, ask for the right support, and confirm the evidence pack.

下午 14:00 香港时间，你是 HKMA 处置规划项目的 PM——一级监管承诺，里程碑 9 月 30 日刚性。你正在参加 Regulator Readiness Call，与香港监管合规负责人 Karen 和集团法务总监 David 通话。HKMA刚发了 监管函，要求三项整改措施在里程碑前完成，并在 9 月 15 日向联合监管组演示新版处置预案。你有 60 分钟过完就绪度、请求支持、确认证据包。

Regulatory projects are the most political work a bank PM does. The language is precise, the evidence is auditable, and the dates are binding. Saying "we'll try" is not an option. This article shows you the language of regulatory PM — supervisor, supervisory letter, remediation, attestation, evidence pack, joint supervisory team.

监管项目是银行 PM 最政治化的工作。语言精确、证据可审、日期刚性。"我们会试一下"不是选项。本文展示监管 PM 语言——supervisor、supervisory letter、remediation、attestation、evidence pack、joint supervisory team。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Karen (Head of Reg Compliance): Wei, let's walk the readiness for the HKMA supervisory letter. What's the status on the three remediation actions?Wei，我们过一下 HKMA 监管函的就绪度。三项整改措施的状态？
You (PM): Karen, let me walk it action by action. Action 1 — refresh the resolution playbook with the 2026 HKMA guidelines — complete, signed off by Group Legal 2 weeks ago. Action 2 — re-test the critical systems recovery procedure — on track, test report due 22 September, 8 days before the milestone. Action 3 — build the management attestation pack — in progress, first draft this Friday, second draft 25 September, signed by the CEO on 28 September.Karen，我逐项过一下。措施 1——按 2026 HKMA 指南更新处置预案——已完成，集团法务 2 周前签批。措施 2——重测关键系统恢复流程——按计划，测试报告 9 月 22 日交，里程碑前 8 天。措施 3——建管理层声明包——进行中，本周五初稿，9 月 25 日二稿，9 月 28 日 CEO 签字。
Karen (Head of Reg Compliance): And the evidence pack? What goes to the HKMA, and what's internal-only?证据包呢？什么送 HKMA，什么仅内部？
You (PM): Two-tier pack. External — to the HKMA: the resolution playbook v3.1, the test report, the CEO attestation, the board resolution, and the joint walkthrough deck for 15 September. Internal-only: the risk register, the internal audit report, the scenario sensitivity analysis, and the remediation action log. Internal is for our records, not for the regulator.两层包。外部——送 HKMA：处置预案 v3.1、测试报告、CEO 声明、董事会决议、9 月 15 日联合走查幻灯片。仅内部：风险登记册、内审报告、情景敏感度分析、整改行动日志。内部仅供我们存档，不送监管。
David (Group Legal): And the regulator demo on 15 September? Who's the audience, and what do they need to see?9 月 15 日监管演示呢？听众是谁，他们要看什么？
You (PM): The audience is the Joint Supervisory Team — two HKMA supervisors, one HKMA observer, and one Group-internal observer from Internal Audit. They need to see three things. One: a live walkthrough of the resolution playbook — decision tree, escalation paths, communication templates. Two: the results of the recovery test — what worked, what didn't, what we fixed. Three: the attestation chain — who attested what, when, with what evidence. It's a 90-minute session, with 30 minutes of Q&A. I'll send the agenda to HKMA by 5 September.听众是联合监管组——两位 HKMA supervisor，一位 HKMA observer，一位内审 observer。他们要看三件事。一：处置预案现场走查——决策树、升级路径、沟通模板。二：恢复测试结果——什么有效、什么失败、我们改了什么。三：声明链——谁、何时、用什么证据做了什么声明。90 分钟会议，30 分钟 Q&A。9 月 5 日前我把议程送 HKMA。
Karen (Head of Reg Compliance): What if the test report flags a residual issue? How do we handle that in the demo?如果测试报告标了遗留问题呢？演示里怎么处理？
You (PM): Honest framing. If there's a residual issue, we name it upfront, show the mitigation in place, and show the date by which it will be closed. We never hide residual issues from the supervisor. Open disclosure is our policy — it's in the Group Reg Code. If we hide it and the supervisor finds it, we lose credibility for the next 12 months.坦诚框架。如果有遗留问题，我们主动点名，展示已就位的缓解措施，展示关闭日期。绝不向 supervisor 隐瞒遗留问题。主动披露是我们的政策——写在集团监管规范里。如果我们隐瞒，supervisor 一旦发现，我们未来 12 个月的信誉都没了。
David (Group Legal): What about the MAS side? Does the parallel MAS TRM project have any read-across to this HKMA milestone?MAS 这边呢？平行的 MAS TRM 项目和这次 HKMA 里程碑有 read-across 吗？
You (PM): Yes, read-across on the third-party risk assessment. The MAS TRM evidence pack is the same evidence we use for the HKMA action 2. We are reusing the work, not duplicating it. Group Compliance is the single owner of the evidence. No version drift between HK and SG regulators.有，三方风险评估的 read-across。MAS TRM 证据包就是 HKMA 措施 2 用的同一份证据。我们是复用，不是重复。集团合规是证据的唯一 owner。香港和新加坡监管之间无版本漂移。
Karen (Head of Reg Compliance): Good. Last item — supervisory dialogue. What if the supervisor calls in the next two weeks — before the milestone — and asks for an unscheduled status update?好。最后一项——supervisory 对话。如果未来两周——里程碑前——supervisor 临时要求非计划状态更新？
You (PM): We respond within 24 hours. Protocol: I, you, or David takes the call — never a developer directly. We confirm the ask in writing within 2 hours, deliver the answer within 24 hours, and log the interaction in the supervisory dialogue log. No surprises to the supervisor, no surprises to Group. That's the protocol.24 小时内响应。协议：我、你、或 David 接电话——绝不直接让开发接。2 小时内书面确认需求，24 小时内答复，并把互动记入 supervisory 对话日志。不惊动 supervisor，不惊动集团。这就是协议。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Walk action by action Let me walk it action by action. 我逐项过一下 The regulator's questions come in lists. Walk action by action, never vaguely. The auditor listens for completeness.
  • External / Internal-only External — to the HKMA: the playbook, the test report, the attestation. Internal-only: the risk register, the internal audit report. 外部——送 HKMA：预案、测试报告、声明；仅内部：风险登记册、内审报告 Always classify: what goes to the regulator, what stays internal. Sending internal-only material to the regulator is a compliance breach.
  • Joint Supervisory Team The audience is the Joint Supervisory Team — two HKMA supervisors, one observer, plus Internal Audit. 听众是联合监管组——两位 HKMA supervisor，一位 observer，加内审 Name the audience precisely. Different roles ask different questions. Know who is in the room before you walk in.
  • Honest framing If there's a residual issue, we name it upfront and show the mitigation. 如果有遗留问题，我们主动点名，展示缓解措施 Open disclosure is the bank policy. Hiding a residual issue is far worse than naming it. Credibility takes 12 months to rebuild.
  • Attestation chain The attestation chain: who attested what, when, with what evidence. 声明链：谁、何时、用什么证据做了什么声明 Regulators audit the chain, not the conclusion. Every attestation needs a name, a date, and an evidence reference.
  • Read-across The MAS TRM evidence pack is the same evidence we use for the HKMA action 2. No read-across gap. MAS TRM 证据包就是 HKMA 措施 2 用的同一份证据。无 read-across 缺口 Read-across = the work that counts for more than one regulator. Reuse, don't duplicate, and own the single source.
  • Supervisory dialogue log Log the interaction in the supervisory dialogue log. 把互动记入 supervisory 对话日志 Every regulator call, email, letter goes in the log. The log is the audit trail. No informal regulator contact.
  • 24-hour protocol We respond within 24 hours. Never a developer directly. 24 小时内响应。绝不直接让开发接 Supervisor calls go to a Compliance, Legal, or PM lead. Never a developer. The protocol is the protection.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • HKMA (Hong Kong Monetary Authority) n. 香港金管局 Hong Kong's integrated banking supervisor.
  • MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore) n. 新加坡金管局 Singapore's central bank and integrated regulator.
  • FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) n. 英国金融行为监管局 UK's conduct regulator for financial services.
  • PRA (Prudential Regulation Authority) n. 审慎监管局 UK's prudential regulator (part of the Bank of England).
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) n. 通用数据保护条例 EU regulation on data protection and privacy.
  • Supervisor n. 监管人 The named regulator contact assigned to the firm.
  • Supervisory letter n. 监管函 Formal letter from a regulator requiring action by a date.
  • Remediation action n. 整改措施 A specific action required by a supervisory letter, with a binding date.
  • Attestation n. 声明 A formal, signed statement by a named individual (e.g. CEO) that something is true.
  • Attestation chain n. 声明链 The full chain of who attested what, when, with what evidence.
  • Evidence pack n. 证据包 The complete set of documents supporting a regulatory submission.
  • Resolution playbook n. 处置预案 A bank's plan for managing a resolution event under HKMA / FCA rules.
  • Joint Supervisory Team n. 联合监管组 Multiple regulators attending the same review (e.g. HKMA + Internal Audit).
  • Read-across n. 跨监管复用 The reuse of one piece of evidence across multiple regulators.
  • Open disclosure n. 主动披露 The bank policy of naming residual issues upfront, not hiding them.
  • Supervisory dialogue log n. 监管对话日志 The audit trail of every regulator interaction.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Action 1 — refresh — complete" — list every item, status every item

Regulators think in lists. Use Action 1 — [name] — [status], Action 2 — [name] — [status]. Vague answers like we're working on it fail; specific answers like Action 1 — refresh the playbook — complete, signed off 2 weeks ago pass.

监管者用清单思考。用 措施 1——[名称]——[状态]、措施 2——[名称]——[状态]。模糊回答（如"我们在做"）不通过；具体回答（如"措施 1——更新预案——完成，2 周前签批"）通过。

② "We never hide residual issues from the supervisor" — present-tense principle

Use present-tense principles for policy. We never hide residual issues. We always respond within 24 hours. No surprises to the supervisor. Present-tense reads as policy, not as a one-off commitment. It's the language of the Group Reg Code.

用现在时表达政策。我们从不隐瞒遗留问题。我们 24 小时内响应。不惊动 supervisor。现在时读起来是政策，不是一次性承诺，是集团监管规范的语言。

③ "What if … how do we handle …" — pre-empt the regulator's question

Don't wait for the regulator to ask. Pre-empt: What if there's a residual issue — how do we handle it? The pre-emption shows you have thought it through. Regulators respect a PM who has already answered the next question.

别等监管者问。主动提：如果有遗留问题——我们怎么处理？主动提问题显示你想清楚了。监管者尊重已经回答下一个问题的 PM。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Regulator milestones are sacred. Never trade a regulator date for scope. The supervisor remembers. Group remembers. The board remembers.
  • Walk action by action. Regulators think in lists. Vague updates fail audits; precise lists pass.
  • Two-tier pack: external and internal-only. Always classify every document. Sending internal-only material to the regulator is a compliance breach.
  • Open disclosure is bank policy. Hiding a residual issue destroys 12 months of credibility. Naming it upfront with mitigation builds it.
  • Attestation needs name, date, evidence. "We attested" is not enough. The CEO attested on 28 September, with the test report dated 22 September as evidence.
  • Reuse, don't duplicate, across regulators. One piece of evidence, one source, one owner. No version drift between HK and SG.
  • Never a developer on a regulator call. Compliance, Legal, or PM lead takes the call. The protocol is the protection.
  • 24-hour protocol is the standard. Confirm ask in 2 hours, deliver answer in 24 hours, log it. The log is the audit trail.
  • Pre-empt the next question. Regulators respect a PM who has already answered the next question. Build the habit.
  • This is the most political work a bank PM does. Speak the regulator's language. Walk the lists. Honour the dates. The rest follows.

Exercise 2 — Spot the regulatory mistake

Which line would damage credibility with the supervisor the most? Why?

  1. "Action 1 — refresh the playbook — complete. Action 2 — re-test — on track. Action 3 — attestation — in progress, signed by CEO 28 Sep."
  2. "The supervisor called a developer directly last week. The developer said 'we're almost there'."
  3. "If there's a residual issue, we name it upfront, show the mitigation, show the close date."
  4. "24-hour protocol: confirm ask in 2 hours, deliver answer in 24, log it."

② is the credibility-destroyer. A developer took a supervisor's call directly and said "we're almost there" — both protocol breaches. Supervisor calls go to Compliance/Legal/PM lead. ① walks the actions with status. ③ uses open disclosure. ④ enforces the 24-hour protocol. ② is the one that damages: wrong person, wrong language, wrong log entry.
