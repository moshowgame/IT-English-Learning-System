Steering Committee (SteerCo) Update — Talking to MDs

Article 06 · Module 2: Governance & Steering · PM English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 RAG, milestones, top 3 risks, decisions needed, regulator dates — the MD-friendly structure

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 14:00 HKT. The monthly Steering Committee (SteerCo) for the Regulatory Reporting Modernisation programme. The SteerCo is chaired by Margaret, MD of Risk & Compliance Technology. Attendees: David (Group COO office), Priya (Group PMO), Steve (Group Cyber), and Ahmed (Head of Operations, HSBC UAE). You are the Programme PM. You have 8 minutes for your update.

周二 14:00 香港时间，监管报送现代化项目群的月度 Steering Committee (SteerCo)。SteerCo 由Margaret（风险与合规技术部 MD）主持。参会有：David（集团 COO 办公室）、Priya（集团 PMO）、Steve（集团 Cyber）、Ahmed（汇丰 UAE 运营负责人）。你是项目群 PM，你有 8 分钟汇报时间。

SteerCo is a different meeting. MDs read the deck in advance. They want top 3 risks, decisions needed, regulator status, and any asks — fast. You speak in programme language, not project language. The tone is executive.

SteerCo 是不同的会议。MD 会提前看 deck。他们想要前 3 大风险、需决策项、监管状态、以及任何 ask——快。你用项目群语言，不是单个项目语言。语气是高管级。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Margaret (Chair): PM, programme update, 8 minutes please.PM，项目群更新，8 分钟。
You (PM): Thank you, Margaret. Programme RAG is amber. Headline: we are on track for the HKMA Q3 milestone and the MAS TRM date, but two items need decisions from this SteerCo.谢谢 Margaret。项目群 RAG 琥珀色。一句话：HKMA Q3 里程碑和 MAS TRM 日期按计划，但两项需要本次 SteerCo 决策。
You (PM): Milestone view across the programme: 4 projects in delivery, 1 in UAT, 1 in hypercare. M2 design sign-off closed on time across all four workstreams. M3 build is 62% complete, on plan. M4 UAT starts 18th. No changes to the regulator milestones.项目群里程碑视角：4 个项目在交付，1 个 UAT，1 个 hypercare。M2 设计签批在所有四个工作流按时关闭。M3 构建完成 62%，按计划。M4 UAT 18 日启动。监管里程碑无变化。
You (PM): Top 3 risks. One: Group Cyber resourcing — they are 2 FTEs short against plan, probability high, impact high. Mitigation: I have an ask for Steve below. Two: vendor cutover window not confirmed for December. Probability medium, impact high. Three: FX exposure on the offshore contractor cost, $180K over budget, probability medium, impact medium.前 3 大风险。一：集团 Cyber 资源——他们比计划缺 2 个 FTE，概率高，影响高。缓解：下方有给 Steve 的 ask。二：12 月厂商切换窗口未确认，概率中，影响高。三：离岸外包成本的汇率敞口，超预算 18 万美元，概率中，影响中。
You (PM): Decisions needed today. Decision 1: approve the FX contingency drawdown of $180K from the central programme reserve. Decision 2: approve a 2-FTE contract uplift for Group Cyber, cost $320K, fully recoverable from the central budget. Both on the decisions page in your pack.今日需决策。决策 1：批准从中央项目群储备金中支出 18 万美元 FX contingency。决策 2：批准为集团 Cyber 增加 2 个 FTE 合同，成本 32 万美元，可从中央预算全额回收。两项都在资料的决策页。
You (PM): Regulator position: HKMA on track, MAS TRM on track, no exceptions raised, no new findings. Group Compliance has no escalations.监管状态：HKMA 按计划，MAS TRM 按计划，无例外，无新发现。集团合规无升级。
Margaret: Decision 1 — approved. Steve, can you take Decision 2 away and come back next month with a plan?决策 1 通过。Steve，决策 2 你来跟进，下月带方案回来？
Steve (Group Cyber): Acknowledged. I'll come back next month with a plan and cost.收到。下月带方案和成本回来。
You (PM): Thank you. Margaret, anything else on the programme?谢谢。Margaret，项目群还有别的吗？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open Programme RAG is amber. Headline: … 项目群 RAG 琥珀色。一句话：…… Same RAG-first rhythm as the standup. MDs read top-down. The headline is the only sentence some will hear.
  • Decisions Two items need decisions from this SteerCo. 两项需要本次 SteerCo 决策 Tell the chair up-front how many decisions are needed. They will plan the agenda around it.
  • Top 3 Top 3 risks across the programme. 项目群前 3 大风险 Three is the magic number. Fewer feels thin. More is too many to remember.
  • P×I Probability high, impact high. 概率高，影响高 Always pair probability with impact. The P×I is what makes the risk scoreable and comparable.
  • Mitigation Mitigation: I have an ask for Steve below. 缓解：下方有给 Steve 的 ask Link risks to the ask. The mitigation is the ask. Don't leave risks hanging.
  • Decisions page Both on the decisions page in your pack. 两项都在资料的决策页 Always point to the page number. MDs read the pack, not the speaker.
  • Recoverable Fully recoverable from the central budget. 可从中央预算全额回收 "Recoverable" is finance vocabulary. It means "this cost lands elsewhere — it doesn't burn your project budget". MDs love this word.
  • Take away Can you take Decision 2 away and come back next month? 你来跟进决策 2，下月带方案回来？ "Take away" is SteerCo language. The chair assigns an action; you don't argue in the room.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Steering Committee (SteerCo) n. 高层指导委员会 The governance body that approves scope, budget, and major changes.
  • Programme n. 项目群 A group of related projects managed together for coordinated benefits.
  • Decisions page n. 决策页 A standard slide in the SteerCo pack listing decisions needed.
  • For decision / For awareness n. 决策项 / 知情项 SteerCo item categories: yes/no required, or FYI only.
  • Central programme reserve n. 中央项目群储备金 The pool of contingency held at the programme level.
  • Drawdown n. 动用 Using money from a reserve or contingency pot.
  • FX exposure n. 汇率敞口 Risk from currency fluctuations, especially on multi-currency contracts.
  • Recoverable n. 可回收 Cost that lands in another budget rather than the project budget.
  • Hypercare n. 强化支持 The post-go-live period of intensive support before transitioning to BAU.
  • BAU (Business as Usual) n. 日常运营 The steady state of running a system, after the project is done.
  • FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) n. 全职等效 A measure of headcount (1 FTE = 1 person full time).

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Top 3 risks across the programme" — speak programme-level, not project-level

At SteerCo, you speak programme language. Don't say "ONB-218 is slipping"; say "the Onboarding workstream has slipped by 3 days". Don't say "I'm chasing Mei"; say "the regulator clarification is in flight with Group Compliance".

在 SteerCo，你用项目群语言。不要说"ONB-218 在滑"；说"Onboarding 工作流推迟 3 天"。

② "Probability high, impact high" — pair them every time

Risks at SteerCo always get a P×I pair, in that order. The pair is what makes the risk comparable across the programme. Probability high, impact high. Probability medium, impact high.

SteerCo 上的风险总是给出 P×I 对，按顺序。P×I 是让风险在项目群中可比的基础。

③ "Fully recoverable from the central budget" — the financial reassurance sentence

When you ask for money, always say where the money lands. "Cost $320K, fully recoverable from the central budget" tells the MD their project budget is safe. The sentence that wins approval is the sentence that shows the impact on the approver's P&L.

当你 ask 钱时，总是说钱从哪出。"成本 32 万美元，可从中央预算全额回收"告诉 MD 他们的项目预算是安全的。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Decide up-front, narrate after. Tell the chair how many decisions are needed in the first 30 seconds. They plan the agenda around it.
  • Three risks is the magic number. Fewer feels thin; more is too many to remember. Always go for top 3.
  • Link risk to ask. "Risk: Group Cyber resourcing. Mitigation: ask for Steve below." The risk and the ask are the same conversation.
  • Speak programme language, not project language. Workstream, not user story. Milestone, not sprint. Regulator date, not release date.
  • "Recoverable" is finance vocabulary. When you ask for money, show where it lands. MDs think in P&L impact.
  • Point to the page number. "Both on the decisions page in your pack" — the MD reads the pack, not your face.
  • Regulator status is non-negotiable. Always have a one-liner on regulator position. "On track, no exceptions, no findings" is a sentence of gold.
  • Don't argue in the room. If a SteerCo member pushes back, acknowledge and take it away. The SteerCo is for decisions, not debate.
  • Close with the chair's question, not yours. "Margaret, anything else?" — the chair closes the agenda, not you.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the SteerCo update

Translate the following SteerCo update into natural Chinese.

"Programme RAG is amber. Headline: on track for the HKMA Q3 milestone and the FCA migration date, but two items need decisions from this SteerCo. Top 3 risks: One, Group Cyber resourcing — they are 2 FTEs short, probability high, impact high. Two, vendor cutover window for the December migration not confirmed, probability medium, impact high. Three, offshore contractor FX exposure, $220K over budget, probability medium, impact medium. Decisions needed today. Decision 1: approve the FX contingency drawdown of $220K from the central programme reserve. Decision 2: approve a 2-FTE contract uplift for Group Cyber, cost $340K, fully recoverable from the central budget. Regulator position: HKMA on track, FCA on track, no exceptions, no findings. Group Compliance has no escalations."

"项目群 RAG 琥珀色。一句话：HKMA Q3 里程碑和 FCA 迁移日期按计划，但两项需要本次 SteerCo 决策。前 3 大风险：一、集团 Cyber 资源——缺 2 个 FTE，概率高，影响高。二、12 月迁移的厂商切换窗口未确认，概率中，影响高。三、离岸外包的汇率敞口，超预算 22 万美元，概率中，影响中。今日需决策。决策 1：批准从中央项目群储备金中支出 22 万美元 FX contingency。决策 2：批准为集团 Cyber 增加 2 个 FTE 合同，成本 34 万美元，可从中央预算全额回收。监管状态：HKMA 按计划，FCA 按计划，无例外，无新发现。集团合规无升级。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the SteerCo mistake

Which line would land worst at SteerCo? Why?

  1. "Programme RAG is amber. Two decisions needed today."
  2. "ONB-218 is slipping because the eKYC vendor is slow."
  3. "Top 3 risks: Group Cyber resourcing, vendor cutover, FX exposure."
  4. "Both decisions on the decisions page in your pack."

② is the worst. It speaks in user-story / dev language, not programme language. SteerCo doesn't know what ONB-218 is. Reframe as "the Onboarding workstream has slipped 3 days due to a third-party eKYC vendor delay". Same fact, programme vocabulary.
