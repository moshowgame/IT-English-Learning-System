Budget Tracking & Financial Reporting — Speaking the CFO's Language

Article 08 · Module 2: Governance & Steering · PM English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 Capex vs Opex, burn rate, forecast, variance, contingency drawdown

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 14:00 HKT. You are running a 30-minute monthly financial review with the Programme Sponsor and Finance Business Partner. The programme is the Regulatory Reporting Modernisation — a 24-month, $8.4M programme. You need to walk this month's actuals, the forecast, the variance, and an ask for a contingency drawdown. On the call: Margaret (Sponsor, MD), Grace (Finance Business Partner, London), and Karen (PMO, London).

周五 14:00 香港时间，你和项目群 Sponsor 及财务业务伙伴开 30 分钟月度财务 review。项目群是监管报送现代化——24 个月、840 万美元的项目群。你需要走本月的实际、预测、差异、以及一个 contingency 动用的 ask。参会有：Margaret（Sponsor，MD）、Grace（财务业务伙伴，伦敦）、Karen（PMO，伦敦）。

Finance meetings are number-driven. The language is actuals, forecast, variance, burn rate, Capex, Opex, drawdown. You speak like a finance person, not a developer. The CFO's office watches these numbers — so do the auditors.

财务会议是数字驱动。语言是实际、预测、差异、burn rate、Capex、Opex、drawdown。你要像财务人员一样说话，不像开发。CFO 办公室看这些数字——审计也看。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (PM): Thanks all. Goal of today's review: walk month 8 actuals vs plan, refresh the forecast to completion, and approve one contingency drawdown.感谢各位。今天 review 的目标：过第 8 个月实际 vs 计划，刷新完工预测 (ETC/EAC)，并批准一个 contingency 动用。
You (PM): Plan to date: $2.8M. Actuals to date: $2.95M. Variance: $150K over plan, that's 5.4% over. Main driver: Group Cyber contract uplift ($120K) approved last month and a 5% FX drift on the offshore contractor ($30K).计划截至今日：280 万美元。实际截至今日：295 万美元。差异：超计划 15 万美元，即超 5.4%。主要原因：上月批准的集团 Cyber 合同增补（12 万美元）以及离岸外包 5% 汇率漂移（3 万美元）。
Grace (Finance): Is the FX drift still rising or flat-lined?汇率漂移还在上升还是走平？
You (PM): Flat-lined for the last 6 weeks. I've locked the rate with a forward contract through month 16. No further exposure after that.过去 6 周走平。我已通过远期合约锁定汇率到第 16 个月。之后无敞口。
You (PM): Burn rate: $310K per month average over the last 3 months. Forecast to completion (EAC): $8.85M against original budget $8.4M. Variance at completion: +$450K, or 5.4%. Recommendation: draw $200K from contingency, the rest recoverable from the offshore workstream saving.Burn rate：过去 3 个月平均每月 31 万美元。完工预测 (EAC)：885 万美元，对比原始预算 840 万。完工差异：+45 万美元，即 5.4%。建议：从 contingency 动用 20 万美元，其余从离岸工作流节省中回收。
Margaret (Sponsor): Is the offshore saving real or paper saving?离岸节省是真的还是账面节省？
You (PM): Real. We've replaced 2 senior contractors with a managed service at $180K per year saving. Contract signed last week, saving starts month 10.真的。我们用托管服务替换了 2 名高级外包，每年节省 18 万美元。合同上周签，第 10 个月开始节省。
Margaret: Approved. $200K drawdown, $250K recoverable from the offshore saving. Grace, please book the journal.批准。20 万美元动用，25 万美元从离岸节省回收。Grace，请记分录。
Grace: Will do. Can you send me the contract confirmation for the journal?好的。能把合同确认发我记分录吗？
You (PM): By EOD today. Anything else on the financials?今日 EOD 前发。财务还有其他吗？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Actuals vs plan Actuals to date: $2.95M. Variance: $150K over plan. 实际截至今日：295 万美元。差异：超计划 15 万 Always lead with the numbers, then the variance. Finance reads numbers first.
  • Main driver Main driver: Group Cyber contract uplift and a 5% FX drift. 主要原因：集团 Cyber 合同增补和 5% 汇率漂移 Don't just say "we're over". Name the driver. Finance respects named drivers.
  • Flat-lined The FX drift is flat-lined for the last 6 weeks. 汇率漂移过去 6 周走平 "Flat-lined" is finance vocabulary. It means the curve has stopped moving.
  • Burn rate Burn rate: $310K per month average over the last 3 months. Burn rate：过去 3 个月平均每月 31 万美元 Burn rate is the monthly cost run-rate. Always give a time window (3-month, 6-month).
  • EAC Forecast to completion (EAC): $8.85M against original budget $8.4M. 完工预测 (EAC)：885 万美元，原始预算 840 万 EAC (Estimate at Completion) is the projected total cost. Always pair it with the original budget.
  • Variance at completion Variance at completion: +$450K, or 5.4%. 完工差异：+45 万美元，即 5.4% VAC is the difference between EAC and budget. Always state in dollars AND percentage.
  • Drawdown Draw $200K from contingency, the rest recoverable from the offshore saving. 从 contingency 动用 20 万美元，其余从离岸节省回收 Drawdown is the formal word for using contingency. Always name the source.
  • Real vs paper Is the offshore saving real or paper saving? 离岸节省是真的还是账面节省？ "Real" = cash in the bank. "Paper" = a forecast on a slide. Sponsors always ask this question.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Actuals n. 实际 The real money spent to date, not the plan.
  • Plan to date n. 计划截至今日 The budgeted spend to date, per the baseline.
  • Variance n. 差异 The difference between actuals and plan (positive = over, negative = under).
  • Burn rate n. 月度消耗率 The monthly cost run-rate, usually over a 3- or 6-month window.
  • EAC (Estimate at Completion) n. 完工预测 The projected total cost of the project at completion.
  • VAC (Variance at Completion) n. 完工差异 EAC minus original budget.
  • Capex (Capital Expenditure) n. 资本支出 Spend on assets that are capitalised (e.g., new platform).
  • Opex (Operational Expenditure) n. 运营支出 Spend on running costs (e.g., licences, support).
  • Contingency n. 应急金 A reserve pot of money for unplanned work.
  • Drawdown n. 动用 Using money from a reserve or contingency pot.
  • Forward contract n. 远期合约 A financial contract that locks an exchange rate for a future date.
  • Recoverable n. 可回收 Cost that lands in another budget or is offset by another saving.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Actuals to date: $2.95M" — number-first, then narrative

Finance meetings are number-first. Actuals: $X. Plan: $Y. Variance: $Z. Narrative comes after. The CFO's eye goes to the number; the narrative is the explanation you owe.

财务会议数字优先。实际：$X。计划：$Y。差异：$Z。叙述在后。

② "Real vs paper saving" — the cash vs accrual distinction

Sponsors always ask whether a saving is real (cash in the bank) or paper (forecast on a slide). Real saving means a signed contract. Paper saving means a forecast. Always be ready to show the contract.

Sponsor 总会问节省是真的还是账面。真节省 = 已签合同。账面节省 = 预测。

③ "Fully recoverable from the offshore saving" — show where the money lands

When you ask for money, always show the source. Draw $200K from contingency, the rest recoverable from the offshore saving. The sentence that wins approval is the one that shows the impact on the sponsor's P&L.

当你 ask 钱时，总是给出来源。从 contingency 动用 20 万，其余从离岸节省回收。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Speak number-first in finance meetings. Actuals, plan, variance, EAC, VAC. Narrative comes after. The CFO's eye goes to the numbers.
  • Always name the driver. "We are $150K over" loses. "We are $150K over, main driver is Group Cyber contract uplift ($120K) and 5% FX drift ($30K)" wins.
  • Burn rate needs a window. "Burn rate is $310K per month" is incomplete. Add "average over the last 3 months" or "trailing 6 months".
  • EAC always pairs with original budget. EAC alone is a number. EAC vs original budget is a story.
  • Real vs paper is the question you will be asked. Always have a contract, a clause, or a booking ready. Paper savings don't survive finance scrutiny.
  • Capex vs Opex matters. A Capex spend lands on the balance sheet; Opex hits the P&L. Sponsors and CFOs think differently about each.
  • Recoverable is finance gold. Show that the cost lands elsewhere. "Fully recoverable from the central budget" or "from the offshore saving".
  • Lock the rate when you can. A forward contract kills FX exposure. Finance loves forward contracts because they remove uncertainty.
  • Contingency drawdown is a normal tool. Don't hide it. If you need it, ask for it cleanly: amount, reason, source. Finance approves clean asks.

Translate the following financial review into natural Chinese.

Burn rate: $140K per month average over the last 3 months. Forecast to completion (EAC): $4.2M against original budget $4M. Variance at completion: +$200K, or 5%. We've signed the new SOW at $80K below original. Approved. $120K drawdown, $80K recoverable from the vendor renegotiation. Grace, please book the journal."

"计划截至今日：120 万美元。实际截至今日：132 万美元。差异：超计划 12 万美元，即超 10%。主要原因：离岸外包汇率漂移 8 万美元，以及计划外的集团 Cyber review 4 万美元。Burn rate：过去 3 个月平均每月 14 万美元。完工预测 (EAC)：420 万美元，原始预算 400 万。完工差异：+20 万美元，即 5%。建议：从 contingency 动用 12 万美元，其余从 Q4 厂商重新谈判回收。Margaret：厂商重新谈判是真的还是账面节省？真的。我们已签新 SOW，比原始低 8 万美元。批准。12 万美元动用，8 万美元从厂商重新谈判回收。Grace，请记分录。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the finance mistake

Which line would land worst in a finance review? Why?

  1. "EAC is $8.85M, 5.4% over the original $8.4M budget."
  2. "We're a bit over budget but we have a plan."
  3. "Burn rate: $310K per month average over the last 3 months."
  4. "Variance at completion: +$450K, main driver Group Cyber contract uplift."

② is the worst. "A bit" and "we have a plan" are not finance vocabulary. Finance wants numbers, drivers, and EAC. ①, ③, ④ all have numbers and windows. PMs must learn to speak in finance language in finance meetings.

You are the PM. Build a 60-second finance update for: $5M project, plan to date $1.8M, actuals $1.95M, main driver offshore contractor FX 5% ($100K), burn rate $200K/month trailing 3 months, EAC $5.3M, ask $200K drawdown + $100K recoverable. End with a question to Finance about journal timing.
