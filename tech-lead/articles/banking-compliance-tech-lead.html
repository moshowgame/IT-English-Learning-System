Banking Compliance for Tech Leads

Article 18 · Module 5: Banking Context · Tech Lead English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Compliance vocabulary, PCI-DSS, MAS TRM, HKMA, Group Standards

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday, 10:00 AM HKT. You are joining a call with Steven from Group Compliance, Rachel from InfoSec, and James (PO). The new customer onboarding flow collects HKID, passport, address proof, and selfie. You need to understand the compliance landscape before writing the design.

周二上午10点香港时间。你正加入一个电话会议，与会者：集团合规的 Steven、InfoSec 的 Rachel、PO James。新的客户开户流程收集香港身份证、护照、地址证明、自拍照。在写设计之前你需要理解合规的全景。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Steven (Group Compliance):Wei, welcome to the compliance walkthrough. The onboarding flow touches 4 regulatory frameworks — HKMA, MAS TRM, PCI-DSS, and GDPR. Each has a different data-classification rule.Wei，欢迎参加合规走查。开户流程触及 4 个监管框架——HKMA、MAS TRM、PCI-DSS、GDPR。每个有不同的数据分类规则。
You (TL):Thanks Steven. Can you walk me through the 4 frameworks and the data-classification matrix?谢谢 Steven。你能带我过一下 4 个框架和数据分类矩阵吗？
Steven:HKMA classifies HKID, passport, address proof as Customer Due Diligence (CDD) data — 7-year retention, encrypted at rest, HK data residency.HKMA把香港身份证、护照、地址证明归类为客户尽职调查（CDD）数据——7 年保留、静态加密、香港数据驻留。
Steven:MAS TRM adds source-of-funds documentation for amounts >SGD 5,000 — 5-year retention, Singapore data residency if the customer is Singapore-domiciled.MAS TRM追加资金来源证明文件（金额 >5000 新币）——5 年保留，如果客户在新加坡居住则新加坡数据驻留。
Steven:PCI-DSS covers the payment card data — no full PAN storage, tokenization only, 1-year retention for tokens.PCI-DSS覆盖支付卡数据——不存完整 PAN，只用 token 化，token 1 年保留。
Steven:GDPR covers EU residents — right to be forgotten, data portability, explicit consent — 2-year retention for marketing data, opt-in only.GDPR覆盖欧盟居民——被遗忘权、数据可携、显式同意——营销数据 2 年保留，仅 opt-in。
Rachel (InfoSec):Add to that our internal Group Standards — SDL (Secure Development Lifecycle) for the code, Group Cyber for the network, Group Resilience for the DR plan.加上我们的内部集团标准——代码的 SDL（安全开发生命周期）、网络的 Group Cyber、容灾的 Group Resilience。
You (TL):That is a lot. Let me confirm the data-classification matrix:挺多的。我确认一下数据分类矩阵：
You (TL):(1) HKID / passport / address proof: CDD, 7-year retention, encrypted, HK data residency. (2) Source of funds: TRM, 5-year retention, SG residency if applicable. (3) PAN: no full storage, token only, 1-year token retention. (4) Marketing data: GDPR opt-in, 2-year retention. (5) Selfie: treat as CDD, 7-year retention, encrypted. Did I miss anything?(1) 香港身份证/护照/地址证明：CDD，7 年保留，加密，香港数据驻留。(2) 资金来源：TRM，5 年保留，适用则新加坡驻留。(3) PAN：不存完整，只 token，token 1 年保留。(4) 营销数据：GDPR opt-in，2 年保留。(5) 自拍：按 CDD 处理，7 年保留，加密。我漏了什么吗？
Steven:Perfect summary. One more: the selfie is biometric data — GDPR treats it as 'special category', HKMA treats it as CDD. Default to the stricter rule.完美的总结。还有：自拍是生物特征数据——GDPR 当作"特殊类别"，HKMA 当 CDD。默认按更严的规则。
You (TL):Got it. Default to the stricter rule across all 4 frameworks. I will incorporate the matrix into the design doc and the data-classification section of the Confluence page. Rachel, can you sign off on the SDL checklist by Friday? James, can you confirm the source-of-funds threshold with the business?好。所有 4 个框架默认按更严的规则。我会把矩阵纳入设计文档和 Confluence 页面数据分类部分。Rachel，你能在周五前签 SDL checklist 吗？James，你能跟业务确认资金来源阈值吗？
Rachel:Yes, Friday EOD.好，周五 EOD。
James:Yes, will confirm by Thursday.好，周四前确认。
You (TL):Perfect. Design doc draft by next Tuesday. Steven, Rachel, James — thanks for the walkthrough.好。设计文档下周二前起草。Steven、Rachel、James——谢谢走查。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Compliance walkthrough Welcome to the compliance walkthrough.欢迎参加合规走查。The first call with compliance is a walkthrough, not a debate. Listen, take notes, summarize at the end.
  • Frameworks The onboarding flow touches 4 regulatory frameworks.开户流程触及 4 个监管框架。Name the frameworks upfront. HKMA, MAS TRM, PCI-DSS, GDPR — and internal Group Standards.
  • Data classification Each has a different data-classification rule.每个有不同的数据分类规则。Each framework classifies the same data differently. The data-classification matrix is the source of truth.
  • CDD data HKID, passport, address proof as Customer Due Diligence (CDD) data.香港身份证、护照、地址证明归类为客户尽职调查（CDD）数据。CDD is the regulator term for 'know your customer' data. 7-year retention is the HKMA default.
  • Data residency HK data residency. Singapore data residency if applicable.香港数据驻留。适用则新加坡驻留。Data residency = where the data physically lives. HKMA says HK. MAS says SG. Cross-border transfer requires additional approval.
  • Tokenization No full PAN storage, tokenization only.不存完整 PAN，只用 token 化。PCI-DSS forbids storing full PAN (Primary Account Number). Tokenization replaces PAN with a non-reversible token.
  • Default to stricter Default to the stricter rule across all 4 frameworks.所有 4 个框架默认按更严的规则。When frameworks conflict, default to the stricter rule. The cost is real, the audit-clean is real too.
  • Matrix summary Let me confirm the data-classification matrix.我确认一下数据分类矩阵。End the walkthrough with a matrix summary. This is your written record and your team source of truth.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • HKMA n.. Hong Kong Monetary Authority — the banking regulator. 香港金管局
  • MAS TRM n.. Monetary Authority of Singapore Technology Risk Management guidelines. 新加坡金管局技术风险管理指引
  • PCI-DSS n.. Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard. 支付卡行业数据安全标准
  • GDPR n.. General Data Protection Regulation (EU). 欧盟通用数据保护条例
  • CDD (Customer Due Diligence) n.. KYC data — identity, address, source of funds. 客户尽职调查
  • Source of funds n.. Documentation proving where a customer's money comes from. 资金来源
  • Data residency n.. The geographic location where data is stored. 数据驻留
  • PAN (Primary Account Number) n.. The 16-digit number on a payment card. 主账号
  • Tokenization n.. Replacing sensitive data with a non-reversible token. Token 化
  • Biometric data n.. Face, fingerprint, voice — GDPR 'special category'. 生物特征数据

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① 'Welcome to the compliance walkthrough' — the framing

Frame the first call as a walkthrough, not a debate. The compliance team walks you through; you listen and summarize.

把第一次会议框定为 walkthrough，不是辩论。

② 'Default to the stricter rule' — the conflict-resolution principle

When frameworks conflict, default to the stricter rule / take the union of the rules. The audit team will accept the stricter; they will not accept the looser.

框架冲突时 默认按更严的规则。

③ 'Let me confirm the data-classification matrix' — the matrix summary

End every compliance walkthrough with a matrix summary. Did I miss anything? The summary is your written record.

用矩阵总结结束合规走查。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Frame the first call as a walkthrough, not a debate. Listen, take notes, summarize.
  • Name the frameworks upfront. HKMA, MAS TRM, PCI-DSS, GDPR — and internal Group Standards.
  • Each framework classifies the same data differently. The matrix is the source of truth.
  • Default to the stricter rule. The cost is real, the audit-clean is real too.
  • End with a matrix summary. Did I miss anything? is the right closer.
  • Data residency matters. HKMA says HK, MAS says SG. Cross-border transfer requires approval.
  • Tokenization for PAN. PCI-DSS forbids full PAN storage. Tokenization is the only acceptable approach.

Exercise 2 — Spot the compliance mistake

Which of these is the most TL-as-compliance-aware?

  1. We will just store everything in a single Postgres table. Easier.
  2. I will apply the data-classification matrix: HKID/passport/address: CDD, 7-year retention, encrypted, HK residency. Source of funds: TRM, 5-year retention, SG if applicable. PAN: tokenization only. Marketing: GDPR opt-in. Default to stricter rule across all 4 frameworks.
  3. Compliance is the legal team problem, not mine.
  4. We do not need to worry about retention — that is storage engineering.

② is the compliance-aware TL. ① ignores retention/residency. ③ delegates ownership. ④ silos. A common mistake is treating compliance as someone else problem. In a bank, compliance is the TL problem — they own the design.

Exercise 3 — Your turn

Your new feature collects: (a) email, (b) phone number, (c) residential address, (d) employment status, (e) annual income. The feature serves HK, SG, and EU residents. Write the data-classification matrix in English. Use the 4 frameworks. Default to the stricter rule.

"Data-classification matrix: (a) email: PII, 3-year retention, encrypted, multi-jurisdiction (HKMA, GDPR, MAS), opt-in for marketing. (b) phone number: PII, 3-year retention, encrypted, multi-jurisdiction. (c) residential address: CDD, 7-year retention (HKMA), 5-year (TRM), encrypted, residency matches customer domicile. (d) employment status: PII, 3-year retention, encrypted. (e) annual income: source-of-wealth context, 7-year retention (HKMA), 5-year (TRM), encrypted.
