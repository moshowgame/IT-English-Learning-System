SIEM Alert Triage — Walk a P1 End-to-End

Article 02 · Module 1: Daily Security Operations · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟢 Beginner-friendly 🎯 True positive vs false positive, alert chain, escalation, evidence

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is 10:30 HKT. You are a SOC analyst. A P1 alert has just fired on the SIEM: "Multiple failed admin logins followed by successful login from a new geography". The asset is a Treasury management server. The user is Kevin, a treasury operator. You have 30 minutes to triage it — confirm true positive or false positive, and either escalate to incident or close as benign. Your team lead Sarah is on a call, so you walk it through with her afterwards.

上午 10:30 香港时间，你是 SOC 分析师。SIEM 刚触发一条 P1 告警："多次管理员登录失败后，从新地理位置成功登录"。资产是资金管理服务器。用户是 Kevin，资金运营。30 分钟内分流——确认真阳/误阳，或升级为事件/关单。组长 Sarah 在通话中，之后你和她过一遍。

Alert triage is the SOC's daily craft. The difference between a junior and a senior analyst is the chain: asset → user → behaviour → evidence → verdict → action. Miss the chain and you either cry wolf (alert fatigue) or miss a breach (analyst failure).

告警分流是 SOC 的日常手艺。初级和高级分析师的差别是链：资产 → 用户 → 行为 → 证据 → 判定 → 动作。缺链，要么狼来了（告警疲劳），要么漏掉失陷（分析师失误）。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (analyst): Sarah, 30 seconds on a P1 alert I just triaged.Sarah，我刚分流完一条 P1 告警，30 秒过一下。
You (analyst): Asset: TREAS-MGR-01, treasury management server, Crown Jewel asset under PCI-DSS scope.资产：TREAS-MGR-01，资金管理服务器，PCI-DSS 范围内的皇冠级资产。
You (analyst): User: Kevin Liu, treasury operator, MFA enrolled, last login 09:15 HKT from Hong Kong office IP.用户：Kevin Liu，资金运营，已注册 MFA，上次登录 09:15 HKT，香港办公室 IP。
You (analyst): Behaviour: 5 failed admin logins between 09:45 and 09:52 from a Singapore IP — 4 on the wrong password, 1 on a stale MFA token. At 09:55, a successful login from the same Singapore IP, MFA prompt approved. The new geography is Singapore; Kevin is in Hong Kong.行为：09:45 到 09:52 之间 5 次管理员登录失败，来自新加坡 IP——4 次密码错，1 次 MFA token 过期。09:55 同一新加坡 IP 成功登录，MFA 提示已批准。新地理位置是新加坡；Kevin 在香港。
Sarah (team lead): Hold on — why did the MFA prompt succeed from a new geography? Didn't our policy require step-up auth on geo-anomaly?等一下——为什么新地理位置 MFA 提示通过了？我们的策略不是要求地理位置异常时 step-up 认证吗？
You (analyst): Good catch. It should have. I pulled the conditional access policy: step-up auth is enabled for geo-anomaly, but the rule has an exclusion for the 'TREAS-ADMIN' group, which Kevin is a member of. That's the gap.抓得好。应该要的。我拉了条件访问策略：地理位置异常启用了 step-up 认证，但规则对 'TREAS-ADMIN' 组有豁免，Kevin 是该组成员。这就是缺口。
Sarah (team lead): Escalate to P1 incident. Open a Sev-1 ticket, page the on-call, loop in IAM, Treasury, and Group Cyber. Three containment actions, in this order.升级为 P1 事件。开 Sev-1 工单，呼叫值班，拉 IAM、资金、集团 Cyber 群。三项遏制动作，按顺序。
You (analyst): One: revoke Kevin's session, rotate his password, force MFA re-enrolment. Two: block the Singapore IP at the edge firewall pending investigation. Three: remove the 'TREAS-ADMIN' exclusion from the conditional access policy, so step-up auth applies to all geo-anomalies — this is the root-cause fix, not just a workaround.一：吊销 Kevin 会话、重置密码、强制 MFA 重新注册。二：在边缘防火墙拉黑新加坡 IP，待调查。三：从条件访问策略中删除 'TREAS-ADMIN' 豁免，让 step-up 认证对所有地理位置异常生效——这是根因修复，不是临时绕过。
Sarah (team lead): Verdict on the alert itself: true positive, possible account compromise, possible policy gap exploit. Document the chain in the ticket: asset, user, behaviour, evidence, verdict, action. I'll need it for the daily SOC standup and the 1-page to the CISO.告警判定：真阳，可能账号失陷，可能策略缺口被利用。把链写进工单：资产、用户、行为、证据、判定、动作。我要做日 SOC 站会和给 CISO 的 1 页摘要。
You (analyst): Chain documented, ticket open, containment in progress. Verdict in the SOC standup by 11:00.链已写进工单，工单已开，遏制进行中。11:00 SOC 站会给出判定。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 30 seconds on a P1 30 seconds on a P1 alert I just triaged. 30 秒过一下我刚分流完的 P1 Set the time budget up front. Senior analysts respect concise updates.
  • Asset / User / Behaviour Asset: TREAS-MGR-01, Crown Jewel, PCI-DSS scope. User: Kevin, MFA enrolled, HK office IP. Behaviour: 5 fails, 1 success, new geo. 资产、用户、行为按顺序报 The chain is fixed. The team lead reads it top-down; missing a field is the alert that gets bounced back.
  • Good catch Good catch — why did the MFA prompt succeed from a new geography? 抓得好——为什么新地理位置 MFA 提示通过了？ Senior analysts always challenge one assumption. "Good catch" signals respect and opens the policy-gap question.
  • Root-cause fix Remove the TREAS-ADMIN exclusion — root-cause fix, not a workaround. 删除豁免——根因修复，不是绕过 Always distinguish the workaround (revoke the session) from the root-cause fix (remove the policy gap). The team lead wants both.
  • True positive Verdict: true positive, possible account compromise, possible policy gap exploit. 判定：真阳，可能账号失陷，可能策略缺口被利用 Always name the verdict and the two possibilities. "True positive" alone is half a verdict.
  • Chain documented Chain documented, ticket open, containment in progress. 链已写进工单，工单已开，遏制进行中 Three short status clauses, one each: documentation, ticket, action. The CISO reads the trio in 5 seconds.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • True positive (TP) n. 真阳 Alert is real; the threat is genuine.
  • False positive (FP) n. 误阳 Alert fires but the activity is benign.
  • Crown Jewel asset n. 皇冠级资产 The most critical assets (treasury, payments, customer data).
  • Geo-anomaly n. 地理位置异常 Login from an unusual location vs. the user's baseline.
  • Step-up auth n. 增强认证 Higher-strength MFA required for sensitive actions (e.g. admin, geo-anomaly).
  • Conditional access policy n. 条件访问策略 Rule that decides which auth method is required under which condition.
  • Session revocation n. 会话吊销 Force-logout a user and invalidate their access tokens.
  • MFA re-enrolment n. MFA 重新注册 Force the user to set up a new MFA factor (often after compromise).
  • Policy gap n. 策略缺口 A rule with an exception that the attacker exploits.
  • Root-cause fix n. 根因修复 The change that closes the gap, not just the immediate symptom.
  • Workaround n. 临时绕过 The immediate action that stops the bleeding, not the fix.
  • Sev-1 ticket n. 严重级 1 工单 The highest-severity incident ticket; pages on-call, loops in leads.
  • On-call n. 值班 The named responder paged for incidents outside business hours.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Asset / User / Behaviour" — three nouns, fixed order

The triage chain is three nouns in fixed order. Asset, user, behaviour. Missing one field signals an incomplete triage. The team lead will always ask for the missing one.

分流链是三个名词、固定顺序。资产、用户、行为。缺一项等于分流不完整。组长一定会问缺的那一项。

② "Good catch" — challenge an assumption, not the analyst

Senior analysts challenge an assumption, not the analyst. Good catch — why did the MFA prompt succeed? The "good catch" preserves respect; the "why" opens the policy question.

高级分析师挑战假设，不挑战分析师。抓得好——为什么 MFA 提示通过了？"抓得好"保留尊重，"为什么"打开策略问题。

③ "Verdict: true positive, possible X, possible Y" — name the verdict and the two possibilities

Always name the verdict and at least two possibilities. True positive, possible account compromise, possible policy gap exploit. One verdict + two possibilities = a complete analysis.

总给判定和至少两个可能性。真阳，可能账号失陷，可能策略缺口被利用。一个判定 + 两个可能 = 完整分析。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Always walk the chain: asset → user → behaviour → evidence → verdict → action. Missing a field is a triage that gets bounced back.
  • "Good catch" is the senior analyst's superpower. Challenge one assumption, not the analyst. The "good catch" preserves respect.
  • Distinguish workaround from root-cause fix. Revoke the session = workaround. Remove the policy gap = root-cause fix. The team lead wants both.
  • True positive alone is half a verdict. Always name the two possibilities. TP, possible account compromise, possible policy exploit.
  • Document the chain in the ticket. The chain is the audit trail. The CISO reads the ticket, not the chat.
  • Policy gaps are the SOC's most underrated finding. An alert that exposes a policy gap is worth more than the alert itself. Fix the gap, not just the symptom.
  • Page the on-call with the chain, not the feelings. "Possible account compromise, new geo, MFA bypass, root cause is policy gap" is the page. The on-call wakes up to the chain.

Exercise 1 — Translate the alert triage

Translate the discussion excerpt into natural Chinese.

"30 seconds on a P1 alert I just triaged. Asset: PAYMENTS-DB-02, payments database, Crown Jewel, PCI-DSS scope. User: Maria Chen, payments operator, MFA enrolled, last login 11:00 HKT from Shanghai office IP. Exercise 2 — Spot the triage mistake

Which line would damage the triage the most? Why?

  1. "Asset: TREAS-MGR-01, Crown Jewel, PCI-DSS scope. User: Kevin, MFA enrolled, HK office. Behaviour: 5 fails, 1 success, new geo."
  2. "The alert looks bad, I think it's a breach, we should panic."
  3. "Conditional access policy has a 'TREAS-ADMIN' exclusion — that's the policy gap."
  4. "Verdict: true positive, possible account compromise, possible policy gap exploit."

② is the triage-damager. "The alert looks bad, I think it's a breach, we should panic" replaces the chain with feelings. The team lead will always bounce this back: which asset, which user, which behaviour, which evidence. ① walks the chain. ③ names the policy gap. ④ gives the verdict + two possibilities. ② is the analyst's failure; it signals no chain.

Exercise 3 — Run a 30-minute P1 triage

You are a SOC analyst. A P1 fires: "Privileged account used from new geography, MFA approved". Asset: PRIV-ACCESS-GW, privileged access gateway, Crown Jewel. User: Daniel, sysadmin, MFA enrolled, last login 14:00 HKT from London office IP. Behaviour: 2 failed logins from a São Paulo IP at 14:25, then a successful login at 14:27, MFA approved. Build a 30-minute script: (1) walk the chain, (2) name the policy gap, (3) escalate, (4) 3 containment actions, (5) verdict.

"Sarah, 30 seconds on a P1 alert I just triaged. Asset: PRIV-ACCESS-GW, privileged access gateway, Crown Jewel, PCI-DSS scope. User: Daniel, sysadmin, MFA enrolled, last login 14:00 HKT from London office IP. Behaviour: 2 failed logins from a São Paulo IP at 14:25, then a successful login at 14:27, MFA approved. New geography is São Paulo; Daniel is in London. Why did MFA pass on new geo? Step-up auth is enabled but the rule excludes the 'PRIV-ADMIN' group, which Daniel is in. That's the gap. Escalate to P1 incident. Three containment actions. One: revoke Daniel's session, rotate password, force MFA re-enrolment. Two: block the São Paulo IP. Three: remove the 'PRIV-ADMIN' exclusion — root-cause fix, not workaround. Verdict: true positive, possible account compromise, possible policy gap exploit. Chain documented, ticket open, containment in progress."
