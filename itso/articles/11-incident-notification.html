Security Incident Notification Email — Facts, Scope, Containment

Article 11 · Module 3: Written Security Communication · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Regulator, board, customer — facts, scope, containment, next steps

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Friday 16:00 HKT. You are the Head of SOC at a global bank. A confirmed data exposure incident just closed the containment phase. A misconfigured cloud storage bucket exposed 2,300 retail-banking customer records (name, account number, balance — no password, no transaction history) to the public internet for 36 hours before detection. The bucket has been secured, IAM policy corrected, exposure closed at 14:00 HKT. You need to draft a notification email that goes to three audiences: the HKMA supervisor, the Group Board Risk Committee, and the affected customers. The Group Legal and Group Communications have approved the facts; you have 60 minutes to draft the master email and the three variants.

周五 16:00 香港时间，你是全球银行的SOC 负责人。一个已确认的数据暴露事件刚完成遏制阶段。一个云存储桶配置错误导致 2,300 个零售银行客户记录（姓名、账号、余额——无密码、无交易历史）在公网暴露 36 小时后被检测到。存储桶已加固，IAM 策略已修正，14:00 HKT 暴露关闭。你需要起草一封通知邮件发到三个受众：HKMA supervisor、集团董事会风险委员会、受影响客户。集团法务和集团传播已批准事实；你有 60 分钟起草主邮件和三个变体。

An incident notification is the most politically loaded email a security professional writes. The structure must be identical across audiences: facts, scope, containment, regulator exposure, customer impact, next steps. The tone changes by audience, never the facts.

事件通知是安全专业人士写的政治性最强的邮件。结构在所有受众中必须一致：事实、范围、遏制、监管暴露、客户影响、下一步。语气按受众变化，事实不变。

2. The Master Email — 6 Sections, Fixed

To: [audience distribution] · From: Head of SOC · Date: Friday 16:00 HKT · Subject: [CONFIRMED] Retail Banking Customer Data Exposure — Containment Complete, Notification in Progress收件人：[受众列表] · 发件人：SOC 负责人 · 日期：周五 16:00 HKT · 主题：[已确认] 零售银行客户数据暴露——遏制完成，通知进行中
Section 1 — Facts. On Thursday 02:00 HKT, a misconfigured cloud storage bucket exposed 2,300 retail-banking customer records to the public internet. Detection at 14:00 HKT on Friday by the SOC monitoring rule. Containment at 14:00 HKT — bucket secured, IAM policy corrected. Total exposure window: 36 hours.第 1 节——事实。周四 02:00 HKT，一个云存储桶配置错误导致 2,300 个零售银行客户记录在公网暴露。周五 14:00 HKT 由 SOC 监控规则检测到。14:00 HKT 完成遏制——存储桶加固，IAM 策略修正。总暴露窗口：36 小时。
Section 2 — Scope. 2,300 customer records. Data fields: customer name, account number, available balance. No passwords, no transaction history, no payment card data, no identity document numbers were exposed. Affected region: Hong Kong retail-banking only. No other lines of business, no other geographies.第 2 节——范围。2,300 客户记录。数据字段：客户姓名、账号、可用余额。未暴露密码、交易历史、支付卡数据、身份证件号。受影响区域：仅香港零售银行。无其他业务线，无其他地区。
Section 3 — Containment. Bucket secured at 14:00 HKT Friday. IAM policy corrected at 14:15 HKT. Public access revoked. Audit trail confirms no external download during the exposure window — cloud provider access logs show 0 GET requests from non-bank IPs. Forensic review in progress; preliminary conclusion: no evidence of data exfiltration.第 3 节——遏制。14:00 HKT 周五存储桶加固。14:15 HKT IAM 策略修正。公网访问吊销。审计跟踪确认暴露窗口内无外部下载——云厂商访问日志显示非银行 IP 0 次 GET 请求。取证复核进行中；初步结论：无数据外泄证据。
Section 4 — Regulator exposure. PDPO (Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance) notification: the Hong Kong Privacy Commissioner is notified under the data-breach guidance. Initial notification this evening, full notification within 5 business days per the PDPO timeline. HKMA supervisor: proactively notified via the standard supervisory channel — call placed at 15:30 HKT, written follow-up by 17:00 HKT.第 4 节——监管暴露。PDPO（个人资料（私隐）条例）通知：已按数据泄露指南通知香港私隐专员公署。今晚初步通知，5 个工作日内按 PDPO 时间线正式通知。HKMA supervisor：通过标准监管渠道主动通知——15:30 HKT 已通话，17:00 HKT 书面跟进。
Section 5 — Customer impact. No fraud detected to date. Account-level monitoring increased for the 2,300 customers for the next 90 days. Customer notification will be sent by 18:00 HKT Saturday, via secure email and the in-app message centre, with a dedicated hotline and FAQ. No password reset required — passwords were not exposed.第 5 节——客户影响。至今无欺诈检测。2,300 客户未来 90 天账户级监控加强。客户通知 18:00 HKT 周六发出，通过安全邮件和应用内消息中心，含专属热线和 FAQ。无需重置密码——密码未暴露。
Section 6 — Next steps. Three workstreams. Workstream 1: forensic and root-cause analysis, closure 26 September. Workstream 2: preventive control — automated cloud config compliance scan, daily, closure 31 October. Workstream 3: lessons learned for the next Cyber Risk Forum, closure 15 October. Progress reported daily to the CISO; next regulator update Friday 26 September.第 6 节——下一步。三个工作流。工作流 1：取证和根因分析，9 月 26 日关闭。工作流 2：预防控制——云配置合规自动扫描，每日，10 月 31 日关闭。工作流 3：为下次 Cyber Risk Forum 起草经验教训，10 月 15 日关闭。进度每日报 CISO；下次监管更新 9 月 26 日周五。

3. The Three Variants — Tone, Not Facts

The master email is sent to all three audiences. The variant is the salutation line and the closing, not the body.

主邮件发给三个受众。变体是称呼行和落款，不是正文。

  • To HKMA supervisor: Dear Mr. Chan, Following our call at 15:30 HKT, please find the written confirmation below. Regards, Head of SOC, cc: Group CISO, Group Legal, Head of Compliance.
  • To Group Board Risk Committee: To the Board Risk Committee, please find below the formal notification of a confirmed retail-banking customer data exposure, as required by the Group Cyber Incident Reporting Standard. Submitted by: Head of SOC, endorsed by: Group CISO.
  • To affected customers (2,300): Dear [Customer Name], we are writing to inform you of a security incident that involved your account information. We are writing to you directly because we believe in transparency, and because you trust us with your banking. — Head of SOC, cc: Group Communications.

4. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 6 sections, fixed Facts, scope, containment, regulator exposure, customer impact, next steps — in that order. 事实、范围、遏制、监管暴露、客户影响、下一步——按顺序 The 6 sections are the standard for any incident notification. The structure is fixed; the content varies. Skipping a section signals a weak incident.
  • What was NOT exposed No passwords, no transaction history, no payment card data, no identity document numbers were exposed. 未暴露密码、交易历史、支付卡数据、身份证件号 Always name what was NOT exposed. Customers and regulators want to know the limits of the damage.
  • No evidence of exfiltration Cloud provider access logs show 0 GET requests from non-bank IPs. Preliminary conclusion: no evidence of data exfiltration. 云厂商访问日志显示非银行 IP 0 次 GET 请求。初步结论：无数据外泄证据 "No evidence of exfiltration" is the precise phrase. "No exfiltration" alone is over-confident; "no evidence" is the right tone for an early finding.
  • Proactively notified HKMA supervisor proactively notified via the standard supervisory channel. HKMA supervisor 通过标准监管渠道主动通知 "Proactively notified" is the phrase that signals the bank called first. The regulator remembers who called first.
  • Three workstreams Three workstreams: forensic, preventive control, lessons learned. 三个工作流：取证、预防控制、经验教训 A confirmed incident always has three workstreams: forensic (what happened), preventive (stop it happening again), lessons (close the loop).
  • Tone, not facts The variant is the salutation and the closing, not the body. The master body is identical for all three audiences. Only the salutation and the closing change. The facts must never vary.

5. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Confirmed incident n. 确认事件 An incident that has been triaged and confirmed as a real security event.
  • Containment phase n. 遏制阶段 The first phase of incident response: stop the bleeding.
  • Misconfigured bucket n. 配置错误存储桶 A cloud storage resource with public access enabled by mistake.
  • Exposure window n. 暴露窗口 The time between misconfiguration and containment.
  • No evidence of exfiltration n. 无外泄证据 The precise early finding before forensic completion.
  • PDPO (Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance) n. 个人资料（私隐）条例 Hong Kong's data protection law.
  • Supervisory channel n. 监管渠道 The named communication path to a regulator.
  • Proactively notified n. 主动通知 The bank called the regulator first, before being asked.
  • Account-level monitoring n. 账户级监控 Enhanced monitoring on specific accounts for a period.
  • Customer notification n. 客户通知 Direct communication to the affected data subjects.
  • Lessons learned n. 经验教训 Post-incident review for the next Cyber Risk Forum.
  • Variant n. 变体 The same body with different salutation / closing, never different facts.

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 6 sections, fixed order. Facts, scope, containment, regulator exposure, customer impact, next steps. The CISO reads the structure, not the prose.
  • Name what was NOT exposed. Customers and regulators want the limits of the damage. "No passwords" is more reassuring than "2,300 records".
  • "No evidence of exfiltration" is the precise phrase. "No exfiltration" is over-confident; "no evidence" is the right tone for an early finding.
  • "Proactively notified" is the magic phrase. The bank called the regulator first, before being asked. The regulator remembers who called first.
  • Three workstreams, every confirmed incident. Forensic, preventive control, lessons learned. The CISO expects the trio; missing one signals an immature SOC.
  • Tone varies, facts never. The master body is identical for all three audiences. Only the salutation and the closing change.
  • Get Legal and Comms approval before sending. Facts are pre-approved; the variant is on top of the approved facts. Never send an incident email solo.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the incident notification

Translate the master email into natural Chinese.

"Section 1 — Facts. On Saturday 03:00 HKT, a misconfigured API on the customer portal exposed 1,800 wealth-management customer records to authenticated users from other customer accounts. Detection at 11:00 HKT Saturday by the SOC monitoring rule. Containment at 11:15 HKT — API secured, IAM policy corrected. Total exposure window: 8 hours 15 minutes. Section 2 — Scope. 1,800 customer records. Data fields: customer name, account number, portfolio summary, last login date. No passwords, no transaction history, no payment card data, no identity document numbers were exposed. Affected region: Singapore wealth-management only. Section 3 — Containment. API secured at 11:15 HKT Saturday. IAM policy corrected at 11:30 HKT. Cross-account access revoked. Audit trail confirms no external access — only authenticated users from other customer accounts, who by design could see their own data, not others. Forensic review in progress; preliminary conclusion: no evidence of cross-account data exfiltration by an attacker. Section 4 — Regulator exposure. PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) notification: the Singapore PDPC is notified under the data-breach notification. Initial notification this evening, full notification within 3 business days. MAS supervisor: proactively notified via the standard supervisory channel — call placed at 12:00 HKT, written follow-up by 14:00 HKT. Section 5 — Customer impact. No fraud detected to date. Account-level monitoring increased for the 1,800 customers for the next 90 days. Customer notification will be sent by 18:00 HKT Sunday, via secure email and the in-app message centre, with a dedicated hotline and FAQ. No password reset required — passwords were not exposed. Section 6 — Next steps. Three workstreams. Workstream 1: forensic and root-cause analysis, closure 5 October. Workstream 2: preventive control — automated API permission review, daily, closure 30 November. Workstream 3: lessons learned for the next Cyber Risk Forum, closure 15 October. Progress reported daily to the CISO; next regulator update Friday 3 October."

"第 1 节——事实。周六 03:00 HKT，客户门户上一个 API 配置错误导致 1,800 个财富管理客户记录对其他客户账户的认证用户暴露。周六 11:00 HKT 由 SOC 监控规则检测到。11:15 HKT 完成遏制——API 加固，IAM 策略修正。总暴露窗口：8 小时 15 分。第 2 节——范围。1,800 客户记录。数据字段：客户姓名、账号、组合摘要、上次登录日期。未暴露密码、交易历史、支付卡数据、身份证件号。受影响区域：仅新加坡财富管理。第 3 节——遏制。11:15 HKT 周六 API 加固。11:30 HKT IAM 策略修正。跨账户访问吊销。审计跟踪确认无外部访问——仅其他客户账户的认证用户，他们按设计只能看自己的数据，看不到别人。取证复核进行中；初步结论：无攻击者跨账户数据外泄证据。第 4 节——监管暴露。PDPA（个人数据保护法）通知：已通知新加坡 PDPC。初步通知今晚，3 个工作日内正式通知。MAS supervisor：通过标准监管渠道主动通知——12:00 HKT 已通话，14:00 HKT 书面跟进。第 5 节——客户影响。至今无欺诈检测。1,800 客户未来 90 天账户级监控加强。客户通知 18:00 HKT 周日发出，通过安全邮件和应用内消息中心，含专属热线和 FAQ。无需重置密码——密码未暴露。第 6 节——下一步。三个工作流。工作流 1：取证和根因分析，10 月 5 日关闭。工作流 2：预防控制——API 权限自动复核，每日，11 月 30 日关闭。工作流 3：下次 Cyber Risk Forum 经验教训，10 月 15 日关闭。进度每日报 CISO；下次监管更新 10 月 3 日周五。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the incident email mistake

Which line would damage the incident notification the most? Why?

  1. "Facts: misconfigured bucket, 2,300 records, exposure 36 hours, containment 14:00 HKT. Scope: name, account, balance. NOT exposed: passwords, transaction history, card data, ID numbers."
  2. "We had a security issue and we are really sorry and we are working on it."
  3. "Regulator exposure: PDPO notification filed, HKMA supervisor proactively notified via standard channel at 15:30 HKT."
  4. "Three workstreams: forensic 26 Sep, preventive control 31 Oct, lessons learned 15 Oct. Daily progress to CISO."

② is the credibility-damager. "We had a security issue and we are really sorry" replaces the 6 sections with an apology. The regulator and the board want the facts, the scope, the containment, the regulator exposure, the customer impact, and the next steps — not feelings. ① leads with facts and what was NOT exposed. ③ covers regulator exposure with proactive notification. ④ names the three workstreams. ② is an immature incident response; the regulator will write a finding.

Exercise 3 — Draft a 6-section incident email

You are the Head of SOC. A confirmed phishing campaign compromised 12 employee mailboxes at a UK subsidiary, no customer data, contained at 11:00 GMT today. Build the 6 sections: (1) facts, (2) scope, (3) containment, (4) regulator exposure (ICO + FCA), (5) customer impact, (6) next steps with 3 workstreams.

"Section 1 — Facts. On Tuesday 02:00 GMT, a targeted phishing campaign compromised 12 employee mailboxes at the UK retail-banking subsidiary. Detection at 10:30 GMT by the SOC monitoring rule. Containment at 11:00 GMT — credentials revoked, sessions invalidated, MFA factors reset, endpoints isolated. Total exposure window: 8 hours. Section 2 — Scope. 12 employee mailboxes. No customer accounts accessed. No customer data exposed. Affected region: UK retail-banking only. Section 3 — Containment. Credentials revoked at 11:00 GMT. Sessions invalidated. MFA factors reset. Endpoints isolated and reimaged. Audit trail confirms no lateral move, no data exfiltration, no fraudulent transactions. Forensic review in progress; preliminary conclusion: no evidence of data exfiltration. Section 4 — Regulator exposure. ICO (Information Commissioner's Office) — no personal data breach, no notification required. FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) — proactively notified via the standard supervisory channel; call placed at 11:30 GMT, written follow-up by 13:00 GMT. PRA — same notification path as FCA. Section 5 — Customer impact. No customer data exposed. No customer notification required. Account-level monitoring increased for any customer transactions originated by the 12 employees in the exposure window. Section 6 — Next steps. Three workstreams. Workstream 1: forensic and root-cause analysis, closure 25 September. Workstream 2: preventive control — phishing-resistant MFA (FIDO2) for the 12 employees and rolling to the rest of UK retail, closure 30 November. Workstream 3: lessons learned for the next Cyber Risk Forum, closure 15 October. Progress reported daily to the CISO; next regulator update Friday 26 September."
