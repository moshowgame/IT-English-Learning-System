Risk Acceptance Memo — Time-Bound, Named Owner, Compensating Control

Article 12 · Module 3: Written Security Communication · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Residual risk, time-bound, named owner, sign-off chain, compensating control

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 11:00 HKT. You are the Group Cyber Risk Lead. The Customer Onboarding 2026 programme has requested a waiver from the Group MFA Standard for the new instant account-opening mobile flow. The business case: market research shows that adding a second factor at sign-up will drop the conversion rate by 18% during the launch window. The waiver request is for 9 months, ending when the FIDO2 biometric flow ships. The Group CISO has asked you to draft the Risk Acceptance Memo for the CISO + CRO joint sign-off. The Group MFA Standard cannot be waived without a memo.

周三 11:00 香港时间，你是集团网络风险负责人。2026 客户开户项目群请求集团 MFA 标准的豁免，用于新的即时开户移动流程。商业理由：市场研究表明注册时增加第二因素会使转化率在发布窗口期下降 18%。豁免请求期限为 9 个月，在 FIDO2 生物识别流程上线时结束。集团 CISO 要求你为 CISO + CRO 联合签批起草风险接受备忘录。没有备忘录，集团 MFA 标准不能豁免。

A risk acceptance is not a rubber stamp. It is a time-bound, named-owner, compensating-control agreement that puts the business owner on the hook for the residual risk. The memo must be defensible to the regulator, the auditor, and the board three years from now.

风险接受不是橡皮图章。它是有时限、有具名负责人、有补偿控制的协议，让业务负责人对剩余风险负责。备忘录必须在三年后对监管、审计、董事会都可辩护。

2. The Memo — 8 Mandatory Sections

To: Group CISO, Group CRO · From: Group Cyber Risk Lead · Date: Wednesday 11:00 HKT · Subject: [FOR SIGN-OFF] Customer Onboarding 2026 — Time-Bound Waiver of Group MFA Standard for Instant Account-Opening Flow (9 months, 31 Mar 2027 expiry)收件人：集团 CISO、集团 CRO · 发件人：集团网络风险负责人 · 日期：周三 11:00 HKT · 主题：[待签批] 2026 客户开户——即时开户流程集团 MFA 标准的有时限豁免（9 个月，2027 年 3 月 31 日到期）
Section 1 — Standard being waived. Group MFA Standard, version 4.2, section 3.1: "All new customer-facing authentication flows must use multi-factor authentication at sign-up, with at least one phishing-resistant factor." The instant account-opening flow on the new mobile app uses single-factor (email + password) at sign-up, with MFA applied only at the first high-value transaction (>$10,000 HKD).第 1 节——被豁免的标准。集团 MFA 标准 4.2 版第 3.1 节："所有新的客户面身份认证流程必须在注册时使用多因素认证，至少包含一个抗钓鱼因素。"新移动应用的即时开户流程在注册时使用单因素（邮箱 + 密码），MFA 仅在首次高价值交易（> 1 万港币）时启用。
Section 2 — Business justification. Market research, 3 pilot markets (HK, SG, UK), 8,400 simulated sign-ups: adding a second factor at sign-up drops the conversion rate by 18%. Projected loss at 18% conversion drop: 62,000 onboarded customers in the launch window (9 months), estimated revenue impact: $14.8M annualised. The instant flow is the bank's competitive response to three digital-only competitors.第 2 节——商业理由。市场研究，3 个试点市场（HK、SG、UK），8,400 次模拟注册：注册时增加第二因素使转化率下降 18%。18% 转化率下降的预计损失：发布窗口（9 个月）流失 6.2 万开户客户，预计收入影响：年化 1,480 万美元。即时流程是银行对三家纯数字竞争对手的竞争响应。
Section 3 — Residual risk. Inherent risk: account takeover (ATO) at sign-up, using credentials harvested from public breaches. Likelihood: medium (credentials available for ~1.2B accounts in public breach corpora). Impact: medium-low (no funds at sign-up; first transaction triggers MFA + identity verification). Net residual risk rating: Medium (was High before compensating controls).第 3 节——剩余风险。固有风险：注册时的账户接管（ATO），使用公网泄露中获取的凭证。可能性：中（公网泄露语料中约 12 亿账户凭证可用）。影响：中低（注册时无资金；首次交易触发 MFA + 身份验证）。净剩余风险评级：中（补偿控制前为高）。
Section 4 — Compensating controls. Control 1: device fingerprint + behavioural biometrics at sign-up (vendor: BioCatch, integrated, live since 1 August). Control 2: MFA enforced at first high-value transaction (>$10K HKD) and at first outbound transfer. Control 3: enhanced account-level monitoring for the first 30 days (SOC rules in production). Control 4: weekly credential-stuffing watch via the Group Threat Intel feed.第 4 节——补偿控制。控制 1：注册时设备指纹 + 行为生物识别（厂商：BioCatch，已集成，8 月 1 日上线）。控制 2：首次高价值交易（> 1 万港币）和首次对外转账时强制 MFA。控制 3：前 30 天加强账户级监控（SOC 规则已投产）。控制 4：通过集团威胁情报源每周观察撞库情况。
Section 5 — Time-bound. Waiver effective 1 September 2026 to 31 March 2027 (7 months). [Correction: original request was 9 months; reduced to 7 months after review.] Expiry trigger: the FIDO2 biometric sign-up flow lands in M5 (target 31 March 2027). If FIDO2 slips, a re-acceptance memo is required 30 days before expiry. No automatic renewal.第 5 节——时限。豁免有效期 2026 年 9 月 1 日至 2027 年 3 月 31 日（7 个月）。[修正：原始请求 9 个月，审核后减为 7 个月。]到期触发：FIDO2 生物识别注册流程在 M5 上线（目标 2027 年 3 月 31 日）。若 FIDO2 推迟，到期前 30 天需重新签批备忘录。不自动续期。
Section 6 — Named owner. Risk owner: Sarah Chen, Head of Digital Onboarding, owns the residual risk and the compensating control operation. Control owner: David Liu, Head of Identity & Access Management, owns the FIDO2 delivery. Both names on the memo; both must sign.第 6 节——具名负责人。风险负责人：Sarah Chen，数字开户负责人，对剩余风险和补偿控制运营负责。控制负责人：David Liu，IAM 负责人，对 FIDO2 交付负责。两个名字在备忘录上；都必须签批。
Section 7 — Sign-off chain. Step 1: Risk owner (Sarah Chen) signs to acknowledge residual risk. Step 2: Control owner (David Liu) signs to commit to FIDO2 delivery date. Step 3: Group CISO signs to acknowledge the standard waiver. Step 4: Group CRO signs to acknowledge the residual risk rating. Step 5: Group Legal counter-signs for the Group Policy exception register.第 7 节——签批链。步骤 1：风险负责人（Sarah Chen）签字确认剩余风险。步骤 2：控制负责人（David Liu）签字承诺 FIDO2 交付日期。步骤 3：集团 CISO 签字确认标准豁免。步骤 4：集团 CRO 签字确认剩余风险评级。步骤 5：集团法务副签纳入集团政策例外登记簿。
Section 8 — Monitoring and revocation. Monthly review at the Cyber Risk Forum: ATO attempts, conversion rate, MFA bypass incidents, compensating control health. Revocation triggers: (a) any confirmed ATO incident originating from the sign-up flow, (b) any compensating control below SLA for 2 consecutive months, (c) FIDO2 delivery slips by more than 60 days. Revocation = instant MFA enforcement on the sign-up flow, effective within 24 hours of the trigger.第 8 节——监控和撤销。Cyber Risk Forum 每月复核：ATO 尝试、转化率、MFA 绕过事件、补偿控制健康度。撤销触发：（a）任何确认的源自注册流程的 ATO 事件，（b）任何补偿控制连续 2 个月低于 SLA，（c）FIDO2 交付推迟超过 60 天。撤销= 立即在注册流程启用 MFA，触发后 24 小时内生效。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Standard being waived Group MFA Standard, version 4.2, section 3.1 — name the standard and the section. 集团 MFA 标准 4.2 版第 3.1 节——指明标准和章节 Always cite the standard version and section. "The MFA rule" is not a memo. "Group MFA Standard v4.2 §3.1" is a memo.
  • Residual risk rating Net residual risk rating: Medium (was High before compensating controls). 净剩余风险评级：中（补偿控制前为高） Always name the rating before AND after compensating controls. The delta is the value of the controls.
  • Time-bound Waiver effective 1 September 2026 to 31 March 2027 (7 months). No automatic renewal. 豁免有效期 2026 年 9 月 1 日至 2027 年 3 月 31 日（7 个月），不自动续期 "Time-bound" is the operative word. A waiver without an expiry is not a waiver — it is a permanent standard change.
  • Named owner Risk owner: Sarah Chen. Control owner: David Liu. Both names on the memo. 风险负责人：Sarah Chen。控制负责人：David Liu。两个名字在备忘录上 "Named owner" is the legal hook. If something goes wrong, the regulator and the court ask: whose name was on the memo?
  • Compensating control Four compensating controls: device fingerprint, MFA at first high-value transaction, enhanced monitoring, credential-stuffing watch. 四个补偿控制：设备指纹、首次高价值交易 MFA、加强监控、撞库观察 Compensating controls are not "nice to have". They are the reason the residual drops from High to Medium. List them; measure them; report them.
  • Revocation trigger Revocation triggers: (a) any confirmed ATO incident, (b) any compensating control below SLA for 2 months, (c) FIDO2 slips by 60 days. 撤销触发：（a）任何确认的 ATO 事件，（b）任何补偿控制连续 2 个月低于 SLA，（c）FIDO2 推迟 60 天 Revocation triggers are the auto-kill switch. If the conditions are met, the waiver dies — no meeting required.
  • Re-acceptance memo If FIDO2 slips, a re-acceptance memo is required 30 days before expiry. No automatic renewal. 若 FIDO2 推迟，到期前 30 天需重新签批备忘录。不自动续期 "Re-acceptance" is the discipline. A waiver that auto-renews is a permanent exception. Force the memo cycle.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Risk Acceptance Memo n. 风险接受备忘录 The formal document for accepting a standard waiver.
  • Standard waiver n. 标准豁免 A formal exception to a Group standard, time-bound and named.
  • Residual risk n. 剩余风险 The risk that remains after compensating controls.
  • Inherent risk n. 固有风险 The risk before any controls are applied.
  • Compensating control n. 补偿控制 A control that reduces risk when the primary control is not in place.
  • Time-bound n. 有时限 An exception with a clear expiry date and no auto-renewal.
  • Named owner n. 具名负责人 A specific person, not a team, on the hook for the risk or the control.
  • Sign-off chain n. 签批链 The sequence of approvers, named in order, with their roles.
  • Revocation trigger n. 撤销触发 A pre-agreed condition that auto-kills the waiver.
  • Re-acceptance memo n. 重新签批备忘录 A new memo required if the waiver needs to extend past expiry.
  • Policy exception register n. 政策例外登记簿 Group Legal's log of all approved exceptions to Group standards.
  • FIDO2 n. FIDO2 标准 Phishing-resistant authentication standard (passkeys, hardware keys).

5. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 8 sections, fixed order. Standard, business justification, residual risk, compensating controls, time-bound, named owner, sign-off chain, monitoring/revocation. The CISO reads the structure, not the prose.
  • Cite the standard with version and section. "Group MFA Standard v4.2 §3.1" is a memo. "The MFA rule" is a request.
  • Residual risk rating — before AND after controls. The delta is the value of the compensating controls. Without the delta, the controls look decorative.
  • Time-bound means expiry date AND no auto-renewal. A waiver that auto-renews is a permanent standard change in disguise.
  • Named owner — risk AND control. Two names: the risk owner (the business) and the control owner (the engineer). Both sign. If you can only get one name, you don't have a memo.
  • Revocation triggers must be auto, not discretionary. "If the CISO decides" is not a trigger. "If ATO count > 0" is a trigger.
  • Group Legal counter-signs for the exception register. The memo goes into the Group Policy Exception Register. That register is what the regulator and the auditor read three years from now.

6. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the memo header

Translate the memo header into natural Chinese.

"To: Group CISO, Group CRO. From: Group Cyber Risk Lead. Date: Thursday 14:30 HKT. Subject: [FOR SIGN-OFF] Wholesale Banking 2026 — Time-Bound Waiver of Group DLP Standard for Trader Workstations (6 months, 30 April 2027 expiry). Standard being waived: Group DLP Standard, version 2.1, section 5.4 — USB removable media must be blocked on all bank-managed endpoints."

"收件人：集团 CISO、集团 CRO。发件人：集团网络风险负责人。日期：周四 14:30 HKT。主题：[待签批] 2026 批发银行——交易员工作站集团 DLP 标准的有时限豁免（6 个月，2027 年 4 月 30 日到期）。被豁免的标准：集团 DLP 标准 2.1 版第 5.4 节——所有银行管理端点必须屏蔽 USB 可移动介质。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the memo mistake

Which line would damage the risk acceptance memo the most? Why?

  1. "Standard being waived: Group MFA Standard v4.2 §3.1. Residual risk: Medium. Compensating controls: 4. Time-bound: 7 months, expiry 31 Mar 2027. Named owner: Sarah Chen."
  2. "We need to waive the MFA rule because the business wants it and the launch is in 2 weeks. The CISO can sign this for us."
  3. "Revocation triggers: (a) any confirmed ATO, (b) any compensating control below SLA for 2 months. Revocation = instant MFA, 24-hour effect."
  4. "Sign-off chain: risk owner, control owner, Group CISO, Group CRO, Group Legal."

② is the credibility-damager. "We need to waive the MFA rule because the business wants it" replaces the 8 sections with a demand. The CISO and CRO will not sign a memo that names no standard, no residual rating, no compensating controls, no expiry, no named owner, and no revocation trigger. ① cites the standard, the residual rating, the controls, the time-bound, the named owner. ③ names the revocation triggers. ④ names the sign-off chain. ② is a request, not a memo.

Exercise 3 — Build a risk acceptance memo

The Cloud Migration programme wants to disable Group Encryption Standard §2.3 (FIPS 140-2 validated modules only) for 3 pilot services in HK for 6 months to use a non-FIPS cloud HSM. Build the 8 sections: (1) standard, (2) business justification, (3) residual risk, (4) compensating controls, (5) time-bound, (6) named owner, (7) sign-off chain, (8) monitoring/revocation.

"Section 1 — Standard. Group Encryption Standard v3.1 §2.3: 'All cryptographic modules used in bank systems must be FIPS 140-2 validated (level 2 or above).' The 3 pilot services will use a cloud HSM that is FIPS 140-2 level 1 (validated) but level 2 (hardware tamper-resistance) is in roadmap for Q4 2027. Section 2 — Business justification. The non-FIPS-level-2 HSM is $1.8M cheaper over 3 years than the FIPS-level-2 equivalent; the 3 pilot services are non-customer-facing internal data pipelines; FIPS level 2 in roadmap by Q4 2027. Section 3 — Residual risk. Inherent risk: medium (key material exposure if HSM is tampered with). Likelihood: low (cloud HSM is physically secured by the provider; no customer data). Impact: medium. Net residual: Low-Medium. Section 4 — Compensating controls. Control 1: cloud HSM is in a physically secured, audited facility with no bank staff access. Control 2: encryption keys are rotated every 30 days, automated. Control 3: HSM access logs streamed to SIEM in real time, SOC monitoring rules in place. Control 4: vendor publishes a SOC 2 Type II report annually. Section 5 — Time-bound. 6 months, 1 January 2027 to 30 June 2027. Expiry trigger: FIPS level 2 HSM lands, target Q4 2027. If slips, re-acceptance memo required 30 days before expiry. No auto-renewal. Section 6 — Named owner. Risk owner: James Wong, Head of Cloud Platform, owns the residual risk. Control owner: Priya Patel, Head of Key Management, owns the key rotation and HSM access logs. Section 7 — Sign-off chain. Risk owner (James) → Control owner (Priya) → Group CISO → Group CRO → Group Legal. Section 8 — Monitoring and revocation. Monthly review at the Cyber Risk Forum: HSM access logs, key rotation health, vendor SOC 2 report status. Revocation triggers: (a) any unauthorised HSM access, (b) key rotation below SLA for 2 consecutive months, (c) FIPS level 2 slips by more than 90 days. Revocation = instant migration to FIPS level 2 HSM, 14-day transition window."
