MAS TRM Cyber Hygiene — 14 Domains, Baseline, Attestation

Article 08 · Module 2: Security Governance & Compliance · ITSO English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 MAS TRM, 14 cyber hygiene domains, baseline, attestation, gap closure

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 14:00 SGT. You are the Cyber Hygiene Programme Manager at a global bank's Singapore subsidiary. The MAS TRM (Technology Risk Management) Cyber Hygiene Notice requires the bank to attest against 14 cyber hygiene domains annually, with a baseline of December 2024 and Year-2 closure of all gaps by December 2026. You are 18 months in. The Group CISO is on a call with Mr. Lim, the MAS supervisor, and Susan, the bank's Head of Compliance. You have 45 minutes to walk the 14 domains, the gap status, the closure plan, and the Year-2 attestation.

周二 14:00 新加坡时间，你是全球银行新加坡子公司的网络卫生项目经理。MAS TRM（技术风险管理）网络卫生通知要求银行每年对14 个网络卫生领域进行声明，基线 2024 年 12 月，第 2 年所有差距 2026 年 12 月前关闭。你已经进行 18 个月。集团 CISO 与 Lim 先生（MAS supervisor）和 Susan（银行合规负责人）通话。45 分钟过完 14 个领域、差距状态、关闭计划、第 2 年声明。

MAS TRM Cyber Hygiene is Singapore's most prescriptive cyber regulation. The 14 domains are a closed list; gaps are tracked, attested, and closed on a 2-year cycle. The professional pattern is: 14 domains, baseline, gap count, closure plan, Year-2 attestation.

MAS TRM 网络卫生是新加坡最具体的网络监管。14 个领域是封闭清单；差距跟踪、声明、按 2 年周期关闭。专业模式是：14 领域、基线、差距数、关闭计划、第 2 年声明。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Mr. Lim (MAS supervisor): "Walk me through the 14 cyber hygiene domains. What's the baseline, the gap status today, and the Year-2 closure plan?""请过 14 个网络卫生领域。基线、当前差距状态、第 2 年关闭计划？"
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Baseline December 2024. 14 domains assessed by an external assessor. 11 domains met baseline at the time of assessment. 3 domains had gaps — Domain 5 (patch management), Domain 8 (multi-factor authentication), Domain 11 (third-party risk). Today, all three are in active closure.基线 2024 年 12 月。14 领域由外部评估师评估。11 领域评估时已达标。3 领域有差距——领域 5（补丁管理）、领域 8（多因素认证）、领域 11（第三方风险）。今天 3 个都在积极关闭中。
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Domain 5 — patch management. Baseline gap: 38% of internet-facing assets were outside the 14-day patch SLA. Today: 92% within SLA, up from 38%, with 8 internet-facing assets still in catch-up. Closure date: 31 December 2026, as committed in the Year-2 plan.领域 5——补丁管理。基线差距：38% 面向互联网资产在 14 天补丁 SLA 外。今天：92% 在 SLA 内，从 38% 上升，8 个面向互联网资产仍在追赶。关闭日期：2026 年 12 月 31 日，按第 2 年计划承诺。
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Domain 8 — multi-factor authentication. Baseline gap: MFA was not enforced on 4 internal admin systems, covering 320 privileged users. Today: MFA enforced on 3 of the 4, covering 240 users. Remaining 1 system is mid-rollout — target 30 September 2026.领域 8——多因素认证。基线差距：4 个内部管理员系统未强制 MFA，覆盖 320 个特权用户。今天：4 个里 3 个已强制 MFA，覆盖 240 用户。剩余 1 个系统部署中——目标 2026 年 9 月 30 日。
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Domain 11 — third-party risk. Baseline gap: 12 critical third-party vendors had no formal cyber risk assessment on file. Today: 9 of the 12 assessed, 3 in progress with on-site reviews scheduled in Q4 2026. Closure date: 31 December 2026.领域 11——第三方风险。基线差距：12 个关键第三方厂商无正式网络风险评估。今天：12 个里 9 个已评估，3 个进行中，2026 年 Q4 现场评估。关闭日期：2026 年 12 月 31 日。
Mr. Lim (MAS supervisor): Domain 8 — the 80 privileged users on the 1 remaining system. How are you managing the risk while the rollout completes?领域 8——剩余 1 个系统上的 80 个特权用户。部署完成前如何管理风险？
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Two compensating controls. One: the 80 users are bound to a privileged access workstation — PAW — since 1 June 2025. Two: session recording and weekly review by the SOC since 1 July 2025. Both are documented in the risk register, with the 30 September 2026 closure as the formal gap-closure date.两个补偿控制。一：80 用户自 2025 年 6 月 1 日起绑定 PAW——特权访问工作站。二：自 2025 年 7 月 1 日起 SOC 录制会话并每周复核。两项都记在风险登记册，2026 年 9 月 30 日为正式差距关闭日期。
Mr. Lim (MAS supervisor): Year-2 attestation — who signs, and when?第 2 年声明——谁签，何时？
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Year-2 attestation by 31 January 2027, signed by the Singapore Country CEO and the Group CISO. Internal Audit provides independent assurance on the 14 domains before the attestation is signed. Evidence pack archived for 7 years per the MAS record-keeping requirement.第 2 年声明 2027 年 1 月 31 日，由新加坡国家 CEO 和集团 CISO 签署。内审对 14 领域提供独立保证在签署声明前完成。证据包按 MAS 记录保存要求归档 7 年。
Susan (Head of Compliance): One process question — if Domain 8 slips past 30 September 2026, what's the escalation path to MAS?一个流程问题——如果领域 8 超过 2026 年 9 月 30 日，到 MAS 的升级路径？
You (Cyber Hygiene PM): Per the Group Cyber Hygiene SOP, if a domain slips past its committed closure date by more than 30 days, we file an early notification to the MAS supervisor — Mr. Lim's office — within 7 days, with a revised closure date and a compensating control update. The Group CISO signs the notification. That's the protocol.按集团网络卫生 SOP，如果某领域超过承诺关闭日期 30 天以上，7 天内向 MAS supervisor——Lim 先生办公室——提交早期通知，附修订关闭日期和补偿控制更新。集团 CISO 签通知。这就是协议。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 14 domains, baseline, gap 14 domains assessed. 11 met baseline. 3 had gaps — Domain 5, 8, 11. 14 领域已评估。11 达标。3 领域有差距——5、8、11 Always lead with the count: 14 domains, baseline met, gap count. The MAS supervisor reads the count.
  • Today vs baseline Domain 5 today: 92% within SLA, up from 38% at baseline. 领域 5 今天：92% 在 SLA 内，从基线 38% 上升 Always quote today vs. baseline. The MAS supervisor reads the trajectory, not the snapshot.
  • Closure date Closure date: 31 December 2026, as committed in the Year-2 plan. 关闭日期：2026 年 12 月 31 日，按第 2 年计划承诺 Always name the closure date, anchored to the Year-2 plan. The MAS supervisor reads the date, not the verb.
  • Compensating control Two compensating controls: PAW binding and SOC session recording. 两个补偿控制：PAW 绑定和 SOC 会话录制 Compensating controls are the answer to a still-open gap. Always name at least one. No control = unmanaged gap.
  • Attestation chain Year-2 attestation by 31 January 2027, signed by Country CEO and Group CISO. 第 2 年声明 2027 年 1 月 31 日，由国家 CEO 和集团 CISO 签署 Always name the date and the two signatories. MAS expects executive accountability, not a programme manager alone.
  • Early notification If a domain slips past committed closure by 30+ days, file an early notification to MAS within 7 days. 如果某领域超过承诺关闭 30 天以上，7 天内向 MAS 提交早期通知 MAS expects early notification, not a surprise at the next supervisory meeting. The protocol is the protection.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore) n. 新加坡金管局 Singapore's central bank and integrated regulator.
  • TRM (Technology Risk Management) n. 技术风险管理 MAS's framework for technology and cyber risk.
  • Cyber Hygiene Notice n. 网络卫生通知 MAS's 2021 notice requiring attestation against 14 cyber hygiene domains.
  • 14 cyber hygiene domains n. 14 网络卫生领域 The closed list: asset inventory, secure config, patch, vulnerability, MFA, access, etc.
  • Baseline n. 基线 The state at a fixed point (Dec 2024) against which Year-2 is measured.
  • Year-2 closure n. 第 2 年关闭 The 2-year deadline (Dec 2026) to close all baseline gaps.
  • Attestation n. 声明 A formal, signed statement that a domain meets the baseline.
  • External assessor n. 外部评估师 The independent firm that assessed the 14 domains at baseline.
  • Compensating control n. 补偿控制 A control that mitigates risk while a formal gap is still open.
  • Independent assurance n. 独立保证 Internal Audit's verification before the executive attestation.
  • Country CEO n. 国家 CEO The named executive accountable for the country subsidiary.
  • Early notification n. 早期通知 A formal notice to MAS before the deadline if a gap will slip.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "X met baseline, Y had gaps" — count, then name

Always lead with the count: 11 met, 3 had gaps. Then name the gap domains. The MAS supervisor reads the count first.

总先报数量：11 达标，3 差距。然后点名差距领域。MAS supervisor 先看数量。

② "Today vs baseline" — quote the trajectory

Always quote today vs. baseline. 92% today, up from 38%. The MAS supervisor reads the trajectory — a 38→92% jump is a strong story; a 38→42% drift is a weak one.

总报今天 vs 基线。今天 92%，从 38% 上升。MAS supervisor 看的是轨迹——38→92% 是好故事，38→42% 是弱故事。

③ "Closure date as committed" — anchor to the plan

Always name the closure date and anchor it to the Year-2 plan. Closure date: 31 Dec 2026, as committed in the Year-2 plan. The MAS supervisor reads the date and the anchor, not the verb.

总报关闭日期并锚定到第 2 年计划。关闭日期：2026 年 12 月 31 日，按第 2 年计划承诺。MAS supervisor 看日期和锚点，不是动词。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 14 domains is a closed list. Don't invent; quote the MAS list. Domain 5, 8, 11 — the supervisor knows the numbers.
  • Baseline + today + closure date, in that order. Three facts, fixed order. The MAS supervisor reads the trio.
  • Quote the trajectory, not the snapshot. 38→92% is a strong story. 92% alone is a snapshot.
  • Compensating control is the answer to a still-open gap. Name at least one. No control = unmanaged gap = audit finding.
  • Attestation needs two signatories. Country CEO + Group CISO. MAS expects executive accountability, not a programme manager alone.
  • Internal Audit before the attestation. Independent assurance is a precondition. The CISO doesn't attest without IA's sign-off.
  • Early notification is the protocol. 30-day slip → 7-day notification. Don't surprise the supervisor at the next meeting.

Exercise 2 — Spot the MAS TRM mistake

Which line would damage the supervisor's confidence the most? Why?

  1. "Baseline December 2024. 14 domains assessed. 11 met baseline. 3 had gaps — Domain 5, 8, 11. Today 92% within SLA, up from 38%."
  2. "We're working on the gaps and we think we'll close them soon."
  3. "Closure date: 31 December 2026, as committed in the Year-2 plan. Attestation by 31 January 2027, signed by Country CEO and Group CISO."
  4. "Compensating control: PAW binding and SOC session recording, documented in the risk register."

② is the credibility-damager. "We're working on the gaps and we think we'll close them" replaces the count, the trajectory, the closure date, and the compensating control with a feeling. The MAS supervisor will always bounce this back: how many gaps, which domains, what is the trajectory, what is the closure date, what is the compensating control. ① leads with count + trajectory. ③ names the date and signatories. ④ names the compensating control. ② is an immature cyber hygiene programme; the supervisor will write a finding.

Exercise 3 — Run a 45-minute MAS TRM walk

You are the Cyber Hygiene PM. 14 domains, baseline Dec 2024, 10 met, 4 had gaps (Domain 2 secure config, Domain 7 access control, Domain 12 incident response, Domain 14 audit logging). Today: 3 of 4 in active closure, Domain 7 just slipped 30 days. Build a 45-minute script: (1) baseline + gap count, (2) 4 gap domains today, (3) Domain 7 early notification, (4) compensating controls, (5) Year-2 attestation.

"Baseline December 2024. 14 domains assessed. 10 met baseline. 4 had gaps — Domain 2, 7, 12, 14. Today, 3 of 4 in active closure. Domain 2 secure config: baseline gap was 18 servers with default config, today 4 remaining, target 30 November 2026. Domain 7 access control: baseline gap was 12 service accounts with no rotation, today 5 remaining — slipped 30 days past committed closure. Early notification filed to MAS on 15 October, revised closure 28 February 2027, Group CISO signed. Compensating control: session recording and weekly SOC review for the 5 accounts. Domain 12 incident response: baseline gap was no formal playbook for ransomware, today playbook published, tabletop exercise scheduled Q1 2027. Domain 14 audit logging: baseline gap was 2 SIEM gaps in the trade finance platform, today 1 remaining, target 31 December 2026. Closure date 31 December 2026 for 3 domains, 28 February 2027 for Domain 7. Year-2 attestation by 31 January 2027 for the 3, supplementary attestation 31 March 2027 for Domain 7. Both signed by Country CEO and Group CISO. Independent assurance from Internal Audit before each attestation."
