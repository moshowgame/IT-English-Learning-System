GDPR Data Subject Request — DSAR, Lawful Basis, 30-Day Clock

Article 07 · Module 2: Security Governance & Compliance · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 DSAR intake, identity verification, lawful basis, 30-day clock, Article 15 / 17

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 09:30 GMT. You are the Privacy Operations Lead at a global bank's European subsidiary. A Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) arrived on Friday at 16:45 GMT from a former retail-banking customer in Frankfurt. The customer is asking for all personal data the bank holds on him, under GDPR Article 15. The statutory 30-day clock started on Friday — 28 days remaining. Joining you: Helena, the DPO (Data Protection Officer), and Tom, the IT lead for the data discovery tool.

周一 9:30 格林威治时间，你是全球银行欧洲子公司的隐私运营负责人。一封数据主体访问请求（DSAR）周五 16:45 GMT 进来，来自法兰克福一位前零售银行客户。该客户依据 GDPR 第 15 条索要银行持有的所有个人数据。法定的 30 天时钟周五开始——还剩 28 天。参加：DPO（数据保护官）Helena、数据发现工具 IT 负责人 Tom。

A DSAR is the most time-pressured piece of privacy work. The 30-day clock is statutory, identity must be verified, the scope must be discovered across systems, and the response must be lawful. The professional pattern is: intake → verify → scope → discover → redact → deliver.

DSAR 是隐私工作中时间压力最大的一项。30 天时钟是法定的，身份必须验证，范围必须跨系统发现，回复必须合法。专业模式是：受理 → 验证 → 定范围 → 发现 → 脱敏 → 交付。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Helena (DPO): "Let's walk the DSAR. Intake summary first.""我们过一下 DSAR。先报受理摘要。"
You (Privacy Ops): DSAR intake: Mr. Stefan Bauer, former retail-banking customer, Frankfurt, relationship closed 14 March 2024. Request received Friday 16:45 GMT via the privacy mailbox. Type: Article 15 access request — all personal data. 30-day clock: started Friday, deadline Friday 17 October, 28 days remaining. No extension requested, no urgency flagged.DSAR 受理：Stefan Bauer 先生，前零售银行客户，法兰克福，关系 2024 年 3 月 14 日关闭。请求周五 16:45 GMT 通过隐私邮箱收到。类型：第 15 条访问请求——所有个人数据。30 天时钟：周五开始，截止 10 月 17 日周五，剩 28 天。无延期请求，无紧急标记。
Helena (DPO): Identity verification? He could be anyone.身份验证呢？他可能任何人。
You (Privacy Ops): Standard 2-step verification, per Group Privacy SOP P-013. Step 1: passport scan received, name and DOB match the closed CIF record. Step 2: video ID check scheduled for Wednesday 14:00 GMT with our KYC team. Expected outcome: verified by Wednesday EOD. If we can't verify, we deny the request under Article 12.6 — inability to verify identity.标准 2 步验证，按集团隐私 SOP P-013。步骤 1：护照扫描已收，姓名和生日匹配已关闭的 CIF 记录。步骤 2：视频身份核验已排周三 14:00 GMT，与 KYC 团队一起。预期结果：周三 EOD 前验证完成。如无法验证，依据第 12.6 条——无法验证身份——拒绝请求。
Tom (IT lead): On data discovery — Mr. Bauer's CIF is MRET-DE-2034-8711. Discovery scope based on the closed-relationship data map. Eight systems in scope. CRM, core banking, payments, KYC, complaints, marketing consent, fraud, and the document archive. Discovery runs Wednesday evening after ID check passes, output in the standard DSAR pack format — JSON for structured data, PDF for documents, both to the secure privacy drop zone.数据发现——Bauer 先生的 CIF 是 MRET-DE-2034-8711。基于已关闭关系数据图的发现范围。8 个系统在范围内。CRM、核心银行、支付、KYC、投诉、营销同意、反欺诈、文档归档。发现于周三晚 ID 检查通过后跑，输出为标准 DSAR 包格式——结构化数据 JSON，文档 PDF，都到安全隐私投递区。
Helena (DPO): Redaction? How do we handle third-party data in his mortgage documents? His ex-wife is on the joint mortgage.脱敏呢？他房贷文件里的第三方数据怎么处理？他前妻在联合房贷上。
You (Privacy Ops): Third-party redaction per Article 15.4. The ex-wife's name, address, DOB, signature, and financial details are redacted — but the fact that there was a joint mortgage is not redacted, because it's material to the data subject's own information. Redaction log kept per the SOP — what was redacted, on what legal basis, by whom.按第 15.4 条做第三方脱敏。前妻的姓名、地址、生日、签名、财务细节脱敏——但"曾有联合房贷"这一事实不脱敏，因为它对数据主体自身信息有实质影响。按 SOP 留脱敏日志——脱敏什么、法律依据、何人操作。
Helena (DPO): And the legal review before delivery?交付前的法务复核呢？
You (Privacy Ops): Standard. Privacy team reviews for redaction correctness and Article exemptions. Legal reviews for privilege, regulator-secrecy, and any third-party rights. Both sign in the privacy workflow tool before delivery to the secure drop zone. Target: both signoffs by Friday 10 October, delivery to Mr. Bauer by Friday 17 October, 3 days ahead of the 30-day clock.标准流程。隐私团队复核脱敏正确性和条款豁免。法务复核特权、监管保密、任何第三方权利。两边在隐私工作流工具签字后交付到安全投递区。目标：10 月 10 日周五两边都签字，10 月 17 日周五交付给 Bauer 先生，比 30 天时钟提前 3 天。
Helena (DPO): One more — if the ex-wife also files a DSAR next week asking for her own data, do we run them in parallel or sequence them?还有——如果前妻下周也提交 DSAR 索要她自己的数据，我们并行还是串行？
You (Privacy Ops): Parallel — two separate DSARs, two separate 30-day clocks, two separate packs. No read-across between data subjects, even if they're on the same mortgage. Each request is independent under the GDPR.并行——两个独立 DSAR，两个独立 30 天时钟，两个独立包。数据主体之间无 read-across，即使在同一房贷上。按 GDPR 每项请求独立。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Intake summary Intake summary first. DSAR intake: name, type, 30-day clock, deadline. 先报受理摘要。DSAR 受理：姓名、类型、30 天时钟、截止日期 Always lead with the intake: who, what type, when the clock started, when it ends. The DPO reads the clock first.
  • 30-day clock 30-day clock started Friday, deadline Friday 17 October, 28 days remaining. 30 天时钟周五开始，截止 10 月 17 日周五，剩 28 天 Always name the start date, the deadline, and the days remaining. The DPO reads the deadline, not the verb.
  • Identity verification Standard 2-step verification per Group SOP P-013. Step 1 passport, Step 2 video ID check. 按集团 SOP P-013 标准 2 步验证。步骤 1 护照，步骤 2 视频身份核验 Identity verification is the gate. Skip it and the entire pack is unlawful. Always name the SOP and the steps.
  • Discovery scope Discovery scope: 8 systems based on the closed-relationship data map. 发现范围：基于已关闭关系数据图，8 个系统 Discovery scope is always derived from a data map, not invented. Name the map and the system count.
  • Third-party redaction Third-party redaction per Article 15.4. The fact is not redacted, the personal data is. 按第 15.4 条做第三方脱敏。事实不脱敏，个人数据脱敏 Distinguish fact from personal data. The fact of a joint mortgage stays; the ex-wife's name, address, financial details are redacted.
  • No read-across Two separate DSARs, no read-across between data subjects. 两个独立 DSAR，数据主体之间无 read-across Each DSAR is independent. The ex-wife's DSAR is not a re-use of the husband's pack. Always re-discover and re-redact.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • DSAR (Data Subject Access Request) n. 数据主体访问请求 A formal request by an individual for access to their personal data.
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) n. 通用数据保护条例 EU regulation on data protection, including the 30-day clock.
  • Article 15 n. 第 15 条 The GDPR article granting the right of access by the data subject.
  • Article 12.6 n. 第 12.6 条 The GDPR article allowing refusal if identity cannot be verified.
  • Article 15.4 n. 第 15.4 条 The GDPR article on third-party redaction in an access response.
  • 30-day clock n. 30 天时钟 The statutory time limit to respond to a DSAR, extendable by 60 days for complex requests.
  • DPO (Data Protection Officer) n. 数据保护官 The named executive accountable for privacy compliance.
  • Identity verification n. 身份验证 Confirming the requester is who they say they are, before disclosure.
  • Data map n. 数据图 The registry of systems and data flows that contain personal data.
  • Discovery scope n. 发现范围 The list of systems searched for the data subject's information.
  • Redaction n. 脱敏 Masking third-party personal data in the disclosure pack.
  • Lawful basis n. 合法依据 The legal grounds under GDPR (consent, contract, legal obligation, etc.) for processing.
  • Secure drop zone n. 安全投递区 The encrypted file transfer location for the disclosure pack.
  • Read-across n. 跨请求复用 Reusing work from one DSAR for another — not allowed between data subjects.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "30-day clock: started X, deadline Y, N days remaining" — three facts, fixed order

Always quote the 30-day clock as three facts. Started Friday, deadline 17 October, 28 days remaining. The DPO reads the deadline, not the request type.

30 天时钟总是报三个事实。周五开始，截止 10 月 17 日，剩 28 天。DPO 读的是截止日期，不是请求类型。

② "Step 1, Step 2" — verification is a chain, in order

Identity verification is a chain in order. Step 1 passport, Step 2 video ID check. Skip a step and the disclosure is unlawful. Always name the SOP and the steps.

身份验证是链，按顺序。步骤 1 护照，步骤 2 视频身份核验。跳过一步等于非法披露。总报 SOP 和步骤。

③ "Fact vs personal data" — distinguish, then redact

Distinguish fact from personal data. The fact is not redacted, the personal data is. The fact of a joint mortgage stays; the ex-wife's name, address, financial details are redacted. Article 15.4 permits this.

区分事实和个人数据。事实不脱敏，个人数据脱敏。"曾有联合房贷"是事实，保留；前妻的姓名、地址、财务细节是个人数据，脱敏。第 15.4 条允许这样做。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Lead with the intake: who, what, when the clock started, when it ends. The DPO reads the clock first.
  • The 30-day clock is statutory. Always name start date, deadline, days remaining. The DPO scans the deadline before the rest.
  • Identity verification is the gate. Skip it and the disclosure is unlawful. Use the Group SOP, name the steps.
  • Discovery scope comes from the data map, not from memory. Name the map and the system count. The auditor reads the map.
  • Distinguish fact from personal data in redaction. Fact stays, personal data redacted. Article 15.4 permits this.
  • Two DSARs, two clocks, two packs. No read-across between data subjects, even on the same mortgage. Each request is independent.
  • Deliver ahead of the clock. Aim for 3 days early. The buffer absorbs the unexpected.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the DSAR walk

Translate the discussion excerpt into natural Chinese.

"Intake summary. DSAR intake: Ms. Anna Müller, current wealth-management customer, Munich, account active. Request received Tuesday 10:15 CET via the privacy mailbox. Type: Article 15 access request + Article 17 erasure request — all personal data, plus erasure at end of relationship. 30-day clock: started Tuesday, deadline Tuesday 18 November, 30 days remaining. Identity verification: standard 2-step per SOP P-013. Step 1: passport scan received, name and DOB match the active CIF. Step 2: video ID check scheduled for Thursday 11:00 CET with KYC team. Discovery scope: 10 systems based on the active-relationship data map — CRM, core banking, payments, KYC, tax, complaints, marketing consent, fraud, document archive, and the wealth-platform. Discovery runs Thursday evening. Redaction: third-party per Article 15.4 — her financial advisor's name and notes are redacted; the fact that she has an advisor is not redacted. Erasure scope: Article 17, but with Article 17.3 exceptions — regulatory retention 7 years for KYC and tax, so those systems keep the records. The CRM, marketing consent, and complaint notes are erased. Two separate packs: access pack delivered 15 November, erasure confirmation delivered 18 November, both ahead of the clock."

"受理摘要。DSAR 受理：Anna Müller 女士，现有财富管理客户，慕尼黑，账户活跃。请求周二 10:15 CET 通过隐私邮箱收到。类型：第 15 条访问请求 + 第 17 条删除请求——所有个人数据，关系结束时删除。30 天时钟：周二开始，截止 11 月 18 日周二，剩 30 天。身份验证：按 SOP P-013 标准 2 步。步骤 1：护照扫描已收，姓名和生日匹配活跃 CIF。步骤 2：视频身份核验已排周四 11:00 CET，与 KYC 团队。发现范围：基于活跃关系数据图，10 个系统——CRM、核心银行、支付、KYC、税务、投诉、营销同意、反欺诈、文档归档、财富平台。发现周四晚跑。脱敏：按第 15.4 条做第三方——她的财务顾问姓名和笔记脱敏；她有顾问这一事实不脱敏。删除范围：第 17 条，但有第 17.3 条豁免——KYC 和税务监管保留 7 年，这些系统保留记录。CRM、营销同意、投诉笔记删除。两个独立包：15 日 11 月交付访问包，18 日 11 月交付删除确认，都提前于时钟。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the DSAR mistake

Which line would damage the DSAR response the most? Why?

  1. "30-day clock: started Friday, deadline 17 October, 28 days remaining. Identity verification per SOP P-013, 2 steps."
  2. "We'll get the data when we can and send it to him."
  3. "Discovery scope: 8 systems based on the closed-relationship data map. Redaction per Article 15.4. Two separate packs, two clocks."
  4. "Target: both signoffs by Friday 10 October, delivery 17 October, 3 days ahead of the clock."

② is the DSAR-damager. "We'll get the data when we can" replaces the 30-day clock, the identity verification, the discovery scope, the redaction, and the signoffs with a vague promise. The DPO will always bounce this back: when does the clock end, who verified the identity, what is the discovery scope. ① leads with the clock and the SOP. ③ names the scope and the Article. ④ commits to a target ahead of the clock. ② is unlawful; the regulator would fine the bank.

Exercise 3 — Run a 30-minute DSAR intake

You are the Privacy Ops lead. A DSAR arrives: Article 15 access request from a former corporate-banking customer in London, relationship closed 6 months ago, request received Monday 11:00 GMT. Build a 30-minute script: (1) intake summary, (2) 30-day clock, (3) identity verification SOP, (4) discovery scope, (5) third-party redaction principle, (6) delivery target.

"Intake summary. DSAR intake: Mr. James Chen, former corporate-banking customer, London, relationship closed 6 months ago. Request received Monday 11:00 GMT via the privacy mailbox. Type: Article 15 access request — all personal data. 30-day clock: started Monday, deadline Monday 1 December, 30 days remaining. Identity verification: standard 2-step per SOP P-013. Step 1: passport scan expected, name and DOB match the closed CIF. Step 2: video ID check scheduled for Wednesday. Discovery scope: 6 systems based on the closed-relationship data map — CRM, core banking, payments, KYC, complaints, document archive. Redaction: third-party per Article 15.4 — counterparty names and signatures in the corporate loan documents are redacted; the fact of the loan is not redacted. Target: discovery Thursday, signoff Friday week 2, delivery Friday 28 November, 3 days ahead of the clock."
