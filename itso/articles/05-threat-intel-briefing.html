Threat Intel Briefing — APT, TTP, IOC, Sector Alert

Article 05 · Module 1: Daily Security Operations · ITSO English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 APT groups, sector-specific TTPs, IOC sharing, sector alerts, hunt plan

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 14:00 HKT. You are the Threat Intelligence Lead at a global bank. The weekly Threat Intel Briefing is on the calendar with the CISO, Head of SOC, and Head of IAM. This week, FS-ISAC has raised the sector alert level for the Asia-Pacific banking sector from Elevated to High, citing two APT campaigns targeting treasury and trade-finance operations. You have 30 minutes to walk the intel, the relevance to the bank, and the hunt plan.

周三 14:00 香港时间，你是全球银行的威胁情报负责人。每周威胁情报简报已安排 CISO、SOC 负责人、IAM 负责人参加。本周 FS-ISAC 把亚太银行业的部门警报级别从提升调至高，理由是两个 APT 行动针对资金和贸易融资业务。30 分钟过完情报、对银行的相关性、猎捕计划。

Threat intel is the SOC's "looking outward" discipline. It is measured in actors, TTPs, IOCs, sector alerts, and hunt hypotheses. The professional pattern is: lead with the sector alert, name the actor, walk the TTP, push the IOC, propose a hunt.

威胁情报是 SOC "向外看"的纪律。用攻击者、TTP、IOC、部门警报、猎捕假设度量。专业模式是：先报部门警报、点名攻击者、过 TTP、推 IOC、提议猎捕。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Threat Intel Lead): Threat intel update from me. Sector alert level raised from Elevated to High for APAC banking, driven by two APT campaigns — APT-41, a China-nexus actor, and APT-38, a North Korea-nexus actor. Both targeting treasury and trade finance.威胁情报层面同步。APAC 银行业部门警报级别从提升调至高，驱动因素是两个 APT 行动——APT-41，中国相关，APT-38，朝鲜相关。两家都针对资金和贸易融资。
You (Threat Intel Lead): First campaign: APT-38. Target: SWIFT operator workstations in trade finance. TTP: spear-phishing on a treasury operator, lateral move to the SWIFT workstation, creation of a fraudulent MT-103 message, wipe of the local logs. IOCs: 3 sender domains, 5 file hashes, 1 C2 IP, all pushed to the SIEM and EDR at 11:00.第一个行动：APT-38。目标：贸易融资的 SWIFT 操作员工作站。TTP：针对资金操作员的鱼叉钓鱼，横向移动到 SWIFT 工作站，伪造 MT-103 报文，清除本地日志。IOC：3 个发件域、5 个文件 hash、1 个 C2 IP，11:00 已全部推送到 SIEM 和 EDR。
You (Threat Intel Lead): Second campaign: APT-41. Target: the customer portal and the API gateway. TTP: exploit of CVE-2025-31872 — the same one in our open Critical queue — web shell, credential theft via the LSASS dump, persistence via a scheduled task. IOCs: 2 web shell paths, 4 scheduled task names, 2 C2 domains, pushed at 12:30.第二个行动：APT-41。目标：客户门户和 API 网关。TTP：利用 CVE-2025-31872——和我们开放严重队列里那个一样——Web shell，通过 LSASS 转储盗取凭据，通过计划任务持久化。IOC：2 个 web shell 路径、4 个计划任务名、2 个 C2 域，12:30 已推送。
CISO: Hold on — APT-41 is exploiting CVE-2025-31872, which is the same one Brian is patching on Friday? That changes the priority.等一下——APT-41 正在利用 CVE-2025-31872，就是 Brian 周五打补丁的那个？这改变优先级。
You (Threat Intel Lead): Yes. Three implications. One: Brian moves 31872 to tonight's change window, not Friday — the patch is ready, the change is small, the risk is active exploitation in the sector. Two: the WAF rule for the 31872 exploit pattern is already pushed — we confirmed active at 13:00. Three: we run a 48-hour hunt on the customer portal and API gateway, looking for the web shell paths and the scheduled task names from the APT-41 IOC list.是。三个影响。一：Brian 把 31872 移到今晚的变更窗口，不是周五——补丁已就绪、变更小、风险是行业级活跃利用。二：31872 利用模式的 WAF 规则已推——13:00 确认激活。三：我们针对客户门户和 API 网关跑 48 小时猎捕，查 APT-41 IOC 列表里的 web shell 路径和计划任务名。
Head of SOC: Confirmed — 48-hour hunt on the customer portal and API gateway, two analysts dedicated, starting 16:00 today. I'll report any hits in the SOC standup.确认——客户门户和 API 网关 48 小时猎捕，两名分析师专职，今天 16:00 启动。任何命中在 SOC 站会汇报。
Head of IAM: From the IAM side, two actions. One: force password reset on all treasury and trade-finance operators today. Two: review MFA enrolment for the same population — anyone without MFA, suspend until enrolled. That's 240 users, 8 hours to complete.IAM 这边，两项动作。一：今天强制重置所有资金和贸易融资操作员密码。二：复核同人群的 MFA 注册——任何未注册 MFA 的，停用直到注册。共 240 用户，8 小时完成。
CISO: Both approved. One last ask — share the APT-41 IOC list with FS-ISAC and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority under the standard protocol. And update the bank's standing threat profile for the next Cyber Risk Forum.两项都批。最后一个请求——按标准协议把 APT-41 IOC 列表分享给 FS-ISAC 和香港金管局。并把银行的常设威胁画像更新到下次 Cyber Risk Forum。
You (Threat Intel Lead): Confirmed. IOCs shared with FS-ISAC and HKMA by 16:00, threat profile updated for the next forum.确认。16:00 前 IOC 分享给 FS-ISAC 和 HKMA，威胁画像更新到下次论坛。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Sector alert Sector alert level raised from Elevated to High for APAC banking. APAC 银行业部门警报级别从提升调至高 Always lead with the sector alert level and the change. The CISO reads the level, not the actor.
  • TTP TTP: spear-phishing, lateral move, fraudulent MT-103, wipe of local logs. TTP：鱼叉钓鱼、横向移动、伪造 MT-103、清除本地日志 TTP = Tactics, Techniques, Procedures. Quote the chain in order. The CISO reads the chain, not the verb.
  • IOC push IOCs pushed to SIEM and EDR at 11:00. 11:00 IOC 已推送到 SIEM 和 EDR Always name the time of the IOC push. The SOC needs the timestamp to confirm the IOC is live.
  • Active exploitation Risk is active exploitation in the sector — patch tonight, not Friday. 风险是行业级活跃利用——今晚打补丁，不是周五 "Active exploitation in the sector" is the phrase that overrides a planned schedule. Use it to accelerate a patch.
  • Hunt Run a 48-hour hunt on the customer portal, looking for the web shell paths and scheduled task names. 针对客户门户跑 48 小时猎捕，查 web shell 路径和计划任务名 A hunt is time-bounded (48 hours), scoped (customer portal), and IOC-driven (web shell paths, task names). Always name all three.
  • Threat profile Update the bank's standing threat profile for the next Cyber Risk Forum. 把银行的常设威胁画像更新到下次 Cyber Risk Forum The standing threat profile is the bank's long-term read of the threat landscape. Update after every sector alert change.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • APT (Advanced Persistent Threat) n. 高级持续性威胁 A state-sponsored or well-resourced attacker with long-term objectives.
  • TTP (Tactics, Techniques, Procedures) n. 战术/技术/过程 How an attacker operates — phishing, lateral move, persistence, exfiltration.
  • IOC (Indicator of Compromise) n. 失陷指标 Artefact (domain, IP, hash, file path) that signals a compromise.
  • FS-ISAC n. 金融服务信息共享与分析中心 Financial Services Information Sharing and Analysis Center — sector intel sharing.
  • Sector alert n. 部门警报 A formal warning from FS-ISAC about threats to the financial sector.
  • Spear-phishing n. 鱼叉钓鱼 Targeted phishing at a specific individual or role.
  • Lateral move n. 横向移动 Attacker moving from one compromised host to another in the network.
  • Web shell n. Web 后门 A malicious script dropped on a web server for remote command execution.
  • LSASS dump n. LSASS 转储 Dumping the Local Security Authority Subsystem Service process to extract credentials.
  • Scheduled task persistence n. 计划任务持久化 Using a Windows scheduled task to re-establish access after reboot.
  • C2 (Command and Control) n. 命令与控制 The attacker-controlled server the malware calls home to.
  • Hunt n. 猎捕 A time-bounded, IOC-driven proactive search for attacker presence.
  • Threat profile n. 威胁画像 The bank's long-term read of the threat landscape — actors, TTPs, sector risks.
  • SWIFT / MT-103 n. SWIFT / MT-103 国际资金报文系统 / 客户汇款报文 SWIFT network / standard customer credit transfer message.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Sector alert level raised from X to Y" — name the level and the change

Always name the level and the change. From Elevated to High. The CISO reads the level, not the actor list. The level is the headline.

总报级别和变化。从提升到高。CISO 读的是级别，不是攻击者列表。级别才是头条。

② "IOCs pushed at HH:MM" — name the time of the push

Always name the time of the IOC push. IOCs pushed to SIEM and EDR at 11:00. The SOC needs the timestamp to confirm the IOC is live in the platform.

总报 IOC 推送时间。11:00 IOC 已推送到 SIEM 和 EDR。SOC 需要时间戳确认 IOC 在平台里是激活的。

③ "Active exploitation in the sector" — the override phrase

"Active exploitation in the sector" is the phrase that overrides a planned schedule. Risk is active exploitation in the sector — patch tonight, not Friday. The CISO approves the override because the sector signal is louder than the local schedule.

"行业级活跃利用"是覆盖计划进度的关键短语。风险是行业级活跃利用——今晚打补丁，不是周五。CISO 批准覆盖，因为部门信号比本地计划响。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Lead with the sector alert level. The CISO reads the level, not the actor list. The level is the headline.
  • TTP is the chain, in order. Quote the chain: phishing → lateral move → objective → log wipe. The CISO reads the chain, not the verb.
  • Always name the time of the IOC push. 11:00 / 12:30. The SOC needs the timestamp to confirm the IOC is live.
  • "Active exploitation in the sector" overrides the schedule. Use the phrase to accelerate a planned patch. The CISO approves the override because the sector signal is louder.
  • A hunt is time-bounded, scoped, IOC-driven. 48 hours / customer portal / web shell paths. Always name all three.
  • Share the IOC with FS-ISAC and the local regulator. Sector intel is a two-way street. The bank both consumes and contributes.
  • Update the standing threat profile after every sector alert change. The threat profile is the bank's long-term read. Without it, the next forum has no anchor.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the threat intel briefing

Translate the discussion excerpt into natural Chinese.

"Threat intel update. Sector alert level raised from Elevated to High for APAC banking, driven by two APT campaigns — APT-29, a Russia-nexus actor, and APT-33, an Iran-nexus actor. Both targeting wealth management and private banking. First campaign: APT-29. Target: relationship manager workstations in private banking. TTP: spear-phishing on a relationship manager, lateral move to the workstation, deploy of a custom RAT, exfiltration of client data over DNS tunneling. IOCs: 4 sender domains, 6 file hashes, 3 DNS tunneling patterns, pushed to SIEM and EDR at 13:00. Second campaign: APT-33. Target: the wealth platform and the client reporting API. TTP: exploit of CVE-2025-50112 — the same one in our open Critical queue — web shell, credential theft via the LSASS dump, persistence via a WMI subscription. IOCs: 3 web shell paths, 5 WMI subscription names, 2 C2 domains, pushed at 14:30. Three implications. One: 50112 moves to tonight's change window, not Friday — risk is active exploitation in the sector. Two: WAF rule for the 50112 exploit pattern pushed — confirmed active at 15:00. Three: 48-hour hunt on the wealth platform and the client reporting API, looking for the web shell paths and WMI subscriptions. From IAM: two actions. Force password reset on all wealth and private banking RMs today. Review MFA enrolment — anyone without MFA, suspend until enrolled. That's 180 users, 6 hours to complete. Share the APT-33 IOC list with FS-ISAC and HKMA under the standard protocol. Update the bank's standing threat profile for the next Cyber Risk Forum."

"威胁情报层面同步。APAC 银行业部门警报级别从提升调至高，驱动因素是两个 APT 行动——APT-29，俄罗斯相关，APT-33，伊朗相关。两家都针对财富管理和私人银行。第一个行动：APT-29。目标：私人银行的客户经理工作站。TTP：针对客户经理的鱼叉钓鱼，横向移动到工作站，部署定制 RAT，通过 DNS 隧道外泄客户数据。IOC：4 个发件域、6 个文件 hash、3 个 DNS 隧道模式，13:00 推送到 SIEM 和 EDR。第二个行动：APT-33。目标：财富平台和客户报告 API。TTP：利用 CVE-2025-50112——和我们开放严重队列里那个一样——web shell，通过 LSASS 转储盗取凭据，通过 WMI 订阅持久化。IOC：3 个 web shell 路径、5 个 WMI 订阅名、2 个 C2 域，14:30 已推送。三个影响。一：50112 移到今晚变更窗口，不是周五——风险是行业级活跃利用。二：50112 利用模式的 WAF 规则已推——15:00 确认激活。三：财富平台和客户报告 API 跑 48 小时猎捕，查 web shell 路径和 WMI 订阅。IAM 两项：今天强制重置所有财富和私人银行 RM 密码；复核 MFA 注册——未注册 MFA 的停用直到注册。共 180 用户，6 小时完成。按标准协议把 APT-33 IOC 分享给 FS-ISAC 和 HKMA。把银行的常设威胁画像更新到下次 Cyber Risk Forum。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the threat intel mistake

Which line would damage the briefing the most? Why?

  1. "Sector alert level raised from Elevated to High for APAC banking."
  2. "There are some hackers out there and they're attacking banks, we should be careful."
  3. "IOCs: 3 sender domains, 5 file hashes, 1 C2 IP, pushed to SIEM and EDR at 11:00."
  4. "48-hour hunt on the customer portal, looking for web shell paths and scheduled task names."

② is the briefing-damager. "There are some hackers out there" replaces the sector alert level, the actor, the TTP, the IOC, and the hunt with a vague statement. The CISO will always bounce this back: which sector, which actor, which TTP, which IOCs, which hunt. ① leads with the alert level. ③ quotes the IOC push with time. ④ names the hunt scope and IOC. ② signals an immature intel function.

Exercise 3 — Run a 30-minute threat intel briefing

You are the intel lead. FS-ISAC raises APAC banking from Guarded to Elevated, citing one APT campaign: APT-40, China-nexus, targeting correspondent banking. TTP: spear-phishing on a correspondent ops manager, lateral move, deploy of a credential stealer, fraudulent cross-border payment. IOCs: 2 sender domains, 4 file hashes, 1 C2 IP. Build a 30-minute script: (1) sector alert, (2) actor + TTP + IOC, (3) implications, (4) IAM actions, (5) regulator share + threat profile.

"Threat intel update. Sector alert level raised from Guarded to Elevated for APAC banking, driven by one APT campaign — APT-40, China-nexus, targeting correspondent banking. TTP: spear-phishing on a correspondent ops manager, lateral move, deploy of a credential stealer, fraudulent cross-border payment. IOCs: 2 sender domains, 4 file hashes, 1 C2 IP, pushed to SIEM and EDR at 15:00. Three implications. One: WAF rule for the known spear-phishing sender domains — pushed at 15:30, confirmed active. Two: 48-hour hunt on the correspondent banking workstations, looking for credential stealer IOCs. Three: force password reset on all correspondent ops today, MFA review, suspend anyone without MFA until enrolled — 90 users, 4 hours. Share the APT-40 IOC list with FS-ISAC and HKMA by 17:00 under the standard protocol. Update the bank's standing threat profile for the next Cyber Risk Forum."
