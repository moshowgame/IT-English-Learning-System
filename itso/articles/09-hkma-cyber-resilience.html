HKMA Cyber Resilience Assessment — iCAST, Stress Test, RTO / RPO

Article 09 · Module 2: Security Governance & Compliance · ITSO English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 iCAST, stress test, RTO/RPO, recovery, regulator expectations

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Thursday 10:00 HKT. You are the Cyber Resilience Programme Manager at a global bank's Hong Kong subsidiary. The HKMA Cyber Resilience Assessment Framework (C-RAF) is in year 3. The HKMA iCAST (Intelligence-led Cyber Attack Simulation Testing) is scheduled for next quarter, and the Joint Supervisory Team has just sent a list of pre-read documents. You have 60 minutes to walk the 3i maturity assessment status, the RTO / RPO for the 7 critical systems, the iCAST scope, and the regulator's pre-reads. Joining you: Karen, the Head of Regulatory Compliance, and David, the Group Legal Director.

周四 10:00 香港时间，你是全球银行香港子公司的网络韧性项目经理。HKMA 网络韧性评估框架（C-RAF）进入第 3 年。HKMA iCAST（情报主导的网络攻击模拟测试）排在下季度，联合监管组刚发了预读文件清单。60 分钟过完3i 成熟度评估状态、7 个关键系统的RTO / RPO、iCAST 范围、监管预读。参加：监管合规负责人 Karen、集团法务总监 David。

HKMA Cyber Resilience is Hong Kong's most prescriptive cyber regulation for major banks. The framework has 5 pillars and 3 maturity levels. The professional pattern is: 3i maturity, RTO/RPO, iCAST scope, regulator pre-reads.

HKMA 网络韧性是香港对主要银行最具体的网络监管。框架有 5 大支柱和 3 个成熟度等级。专业模式是：3i 成熟度、RTO/RPO、iCAST 范围、监管预读。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Karen (Reg Compliance): "Walk the 3i maturity assessment first. Where are we on the 5 pillars, and what's the gap to the next maturity level?""先过 3i 成熟度评估。5 大支柱我们在哪？距下一成熟度等级的差距？"
You (Cyber Resilience PM): 3i maturity — Intermediate level, per the 2024 self-assessment, externally validated by Group Internal Audit in March 2025. 5 pillars — Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover. Today: Identify is at Intermediate-plus, Protect at Intermediate, Detect at Intermediate, Respond at Intermediate, Recover at Intermediate. Gap to Advanced: concentrated in Detect and Respond — we need full automation of incident triage and threat-hunting capacity.3i 成熟度——中级，按 2024 年自评，2025 年 3 月由集团内审外部验证。5 大支柱——识别、保护、检测、响应、恢复。今天：识别中级偏上，保护中级，检测中级，响应中级，恢复中级。距高级的差距：集中在检测和响应——我们需要事件分流全自动化和威胁猎捕能力。
Karen (Reg Compliance): And the 7 critical systems — what's the RTO / RPO?7 个关键系统——RTO / RPO？
You (Cyber Resilience PM): HKMA's expectation is RTO 2 hours, RPO 15 minutes for Tier-1 systems. Our 7 Tier-1 systems today. Payments — RTO 1 hour, RPO 5 minutes. Core banking — RTO 1 hour, RPO 15 minutes. Trading — RTO 2 hours, RPO 10 minutes. Customer portal — RTO 2 hours, RPO 30 minutes — this one is over the 15-minute RPO target. Trade finance — RTO 4 hours, RPO 1 hour — both over the Tier-1 target, but trade finance is being re-classified to Tier-2 in the new framework. Treasury — RTO 2 hours, RPO 15 minutes. API gateway — RTO 2 hours, RPO 15 minutes. Customer portal RPO is the open gap.HKMA 期望一级系统 RTO 2 小时、RPO 15 分钟。我们的 7 个一级系统今天。支付——RTO 1 小时，RPO 5 分钟。核心银行——RTO 1 小时，RPO 15 分钟。交易——RTO 2 小时，RPO 10 分钟。客户门户——RTO 2 小时，RPO 30 分钟——这个超 15 分钟 RPO 目标。贸易融资——RTO 4 小时，RPO 1 小时——两个都超一级目标，但贸易融资在新框架中正被重新归类为二级。资金——RTO 2 小时，RPO 15 分钟。API 网关——RTO 2 小时，RPO 15 分钟。客户门户 RPO 是开放差距。
David (Group Legal): Customer portal RPO 30 vs target 15 — what's the closure plan?客户门户 RPO 30 vs 目标 15——关闭计划？
You (Cyber Resilience PM): Closure plan: continuous data replication from primary to secondary site, reducing RPO from 30 minutes to 5 minutes, with a 4-hour maintenance window in November 2026. Compensating control today: 4-hour backup at the secondary site, with a documented recovery runbook and a quarterly DR test in September 2026. Closure date 30 November 2026, attested by the platform engineering lead and the CISO.关闭计划：从主站到备站的持续数据复制，把 RPO 从 30 分钟降到 5 分钟，2026 年 11 月 4 小时维护窗口。今日补偿控制：备站 4 小时备份，有书面恢复 runbook，2026 年 9 月季度 DR 测试。关闭日期 2026 年 11 月 30 日，由平台工程负责人和 CISO 声明。
Karen (Reg Compliance): iCAST — what's the scope, and the timeline?iCAST——范围、时间线？
You (Cyber Resilience PM): iCAST is scheduled for Q1 2027, externally facilitated by an approved vendor, scope: Tier-1 systems — payments, core banking, customer portal, API gateway. Scenario: an end-to-end ransomware attack on the customer portal, with lateral movement to the API gateway, exfiltration attempt, and a regulator-mandated recovery time pressure. Tabletop walkthrough next Tuesday, full simulation 15 February 2027.iCAST 排期 2027 年 Q1，由批准厂商外部主持，范围：一级系统——支付、核心银行、客户门户、API 网关。情景：针对客户门户的端到端勒索攻击，横向移动到 API 网关，外泄尝试，加监管要求的恢复时间压力。桌面演练下周二，完整模拟 2027 年 2 月 15 日。
David (Group Legal): And the regulator's pre-reads? What have they asked for, and when?监管预读呢？他们要什么，何时要？
You (Cyber Resilience PM): Six documents requested by the Joint Supervisory Team. One: 3i maturity self-assessment, externally validated, due 31 October. Two: RTO / RPO register for the 7 Tier-1 systems, due 31 October. Three: the customer portal RPO closure plan, due 31 October. Four: iCAST scope and scenario, due 15 November. Five: DR test reports for the last 12 months, due 15 November. Six: attestation pack signed by the Country CEO and the Group CISO, due 15 December.联合监管组要 6 份文件。一：3i 成熟度自评（外部验证），10 月 31 日交。二：7 个一级系统的 RTO / RPO 登记册，10 月 31 日交。三：客户门户 RPO 关闭计划，10 月 31 日交。四：iCAST 范围和情景，11 月 15 日交。五：过去 12 个月的 DR 测试报告，11 月 15 日交。六：由国家 CEO 和集团 CISO 签署的声明包，12 月 15 日交。
Karen (Reg Compliance): All on track, with named owners and weekly progress to the CISO. Anything for next week?全部按计划，有指定 owner，每周给 CISO 进度。下周有什么？
You (Cyber Resilience PM): Two items. One: the iCAST tabletop walkthrough next Tuesday, with the platform engineering lead, the SOC lead, the CISO, and an external facilitator. Two: a draft of the 3i maturity self-assessment for Karen's review, by Friday EOD.两项。一：下周二 iCAST 桌面演练，和平台工程负责人、SOC 负责人、CISO、外部主持人一起。二：周五 EOD 前给 Karen 一份 3i 成熟度自评草稿审阅。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • 3i maturity 3i maturity — Intermediate level, per the 2024 self-assessment, externally validated by Group IA in March 2025. 3i 成熟度——中级，按 2024 年自评，2025 年 3 月由集团内审外部验证 Always name the level, the self-assessment date, and the external validation. The HKMA reads the validation, not the self-assessment alone.
  • RTO / RPO Payments — RTO 1 hour, RPO 5 minutes. Customer portal — RTO 2 hours, RPO 30 minutes, this one is over target. 支付——RTO 1 小时，RPO 5 分钟。客户门户——RTO 2 小时，RPO 30 分钟，这个超目标 Always quote RTO and RPO per system. The HKMA reads the table, not the average.
  • Compensating control Compensating control: 4-hour backup at the secondary site, with quarterly DR test. 补偿控制：备站 4 小时备份，季度 DR 测试 Compensating control is the answer to an RPO / RTO gap. Always name at least one.
  • iCAST scope iCAST scope: Tier-1 systems — payments, core banking, customer portal, API gateway. Scenario: ransomware on the customer portal, lateral move to API gateway, exfiltration. iCAST 范围：一级系统——支付、核心银行、客户门户、API 网关。情景：客户门户勒索，横向移动到 API 网关，外泄 iCAST scope is always system + scenario. Name both. The HKMA reads the scenario, not just the system list.
  • Pre-reads Six documents requested by the Joint Supervisory Team, with named due dates. 联合监管组要 6 份文件，有指定截止日期 Pre-reads are never a vague list. Always name the document, the owner, the due date.
  • On track All on track, with named owners and weekly progress to the CISO. 全部按计划，有指定 owner，每周给 CISO 进度 "On track" is binary. Always name the owners and the cadence. "We're working on it" is not on track.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • HKMA (Hong Kong Monetary Authority) n. 香港金管局 Hong Kong's integrated banking supervisor.
  • C-RAF (Cyber Resilience Assessment Framework) n. 网络韧性评估框架 HKMA's framework for major banks' cyber resilience.
  • iCAST (Intelligence-led Cyber Attack Simulation Testing) n. 情报主导的网络攻击模拟测试 HKMA's threat-led red-team exercise.
  • 3i maturity n. 3i 成熟度 HKMA's three-level rating: Initial, Intermediate, Advanced.
  • 5 pillars n. 5 大支柱 Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover.
  • Tier-1 system n. 一级系统 A critical system whose disruption would have systemic impact.
  • RTO (Recovery Time Objective) n. 恢复时间目标 Maximum acceptable time to restore service after an incident.
  • RPO (Recovery Point Objective) n. 恢复点目标 Maximum acceptable data loss measured in time.
  • Joint Supervisory Team n. 联合监管组 HKMA-led team that conducts the C-RAF assessment.
  • Continuous replication n. 持续复制 Real-time or near-real-time data copy from primary to secondary site.
  • DR test n. 灾难恢复测试 Scheduled exercise to validate the recovery runbook.
  • Tabletop walkthrough n. 桌面演练 A discussion-based rehearsal of an incident scenario.
  • Externally facilitated n. 外部主持 Run by an approved independent vendor, not internal staff.
  • Pre-read n. 预读 Documents the regulator requests before the next meeting.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "3i maturity — Intermediate — self-assessment — external validation" — four facts

Always quote 3i maturity as four facts: level, self-assessment date, validation date, validator. The HKMA reads the validation, not the self-assessment alone.

3i 成熟度总是报四个事实：等级、自评日期、验证日期、验证方。HKMA 看的是验证，不是仅看自评。

② "RTO X, RPO Y" — quote per system

Always quote RTO and RPO per system. Payments — RTO 1, RPO 5. Customer portal — RTO 2, RPO 30. The HKMA reads the table, not the average.

每个系统单独报 RTO 和 RPO。支付——RTO 1、RPO 5。客户门户——RTO 2、RPO 30。HKMA 看的是表格，不是平均数。

③ "On track with named owners and weekly cadence" — the binary close

"On track" is binary, not vague. Always name the owners and the cadence. All on track, with named owners and weekly progress to the CISO. "We're working on it" is not on track.

"按计划"是二元的，不是模糊的。总报 owner 和节奏。全部按计划，有指定 owner，每周给 CISO 进度。"我们在做"不算按计划。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • 3i maturity is four facts, in order. Level, self-assessment, validation, validator. Skip the validation and the regulator doesn't believe the level.
  • RTO / RPO per system, not the average. The HKMA reads the table. The worst system is the gap.
  • Compensating control is the answer to an RPO / RTO gap. Name at least one. No control = unmanaged gap.
  • iCAST scope = systems + scenario. Name both. The scenario is the test; the system list is the scope.
  • Pre-reads are never vague. Always name the document, the owner, the due date. The regulator scans the table.
  • "On track" with named owners and weekly cadence. Binary close. "We're working on it" is not on track.

Exercise 2 — Spot the HKMA mistake

Which line would damage the C-RAF walk the most? Why?

  1. "3i maturity — Intermediate, 2024 self-assessment, externally validated by Group IA in March 2025. Payments RTO 1, RPO 5."
  2. "We are doing OK on resilience, no major concerns."
  3. "Customer portal RPO 30 vs target 15, closure plan: continuous replication, RPO 5, November 2026 maintenance window."
  4. "iCAST scope: Tier-1 systems, ransomware scenario, tabletop Tuesday, full simulation 15 Feb 2027. Six pre-reads, all on track."

② is the credibility-damager. "We are doing OK on resilience" replaces the 3i maturity, the 5 pillars, the RTO/RPO, the iCAST scope, and the pre-reads with a feeling. The HKMA will always bounce this back: which level, which pillar, which system, which RTO/RPO, which pre-read. ① leads with the 3i facts and the system RTO/RPO. ③ names the gap and the closure. ④ covers iCAST and pre-reads. ② is an immature cyber resilience programme; the regulator will write a finding.
