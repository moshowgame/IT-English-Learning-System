Pushing Back on a Security Waiver — Yes, But, with Compensating Control

Article 16 · Module 4: Difficult Security Conversations · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 Yes-but, compensating control, time-bound, named residual risk, no permanent waiver

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 14:00 HKT. You are the Group Cyber Risk Lead. The Wholesale Banking Modernisation programme has submitted a waiver request to disable the Group Database Activity Monitoring (DAM) Standard for the new real-time trade-settlement database. The business case: DAM adds 4 milliseconds of latency per transaction, and the real-time flow is contractually committed at 20 ms. The waiver is requested indefinitely — "for the lifetime of the platform". The Group CISO has asked you to push back at the waiver review meeting. The Programme Director, Tom, expects the waiver to be approved in 30 minutes.

周二 14:00 香港时间，你是集团网络风险负责人。批发银行现代化项目群提交了豁免请求，禁用新实时交易结算数据库的集团数据库活动监控（DAM）标准。商业理由：DAM 每笔交易增加 4 毫秒延迟，而实时流合同承诺 20 毫秒。豁免请求无固定期限——"平台生命周期内"。集团 CISO 要求你在豁免评审会上反对。项目群总监 Tom 期望豁免 30 分钟内批准。

Pushing back on a waiver is a "yes, but" conversation. The instinct is to say no and lose the relationship. The professional pattern is: acknowledge the latency, propose a compensating control, insist on time-bound, name the residual risk, never accept "indefinite". The programme gets a path; the bank keeps the standard.

反对豁免是"yes, but"对话。本能是说 no 失去关系。专业模式是：承认延迟、提出补偿控制、坚持时限、具名剩余风险、绝不接受"无固定期限"。项目群得到路径；银行保留标准。

2. The Waiver Review — 6 Sections, 30 Minutes

You (Cyber Risk): Tom, thanks for the waiver request. I hear the latency constraint — 20 ms contractual, DAM adds 4 ms, that is a real engineering problem. But the request as written — "indefinite waiver" — I can't recommend that. Let me walk the trade.Tom，感谢你的豁免请求。我听到了延迟约束——合同 20 毫秒，DAM 增加 4 毫秒，这是真实的工程问题。但按现在写的——"无固定期限豁免"——我不能推荐。让我过一下 trade。
Tom: Tom: The 4 ms isn't negotiable. The vendor SLA is 20 ms, and we're already at 16 ms under load. If we add DAM, we miss the SLA and breach the contract.Tom：4 毫秒不可谈。厂商 SLA 是 20 毫秒，我们负载下已经在 16 毫秒。如果加 DAM，我们就超出 SLA 违约。
You (Cyber Risk): Understood. Three options. Option A: in-line DAM with sampling — monitor 10% of transactions, statistical sampling, adds under 1 ms of latency. Catches 95% of anomalies by statistical design. Option B: out-of-band DAM via database audit log streaming — logs streamed to the SIEM, monitored asynchronously, zero latency on the transaction path. Catches 100% of audit-log events but with 30-second delay. Option C: in-line DAM for non-peak hours only — 22:00 HKT to 07:00 HKT, full DAM on; peak hours, sampling only.明白。三个方案。方案 A：内联 DAM 采样——监控 10% 交易，统计采样，增加不到 1 毫秒延迟。统计设计上捕获 95% 异常。方案 B：通过数据库审计日志流的外带 DAM——日志流到 SIEM，异步监控，交易路径零延迟。捕获 100% 审计日志事件但有 30 秒延迟。方案 C：仅非高峰时段内联 DAM——22:00 HKT 至 07:00 HKT，全量 DAM；高峰时段仅采样。
Tom: Tom: Option B works for the latency. 30-second delay is fine for our use case. But the original request was "indefinite".Tom：方案 B 在延迟上可行。30 秒延迟对我们的用例可以。但原始请求是"无固定期限"。
You (Cyber Risk): On the "indefinite" — I can't recommend that. Group standards are not waived indefinitely. The risk is the standard drifts over time: Option B is a compensating control, not a replacement. If we accept "indefinite" today, we never come back to the question. Counter-proposal: time-bound waiver, 18 months, expiring 31 March 2028. At expiry, two outcomes are possible — (a) the platform is re-platformed and in-line DAM is feasible, (b) a re-acceptance memo is required, with new evidence. Either way, the question comes back.关于"无固定期限"——我不能推荐。集团标准不会无固定期限豁免。风险是标准随时间漂移：方案 B 是补偿控制，不是替代品。如果我们今天接受"无固定期限"，我们永远不会回到这个问题。反建议：有时限豁免，18 个月，2028 年 3 月 31 日到期。到期时，两种可能的结果——（a）平台重新搭建，内联 DAM 可行，（b）需要重新签批备忘录，含新证据。无论哪种，问题会回来。
Tom: Tom: 18 months works. What else?Tom：18 个月可以。还有呢？
You (Cyber Risk): Three more things. Named owner: risk owner is you, Tom — you own the residual risk of Option B. Control owner is David Liu, Head of IAM — he owns the SIEM rule and the audit log integrity. Residual risk rating: Medium, with 30-second detection delay on any anomalous DB activity. Revocation triggers: (a) any anomalous DB activity that requires more than 30 seconds to detect, (b) the SIEM rule below SLA for 2 consecutive months, (c) the 18-month expiry. Revocation = switch to Option A or C within 14 days.三件事。具名负责人：风险负责人是你，Tom——你对方案 B 的剩余风险负责。控制负责人是 David Liu，IAM 负责人——他对 SIEM 规则和审计日志完整性负责。剩余风险评级：中，对任何异常 DB 活动有 30 秒检测延迟。撤销触发：（a）任何异常 DB 活动需要超过 30 秒检测，（b）SIEM 规则连续 2 个月低于 SLA，（c）18 个月到期。撤销 = 14 天内切换到方案 A 或 C。
Tom: Tom: Agreed to all three. When do we sign?Tom：三件都同意。什么时候签？
You (Cyber Risk): I'll draft the Risk Acceptance Memo today — 8 sections, as per the standard template. Sign-off chain: you, David, Group CISO, Group CRO, Group Legal. Target: signed by Friday 17:00 HKT.我今天起草风险接受备忘录——8 个章节，按标准模板。签批链：你、David、集团 CISO、集团 CRO、集团法务。目标：周五 17:00 HKT 前签批。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • I hear the latency constraint I hear the latency constraint — 20 ms contractual, DAM adds 4 ms. 我听到了延迟约束——合同 20 毫秒，DAM 增加 4 毫秒 Always open by acknowledging the engineering constraint. "I hear it" is the line that prevents the meeting from becoming a fight.
  • Compensating control, not replacement Option B is a compensating control, not a replacement. 方案 B 是补偿控制，不是替代品 This is the precise language. A compensating control is a substitute for the standard; a replacement would be a new standard. Banks only do compensating controls by exception.
  • I can't recommend "indefinite" On the "indefinite" — I can't recommend that. 关于"无固定期限"——我不能推荐 "I can't recommend" is the polite refusal. The CISO is the approver; the Cyber Risk Lead is the recommender. The recommender's refusal forces the conversation to a different shape.
  • Counter-proposal Counter-proposal: time-bound waiver, 18 months, expiring 31 March 2028. 反建议：有时限豁免，18 个月，2028 年 3 月 31 日到期 A counter-proposal replaces "indefinite" with a concrete date. The date is what the conversation now negotiates.
  • Either way, the question comes back Either way, the question comes back. 无论哪种，问题会回来 "The question comes back" is the line that prevents permanent drift. A time-bound waiver is a question that the bank keeps asking.
  • Residual risk rating Residual risk rating: Medium, with 30-second detection delay on any anomalous DB activity. 剩余风险评级：中，对任何异常 DB 活动有 30 秒检测延迟 Always name the rating AND the delay. The rating is the headline; the delay is the trade. The CISO reads both.
  • Revocation triggers Revocation triggers: (a) any anomaly requiring more than 30 seconds to detect, (b) SIEM rule below SLA for 2 months, (c) 18-month expiry. 撤销触发：（a）任何异常需要超过 30 秒检测，（b）SIEM 规则连续 2 个月低于 SLA，（c）18 个月到期 Three triggers, all auto. The waiver dies when any one fires — no meeting required. That's the discipline.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • Waiver n. 豁免 A formal exception to a Group standard, time-bound and signed.
  • Indefinite waiver n. 无固定期限豁免 A waiver with no expiry — the bank never comes back to the question.
  • Compensating control n. 补偿控制 A control that substitutes for the waived control, accepted by exception.
  • Time-bound waiver n. 有时限豁免 A waiver with a clear expiry date and no auto-renewal.
  • Risk Acceptance Memo n. 风险接受备忘录 The formal document that records the waiver, owners, and triggers.
  • Residual risk rating n. 剩余风险评级 The risk that remains after the compensating control is in place.
  • Revocation trigger n. 撤销触发 A pre-agreed condition that auto-kills the waiver.
  • Counter-proposal n. 反建议 A specific alternative offered in place of the original request.
  • DAM (Database Activity Monitoring) n. 数据库活动监控 The control that records and alerts on DB queries.
  • In-line DAM n. 内联 DAM DAM that sits on the transaction path, adding latency.
  • Out-of-band DAM n. 外带 DAM DAM that monitors asynchronously via audit log streaming.
  • Audit log streaming n. 审计日志流 Database audit events sent to a SIEM in real time.

5. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Yes-but is the professional pattern. Acknowledge the constraint, propose a compensating control, insist on time-bound, name the residual risk. The business gets a path; the bank keeps the standard.
  • "I can't recommend 'indefinite'" is the polite refusal. The recommender's refusal forces the conversation to a different shape. Saying "no" directly damages the relationship; saying "I can't recommend" preserves it.
  • Compensating control, not replacement. Banks do compensating controls by exception. A replacement would require a new standard — a much longer process.
  • Time-bound is non-negotiable. An indefinite waiver is a permanent standard change in disguise. Always insist on a date.
  • Named owner — risk AND control. Two names: the programme director (risk) and the IAM head (control). Both sign. Without names, the waiver is not a waiver.
  • Revocation triggers must be auto, not discretionary. Three pre-agreed conditions, any one of which auto-kills the waiver. "If the CISO decides" is not a trigger.
  • Either way, the question comes back. This is the line that prevents permanent drift. A time-bound waiver is a question the bank keeps asking.

6. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the opening lines

Translate the opening lines into natural Chinese.

"Tom, thanks for the waiver request. I hear the latency constraint — 20 ms contractual, DAM adds 4 ms, that is a real engineering problem. But the request as written — 'indefinite waiver' — I can't recommend that. Let me walk the trade. Three options. Option A: in-line DAM with sampling, 10% of transactions, under 1 ms latency. Option B: out-of-band DAM via audit log streaming, zero transaction latency, 30-second detection delay. Option C: in-line DAM for non-peak hours only, 22:00 to 07:00 HKT, sampling on peak hours. Counter-proposal: time-bound waiver, 18 months, expiring 31 March 2028. Either way, the question comes back."

"Tom，感谢你的豁免请求。我听到了延迟约束——合同 20 毫秒，DAM 增加 4 毫秒，这是真实的工程问题。但按现在写的——'无固定期限豁免'——我不能推荐。让我过一下 trade。三个方案。方案 A：内联 DAM 采样，10% 交易，不到 1 毫秒延迟。方案 B：通过审计日志流的外带 DAM，交易路径零延迟，30 秒检测延迟。方案 C：仅非高峰时段内联 DAM，22:00 至 07:00 HKT，高峰时段仅采样。反建议：有时限豁免，18 个月，2028 年 3 月 31 日到期。无论哪种，问题会回来。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the waiver pushback mistake

Which line would damage the waiver pushback the most? Why?

  1. "I hear the latency constraint. Option B works for the latency. Compensating control, not a replacement. Time-bound 18 months, expiring 31 March 2028. Named owner: Tom (risk), David (control)."
  2. "No, we cannot waive the DAM standard. The CISO will never approve it. Find another way."
  3. "Revocation triggers: (a) any anomaly requiring more than 30 seconds to detect, (b) SIEM rule below SLA for 2 months, (c) 18-month expiry. Revocation = switch to Option A or C within 14 days."
  4. "Residual risk rating: Medium, with 30-second detection delay. Sign-off chain: Tom, David, Group CISO, Group CRO, Group Legal."

② is the credibility-damager. "No, we cannot waive. Find another way" is a flat refusal with no options, no compensating control, no time-bound, no named owner, and no residual rating. Tom walks out of the meeting with no path; the CISO will hear about the relationship damage. ① acknowledges the constraint, names the option, the time-bound, and the owners. ③ names the triggers and the revocation. ④ names the residual rating and the sign-off chain. ② turns a 30-minute waiver review into an escalation to the CISO.

Exercise 3 — Build a waiver pushback

The Trading Platform team wants to disable Group DLP Standard §3.2 (USB removable media blocking) for 30 trading desks, indefinitely, because the desks need to move large files to air-gapped analysis machines. Build the 6 sections: (1) opening, (2) options, (3) counter-proposal, (4) named owners, (5) residual risk, (6) revocation triggers.

"Sarah, thanks for the waiver request. I hear the file-transfer constraint — large files to air-gapped analysis machines, the corporate file-share is too slow. But 'indefinite' — I can't recommend that. Three options. Option A: encrypted USB drives — bank-issued, hardware-encrypted, with audit logging on insert/eject, no latency impact. Option B: one-way file-transfer diode — hardware device that only allows writes to the air-gapped network, no reads back, zero malware risk. Option C: scheduled batch transfer to a managed file-transfer gateway — runs overnight, 8-hour cycle, audit log on every file. Counter-proposal: time-bound waiver, 12 months, expiring 31 July 2027, with re-acceptance memo required 30 days before expiry. Named owners. Risk owner: Sarah Chen, Head of Trading Platform. Control owner: David Liu, Head of IAM. Both names on the memo. Residual risk: Medium, with hardware-encrypted USB as the primary control. Revocation triggers: (a) any USB drive lost or stolen, (b) any audit log below SLA for 2 months, (c) 12-month expiry. Revocation = instant re-enable of DLP on the 30 trading desks, 24-hour effect."
