PCI-DSS Audit Walkthrough — QSA, ROC, Control Mapping

Article 06 · Module 2: Security Governance & Compliance · ITSO English

⏱ 9 min read 🟠 Advanced 🎯 QSA, ROC, AoC, control mapping, evidence pack, in-scope systems

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Tuesday 09:00 HKT. You are the PCI-DSS Compliance Lead at a global bank. The annual PCI-DSS v4.0 audit is in week 2 of 4. The QSA (Qualified Security Assessor) from the external auditor is on site: Mr. Henderson. Today's walkthrough is on Requirement 8 (Identify Users and Authenticate Access to System Components) and Requirement 10 (Log and Monitor All Access). You have 90 minutes to walk 12 controls, 8 pieces of evidence, and 3 in-scope systems. Joining: Michelle, the IAM lead, and Steve, the SOC lead.

周二 9:00 香港时间，你是全球银行的PCI-DSS 合规负责人。年度PCI-DSS v4.0 审计进入第 2 周（共 4 周）。外审的 QSA（Qualified Security Assessor，合格安全评估师）Henderson 先生在现场。今天的走查是要求 8（识别用户并认证对系统组件的访问）和要求 10（日志记录并监控所有访问）。90 分钟过 12 项控制、8 个证据、3 个范围内系统。参加：IAM 负责人 Michelle、SOC 负责人 Steve。

A QSA walkthrough is the most formal English-speaking test a security professional faces. The QSA's questions are short, the answers must be short, and every claim must be backed by evidence. The professional pattern is: control → test → evidence → result.

QSA 走查是安全专业人士面临的最正式英语测试。QSA 的问题短，回答必须短，每个主张必须有证据支持。专业模式是：控制 → 测试 → 证据 → 结果。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

QSA (Mr. Henderson): "Let's start with Requirement 8.3 — strong authentication for all access into the CDE." Walk me through the control, the test, and the evidence."我们从要求 8.3——CDE 所有访问的强认证开始。"请过一下控制、测试、证据。
You (PCI lead): Control 8.3.1 — all access into the CDE requires MFA. Test: I selected 25 users from the CDE access list, verified each has MFA enrolled, and confirmed the conditional access policy enforces MFA at sign-in. Result: 25 of 25 compliant. Evidence: the IAM report 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf', and the conditional access policy 'CA-CDE-001' exported from the IAM platform.控制 8.3.1——CDE 所有访问要求 MFA。测试：我从 CDE 访问列表抽 25 个用户，核实每个都已注册 MFA，确认条件访问策略在登录时强制 MFA。结果：25/25 合规。证据：IAM 报告 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf'，以及从 IAM 平台导出的条件访问策略 'CA-CDE-001'。
QSA (Mr. Henderson): Good. What about non-console administrative access? Does 8.3.6 also apply?好。非控制台管理员访问呢？8.3.6 也适用吗？
You (PCI lead): Yes. Control 8.3.6 — non-console admin access into the CDE requires MFA plus a privileged access workstation. Test: I pulled the 12 CDE admins from the privileged access gateway, verified each is bound to a PAW, and confirmed the gateway session recording is active. Result: 12 of 12 compliant. Evidence: the PAM report 'CDE_Admin_PAW_Binding_2025Q3.xlsx', and a sample session recording 'SR-2025-09-12-004' for one of the admins.是。控制 8.3.6——CDE 非控制台管理员访问要求 MFA 加特权访问工作站。测试：我从特权访问网关拉 12 个 CDE 管理员，核实每个都绑定 PAW，确认网关会话录制激活。结果：12/12 合规。证据：PAM 报告 'CDE_Admin_PAW_Binding_2025Q3.xlsx'，以及一名管理员的样本会话录制 'SR-2025-09-12-004'。
QSA (Mr. Henderson): And Requirement 10.2 — audit log events. What events are you logging, and where?要求 10.2——审计日志事件。你们记录哪些事件，存哪里？
Steve (SOC lead): We log all 10.2.1 events — user access, root/admin actions, access to audit logs, invalid logical access attempts, changes to identification / authentication mechanisms, and modifications to audit logs — to the SIEM, with a 13-month hot retention and 7-year cold retention. Test: I selected 30 sample events across the 10.2.1 categories — 5 per category — and confirmed each is in the SIEM and immutable. Result: 30 of 30 found, all immutable. Evidence: the SIEM export 'SIEM_CDE_Events_2025Q3.csv', and the immutability policy 'LOG-IMMUT-001'.我们记录所有 10.2.1 事件——用户访问、root/管理员动作、审计日志访问、无效逻辑访问尝试、识别/认证机制变更、审计日志修改——写入 SIEM，热保留 13 个月，冷保留 7 年。测试：我抽 30 条样本事件覆盖 10.2.1 各类——每类 5 条——确认每条都在 SIEM 中且不可变。结果：30/30 找到，全部不可变。证据：SIEM 导出 'SIEM_CDE_Events_2025Q3.csv'，以及不可变策略 'LOG-IMMUT-001'。
QSA (Mr. Henderson): 10.4.1 — log review. How often, and by whom?10.4.1——日志复核。频率？谁做？
Steve (SOC lead): Daily, by the SOC analyst on shift, documented in the daily SOC log review report. Test: I pulled 30 daily log review reports for Q3 — 1 per business day — and confirmed each is signed by the SOC analyst on shift. Result: 30 of 30 found, all signed. Evidence: the daily review folder 'SOC_Daily_Log_Review_2025Q3/' with 30 PDFs.每日，由 SOC 值班分析师，记录在每日 SOC 日志复核报告。测试：我抽 Q3 共 30 份每日复核报告——每个工作日 1 份——确认每份由 SOC 值班分析师签字。结果：30/30 找到，全部签字。证据：每日复核文件夹 'SOC_Daily_Log_Review_2025Q3/'含 30 份 PDF。
QSA (Mr. Henderson): One last control — 8.2.1, accounts are assigned based on job function and least privilege. How do you prove that?最后一项控制——8.2.1，账号按岗位职责和最小权限分配。如何证明？
Michelle (IAM lead): Two pieces of evidence. One: the CDE access matrix, mapped from job functions to roles, signed by the CDE system owners last quarter. Two: the quarterly access review report, where 18 CDE system owners recertified 2,400 CDE user-role assignments, with 38 removals for role change and 12 for leaver. Result: 100% reviewed, 0 unauthorized access detected.两个证据。一：CDE 访问矩阵，从岗位职责映射到角色，上季度由 CDE 系统 owner 签字。二：季度访问复核报告，18 个 CDE 系统 owner 重新认证了 2,400 个 CDE 用户-角色分配，因调岗移除 38 个、离职移除 12 个。结果：100% 复核，0 个未授权访问。
QSA (Mr. Henderson): Excellent. No findings on this session. Next session — Requirement 6, Thursday 09:00.非常好。本次会议无发现。下次——要求 6，周四 9:00。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Control / Test / Evidence Walk me through the control, the test, and the evidence. 请过一下控制、测试、证据 The QSA's structure is fixed: control, test, evidence. Always answer in that order. Skipping a field signals a weak control.
  • Selected N users I selected 25 users from the CDE access list, verified each has MFA enrolled. 我从 CDE 访问列表抽 25 个用户，核实每个都已注册 MFA Always name the sample size and the population. "Selected 25" + "from the CDE access list" = auditable sampling.
  • Result Result: 25 of 25 compliant. 结果：25/25 合规 Always quote the result as a fraction: 25/25, 12/12, 30/30. The QSA reads the fraction, not the words "all compliant".
  • Evidence file Evidence: the IAM report 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf'. 证据：IAM 报告 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf' Always name the evidence file with its versioned name. The QSA needs the file, not the description.
  • No findings No findings on this session. 本次会议无发现 "No findings" is the QSA's gold-standard close. The PCI lead should hear it and not push for more.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) n. 支付卡行业数据安全标准 The standard for protecting cardholder data.
  • QSA (Qualified Security Assessor) n. 合格安全评估师 The external auditor certified to assess PCI-DSS compliance.
  • CDE (Cardholder Data Environment) n. 卡数据环境 The systems that store, process, or transmit cardholder data.
  • ROC (Report on Compliance) n. 合规报告 The QSA's formal report on the bank's PCI-DSS compliance.
  • AoC (Attestation of Compliance) n. 合规声明 The bank's formal attestation, signed by an executive.
  • In-scope system n. 范围内系统 A system that is part of the CDE and must meet PCI-DSS controls.
  • Compensating control n. 补偿控制 An alternative control that meets the intent of a PCI-DSS requirement when the primary cannot.
  • PAW (Privileged Access Workstation) n. 特权访问工作站 A hardened workstation used by admins to access the CDE.
  • SIEM immutability n. SIEM 不可变性 The property that audit logs cannot be modified or deleted.
  • Access matrix n. 访问矩阵 The mapping from job functions to roles and entitlements.
  • Quarterly access review n. 季度访问复核 Recertification by system owners of every user-role assignment.
  • Sample size n. 样本量 The number of records tested for a control — auditor chooses; lead must defend it.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Control / Test / Evidence" — three nouns, fixed order

The QSA's structure is fixed. Control, test, evidence. Always answer in that order. Skipping a field signals a weak control, and the QSA will ask for it.

QSA 的结构是固定的。控制、测试、证据。总是按顺序回答。缺一项等于控制弱，QSA 会追问。

② "Selected N of M" — sample size + population

Always name the sample size and the population. Selected 25 users from the CDE access list. The QSA needs both: 25 is the test, CDE access list is the population. Without the population, the sample is unauditable.

总报样本量和总体。从 CDE 访问列表抽 25 个用户。QSA 需要两个：25 是测试，CDE 访问列表是总体。没有总体，样本不可审。

③ "X of X compliant" — the result is a fraction

Always quote the result as a fraction. 25 of 25 compliant. The QSA reads the fraction, not the words. "All compliant" is a sentence; "25 of 25" is the proof.

结果总是报分数。25/25 合规。QSA 读的是分数，不是句子。"全部合规"是句子，"25/25"是证据。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Control, test, evidence — in that order. The QSA's structure is fixed. Skip one and the QSA asks for it.
  • Sample size + population, always. "Selected 25 from the CDE access list" is auditable. "Tested MFA" is not.
  • Result is a fraction, not a sentence. "25 of 25 compliant" is the proof. "All compliant" is the sentence.
  • Name the evidence file with its versioned name. 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf' is auditable. "The IAM report" is not.
  • Bring the right people. IAM lead for 8.x, SOC lead for 10.x. The QSA expects the named owner to answer, not the PCI lead alone.
  • Answer short. The QSA's questions are short. One sentence per field. Three fields. Three sentences. Then wait.
  • "No findings" is the gold standard close. Hear it, thank the QSA, and confirm the next session. Don't push for more.

Exercise 2 — Spot the QSA-walkthrough mistake

Which line would damage the QSA session the most? Why?

  1. "Control 8.3.1, MFA required. Test: selected 25 from the CDE access list, verified MFA. Result: 25 of 25 compliant. Evidence: 'CDE_MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf'."
  2. "We have MFA on the CDE. It's important and we take it seriously."
  3. "Control 10.2.1, audit log events. Test: 30 sample events, confirmed in SIEM, immutable. Result: 30 of 30. Evidence: 'SIEM_CDE_Events_2025Q3.csv'."
  4. "Control 8.2.1, least privilege. Evidence: CDE access matrix, quarterly access review, 100% reviewed, 0 unauthorized."

② is the session-damager. "We have MFA and we take it seriously" replaces the control, the test, the result, and the evidence with a feeling. The QSA will always bounce this back: which control, which test, which sample, which evidence. ① walks the four fields with file. ③ does the same. ④ names the matrix and the review. ② signals an immature compliance function; the QSA will write a finding.

Exercise 3 — Run a 90-minute QSA walkthrough

You are the PCI lead. The QSA asks you to walk Requirements 7 (restrict access by business need-to-know), 9 (restrict physical access), and 12 (information security policy). Build a 90-minute script: for each requirement, walk control, test, evidence, result. Use sample sizes, file names, and result fractions.

"Requirement 7 — restrict access by business need-to-know. Control 7.2.1 — access is restricted to the least privilege required for job function. Test: I selected 30 CDE user-role assignments from the quarterly access review, verified each is supported by a job-function justification, confirmed no role has more entitlements than required. Result: 30 of 30 justified. Evidence: the access matrix 'CDE_Access_Matrix_2025Q3.pdf' and the review sheet 'CDE_Review_2025Q3.xlsx'. Requirement 9 — restrict physical access. Control 9.4.1 — all media with cardholder data is physically secured. Test: I walked the 3 CDE datacentres, verified badge access, CCTV coverage, and media inventory log. Result: 3 of 3 compliant, all media inventoried. Evidence: the walkthrough photos 'CDE_DC_Walkthrough_2025Q3/' and the media inventory 'CDE_Media_Inventory_2025Q3.xlsx'. Requirement 12 — information security policy. Control 12.1.1 — the information security policy is established, published, and reviewed annually. Test: I pulled the InfoSec policy v3.4 published 2024-12, the annual review minutes signed by the CISO 2025-01, and the staff acknowledgement report (98% of CDE staff). Result: policy current, reviewed, acknowledged. Evidence: the policy 'InfoSec_Policy_v3.4.pdf', the minutes 'InfoSec_Annual_Review_2025-01.pdf', the acknowledgement report 'InfoSec_Ack_2025Q3.xlsx'. No findings on this session. Next session — Requirement 1, Thursday 09:00."
