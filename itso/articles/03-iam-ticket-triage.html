IAM Ticket Triage — Joiner, Mover, Leaver, Exception

Article 03 · Module 1: Daily Security Operations · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟢 Beginner-friendly 🎯 JML, RBAC exceptions, access reviews, SoD conflicts, leaver SLA

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Monday 11:00 HKT. You are the IAM team lead. The weekend ticket queue has 47 tickets waiting: 28 joiners, 6 movers, 4 leavers, 9 access requests. The leaver SLA is 4 hours from termination — 1 of the 4 leavers is already at 3 hours 40 minutes. Joining you on the standup: Linda, the HR operations partner, and James, the IT service desk lead.

周一 11:00 香港时间，你是 IAM 团队负责人。周末工单队列有 47 张等待：28 张入职、6 张调岗、4 张离职、9 张访问申请。离职 SLA是终止后 4 小时——4 张离职单里 1 张已 3 小时 40 分。参加站会的：HR 运营伙伴 Linda、IT 服务台负责人 James。

IAM is the SOC's quiet twin. It is measured in tickets, SLAs, JML (Joiner-Mover-Leaver), and SoD (Segregation of Duties) conflicts. The cost of a missed leaver is a data breach; the cost of a missed joiner is a productivity loss; the cost of a missed SoD conflict is an audit finding.

IAM 是 SOC 的安静孪生兄弟。用工单、SLA、JML（入职-调岗-离职）、SoD（职责分离）冲突度量。漏掉离职单的代价是数据泄露；漏掉入职单的代价是生产力损失；漏掉 SoD 冲突的代价是审计发现。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (IAM lead): IAM update from me. Queue status: 47 tickets in the weekend backlog. 28 joiners, 6 movers, 4 leavers, 9 access requests. One leaver is at 3 hours 40 minutes against the 4-hour SLA — that's the priority.IAM 层面同步一下。队列状态：周末积压 47 张工单。28 张入职、6 张调岗、4 张离职、9 张访问申请。一张离职单已 3 小时 40 分，离 4 小时 SLA 还有 20 分钟——这是优先级。
You (IAM lead): Leaver priority: Peter Wu, terminated Friday 16:00, sales ops role. Termination notice came through from HR at 07:20. Disable, revoke, recover — in this order. Disable: AD account disabled, mailbox blocked. Revoke: SaaS sessions invalidated, MFA factors removed, VPN profile deleted. Recover: laptop returned, encrypted, awaiting IT asset recovery. Time to close: 20 minutes.离职优先级：Peter Wu，周五 16:00 终止，销售运营岗。HR 终止通知 07:20 进来。禁用、吊销、回收——按顺序。禁用：AD 账号禁用，邮箱封禁。吊销：SaaS 会话作废，MFA 因子删除，VPN profile 清除。回收：笔记本已回收、加密，等 IT 资产回收。关闭时间：20 分钟。
James (IT service desk): I'll handle the laptop recovery now. Anything else on Peter?我现在处理笔记本回收。Peter 还有别的吗？
You (IAM lead): One thing — he had temporary elevated access to the CRM last month for the price-list upload. That access was time-bound to expire on 30 Sep, but I want to confirm it's been revoked. Pull the access review report for the last 30 days and confirm.一件事——他上月为了价目表上传被临时授予 CRM 高级权限。那权限本应在 9 月 30 日到期，但我要确认已吊销。拉过去 30 天的访问复核报告确认一下。
Linda (HR ops): On the joiners — 18 of the 28 are routine onboarding for the new graduate intake, access templates already approved by line managers. 10 are lateral hires with bespoke access requests. The 10 are the workload.入职这边——28 张里 18 张是应届生入职的常规开通，权限模板已经直线经理批准。10 张是有定制权限请求的社招。10 张才是工作量。
You (IAM lead): Understood. For the 10 lateral hires, run the SoD conflict check against their previous role and the new role. If there's a conflict — initiator and approver, or maker and checker — flag it to the hiring manager, do not grant access until the conflict is resolved.明白。10 张社招，针对他们的新旧角色跑 SoD 冲突检查。如果有冲突——发起人与审批人，或 maker 与 checker——标记给用人经理，冲突解决前不开权限。
Linda (HR ops): On the access requests — 3 of the 9 are RBAC exception requests where the requester is asking for a role that conflicts with policy. What do we do with those?访问申请这边——9 张里 3 张是 RBAC 例外申请，申请人要的角色和策略冲突。这几张怎么处理？
You (IAM lead): Three options. Option A: approve the exception with a time-bound expiry — max 30 days — and a named approver. Option B: deny and propose a compensating role that meets 80% of the need without the conflict. Option C: escalate to Group IAM for a policy exception — that takes 5 days and an attestation from the requester's MD. Default is Option B. Use Option A only if there's a regulatory or audit deadline.三个方案。方案 A：批准例外但限时——最长 30 天——加指定审批人。方案 B：拒绝，提出一个补偿角色，满足 80% 需求但无冲突。方案 C：升级到集团 IAM 走策略例外——需要 5 天和申请人 MD 的声明。默认方案 B。只在有监管或审计截止日期时用方案 A。
James (IT service desk): And the leaver SLA breach policy? What do we log if we miss the 4-hour mark?离职 SLA 违约策略呢？超过 4 小时我们记什么？
You (IAM lead): Every breach is logged in the IAM breach register with root cause and remediation. Group IAM reports breaches monthly. Above 2 breaches per quarter triggers a control finding in the next Group Audit. We don't want that. Target: zero breaches, 100% on-time.每次违约都记进 IAM 违约登记册，写明根因和修复。集团 IAM 每月报一次违约。单季度超过 2 次违约触发下次集团审计的控制发现。我们不想要那个。目标：零违约，100% 准时。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • IAM update IAM update from me. Queue status: 47 tickets, 28 joiners, 6 movers, 4 leavers, 9 access requests. IAM 层面同步。队列状态：47 张工单，28 入职，6 调岗，4 离职，9 访问申请 Lead with the queue and the JML split. The HR partner reads the JML split; the IT lead reads the counts.
  • Disable, revoke, recover Disable: AD account disabled. Revoke: SaaS sessions invalidated, MFA removed. Recover: laptop returned. 禁用、吊销、回收——按顺序 The leaver checklist is fixed: disable (cut off access), revoke (kill sessions and factors), recover (asset). Order matters.
  • Time-bound Approve the exception with a time-bound expiry — max 30 days. 批准例外但限时——最长 30 天 Time-bound is the magic phrase. An open-ended exception is an audit finding; a time-bound one is a controlled risk.
  • SoD conflict Run the SoD conflict check. If initiator and approver, flag it to the hiring manager. 跑 SoD 冲突检查。如果发起人和审批人冲突，标记给用人经理 SoD = Segregation of Duties. The two-role conflict (initiator/approver, maker/checker) is the classic SoD finding.
  • Compensating role Propose a compensating role that meets 80% of the need without the conflict. 提出补偿角色，满足 80% 需求但无冲突 Compensating role = the professional answer to a policy conflict. 80% is realistic; 100% usually means a new exception.
  • Breach register Every breach is logged in the IAM breach register with root cause and remediation. 每次违约都记进 IAM 违约登记册，写明根因和修复 The breach register is the audit trail. Root cause + remediation is the structure. Auditors read the register, not the chat.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • JML (Joiner-Mover-Leaver) n. 入职-调岗-离职 The IAM lifecycle: new hire, role change, departure.
  • RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) n. 基于角色的访问控制 Access granted by role, not by individual.
  • ABAC (Attribute-Based Access Control) n. 基于属性的访问控制 Access granted by attributes (department, geography, time).
  • SoD (Segregation of Duties) n. 职责分离 Two roles that conflict (initiator/approver, maker/checker) must not be held by the same person.
  • Leaver SLA n. 离职 SLA The time window to revoke all access after termination (typically 4 hours).
  • Time-bound exception n. 限时例外 An access exception that auto-expires on a date.
  • Compensating role n. 补偿角色 A role that meets most of the need without the policy conflict.
  • Access review n. 访问复核 Periodic certification that an access is still required.
  • Access request n. 访问申请 A ticket asking for new or modified access.
  • Lateral hire n. 社招 A new hire joining from another company, with bespoke access needs.
  • Onboarding template n. 入职模板 Pre-approved access bundle for standard new hires.
  • Session revocation n. 会话吊销 Force-logout and invalidate all active sessions.
  • MFA factor removal n. MFA 因子删除 Removing a registered MFA device (e.g. phone) so it cannot be reused.
  • Breach register n. 违约登记册 The log of every SLA breach with root cause and remediation.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Disable, revoke, recover" — three verbs, fixed order

The leaver checklist is three verbs in fixed order. Disable, revoke, recover. Skip "recover" and you have an asset leak; skip "revoke" and you have a session leak; skip "disable" and you have an access leak. The order is the chain.

离职清单是三个动词、固定顺序。禁用、吊销、回收。跳过"回收"会资产泄露；跳过"吊销"会话泄露；跳过"禁用"访问泄露。顺序就是链。

② "Time-bound" — the magic adjective

Time-bound is the magic adjective in IAM. Approve with a time-bound expiry — max 30 days. An open-ended exception is an audit finding; a time-bound one is a controlled risk. Always use the word.

Time-bound 是 IAM 的魔法形容词。批准但限时——最长 30 天。开放式例外是审计发现；限时例外是受控风险。总是用这个词。

③ "Compensating role meets 80%" — the realistic answer

Always propose a compensating role that meets 80% of the need, not 100%. Meets 80% of the need without the conflict. 100% usually means a new exception. 80% is the professional answer to a policy conflict.

补偿角色满足 80% 需求，不是 100%。满足 80% 需求但无冲突。100% 通常意味着新例外。80% 是对策略冲突的专业答案。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Leavers first. Always. A leaver at 3h40m against a 4-hour SLA is the priority. The cost of a missed leaver is a data breach.
  • Disable, revoke, recover — in that order. The order is the chain. Skip one and the audit fails.
  • Time-bound is the magic word. Open-ended exceptions are audit findings. Time-bound exceptions are controlled risks.
  • SoD conflict is a flag, not a deny. Flag it to the hiring manager, do not grant until resolved. The hiring manager owns the decision.
  • Compensating role beats exception. A role that meets 80% without the conflict is the professional answer. Exceptions are the last resort.
  • Breach register is the audit trail. Root cause + remediation. Auditors read the register, not the chat.
  • Zero breaches is the target, not 95%. IAM is binary: access is granted or not, on time or late. 95% on-time still means a breach.

Exercise 2 — Spot the IAM mistake

Which line would damage the leaver SLA the most? Why?

  1. "Leaver priority: Anna Park, terminated Friday 17:00. Disable, revoke, recover — in this order."
  2. "I'll do the disable tomorrow morning, then revoke in the afternoon."
  3. "Time to close: 50 minutes, against the 4-hour SLA."
  4. "Every breach is logged in the IAM breach register with root cause and remediation."

② is the SLA-damager. "Tomorrow morning" is well past the 4-hour SLA. The leaver's access must be cut off within 4 hours of termination, not the next business day. ① walks the disable-revoke-recover chain. ③ quotes the time. ④ logs the breach. ② breaches the SLA by 12+ hours; the audit will fail.

Exercise 3 — Run a 30-minute IAM triage

You are the IAM lead. The queue has 38 tickets: 22 joiners, 5 movers, 2 leavers (one at 3h50m), 9 access requests (3 RBAC exceptions). Build a 30-minute script: (1) queue + JML split, (2) priority leaver with disable/revoke/recover, (3) joiner SoD check, (4) exception options, (5) breach policy.

"IAM update. Queue: 38 tickets, 22 joiners, 5 movers, 2 leavers, 9 access requests. Priority leaver: David Lee, terminated Friday 18:00, finance ops. At 3h50m, 10 minutes to the 4-hour SLA. Disable, revoke, recover — in this order. Disable: AD account, mailbox. Revoke: SaaS sessions, MFA factors, VPN. Recover: laptop. Time to close: 8 minutes. Joiners: 15 standard templates, 7 lateral hires. SoD check on the 7. Conflict → flag to hiring manager, hold access. Access requests: 3 RBAC exceptions. Three options. A: time-bound, max 30 days, named approver. B: compensating role, 80% need, no conflict. C: Group IAM, 5 days, MD attestation. Default B. Use A only for regulator or audit deadline. Breach register: root cause + remediation. Above 2 per quarter = control finding. Target: zero breaches."
