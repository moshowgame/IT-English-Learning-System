ISO 27001 & SOX Control Evidence — Owner, Test, Deficiency

Article 10 · Module 2: Security Governance & Compliance · ITSO English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🎯 ISO 27001 Annex A, SOX ITGC, control owner, evidence pack, deficiency triage

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday 11:00 HKT. You are the IT Controls & Compliance Lead at a global bank. The annual ISO 27001 surveillance audit is in week 1, and the SOX 404 ITGC testing by the external auditor is in week 2. Today's walk is the joint session covering ISO 27001 Annex A.9 (Access Control) and SOX ITGC IT-A03 (User Access Management). Joining: Mrs. Garcia, the ISO 27001 lead auditor from BSI, and Mr. Davis, the SOX ITGC partner from the Big-4 firm. You have 60 minutes to walk 8 controls, 14 evidence artefacts, and 1 known deficiency.

周三 11:00 香港时间，你是全球银行的IT 控制与合规负责人。年度ISO 27001 监督审核进入第 1 周，SOX 404 ITGC 测试由外审在第 2 周进行。今天的走查是联合会议，覆盖ISO 27001 附录 A.9（访问控制）和SOX ITGC IT-A03（用户访问管理）。参加：BSI 的 ISO 27001 主任审核员 Garcia 女士，四大 SOX ITGC 合伙人 Davis 先生。60 分钟过完 8 项控制、14 个证据、1 个已知缺陷。

ISO 27001 + SOX walks share the same skeleton: control → owner → test → evidence → result. The difference is the control catalogue (Annex A vs SOX ITGC) and the auditor (BSI vs Big-4). The professional pattern is to walk both in parallel, using the same evidence pack, and never re-define a control.

ISO 27001 + SOX 走查共享同一骨架：控制 → owner → 测试 → 证据 → 结果。差别在控制目录（附录 A vs SOX ITGC）和审核员（BSI vs 四大）。专业模式是并行走，使用同一证据包，永不重定义控制。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Mrs. Garcia (ISO 27001 lead auditor): "Let's start with ISO 27001 Annex A.9.2.1 — user registration and de-registration. Walk me through the control, the owner, the test, and the evidence.""我们从ISO 27001 附录 A.9.2.1——用户注册和注销开始。请过控制、owner、测试、证据。"
You (IT Controls lead): Control A.9.2.1 — formal user lifecycle for joiners, movers, and leavers. Owner: the IAM lead, Michelle. Test: I selected 30 joiners, 20 movers, and 25 leavers from the HR system, verified the IAM record for each, and confirmed the joiner was created within 1 business day, the mover access was updated within 3 business days, and the leaver was disabled within 4 hours. Result: 30/30 joiners compliant, 19/20 movers compliant, 24/25 leavers compliant. One mover outside the 3-day SLA — the deficiency I'll walk in a moment. Evidence: the IAM lifecycle report 'IAM_JML_Q3_2025.xlsx', and a sample ticket for the 1 deficient mover.控制 A.9.2.1——入职、调岗、离职的正式用户生命周期。Owner：IAM 负责人 Michelle。测试：我从 HR 系统抽 30 个入职、20 个调岗、25 个离职，核实每个的 IAM 记录，确认入职 1 个工作日内创建，调岗 3 个工作日内更新权限，离职 4 小时内禁用。结果：30/30 入职合规，19/20 调岗合规，24/25 离职合规。1 个调岗超 3 天 SLA——就是我马上要走的那个缺陷。证据：IAM 生命周期报告 'IAM_JML_Q3_2025.xlsx'，以及 1 个有缺陷调岗的样本工单。
Mr. Davis (SOX ITGC partner): Walk the same control under SOX. IT-A03 — user access management for the in-scope financial systems."在 SOX 下过同一控制。IT-A03——范围内财务系统的用户访问管理。"
You (IT Controls lead): Same control, same evidence pack. SOX IT-A03 — formal user access provisioning and revocation for the 12 in-scope financial systems. Owner: the IAM lead, jointly with the 12 system owners. Test: I selected 25 leavers from the last 6 months, verified the leaver's access was revoked in the 12 systems within 4 hours, and confirmed the revocation was logged in the SIEM. Result: 25/25 leavers compliant, no access remained in any of the 12 systems. Evidence: the same IAM lifecycle report, plus the SIEM leaver access-revocation log 'SIEM_Leaver_Access_2025Q3.csv'.同一控制，同一证据包。SOX IT-A03——12 个范围内财务系统的正式用户访问开通和吊销。Owner：IAM 负责人，与 12 个系统 owner 共同。测试：我抽过去 6 个月 25 个离职，核实离职者在 12 个系统的访问 4 小时内吊销，确认吊销记录在 SIEM。结果：25/25 离职合规，12 个系统均无残留访问。证据：同一 IAM 生命周期报告，加 SIEM 离职访问吊销日志 'SIEM_Leaver_Access_2025Q3.csv'。
Mr. Davis (SOX ITGC partner): And the deficiency — the 1 mover outside the 3-day SLA. What's the root cause, the remediation, and the due date?缺陷呢——超 3 天 SLA 的 1 个调岗。根因、修复、截止日期？
You (IT Controls lead): Deficiency D-2025-IT-014. Root cause: the manager submitted the access-change ticket 4 business days after the role change, not the same day. Remediation: we're rolling out a ServiceNow workflow that auto-creates the access-change ticket from the HR role-change event, removing the manager manual step. Due date: 30 November 2026, attested by the IAM lead and Internal Audit. Compensating control today: the daily IAM-vs-HR reconciliation report, run by the SOC, flags any role change without a corresponding access ticket within 24 hours.缺陷 D-2025-IT-014。根因：经理在调岗后 4 个工作日才提交访问变更工单，不是同一天。修复：我们正在部署 ServiceNow 工作流，从 HR 调岗事件自动创建访问变更工单，去掉经理的手动步骤。截止日期：2026 年 11 月 30 日，由 IAM 负责人和内审声明。今日补偿控制：每日 IAM 对 HR 对账报告，由 SOC 跑，24 小时内标记任何无对应访问工单的调岗。
Mrs. Garcia (ISO 27001 lead auditor): On Annex A.9.4.1 — information access restriction. How do you enforce least privilege on the in-scope systems?附录 A.9.4.1——信息访问限制。如何在范围内系统强制最小权限？
You (IT Controls lead): Three components. One: role-based access control — RBAC — through the central IAM platform, 12 in-scope systems integrated. Two: quarterly access review by the 12 system owners, covering 8,400 user-role assignments, last cycle 38 removals for role change and 12 for leaver. Three: privileged access management — PAM — for the 240 privileged users, with session recording and just-in-time elevation. Test: I selected 30 user-role assignments, verified each is supported by a job-function justification, and confirmed the role has the minimum entitlements required. Result: 30/30 justified, no excessive entitlements detected. Evidence: the access matrix 'CDE_Access_Matrix_2025Q3.pdf', the review sheet 'Access_Review_2025Q3.xlsx', and the PAM configuration export 'PAM_Config_2025Q3.json'.三部分。一：RBAC——基于角色的访问控制——通过中央 IAM 平台，12 个范围内系统已集成。二：12 个系统 owner 季度访问复核，覆盖 8,400 个用户-角色分配，上一周期因调岗移除 38 个、离职移除 12 个。三：240 个特权用户的 PAM——特权访问管理，含会话录制和即时提权。测试：我抽 30 个用户-角色分配，核实每个有岗位依据，确认角色仅有所需最小权限。结果：30/30 有依据，未发现过度授权。证据：访问矩阵 'CDE_Access_Matrix_2025Q3.pdf'，复核表 'Access_Review_2025Q3.xlsx'，以及 PAM 配置导出 'PAM_Config_2025Q3.json'。
Mrs. Garcia (ISO 27001 lead auditor): One last — A.9.4.2 — secure log-on. What's the technical control?最后一项——A.9.4.2——安全登录。技术控制？
You (IT Controls lead): MFA on all access into the in-scope systems, with conditional access enforcing step-up auth on geo-anomaly and on privileged actions. Test: I selected 25 users from the in-scope access list, verified each has MFA enrolled, verified the conditional access policy 'CA-CDE-001' enforces MFA, and reviewed a sample of 10 geo-anomaly logins for step-up auth. Result: 25/25 MFA enrolled, 10/10 step-up auth triggered on geo-anomaly. Evidence: the MFA enrolment report 'MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf', the policy export 'CA-CDE-001.json', and the step-up auth log 'StepUp_Geo_2025Q3.csv'.范围内系统的所有访问要求 MFA，条件访问对地理位置异常和特权操作强制 step-up 认证。测试：我从范围内访问列表抽 25 用户，核实每个注册 MFA，核实条件访问策略 'CA-CDE-001' 强制 MFA，并复核 10 个地理位置异常登录的 step-up 认证样本。结果：25/25 注册 MFA，10/10 地理位置异常触发 step-up。证据：MFA 注册报告 'MFA_Enrolment_2025Q3.pdf'，策略导出 'CA-CDE-001.json'，以及 step-up 日志 'StepUp_Geo_2025Q3.csv'。
Mrs. Garcia (ISO 27001 lead auditor): Excellent. One non-conformity raised on the deficiency D-2025-IT-014, acceptable per the corrective action plan, due 30 November 2026. No other findings on this session.非常好。对缺陷 D-2025-IT-014 开一项不符合，按纠正措施计划可接受，截止 2026 年 11 月 30 日。本次会议无其他发现。
Mr. Davis (SOX ITGC partner): SOX side — one deficiency, also acceptable, same corrective action plan, same due date. No other findings.SOX 这边——一项缺陷，同样可接受，同一纠正措施计划，同一截止日期。无其他发现。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Control / Owner / Test / Evidence Walk me through the control, the owner, the test, and the evidence. 请过控制、owner、测试、证据 ISO 27001 + SOX walk structure is fixed: control, owner, test, evidence. Always answer in that order. Skipping a field signals a weak control.
  • Same control, same evidence Same control, same evidence pack. SOX IT-A03 — formal user access provisioning and revocation for the 12 in-scope financial systems. 同一控制，同一证据包。SOX IT-A03——12 个范围内财务系统的正式用户访问开通和吊销 When the same evidence serves two frameworks, name it explicitly. The auditors reuse the same pack.
  • Deficiency with 4 fields Deficiency D-2025-IT-014. Root cause, remediation, due date, compensating control. 缺陷 D-2025-IT-014。根因、修复、截止日期、补偿控制 A deficiency always has 4 fields: ID, root cause, remediation, due date. The auditor reads all 4.
  • Compensating control Compensating control: daily IAM-vs-HR reconciliation, run by SOC. 补偿控制：每日 IAM 对 HR 对账，由 SOC 跑 Compensating control is the answer to a known deficiency. Always name at least one.
  • One non-conformity, no other findings One non-conformity raised, acceptable per the corrective action plan. No other findings. 开一项不符合，按纠正措施计划可接受。无其他发现 This is the gold-standard close. Name the non-conformity, anchor to the corrective action plan, and stop.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • ISO 27001 n. ISO 27001 The international standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).
  • Annex A n. 附录 A The control catalogue in ISO 27001 — A.5 to A.18 in the 2022 version.
  • SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) n. 萨班斯-奥克斯利法案 US law requiring financial controls and IT general controls.
  • SOX 404 n. SOX 404 Section 404 — management assessment of internal controls.
  • ITGC (IT General Controls) n. IT 通用控制 Controls over IT systems that support financial reporting.
  • Surveillance audit n. 监督审核 Annual ISO 27001 audit to confirm continued compliance.
  • BSI n. 英国标准协会 One of the major ISO 27001 certification bodies.
  • Control owner n. 控制 owner The named individual accountable for a control's operation.
  • Deficiency n. 缺陷 A control instance that did not operate as designed.
  • Non-conformity n. 不符合 ISO 27001 term for a control failure requiring corrective action.
  • Corrective action plan (CAP) n. 纠正措施计划 The documented plan to remediate a deficiency.
  • Compensating control n. 补偿控制 A control that mitigates risk while a deficiency is open.
  • RBAC n. 基于角色的访问控制 Access granted by role, not by individual.
  • PAM n. 特权访问管理 The platform that controls privileged access with session recording.
  • Just-in-time elevation n. 即时提权 Privileged access granted for a bounded time window, then revoked.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Control, owner, test, evidence" — four nouns, fixed order

ISO 27001 + SOX walk structure is fixed. Control, owner, test, evidence. Always answer in that order. Skipping a field signals a weak control.

ISO 27001 + SOX 走查结构是固定的。控制、owner、测试、证据。总按顺序回答。缺一项等于控制弱。

② "Same control, same evidence" — reuse explicitly

When the same evidence serves two frameworks, name it. Same control, same evidence pack. The auditors reuse the same pack; the bank saves time and the auditors see consistency.

同一证据服务两个框架时，明确说出来。同一控制，同一证据包。审核员复用同一包；银行省时间，审核员看到一致性。

③ "Deficiency: ID, root cause, remediation, due date" — four fields, fixed order

A deficiency always has 4 fields: ID, root cause, remediation, due date. The auditor reads all 4. Skip one and the deficiency is incomplete.

缺陷总是有 4 个字段：ID、根因、修复、截止日期。审核员读全部 4 项。缺一项等于缺陷不完整。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Control, owner, test, evidence — in that order. The walk structure is fixed. Skip one and the auditor asks for it.
  • Reuse the evidence pack across frameworks. Same control, same evidence. The auditors love the consistency.
  • Deficiency with 4 fields: ID, root cause, remediation, due date. The auditor reads all 4. Skip one and the deficiency is incomplete.
  • Compensating control is the answer to a known deficiency. Name at least one. No control = unmanaged deficiency = material weakness.
  • "One non-conformity, no other findings" is the gold-standard close. Name the non-conformity, anchor to the corrective action plan, stop.
  • Bring the right owner. IAM lead for A.9, system owners for A.9.4. The auditor expects the named owner to answer, not the controls lead alone.

"Control A.9.2.4 — secret authentication information (passwords, API keys, tokens) is managed through the central vault, never stored in plain text, rotated on a 90-day cycle. Owner: the IAM lead, Michelle. Test: I selected 30 privileged accounts, verified the secret is stored in the central vault, verified the rotation date is within 90 days, and confirmed no secrets in plain text in code, config, or tickets. Exercise 2 — Spot the ISO/SOX walk mistake

Which line would damage the joint walk the most? Why?

  1. "Control A.9.2.1 — formal user lifecycle. Owner: IAM lead. Test: 30 joiners, 20 movers, 25 leavers. Result: 30/30, 19/20, 24/25. Evidence: 'IAM_JML_Q3_2025.xlsx'."
  2. "We do identity management and it's working OK."
  3. "Deficiency D-2025-IT-014, root cause manager late ticket, remediation ServiceNow workflow, due 30 Nov 2026, compensating control daily IAM-vs-HR reconciliation."
  4. "SOX IT-A03 same control same evidence. Test: 25 leavers, 12 systems, 4-hour SLA. Result: 25/25. No remaining access."

② is the walk-damager. "We do identity management and it's working OK" replaces the control, the owner, the test, the result, and the evidence with a feeling. The auditor will always bounce this back: which control, which owner, which sample, which result, which evidence. ① walks the four fields. ③ covers the 4 deficiency fields. ④ reuses the evidence under SOX. ② is an immature controls function; the auditor will write a finding.

Exercise 3 — Run a 60-minute joint ISO/SOX walk

You are the IT Controls lead. ISO A.9.2.5 (review of user access rights) + SOX IT-A05 (periodic access review). Build a 60-minute script: control, owner, test (sample 30), evidence, 1 deficiency with 4 fields, compensating control, close with "one non-conformity, no other findings".

"Control A.9.2.5 — review of user access rights. Owner: the 12 system owners, with the IAM lead. Test: I selected 30 user-role assignments from the quarterly access review, verified each is supported by a job-function justification, confirmed the last review date is within 90 days, and confirmed the reviewer is the named system owner, not a delegate. Result: 29/30 reviewed within 90 days by the named system owner. 1 reviewed by a delegate without the formal delegation letter. Evidence: the access review sheet 'Access_Review_2025Q3.xlsx' and the delegation letter register 'Delegation_2025Q3.pdf'. SOX IT-A05 same control same evidence. Test: same 30, verified the same fields under SOX. Result: 29/30 SOX-compliant. Deficiency D-2025-IT-022. Root cause: the system owner was on PTO and the delegate reviewed without the formal delegation letter. Remediation: the delegation letter added retroactively 18 October, the access review re-attested by the named system owner 22 October, the delegation workflow now requires the letter before the review is accepted. Due date: closed 25 October 2025, within the 30-day CAP window. Compensating control: the IAM platform blocks any access review submitted by a delegate without a letter on file. Evidence: the delegation letter 'Delegate_2025-10-18.pdf', the re-attestation 'Re-attest_2025-10-22.xlsx', and the IAM platform rule 'IAM_Delegate_Block_2025-10-25.json'. One non-conformity raised, closed within the corrective action window. No other findings."
