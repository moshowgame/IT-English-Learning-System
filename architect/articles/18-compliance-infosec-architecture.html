Compliance & InfoSec Architecture

Article 18 · Module 5: HSBC Banking Context — Compliance & InfoSec · Architect English

⏱ 10 min read 🔴 Advanced 🎯 PCI-DSS, MAS TRM, HKMA, GDPR, SDL, Group Standards — speak the regulator's language

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday, 2:30 PM SGT. You are a Solution Architect in the GPB Digital tribe, based in Singapore. The OnePay squad is mid-flight on a card-data tokenisation redesign and has hit three regulatory questions they can't answer: scope of PCI-DSS for the new token vault, audit-trail requirements under MAS TRM, and data-subject rights under GDPR for EU customers. You have booked a 45-min joint session with Sarah from Group InfoSec (Hong Kong), Ravi from Group Compliance (Bangalore), and Liam, the OnePay Tech Lead (London). ADR-0207 is the redesign.

周三下午 2 点 30 新加坡时间，你是驻新加坡的 GPB 数字化 tribe Solution 架构师。 OnePay squad 正在做卡数据 token 化重设计，遇到 3 个监管问题答不上来： 新 token 库的 PCI-DSS 适用范围、MAS TRM 下的审计追踪要求、 以及 GDPR 下 EU 客户的数据主体权利。你约了 45 分钟的联合会议，与会人： 集团 InfoSec 的 Sarah（香港）、集团合规的 Ravi（班加罗尔）、OnePay Tech Lead Liam（伦敦）。

The redesign is in ADR-0207. The Secure Development Lifecycle (SDL) requires InfoSec sign-off at the design review stage. The MAS TRM audit window opens in 6 weeks. You need to walk out of this meeting with a clear path: which controls are mandatory, which are nice-to-have, and which regulator you have to brief first.

重设计在 ADR-0207 里。安全开发生命周期（SDL）要求设计评审阶段拿到 InfoSec 签批。MAS TRM 审计窗口 6 周后开启。你需要从这次会议带走清晰的路径： 哪些控制项是强制的、哪些是 nice-to-have、监管方要先汇报谁。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

You (Architect): Thanks all for joining. Quick context — OnePay squad is mid-flight on the tokenisation redesign, ADR-0207. We have three regulatory questions that are blocking the design review. I'd like to walk through them one by one and capture your input on the call.感谢各位。简单背景——OnePay squad 正在做 token 化重设计（ADR-0207），有 3 个监管问题卡住了设计评审。我想逐个过一下，把你们的意见记在会议纪要里。
Sarah (InfoSec, HK): Quick one before we start — is the new token vault going to be on AWS or on-prem? The Group Cyber baseline is stricter for on-prem.开始前问一句——新 token 库是上 AWS 还是 on-prem？集团 Cyber 基线对 on-prem 更严。
You (Architect): Good question. Currently proposed on AWS, in the Singapore region, with KMS-managed keys. We chose AWS because the OnePay squad already operates there and we're avoiding a hybrid split.好问题。目前提议上 AWS，新加坡 region，KMS 管密钥。选 AWS 是因为 OnePay squad 已经在 AWS 上跑，避免变成混合架构。
Ravi (Compliance, Bangalore): Right, so — question one. What's the PCI-DSS scope of the new token vault? Does it store the PAN at any point, or only the token + last-four?那——第一个问题。新 token 库的 PCI-DSS 适用范围是什么？任何环节会存 PAN，还是只存 token + 卡号后 4 位？
Liam (Tech Lead, London): Honest answer — in the current draft we store the PAN for up to 60 seconds during token issuance, then discard. The token is what's persisted.实话——当前草案里 PAN 最长存 60 秒，在签发 token 期间持有，然后就丢弃。持久化的是 token。
Sarah (InfoSec): OK, that means PCI-DSS scope applies for those 60 seconds — the issuing service, its host, its logging, and the network path. Group Standard GCS-PCI-04 requires you to file a scope diagram with InfoSec and have it re-attested every quarter. I'll send you the template.好，那意味着 这 60 秒内 PCI-DSS 适用——签发服务、它所在主机、它的日志、以及网络路径。集团标准 GCS-PCI-04要求你向 InfoSec 提交一份范围图，并每季度重新确认。我发你模板。
Ravi (Compliance): Good, that's the PCI piece. Question two — MAS TRM. The audit trail for token issuance and lookup must be immutable and retained for 7 years. Are we capturing the issuing service identity in the audit event, or just the user identity?好，PCI 这块过了。第二个问题——MAS TRM。token 签发和查询的审计追踪必须不可篡改并保留 7 年。审计事件里你们捕获的是签发服务的身份，还是只有用户身份？
Liam (Tech Lead): Currently only the user identity. We'd need to add a service-principal field to the audit schema. Honestly, that's a 2-week change to the event schema and a backfill of the last 30 days.目前只有用户身份。需要在审计 schema 里加一个服务主体字段。这要改 2 周的 schema，还要回填最近 30 天的数据。
Ravi (Compliance): Just to flag — the MAS TRM audit window opens in 6 weeks. I recommend you get the schema change merged this sprint and the backfill done before the audit opens. Happy to review the audit-event design on a 30-min call.提一下——MAS TRM 审计窗口 6 周后开。我建议这个 Sprint 把 schema 改动合进去，回填在审计开窗前完成。我可以花 30 分钟过一下审计事件设计。
You (Architect): Good. Question three — GDPR. Under GDPR, EU customers have the right to erasure. If we issue a token for an EU customer and they later request deletion, what's the regulatory path — do we delete the token, the PAN, or both? And how do we prove deletion to the regulator?好。第三个问题——GDPR。GDPR 下 EU 客户有被遗忘权。如果我们给 EU 客户签发了 token，他们事后要求删除，监管路径是什么——删 token、删 PAN、还是两个都删？怎么向监管方证明已删除？
Ravi (Compliance): Both. The token is personal data under GDPR because it's linkable to the customer. You (Architect): Right, so to summarise — three actions: (1) Liam, you'll file the PCI-DSS scope diagram with Sarah by Friday; (2) the audit-event schema change goes in this sprint with a 30-day backfill before the TRM audit; (3) I'll add a GDPR cascade-and-certify workstream to ADR-0207 and book a follow-up next week. Agreed?那总结一下——3 件事：(1) Liam，周五前把 PCI-DSS 范围图提交给 Sarah；(2) 审计事件 schema 改动这个 Sprint 合入，TRM 审计前完成 30 天回填；(3) 我把 GDPR 级联+凭证工作流加进 ADR-0207，下周再开一次跟进会。大家同意吗？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open We have three regulatory questions blocking the design review. 有 3 个监管问题卡住了设计评审 Name the count up front. Compliance and InfoSec respect numbered lists — they know exactly how long the meeting is.
  • Scope What's the PCI-DSS scope of the new token vault? 新 token 库的 PCI-DSS 适用范围是什么？ "Scope" is the regulator's first question. If you can't draw the scope diagram, you can't pass the audit.
  • Standard Group Standard GCS-PCI-04 requires… 集团标准 GCS-PCI-04 要求… Always cite the Group Standard by its code (GCS-PCI-04, GCS-GDPR-12). Numbers beat descriptions in compliance reviews.
  • Audit Audit trail must be immutable and retained for 7 years. 审计追踪必须不可篡改并保留 7 年 MAS TRM retention is 7 years. HKMA is 7 years. PCI-DSS audit logs are 12 months online, 3 months immediate. Know the numbers.
  • Re-attest Re-attested every quarter. 每季度重新确认 "Re-attest" is the compliance verb. Compliance is not a one-off — it is a quarterly re-confirmation that scope hasn't drifted.
  • Cascade The erasure request must cascade to all dependent systems. 删除请求必须级联到所有依赖系统 "Cascade" is GDPR's key word. If your deletion logic doesn't cascade, you have a finding on your hands.
  • Evidence How do we prove deletion to the regulator? 怎么向监管方证明已删除？ Regulators don't take your word for it. You need a deletion certificate — a machine-readable artifact the regulator can verify.
  • Audit window The MAS TRM audit window opens in 6 weeks. MAS TRM 审计窗口 6 周后开 "Audit window" = a fixed date range. Any change must land before it opens, or you carry the risk into the audit.
  • SDL Group Standard SDL-GCS-12 requires InfoSec sign-off at the design review. 集团标准 SDL-GCS-12 要求设计评审阶段 InfoSec 签批 "SDL" = Secure Development Lifecycle. HSBC's mandatory secure-by-design process. Bypass it and the CAB will reject your release.
  • Baseline The Group Cyber baseline is stricter for on-prem. 集团 Cyber 基线对 on-prem 更严 "Baseline" = the minimum control set. Cloud and on-prem have different baselines — pick the right one before you start designing.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • PCI-DSS n. 支付卡行业数据安全标准 Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard. The global standard for protecting card data.
  • MAS TRM n. 新加坡金管局技术风险管理 Monetary Authority of Singapore — Technology Risk Management guidelines.
  • HKMA n. 香港金管局 Hong Kong Monetary Authority. The HK banking regulator; supervises HK licensed banks.
  • GDPR n. 通用数据保护条例 EU General Data Protection Regulation. Covers data-subject rights including right to erasure.
  • SDL (Secure Development Lifecycle) n. 安全开发生命周期 HSBC's mandatory secure-by-design process; required at every design review.
  • Group Standard n. 集团标准 A bank-wide mandatory standard with a code (e.g., GCS-PCI-04). Overrides local preferences.
  • Group Cyber n. 集团网络安全 The bank-wide cybersecurity function. Owns the secure-by-default baseline.
  • Tokenisation n. 令牌化 Replacing sensitive data (e.g., PAN) with a non-sensitive token that maps back to the original.
  • Token vault n. Token 库 The secure store that holds the token-to-PAN mapping.
  • PAN (Primary Account Number) n. 主账号 The 16-digit card number. PCI-DSS scope the moment it is processed or stored.
  • Scope diagram n. 范围图 A diagram showing which systems, networks, and people are in PCI-DSS scope.
  • Right to erasure n. 被遗忘权 GDPR Article 17. The data subject's right to have their personal data deleted on request.
  • Audit trail n. 审计追踪 A chronological, immutable record of events — required for MAS TRM, HKMA, SOX.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① Passive voice for regulatory requirements

Compliance and InfoSec speak in the passive voice because the law applies to the data or the system, not to you. Notice the audit trail must be immutable and retained for 7 years — not we must keep the audit trail for 7 years. The passive form signals the obligation is regulatory, not personal — a subtle but important register shift in compliance meetings.

合规和 InfoSec 习惯用被动语态——强调是法规要求，不是个人要求。

② "Group Standard GCS-XXX-NN requires…" — citing by code

When citing a regulatory or group obligation, use the subject + code + requires + object + to-infinitive pattern: Group Standard GCS-GDPR-12 requires the erasure request to cascade to all dependent systems. The code is the noun phrase, requires is the verb, and the to-infinitive carries the action. This is the HSBC-architect's standard sentence for "you must do X".

引用标准时用 "Group Standard GCS-XXX-NN requires + 宾语 + to do" 句式——汇丰架构师的标准"必须做 X"说法。

③ "Honest answer —" / "Just to flag —" — the compliance softenings

Two of the most useful softeners in a compliance conversation: honest answer — (signals you are about to say something uncomfortable) and just to flag — (signals you are about to raise a concern, not demand action). Both are polite previews that prepare the room for a sensitive point. In a meeting with InfoSec and Compliance, they are the difference between a finding and a follow-up.

Honest answer 和 just to flag 是合规对话的"软启动器"——让对方先有心理准备。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • Speak the regulator's language, not the engineer's. "PCI-DSS scope diagram" lands with InfoSec; "we encrypt the PAN" does not. The first is a control; the second is a feature. Architects translate between the two.
  • Always cite the Group Standard code. "GCS-PCI-04" carries more weight than "the PCI standard". A cited code is also a self-documenting audit trail — Compliance can look it up in the Group Standards portal.
  • The SDL gate is not optional. At HSBC, every design review must include an InfoSec sign-off against the SDL. A "design done, security later" attitude will get rejected at CAB or eCAB. Plan the security review at the design stage, not at the release stage.
  • Cross-region means cross-regulator. A Singapore-hosted service can still touch HKMA, MAS, FCA, and GDPR. The architecture is Singapore; the regulatory map is global. Always ask "which regulators can read this data?" before you finalise the design.
  • Compliance is a date, not a feature. The MAS TRM audit window opens in 6 weeks. That is a hard date. Compliance work is sequenced backwards from that date — not forwards from "whenever we have time".
  • "Right to erasure" cascades, not just deletes. GDPR's Article 17 doesn't just delete the row — it cascades to dependent systems, backups, replicas, and analytics stores. If your architecture has any side-channel that holds personal data, GDPR applies to it too.
  • Re-attestation is forever. A scope diagram signed this quarter must be re-attested next quarter. Compliance is a quarterly rhythm, not a one-off. Build the re-attestation process into your delivery cadence from day one.
  • When in doubt, escalate to Group InfoSec and Group Compliance. Tribal InfoSec can answer "how do we do this?". Group InfoSec and Group Compliance can answer "should we do this?". A 30-min call with Group is the cheapest insurance against a finding.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the compliance conversation

Translate the following compliance conversation into natural Chinese. Pay attention to the regulatory terms and the Group Standard codes.

"Ravi: Honest answer — under MAS TRM, the audit trail for any customer-facing transaction must be immutable and retained for 7 years. Group Standard GCS-TRM-08 requires the audit event to include the service principal, the user identity, and a cryptographic hash of the payload. If your OnePay redesign doesn't capture all three, you'll have a finding in the next TRM audit. Just to flag — the audit window opens in 6 weeks, so I'd recommend getting the schema change merged this sprint. Happy to review the event design on a 30-min call this week."

"Ravi：实话实说——根据 MAS TRM，任何面向客户的交易审计追踪必须不可篡改并保留 7 年。集团标准 GCS-TRM-08 要求审计事件必须包含服务主体、用户身份以及 payload 的加密哈希。如果你们 OnePay 的重设计没把 3 项都捕获，下一次 TRM 审计就会有 finding。提一下——审计窗口 6 周后开，所以我建议这个 Sprint 把 schema 改动合进去。这周我可以花 30 分钟过一下事件设计。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the compliance error

A junior architect has drafted the following design-review response. Which lines contain compliance errors that must be fixed before InfoSec sign-off? Why?

  1. "We'll add TLS to the API."
  2. "The token vault will store PANs encrypted with AES-256."
  3. "We'll keep audit logs for 90 days."
  4. "The PCI-DSS scope diagram will be drawn at release time."
  5. "EU customers can request data deletion — we'll handle it manually."

① is too vague — "TLS" doesn't say which version (TLS 1.2+ is the Group Cyber baseline), and it doesn't address mTLS for partner APIs. ② is dangerous — storing PAN at all means the full PCI-DSS scope applies; the design should aim to avoid PAN storage, not just encrypt it. ③ is wrong — MAS TRM and HKMA require 7-year retention for transaction audit logs, not 90 days. ④ is too late — the scope diagram must be filed with InfoSec at the design stage, not at release (per GCS-PCI-04). ⑤ is risky — manual deletion is not a control; GDPR requires an automated, auditable cascade with a deletion certificate. All five lines need to be rewritten before InfoSec sign-off.

Write your 90-second chapter briefing in English. Use at least 3 patterns from the Key Phrases section.
