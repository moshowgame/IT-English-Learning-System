Tech Risk Assessment Memo

Article 13 · Module 4: Risk & Governance Communication · Architect English

⏱ 10 min read 🟠 Upper-intermediate 🎯 Quantify the risk, propose mitigation, name the residual risk — for the RIB and Risk forum

1. Scenario 场景背景

It is Wednesday afternoon, 3:00 PM HKT. The GPB Onboarding squad is preparing to onboard a new sanctions-screening vendor (Vendor X) for the high-net-worth customer screening flow. The vendor is 30% cheaper and ~40% faster than the incumbent, but it has never been used in production at HSBC, and it sits outside the bank's Group Cyber approved vendor list.

周三下午 3 点香港时间，GPB 开户 squad 准备接入一个新的制裁名单筛查厂商（Vendor X）， 用于高净值客户筛查流程。厂商比现有方案便宜 30%、速度快约 40%， 但从未在汇丰生产环境使用过，也不在银行的集团 Cyber 批准厂商名单上。

As the Solution Architect covering GPB, you have been asked by Margaret (Chief Architect, HK) to draft a 1-page Tech Risk Assessment Memo for next week's Resilience & Risk Forum (RIB). The forum includes representatives from Group Cyber, Group Resilience, Compliance, and the tribe lead. The memo must quantify the risk, propose mitigation, and explicitly name the residual risk after mitigation.

作为覆盖 GPB 的 Solution 架构师，Margaret（Chief Architect，香港）请你 起草一份 1 页的技术风险评估备忘录，提交给下周的 Resilience & Risk Forum (RIB)。 论坛成员包括 集团 Cyber、集团 Resilience、合规 和 tribe lead。 备忘录必须量化风险、提出缓解措施，并明确指出缓解后的残留风险（residual risk）。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Margaret (Chief Architect): Wei, quick one. The GPB Onboarding squad wants to onboard Vendor X for sanctions screening. It's not on the Group Cyber approved list. I need a 1-pager for the RIB by Monday. Can you draft it?Wei，插个会外的事。GPB 开户 squad 想接入 Vendor X 做制裁名单筛查，它不在集团 Cyber 批准名单上。我需要一份 1 页的备忘录下周一前给 RIB，你起草一下？
You (Architect): Will do. Before I start, I want to align on the structure — the last RIB memo I read had: risk description, likelihood × impact, mitigation, residual risk, and sign-off ask. Anything you want to add or cut?没问题。开始前先对齐一下结构——我上次看的 RIB 备忘录包括：风险描述、可能性 × 影响、缓解、残留风险，以及签批请求。有要加的或要删的吗？
Margaret (Chief Architect): Structure's fine. One addition — name the regulatory exposure explicitly. If Vendor X misses a sanctions hit, the exposure is HKMA + MAS TRM + FCA. Quantify where you can. "Significant" or "high" are not numbers.结构没问题。加一点——明确写出监管暴露。如果 Vendor X 漏掉一次制裁命中，监管暴露是 HKMA + MAS TRM + FCA。能量化就量化。"显著"或"高"不是数字。
You (Architect): Got it. I'll use a 5×5 heat map — likelihood 3 (new vendor, no production track record) × impact 5 (regulatory + reputational). That's inherent risk = 15, which is above the RIB escalation threshold of 12.明白了。我用 5×5 热力图——可能性 3（新厂商，无生产记录）× 影响 5（监管 + 声誉），固有风险 = 15，超过 RIB 上报阈值 12。
Margaret (Chief Architect): Good. For mitigation, I'm thinking — keep the incumbent vendor in shadow mode for 90 days, run a parallel screening for the first 10,000 customers, and require a Group Cyber security assessment before go-live. Does that bring residual risk down to acceptable?好的。缓解方面我想——让现有厂商shadow 模式跑 90 天，前 10,000 个客户做并行筛查，上线前必须有 集团 Cyber 安全评估。这样能把残留风险降到可接受范围吗？
You (Architect): Honest answer — it brings it to around 6 (likelihood 2 × impact 3), which is below the 12 threshold. But there's still a residual risk on the data-residency side — Vendor X's API is hosted in Singapore, not HK. Does that breach our MAS TRM data-residency rule for SG-customer traffic?坦白说——能降到大约 6（可能性 2 × 影响 3），低于 12 阈值。但还有一个数据驻留的残留风险——Vendor X 的 API 托管在新加坡而不是香港。这会违反 MAS TRM 对新加坡客户流量的数据驻留规则吗？
Margaret (Chief Architect): Good flag. Add it to the memo as a second residual-risk item. Loop in Daniel from InfoSec UK — he'll know the MAS TRM answer. I want the memo to name both residual risks explicitly, not bury them in a footnote.好问题。把它加进备忘录作为第二项残留风险。拉上英国的 InfoSec Daniel——他清楚 MAS TRM 的答案。我希望备忘录把两项残留风险都明确写出来，不要藏在脚注里。
You (Architect): Noted. I'll send a draft to you and Daniel by Friday EOD HKT. If the data-residency risk is a real breach, I'll recommend a third mitigation — a HK-resident data proxy in front of the Vendor X API. That adds ~20ms latency but keeps us compliant.明白。我会在香港时间周五 EOD 前发你和 Daniel 一份草稿。如果数据驻留是真违反，我会建议第三项缓解措施——在 Vendor X API 前加香港驻留的数据代理。会多约 20ms 延迟，但能保持合规。
Margaret (Chief Architect): Perfect. Just to flag — the tribe lead is pushing for go-live by end of Q3 to hit the cost-savings target. So the memo also needs an explicit go / no-go recommendation. My instinct: conditional go, contingent on the three mitigations. You agree?好的。提一下——tribe lead 在推 Q3 底前上线以达成成本节约目标。所以备忘录还要有明确的 go / no-go 建议。我的判断是：有条件放行，取决于 3 项缓解措施。你同意吗？

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Open Quick one — I need a 1-pager for the RIB by Monday. 插个会外的事——我需要一份 1 页的备忘录下周一前给 RIB "Quick one" is the chief-architect's signal that this is small but time-sensitive. Don't mistake it for low importance.
  • Quantify "Significant" or "high" are not numbers. Quantify where you can. "显著"或"高"不是数字。能量化就量化 The RIB will reject adjectives. Use a 5×5 heat map: likelihood × impact. Anything above 12 escalates.
  • Inherent Inherent risk = 15, which is above the RIB escalation threshold of 12. 固有风险 = 15，超过 RIB 上报阈值 12 "Inherent risk" = the risk before any mitigation. Always state it explicitly — it's the worst case.
  • Shadow Keep the incumbent vendor in shadow mode for 90 days. 让现有厂商 shadow 模式跑 90 天 "Shadow mode" = runs in parallel, no production traffic. Lets you diff outputs against the incumbent before cutover.
  • Residual The memo should name both residual risks explicitly. 备忘录要把两项残留风险都明确写出来 "Residual risk" = what's left after mitigation. Always name it, never bury it. The RIB will ask "and after all that, what's still on the table?"
  • Blocker If the residency issue is a hard breach, I'd flip it to no-go until resolved. 如果驻留问题是硬性违反，我会把建议翻到 no-go "Hard breach" = a Group Standard, regulator, or law is violated. Soft breach = group preference. Always distinguish.
  • Conditional My instinct: conditional go, contingent on the three mitigations. 我的判断是：有条件放行，取决于 3 项缓解 Three RIB options: go / no-go / conditional go. "Conditional" is the most common and the safest. Always name the conditions.
  • Regulatory If Vendor X misses a sanctions hit, the exposure is HKMA + MAS TRM + FCA. 如果 Vendor X 漏掉一次制裁命中，监管暴露是 HKMA + MAS TRM + FCA Always name the regulators by acronym. HKMA = Hong Kong; MAS TRM = Singapore; FCA = UK; OCC = US. The RIB expects acronyms.
  • Loop Loop in Daniel from InfoSec UK — he'll know the MAS TRM answer. 拉上英国的 InfoSec Daniel——他清楚 MAS TRM 的答案 "Loop in" = bring into the conversation. Use it for people whose input is needed before the memo can be finalised.
  • Recommend I'll recommend a third mitigation — a HK-resident data proxy. 我会建议第三项缓解措施——加一个香港驻留的数据代理 Always pair mitigation with a cost (latency, money, complexity). The RIB weighs the trade-off, not just the benefit.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • RIB (Resilience & Risk Forum) n. 韧性 & 风险论坛 Monthly cross-functional forum that approves technology risks above a heat-map threshold.
  • Tech Risk Assessment Memo n. 技术风险评估备忘录 A 1-page memo quantifying risk, mitigation, and residual risk for the RIB.
  • Inherent risk n. 固有风险 The risk before any mitigation is applied. The "worst case" number.
  • Residual risk n. 残留风险 The risk that remains after all mitigations are applied. Must be named, not buried.
  • Heat map (5×5) n. 热力图 A risk-scoring matrix: likelihood (1–5) × impact (1–5). Anything above 12 escalates to the RIB.
  • Shadow mode n. 影子模式 A vendor runs in parallel with the incumbent; outputs are compared but no production traffic is routed.
  • Group Cyber approved vendor list n. 集团 Cyber 批准厂商名单 The bank's vetted vendor list. New vendors require a security assessment.
  • MAS TRM (Monetary Authority of Singapore Technology Risk Management) n. 新加坡金管局技术风险管理指南 Singapore's regulator's technology risk guidelines.
  • HKMA (Hong Kong Monetary Authority) n. 香港金管局 Hong Kong's banking regulator. Owns the SA-2 (outsourcing) and TM-G-1 (technology risk) circulars.
  • FCA (Financial Conduct Authority, UK) n. 英国金融行为监管局 UK regulator; oversees operational resilience (PS21/3) and outsourcing (SYSC 8).
  • Data residency n. 数据驻留 The legal requirement that customer data must reside in a specific jurisdiction (e.g., SG customer data in SG).
  • Conditional go / no-go n. 有条件放行 / 不放行 The three RIB recommendation options. "Conditional" requires named conditions.
  • Regulatory exposure n. 监管暴露 The set of regulators + frameworks that would respond to a failure. Always named by acronym.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Likelihood × impact" — the math of risk writing

HSBC risk memos use the 5×5 heat map: likelihood (1–5) × impact (1–5) = inherent risk score. A score of 12 or above escalates to the RIB. After mitigation, you re-score to get the residual risk. The grammar pattern: "Inherent risk = likelihood 3 × impact 5 = 15, above the RIB threshold of 12. Residual risk = likelihood 2 × impact 3 = 6, below threshold." — always with the equation written out, never just the number.

HSBC 风险备忘录用 5×5 热力图：可能性 × 影响 = 风险分。12 分以上上报 RIB。

② "I'd flip it to no-go until resolved" — the conditional past

The pattern If X, I'd Y uses the past subjunctive (I'd, I'd recommend, I'd flip) to soften a recommendation. Compare: "If it's a breach, I recommend no-go." (firmer) vs. "If it's a breach, I'd flip it to no-go." (more measured). The past form signals "this is one possible path" rather than "this is the verdict". Use it when the data isn't yet final.

If X, I'd Y 的过去时让建议更柔和——在数据没敲定时更合适。

③ "Contingent on" vs "subject to" vs "pending"

Three prepositions to chain conditions: conditional go, contingent on the three mitigations (= the conditions must happen first); subject to InfoSec approval (= a specific person/body must approve); pending the data-residency check (= waiting on a specific event). Mixing them up is a tell that the architect is thinking in their first language. Use the right one for the right kind of dependency.

三个连词区分不同的依赖关系：contingent on（条件前置）、subject to（须经…批准）、pending（等待某事）。

6. Cultural Tips 文化 / 职场 Tips

  • The RIB expects numbers, not adjectives. "High" / "significant" / "concerning" are red flags that the architect didn't do the math. Use the 5×5 heat map. Show the equation.
  • Always name the residual risk. The RIB's #1 question is "after all the mitigations, what's still on the table?". Burying residual risk in a footnote is a career-limiting move at HSBC.
  • Pair every mitigation with a cost. "Add HK-resident proxy — adds 20ms latency, ~$8k/month infra" is good. "Add HK-resident proxy" is incomplete. The RIB weighs trade-offs, not just benefits.
  • Loop in the right expert before the memo. Daniel (InfoSec) for MAS TRM; Margaret (Chief Architect) for chapter; tribe lead for cost-benefit. Don't write the memo in a vacuum and then ask for sign-off — that triggers a re-write.
  • Cross-region timing: IST + HKT + GMT. Rohit thinks in IST, you in HKT, Daniel in GMT. Always state deadlines in two zones: "Friday EOD HKT / 5 PM GMT". The RIB will assume you mean HKT unless told otherwise.
  • Conditional go is the default recommendation. Blanket go is naïve; blanket no-go is obstructionist. Conditional go with named conditions is the architect's signature — and what the RIB expects.
  • Cite the Group Standard by name. "G-SDL-2024-09", "G-DR-2023-11", "Group Cyber Standard 7.3" — the RIB knows the standards by number. Vague references ("the security policy") will be sent back.
  • Distinguish hard breach from soft preference. "MAS TRM data residency is a hard breach (regulator)." vs. "Group prefers HK-resident; not a breach but a strong preference." The RIB reacts very differently to the two.

7. Practice 练习题

Exercise 1 — Translate the risk memo intro

Translate the following risk-memo opening into natural Chinese. Notice the math being done out loud.

"Risk: Onboarding of Vendor Y for KYC document OCR in the CMB Onboarding squad. Inherent risk: likelihood 4 (new vendor, no production track record at HSBC) × impact 4 (regulatory exposure = HKMA + MAS TRM + GDPR for EU customers) = 16, above the RIB escalation threshold of 12. Mitigation: (1) shadow mode for 60 days, (2) Group Cyber security assessment before go-live, (3) data residency in HK or SG only. Residual risk: estimated at 6 (likelihood 2 × impact 3), below threshold. Recommend: conditional go, contingent on all three mitigations and InfoSec sign-off."

"风险：CMB 开户 squad 接入 Vendor Y 做 KYC 文件 OCR。固有风险：可能性 4（新厂商，在汇丰无生产记录）× 影响 4（监管暴露 = HKMA + MAS TRM + 欧盟客户的 GDPR）= 16，超过 RIB 上报阈值 12。缓解措施：(1) shadow 模式跑 60 天，(2) 上线前必须经集团 Cyber 安全评估，(3) 数据驻留仅限香港或新加坡。残留风险：估计为 6（可能性 2 × 影响 3），低于阈值。建议：有条件放行，3 项缓解措施全部到位、且经 InfoSec 签批为前提。"

Exercise 2 — Spot the difference: strong vs weak risk writing

Which of these sentences would survive a RIB review, and which would be sent back? Why?

  1. "The risk is high and we need to be careful."
  2. "Inherent risk = likelihood 3 × impact 5 = 15, above the RIB threshold of 12. Residual risk after mitigation ≈ 6, below threshold."
  3. "Vendor X might have a data residency issue, which is bad."
  4. "Vendor X API is hosted in Singapore. MAS TRM data-residency rule for SG-customer traffic requires SG-resident processing. This is a hard breach if SG customers are in scope. Recommend a HK/SG-resident data proxy as mitigation — adds ~20ms p99 latency."

② and ④ survive. ② uses the 5×5 heat map equation explicitly. ④ names the regulator (MAS TRM), the rule (data residency), the breach type (hard), the mitigation (HK/SG-resident proxy), and the cost (~20ms p99). ① and ③ fail — adjectives, no numbers, no regulator, no mitigation, no cost.

Exercise 3 — Your turn

You are the Solution Architect for the Wealth Self-Service squad. The squad wants to migrate the OneWealth portfolio-rebalancing API from on-prem to AWS Lambda. The new design has: (a) no DR drill yet, (b) a new dependency on AWS Step Functions, (c) regulatory exposure to HKMA (TM-G-1) and MAS TRM. Write a 1-paragraph Tech Risk Assessment Memo opening that: (1) names the inherent risk using a 5×5 score, (2) proposes two mitigations, (3) names the residual risk, (4) recommends conditional go / no-go. Use at least 3 patterns from the Key Phrases section.

"Risk: migration of the OneWealth portfolio-rebalancing API from on-prem to AWS Lambda. Inherent risk: likelihood 4 (new platform, no production track record at HSBC, no DR drill yet) × impact 4 (regulatory exposure = HKMA TM-G-1 + MAS TRM, plus potential customer impact during rebalancing failure) = 16, above the RIB escalation threshold of 12. Mitigation: (1) shadow mode for 90 days running 5% of production traffic, (2) full DR drill before go-live with Group Resilience sign-off, (3) AWS Step Functions dependency reviewed by InfoSec for SDL compliance. Residual risk: estimated at 6 (likelihood 2 × impact 3), below threshold — but contingent on Group Cyber sign-off on Step Functions and a successful DR drill. Recommend: conditional go, contingent on the three mitigations and InfoSec sign-off. If the DR drill fails or Step Functions fails the SDL review, flip to no-go until resolved."
