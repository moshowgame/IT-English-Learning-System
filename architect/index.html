Welcome, Architect / 架构师你好

As an architect at HSBC, you sit at the intersection of technology, risk, and business strategy. You write ADRs, lead design reviews, defend NFR budgets, negotiate tech debt vs. new features, and talk to CTOs, regulators, BAs, and developers across the UK, Hong Kong, mainland China, and India. This section focuses on the spoken English scenarios you actually face — from architecture standups to saying "no" to a tech that doesn't fit.

How to Use This Section / 学习建议

💡 Daily Practice Tip

Pick one article per day. Architects live in meetings — focus on the trade-off framing: always present 2-3 options, the chosen one, and the consequences. That rhythm is what makes an architect sound senior.

⚠️ For Real HSBC Work

Always validate architecture decisions with your local Chief Architect, InfoSec, and Risk before circulating widely. Group Standards (SDL, Group Cyber, Group Resilience) override local preferences.
