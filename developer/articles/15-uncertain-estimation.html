Estimating Uncertain Tasks

Article 15 · Module 4: Difficult Conversations · Developer English

⏱ 8 min read 🟡 Intermediate 📏 Express uncertainty, give ranges, protect the team

1. Scenario 场景背景

In sprint planning, your PM Sarah (London) is asking for estimates on PAY-2950 — "Migrate the trade order service from WebSphere on-prem to AWS EKS." This is a big, multi-week effort with many unknowns: database migration approach, security review, network topology, performance testing strategy. Sarah wants a single number. You need to communicate the uncertainty honestly.

冲刺规划会上，PM Sarah（伦敦）要求给 PAY-2950（把交易订单服务从 WebSphere 本地迁移到 AWS EKS）估时。这是一个多周的工作，很多未知：数据库迁移方法、 安全评审、网络拓扑、性能测试策略。Sarah 要一个数字。你需要诚实沟通不确定性。

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sarah (PM): OK Wei, what's your estimate for PAY-2950? Single number please — I need it for the SteerCo slide.Wei，PAY-2950 你估多少？请给个单一数字——我要写进 SteerCo 幻灯片。
You: Sarah, honestly, I'd push back gently on a single number. This story has too many unknowns to estimate accurately today.Sarah，坦白说，我想温和地反对只给一个数字。这个故事未知因素太多，今天没法准确估算。
Sarah (PM): I appreciate that, but the SteerCo wants a single point estimate. Can you give me your best guess?谢谢，但 SteerCo 想要一个单点估算。你能给个最佳猜测吗？
You: Sure, here's how I'd think about it:好的，我是这样想的：
You: If everything goes well — the AWS account is ready, the security review is fast, the network topology is straightforward, and we hit the happy path first try — I'd say 5 sprints (~10 weeks).如果一切顺利——AWS 账号就绪、安全评审快、网络拓扑简单、一次走通——我估 5 个 sprint（约 10 周）。
You: If we hit a few of the typical migration hiccups — say, a 2-week wait for InfoSec on the VPC peering, a DB migration issue, and some performance tuning — I'd say 8 sprints (~16 weeks).如果遇到一些典型迁移问题——比如 InfoSec 在 VPC 对等连接上要等 2 周、DB 迁移有问题、需要性能调优——我估 8 个 sprint（约 16 周）。
You: If things go really sideways — say, InfoSec requires a re-architecture, or we discover a hidden WebSphere dependency — I'd say 13 sprints (~26 weeks). Hopefully not, but I want to be honest about the tail risk.如果情况很糟糕——比如 InfoSec 要求重新架构，或者发现隐藏的 WebSphere 依赖——我估 13 个 sprint（约 26 周）。希望不会，但我要诚实面对尾部风险。
You: So my best single-number estimate is 8 sprints, but I'd flag it as at risk from day one. For SteerCo, I'd suggest showing a range — maybe 5 to 13 sprints, 8 as the most likely. That's more honest than a single number.所以我最佳的单点估算是 8 个 sprint，但要从第一天就标记有风险。建议给 SteerCo 展示区间——比如 5 到 13 个 sprint，8 是最可能值。这比单一数字更诚实。
You: My strong recommendation is that we do a 1-sprint spike first — just to de-risk the unknowns: AWS account setup, security scoping, DB migration POC. After the spike, I can give you a much tighter estimate — probably ±2 sprints instead of ±5 sprints.我强烈建议先做一个 1 sprint 的 spike——专门解决未知项：AWS 账号搭建、安全范围、DB 迁移 POC。Spike 之后我可以给出更紧的估算——可能 ±2 sprint 而不是 ±5。
Sarah (PM): OK, that makes sense. Let me put 8 sprints in the slide as the most likely, with a range of 5-13 in the footnotes. Can you do the spike in sprint 1 and re-estimate after?好的，有道理。我把 8 个 sprint 写进幻灯片作为最可能值，区间 5-13 放在脚注。你能在 sprint 1 做 spike 然后重新估算吗？
You: Yes, happy to. I'll set up the spike stories in the next refinement and come back with a revised estimate at the end of the spike.没问题，很乐意。我会在下次 backlog refinement 里建好 spike 故事，spike 结束后给你修订后的估算。
David (SM): Good call on the spike, Wei. I'll add it to the backlog with 3 points allocated for the spike work.决定做 spike 很对，Wei。我会把它加到 backlog 里，给 spike 工作分配 3 个点。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Honest Honestly, I'd push back on a single number. 说实话，我会 push back 单个数字 Use honestly / to be honest / truth be told to flag a real concern.
  • Unknowns This story has too many unknowns. 这张卡有太多未知 Always name the source of uncertainty. "Unknowns" is the standard term.
  • Scenario If everything goes well… / If we hit a few hiccups… / If things go sideways… 如果一切顺利 / 如果遇到一些问题 / 如果很不顺利 Three-scenario estimation is the gold standard for high-uncertainty tasks.
  • Best guess My best single-number estimate is 8 sprints. 我最佳单数字估计是 8 个冲刺 Even when giving a single number, label it as best guess / most likely.
  • Range I'd suggest showing a range — 5 to 13 sprints. 我建议展示区间 — 5 到 13 冲刺 Ranges are more honest than point estimates. "5 to 13, 8 most likely".
  • Tail risk Want to be honest about the tail risk. 想诚实说明尾部风险 Tail risk = the low-probability but high-impact worst case.
  • De-risk Do a 1-sprint spike first to de-risk the unknowns. 先做 1 冲刺 spike 降低未知风险 De-risk = reduce the unknowns. A spike de-risks the future estimate.
  • Tighter A much tighter estimate after the spike. Spike 后估计会更紧 Spikes trade a small upfront cost for a much tighter (less uncertain) future estimate.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • point estimate n. 点估计 (单个数字) A single number. Often required by management, but rarely accurate.
  • range estimate / confidence interval n. 区间估计 / 置信区间 A range with a most-likely value. More honest than a point estimate.
  • unknowns n. 未知因素 Things we don't know yet. Source of estimation uncertainty.
  • tail risk n. 尾部风险 Low-probability, high-impact worst case. Like a long tail in a probability distribution.
  • de-risk v. 降低风险 Reduce uncertainty before committing to a plan.
  • VPC peering AWS VPC 对等连接 Network connection between two AWS VPCs. Common blocker in cloud migrations.
  • POC (Proof of Concept) n. 概念验证 A small-scale implementation to validate a technical approach.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① Conditional Sentences for Scenarios

When estimating, use conditionals to describe scenarios:

  • If everything goes well, I'd say 5 sprints. (best case)
  • If we hit a few hiccups, I'd say 8 sprints. (most likely)
  • If things go really sideways, I'd say 13 sprints. (worst case)

② "I'd say" / "My estimate is" / "My best guess is"

Three levels of confidence in your estimate:

  • I'd say 8 sprints. (most hedging, casual)
  • My estimate is 8 sprints. (more confident)
  • My best guess is 8 sprints. (acknowledges uncertainty)

③ "Want to be honest" — Softening Predictions

When you want to flag a risk without sounding alarmist:

  • ❌ "This could take 26 weeks." (sounds like a threat)
  • ✅ "I want to be honest about the tail risk — if things go sideways, it could be 26 weeks."

6. Cultural Tips 文化/职场 Tips

📏 The "Cone of Uncertainty" 估算的不确定性锥

Steve McConnell's classic principle: at the start of a project, estimates can be off by 4x. As you progress, the cone narrows.

  • Initial estimate: ±4x (huge uncertainty)
  • After requirements: ±2x
  • After design: ±1.25x
  • After implementation: ±0.25x

This is why spikes are so valuable — they narrow the cone before you commit to a plan.

⚠️ Estimation Anti-Patterns
  • ❌ "It'll take 2 weeks." (over-confident, single point estimate)
  • ✅ "I'd estimate 1 to 4 weeks, 2 most likely. Want me to do a spike to tighten this?"
  • ❌ Padding: "Add 50% for safety." (Hofstadter's Law: it always takes longer than expected, even with padding)
  • ✅ Spike first, then re-estimate with reduced uncertainty.
  • ❌ Committing to a date your team can't meet (damages credibility long-term).
  • ✅ Giving a range + spike proposal (shows maturity, not weakness).

7. Practice 练习题

✍️ Practice Drills
Q1. Translate to native-sounding English:

"Sarah，单个数字我给不了。这卡太多未知。我估5到13个冲刺，8最可能。我建议先做2周spike降低风险。"

"Sarah, to be honest, I'd push back on a single number — this story has too many unknowns to estimate accurately today. My best estimate is 8 sprints, with a range of 5 to 13 sprints. My strong recommendation is to do a 1-sprint spike first to de-risk the unknowns — after that, I can give you a much tighter estimate."
Q2. Convert this over-confident estimate into a range:

"This migration will take exactly 12 weeks."

"Honestly, I'd push back on a single number for a migration of this scale. My best estimate is 12 weeks, but there's a wide range:
  • Best case (everything goes well, no surprises): 8 weeks
  • Most likely (typical migration hiccups): 12 weeks
  • Worst case (InfoSec re-architecture, hidden dependencies): 20 weeks
For the SteerCo, I'd suggest 12 weeks most likely, 8-20 range in the footnotes. Want me to do a 1-sprint spike to tighten this?"
Q3. Fill in the right estimation phrases:

"This story has too many _____. / If everything goes _____, 5 sprints. / My best _____ is 8 sprints. / I'd suggest showing a _____. / A 1-sprint spike would help de-_____ the unknowns."

unknowns / well / estimate (or guess) / range / risk
Q4. Role-play:

Sarah asks for a single-number estimate on migrating 50 microservices from WebSphere to EKS in 6 months. You believe 6 months is unrealistic — each microservice has its own complexity, and they share databases and APIs. Write a 3-scenario estimate + spike recommendation.

"Sarah, honestly, I'd push back on a single number for 50 microservices in 6 months. Too many cross-dependencies (shared DBs, API contracts) to estimate accurately today. Three scenarios:
  • Best case (50 independent services, parallel teams, no shared state): 6 months — possible but unlikely.
  • Most likely (we discover shared dependencies, need to migrate some in pairs): 9-12 months.
  • Worst case (architectural re-design needed, e.g., database consolidation): 18+ months.
My best guess is 9 months, with a 6-18 month range. For SteerCo, I'd show 9 months most likely, 6-18 range in the footnotes.

Strong recommendation: do a 2-sprint spike on 5 representative services (1 simple, 2 medium, 2 complex) to de-risk the cross-dependency question. After the spike, I can re-estimate with much tighter confidence — probably ±1 month instead of ±6 months."
