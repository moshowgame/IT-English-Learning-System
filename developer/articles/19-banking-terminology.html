Banking Terminology

Article 19 · Module 5: HSBC Banking Context · Developer English

⏱ 10 min read 🔴 Advanced 🏦 KYC / AML / Trade Finance / Sanctions / Credit Risk

1. Scenario 场景背景

You are joining a sprint on the Trade Finance Onboarding service. The BA (Priya) is walking you through the user stories, and the Tech Lead (David) keeps dropping acronyms: KYC, CDD, EDD, AML, sanctions screening, trade-based money laundering (TBML), SWIFT MT103, Letter of Credit (LC), UCP 600, sanctions list (OFAC, UN, EU, HMT), credit risk scoring. The standup chat has even more terms you don't recognise: PEP, adverse media, false positive, name match, fuzzy match, regulatory reporting (SAR/STR).

你刚加入 贸易融资开户 服务的 Sprint。BA Priya 在给你讲 user story，Tech Lead David 一直 抛缩写：KYC、CDD、EDD、AML、制裁筛查、贸易洗钱（TBML）、SWIFT MT103、信用证（LC）、UCP 600、 制裁名单（OFAC、UN、EU、HMT）、信贷风险打分。站会聊天里还有更多：PEP、负面媒体、误报、姓名匹配、 模糊匹配、监管报告（SAR/STR）。

You need to understand these terms in spoken English so you can ask sharp questions in refinement, follow the standup, and write code that matches the business intent.

2. Dialogue Part 1: Onboarding & KYC

Priya (BA): Wei, this story is about the onboarding flow for corporate trade finance customers. Per Group Policy, every new customer has to go through KYC — Know Your Customer — before we can open a relationship.Wei，这个故事是关于企业贸易融资客户的开户流程。根据集团政策，每个新客户在开户前都必须做 KYC（了解你的客户）。
You: Got it. Quick check — is the KYC for the standard CDD level, or do we need EDD for high-risk customers?明白。快速确认——KYC 是标准 CDD 级别，还是高风险客户要做 EDD？
Priya (BA): Both. By default, every customer goes through Customer Due Diligence (CDD) — ID, address, beneficial ownership. But if the customer is a PEP — Politically Exposed Person — or operates in a high-risk jurisdiction, the system should trigger Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), which is a deeper review.都要。默认情况下每个客户都要做客户尽职调查（CDD）——身份证、地址、实益所有人。但如果客户是 PEP（政治敏感人物），或在高风险司法管辖区运营，系统应触发强化尽职调查（EDD）——更深入的审查。
You: OK. From a tech standpoint, does EDD need a manual approval workflow, or is it fully automated via a rules engine?好的。从技术角度，EDD 需要人工审批流程吗？还是完全由规则引擎自动化？
Priya (BA): It's partially automated. The risk score is calculated automatically, but if the score is above the EDD threshold, the case gets routed to a compliance analyst for manual review. That's the standard AML pattern — Anti-Money Laundering.部分自动化。风险分数自动计算，但如果分数超过 EDD 阈值，案件会路由到合规分析师做人工审查。这是标准的反洗钱（AML）模式。
You: Understood. One more thing — when a customer fails KYC, do we block the onboarding outright, or is there a remediation path like re-submission of documents?明白。还有一件——客户 KYC 失败时，是直接阻止开户，还是有补救路径比如重新提交文件？
Priya (BA): There's a remediation path. The customer can re-submit the missing or expired documents. But if the failure is due to a sanctions hit, the case goes straight to compliance — no remediation, account opening is blocked.有补救路径。客户可以重新提交缺失或过期的文件。但如果失败是因为命中制裁名单，案件会直接给合规——没有补救，开户被阻止。

2. Dialogue Part 2: Sanctions Screening & SWIFT Messages

David (Tech Lead): Wei, just to set context for the next story: every payment goes through sanctions screening against the OFAC, UN, EU, and HMT lists. The system does a name match — and I mean a fuzzy match, not just exact match — against the watchlists.Wei，为下一个故事铺垫：每笔支付都要对照 OFAC、UN、EU 和 HMT 名单做制裁筛查。系统做名字匹配——我指的是模糊匹配，不只是精确匹配——对照观察名单。
You: Right. And on a hit, do we block the transaction immediately, or do we route it to a sanctions analyst for review first?好的。命中后，是立即阻止交易，还是先路由给制裁分析师审查？
David (Tech Lead): It depends on the match score. If the score is above 95%, the system auto-blocks the transaction. If it's between 70–95%, it goes to a human reviewer. Below 70% is a false positive and the system auto-clears it.取决于匹配分数。如果分数超过 95%，系统自动阻止交易；如果在 70-95% 之间，交给人工审查员；低于 70% 是误报，系统自动放行。
You: Got it. On the false positive side — are we allowed to learn from past false positives to improve the match algorithm, or is the matching logic locked down by Group Compliance?明白。关于误报——我们能从过去的误报中学习、改进匹配算法吗？还是匹配逻辑被集团合规部锁定了？
David (Tech Lead): Good question. The base algorithm is provided by the vendor — we can't change it. But we can tune the match thresholds and the auto-clear / manual review bands, as long as the changes are approved by Compliance and logged for audit.好问题。基础算法由供应商提供——我们不能改。但我们可以调整匹配阈值和自动放行 / 人工审查的区间，只要变更经合规部批准并记录供审计。
You: Understood. One last thing on the SWIFT side: for cross-border payments, is the outbound message an MT103 or an MT202? Just want to make sure I'm looking at the right message schema.明白。最后一个 SWIFT 的问题：跨境支付，出站报文是 MT103 还是 MT202？想确认我看的是正确的报文 schema。
David (Tech Lead): For a single customer transfer, it's MT103. For a bank-to-bank cover payment — like a correspondent bank settlement — it's MT202. For trade finance, we also use MT700 for the Letter of Credit (LC) issuance, governed by UCP 600.单笔客户转账用 MT103。银行间头寸调拨——比如代理行结算——用 MT202。贸易融资还用 MT700 开立信用证（LC），受 UCP 600 约束。
You: Perfect, that's exactly the clarification I needed. I'll dig into the SWIFT message library this afternoon and circle back with any open questions.完美，正是我需要的澄清。我今天下午研究 SWIFT 报文库，有开放问题再回来。

2. Dialogue Part 3: Trade Finance & TBML

Priya (BA): Wei, this story is about monitoring for Trade-Based Money Laundering (TBML). In a nutshell: customers can use trade transactions — invoices, letters of credit, bill of lading — to move value across borders in ways that don't match the underlying goods.Wei，这个故事是关于监测贸易型洗钱（TBML）。简而言之：客户可以通过贸易交易——发票、信用证、提单——跨境转移价值，且与实际货物不匹配。
You: OK. So in practice, the system needs to flag transactions where the invoice value is significantly off the market price for that commodity, or where the trade volume doesn't match the customer's profile?好的。实际中，系统需要标记以下交易：发票金额与该商品市价明显不符，或交易量与客户画像不匹配。
Priya (BA): Exactly. One common red flag is over- or under-invoicing — the customer overstates the invoice to move money out of the country, or understates it to bring money in. Another is phantom shipping — bill of lading with no actual goods.没错。常见红旗之一是溢开或低开发票——客户高开发票把钱转出境外，或低开发票把钱转进。另一个是虚假运输——有提单但没有实际货物。
You: Got it. On the data side — do we have access to commodity price feeds in real time, or is this batch-based at end of day?明白。数据方面——我们能拿到实时商品价格数据吗？还是只能每日批量？
Priya (BA): Mostly batch — we get a daily feed from Refinitiv for reference prices. The monitoring job runs nightly and generates alerts. Anything high-risk is routed to a TBML analyst.主要是批量——我们每天从 Refinitiv 拿到参考价格。监测任务每晚跑，生成告警。高风险的会路由给 TBML 分析师。
You: That makes sense. And if the analyst confirms the alert, we file an SAR — Suspicious Activity Report — with the regulator, right?合理。如果分析师确认告警，我们就向监管机构提交 SAR（可疑活动报告），对吗？
Priya (BA): Correct. Locally it's called an STR — Suspicious Transaction Report — and it goes to the HKMA in Hong Kong, or FCA in the UK, depending on the booking entity. Don't worry about the filing itself — that's a manual process by Compliance. Our job is to generate the alert cleanly.对。本地叫 STR（可疑交易报告），根据入账主体提交给香港 HKMA 或英国 FCA。报告本身不用担心——那是合规部的手工流程。我们要做的是把告警生成干净。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Clarify Quick check — is this standard CDD or do we need EDD? 快速确认——是标准 CDD 还是要 EDD？ Always clarify the level of due diligence before designing the workflow.
  • Trigger …the system should trigger Enhanced Due Diligence. 系统应该触发增强型尽职调查 "Trigger" is the standard verb for risk-based rules.
  • Route The case gets routed to a compliance analyst. 案件被路由到合规分析师 "Route to" = 自动/手工分派。HSBC 大量使用此表达。
  • Block Account opening is blocked. 开户被阻止 "Block" = 阻止/拒绝。区别于"reject"——"block"通常是风控/合规强制。
  • Fuzzy match It's a fuzzy match, not just an exact match. 这是模糊匹配，不只是精确匹配 Sanctions screening always uses fuzzy matching to catch aliases.
  • Tune We can tune the match thresholds. 我们可以调整匹配阈值 区别于"change"——"tune"是参数微调，受 Compliance 监管。
  • File We file an SAR with the regulator. 我们向监管机构提交 SAR "File a report" = 正式提交监管报告。HK 叫 STR，UK 叫 SAR。
  • Confirm Don't worry about the filing itself — our job is to generate the alert cleanly. 提交报告本身不用你管——我们的职责是把告警生成得干净 明确开发 vs 合规的职责边界，避免 scope creep。

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • KYC (Know Your Customer) banking 了解你的客户 Mandatory process of verifying customer identity before onboarding.
  • CDD (Customer Due Diligence) n. 客户尽职调查（标准级） Standard level: ID + address + beneficial ownership.
  • EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence) n. 增强型尽职调查 Deeper review for PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions, or complex structures.
  • PEP (Politically Exposed Person) n. 政治敏感人物 Someone holding a prominent public function. Triggers EDD automatically.
  • AML (Anti-Money Laundering) n. 反洗钱 The framework of laws, regulations, and procedures to detect and prevent money laundering.
  • TBML (Trade-Based Money Laundering) n. 贸易洗钱 Using trade transactions (invoices, LCs) to move value illicitly across borders.
  • Sanctions screening n. 制裁名单筛查 Screening customers and payments against OFAC / UN / EU / HMT watchlists.
  • OFAC / HMT / UN / EU sanctions list n. 制裁名单（美国/英国/联合国/欧盟） OFAC (US), HMT (UK), UN (UN), EU (EU). All must be screened.
  • false positive n. 误报 A match that turns out to be incorrect. System auto-clears below threshold.
  • SWIFT MT103 / MT202 / MT700 n. SWIFT 报文类型 MT103 = customer credit transfer. MT202 = bank-to-bank cover. MT700 = LC issuance.
  • Letter of Credit (LC) n. 信用证 Bank guarantee of payment to the seller, conditional on shipping documents.
  • UCP 600 n. 跟单信用证统一惯例 (2007 修订版) The ICC rules governing LCs. Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits.
  • SAR / STR (Suspicious Activity / Transaction Report) n. 可疑交易报告 SAR (US/UK), STR (HK/SG). Filed with the regulator when AML alerts are confirmed.
  • Beneficial Ownership n. 实益所有权 The real person who ultimately owns or controls the entity, not the nominee.
  • Adverse media screening n. 负面媒体筛查 Screening customers against negative news (sanctions, fraud, criminal activity).

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① "Should trigger / gets routed / is blocked" — Passive for Compliance Rules

Compliance and AML rules are usually described passively — they act on the customer:

  • The system triggers EDD (active, but rare)
  • ✅ EDD is triggered (passive — much more common in compliance speak)
  • ✅ The case is routed to a compliance analyst.
  • ✅ Account opening is blocked.

② "Per Group Policy" / "Per the Standard" — Citing a Rule

Always cite the source of a compliance rule:

  • ❌ "Customers have to do KYC." (too vague)
  • ✅ "Per Group Policy, customers have to do KYC."
  • ✅ "Per the Group AML Standard, …"

③ "In a nutshell" / "Just to set context" — Framing Helpers

Useful phrases to start a topic or summarise:

  • In a nutshell — customers use trade transactions to move value across borders.
  • Just to set context — every payment goes through sanctions screening.
  • By default, every customer goes through CDD.

6. Cultural Tips 文化/职场 Tips

🏦 Banking Jargon at HSBC
  1. Acronyms are the norm. Everyone drops KYC, AML, CDD, EDD, TBML, SAR, MT103 mid-sentence. Don't panic — ask for spelling and meaning. It's expected.
  2. Region matters. SAR (US/UK) vs STR (HK/SG) is the most common gotcha. Always confirm which jurisdiction the user story targets.
  3. Compliance is the authority. Devs can tune thresholds, but the base algorithm is owned by Compliance / the vendor. Don't try to change it.
  4. "The Group Standard" is sacred. When someone says "per the Group Standard", that's the end of the argument. Follow it.
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • ❌ Saying "I think we can skip KYC for low-value customers" — never acceptable at HSBC.
  • ✅ "Per the Group KYC Standard, all customers must complete CDD. For low-value, do we apply a simplified due diligence (SDD) tier?"
  • ❌ "I'll just hard-code the sanctions list." — Lists change weekly. Use the vendor feed.
  • ❌ Calling KYC a "checkbox" — at HSBC, KYC is a regulatory obligation, not a UX step.

7. Practice 练习题

✍️ Practice Drills
Q1. Translate to banking English:

"所有跨境支付都要做OFAC、UN、EU、HMT制裁名单筛查。如果匹配分数高于95%，系统自动阻止；70-95%之间，路由到人工审核；70%以下，自动放行。"

"All cross-border payments go through sanctions screening against the OFAC, UN, EU, and HMT lists. If the match score is above 95%, the transaction is auto-blocked. Between 70–95%, it gets routed to a human reviewer. Below 70% is a false positive and is auto-cleared."
Q2. Fill in the banking terms:

"Every new customer must go through ____ (KYC) — standard ____ (CDD) by default. PEPs and high-risk customers trigger ____ (EDD). Payments are screened against the ____ (sanctions) list with a ____ (fuzzy) match. Confirmed AML alerts are filed as ____ (SAR) or ____ (STR) with the regulator."

KYC / CDD / EDD / sanctions / fuzzy / SAR / STR
Q3. Role-play: Talk to a BA about a KYC story

You are a developer. A new user story says: "All onboarding flows must perform KYC." You need to ask: (a) standard CDD or EDD, (b) which jurisdictions, (c) what happens on KYC fail, (d) who owns the rule engine. Write 4 sharp clarifying questions.

"Hi Priya, a few clarifying questions on the KYC story before I start:

(1) Per the Group Standard, is this standard CDD only, or do we also need to trigger EDD for PEPs and high-risk jurisdictions?

(2) Which jurisdictions are in scope for this sprint — UK, HK, mainland China only, or also cross-border?

(3) On KYC failure — do we block the onboarding outright, or is there a remediation path for re-submission of documents?

(4) Who's owning the rule engine for the EDD trigger logic — is it an existing Compliance service, or are we building it from scratch?

Once I have these answers, I can scope the story cleanly."
Q4. Explain a TBML monitoring concept in plain English

Explain Trade-Based Money Laundering to a junior dev who has no banking background. Cover: (a) what it is, (b) one common red flag, (c) what the system should do.

"TBML — Trade-Based Money Laundering — is when a customer uses trade transactions, like invoices or letters of credit, to move value across borders in ways that don't match the underlying goods. In a nutshell, it's a way to launder money through trade.

One common red flag is over- or under-invoicing — the customer overstates the invoice to move money out of the country, or understates it to bring money in. Another is phantom shipping — a bill of lading with no actual goods behind it.

What the system should do: compare the invoice value against the market reference price for that commodity, and flag anything significantly off. High-risk alerts are routed to a TBML analyst for review, and confirmed cases are filed as an SAR / STR with the regulator."
