Reporting Progress: On Track / At Risk / Blocked

Article 03 · Module 1: Daily Standup · Developer English

⏱ 9 min read 🟡 Intermediate 🚦 Three core status states with the right tone

1. Scenario 场景背景

You are working on the OneWealth trade order service in the Shenzhen office. Today is the 10th day of a two-week sprint. Your PM, Sarah (London), and tech lead, Priya (London), are checking in on your three active tickets. You need to give an honest, calibrated update on each.

你在深圳做 OneWealth 交易订单服务，今天是一个双周冲刺的 第10天。你的PM Sarah 在伦敦，技术负责人 Priya 也在伦敦，他们要检查你三张活跃卡片的进度。你需要诚实、恰当地汇报每一张卡的状态。

  • TRD-882: You are on track, just finished the happy path yesterday.
  • TRD-901: You are at risk because the upstream API team hasn't delivered their stub yet.
  • TRD-915: You are blocked on a database access request that's been pending with DBA team for 3 days.

2. Dialogue 英文对话

Sarah (PM): Wei, before we wrap, can you give us a status on your three tickets? We need to update the sprint burndown for the SteerCo on Friday.Wei，结束前能同步一下三张卡的状态吗？周五 SteerCo 要更新冲刺燃尽图。
You: Sure, Sarah. Here's the latest.没问题 Sarah，最新情况如下。
You: On TRD-882, the trade order placement is fully on track. Happy path is done, and I'm running the regression suite today. Should be code-complete by EOD tomorrow.TRD-882，交易下单功能完全按计划推进。主流程已经做完，今天在跑回归测试，明天 EOD 应该能 code-complete。
You: On TRD-901, I'm flagging this as at risk. The upstream market data team hasn't delivered the price feed stub yet — they slipped from Wednesday to Monday. I can keep working on the consumer side, but the end-to-end test depends on their stub. There's a risk we might slip into the next sprint if they slip again.TRD-901，我标记为 at risk。上游行情数据团队的价格源 stub还没交付——他们从周三延到了周一。我可以继续做消费侧，但端到端测试依赖他们的 stub。如果他们再延一次，有风险会滑到下个冲刺。
Priya (TL): Hmm, good to know early. Have you escalated to the market data team's lead?嗯，早点知道好。你升级到行情团队的负责人了吗？
You: Not yet. I was going to reach out to Rajesh today. Should I also loop in Sarah in case we need to re-prioritise at the SteerCo?还没，我打算今天联系 Rajesh。要不要也把 Sarah拉进来，万一要在 SteerCo 上重新排优先级？
Sarah (PM): Yes, please keep me in the loop. If it slips again, we'll need to flag it up to the portfolio level. Go ahead with Rajesh first.好的，请保持同步。如果再延就要升级到组合层面。先联系 Rajesh。
You: Will do. And finally, TRD-915 is blocked. I need read access to the historical trade database — the DBA ticket's been pending for three business days. I've chased twice in the DBA channel but no movement. I'd appreciate some help unblocking it.好的。最后，TRD-915 被阻塞。我需要历史交易数据库的读权限，DBA 工单已经pending 三个工作日了。在 DBA 频道里催了两次没动静，麻烦帮忙解除阻塞。
Priya (TL): Let me drop a message in the DBA channel — I know their team lead. Apologies, Wei, this shouldn't have sat for three days. We'll get it sorted today.我在DBA 频道里发个消息——我认识他们的负责人。抱歉Wei，不应该卡三天的，今天搞定。
You: Thanks, Priya. Appreciate it. I'll keep the ticket moving as soon as the access is granted.感谢 Priya。辛苦了。权限一开我立刻推进。

3. Key Phrases 关键句型

  • Green Fully on track. 完全按计划推进 The strongest positive status. Use when you're confident of meeting the deadline.
  • At Risk I'm flagging this as at risk. 我把这个标记为 at risk Flag as at risk is the standard verb. Don't just say "this is late" — always flag.
  • Blocked TRD-915 is blocked. TRD-915 被卡了 Blocked = I literally cannot make progress. At risk = I can progress, but the deadline is in danger.
  • Slip They slipped from Wednesday to Monday. 他们从周三延后到周一 Slip (verb) = to miss a deadline. Slipped by 2 days is a common phrase.
  • Escalate Have you escalated to the team lead? 你升级到团队负责人了吗？ Escalate = take the issue to a higher authority. Always offer to escalate, don't just do it unilaterally.
  • Loop in Loop in Sarah in case we need to re-prioritise. 把 Sarah 拉进来（万一要重新排优先级） Loop in = keep someone informed / bring them into the conversation.
  • Chase I've chased twice in the DBA channel. 我在 DBA 频道催了两次 Chase = actively follow up to get something done. Pairs with "chase it up".
  • Unblock I'd appreciate some help unblocking it. 麻烦帮忙解除阻塞 Asking for unblock help is completely normal. Unblock is a verb.
  • Polite Apologies — this shouldn't have sat for three days. 抱歉，不应该卡三天的 A graceful apology when someone is at fault. "This shouldn't have happened" is a common pattern.

4. Vocabulary 词汇表

  • on track adj. 按计划推进 / 在轨 Positive status. Opposite: off track / at risk.
  • at risk adj. 有风险 / 状态变红 May slip. Not yet blocked, but trajectory is concerning.
  • blocked adj. 被阻塞 / 状态卡死 Cannot make progress without external intervention.
  • sprint burndown n. 冲刺燃尽图 Chart showing remaining work over sprint duration. Key SteerCo artifact.
  • SteerCo (Steering Committee) n. 指导委员会 (高层汇报会) Senior leadership meeting to review project status.
  • code-complete adj. 代码完成 (开发完成, 待测试) All code written, not yet tested. Milestone in delivery.
  • stub / mock n. 桩代码 / 模拟实现 A simplified implementation used to test integration before the real one is ready.
  • end-to-end test (e2e) n. 端到端测试 Test that exercises the full flow from start to finish.
  • DBA (Database Administrator) n. 数据库管理员 Owns database access, performance, and schema changes.

5. Grammar Notes 语法点

① Three Status Verbs — 状态动词三件套

Memorize these three structures — they appear in every standup:

  • The ticket is on track. (state)
  • The ticket is at risk because… (state + reason)
  • The ticket is blocked on X. (state + dependency)

② Hedging on Risk

When flagging risk, soften the prediction to avoid over-committing or alarming the team:

  • ❌ "It will slip." (over-confident)
  • ✅ "There's a risk it might slip."
  • ✅ "I'm concerned we could slip if X happens."

③ "I'd appreciate…" — Polite Asks for Help

"I'd appreciate some help unblocking it" is a graceful way to ask for help. Alternatives:

  • "Could you help me unblock this?"
  • "Any chance you could push this with the DBA team?"
  • "Would you mind taking a look?"

6. Cultural Tips 文化/职场 Tips

🚦 The Traffic Light Metaphor 交通灯比喻

HSBC and most banks use a "traffic light" status reporting system. The status you give in standup flows up to SteerCo reports. Be honest — overstating "on track" is a much worse mistake than flagging "at risk" early. Better to surface risk 5 days early than 1 day late.

⚠️ HSBC-Specific Risk Culture
  • Flag early, flag often. If you see risk forming, flag it the same day. Don't wait for the next standup.
  • Never say "I can recover" without a plan. Always pair the optimistic claim with a recovery plan and an owner.
  • Status accountability. Your status updates feed into the portfolio dashboard and the group risk register. Inaccurate statuses are taken seriously.
  • "Pending" is a status, not an excuse. When something is "pending for 3 days", it's your responsibility to chase or escalate — not wait.

7. Practice 练习题

✍️ Practice Drills
Q1. Translate to professional English (口语化):

"PAY-3301 完全按计划，今天会做最后的压力测试。PAY-3310 我标记为 at risk，因为需要等安全团队的渗透测试报告，他们说周五才能给。"

"PAY-3301 is fully on track — I'll be running the final stress test today. PAY-3310 — I'm flagging this as at risk. We're waiting on the pen-test report from the security team, and they say it'll be Friday. I'll keep working on the integration side in the meantime."
Q2. Fill in the right status verb:

"This ticket _____ on track. / This ticket _____ at risk because… / This ticket _____ blocked on the API access."

is / is / is — all use the linking verb "is" because they're states, not actions.
Q3. Make this status update more diplomatic and clear:

"This ticket is late. The DBA team is slow. I can't work."

"TRD-915 is currently blocked on the database access request. The DBA ticket has been pending for three business days. I've chased twice in the DBA channel but haven't had movement. I'd appreciate some help unblocking it — happy to provide more context if needed."
Q4. Role-play scenario:

It's day 9 of a 2-week sprint. You have 4 tickets: (a) 2 are on track; (b) 1 is at risk because the upstream team slipped; (c) 1 is blocked on a credential from a different team. Write a 90-second status update to your PM and TL.

"Sure, here's the latest. Two are on track — PAY-2100 and PAY-2115 are both code-complete, and I'm running the regression tests today. One is at risk — PAY-2120 depends on the upstream payments team's API stub, which slipped from yesterday to next Tuesday. I'm continuing on the consumer side, but the e2e test can't start until the stub is ready. I'll reach out to the upstream lead today to see if we can unblock earlier. And one is blocked — PAY-2130 needs a new OAuth credential from the platform team, the ticket's been pending since Monday. I've chased once in the channel. Could someone help escalate? Once unblocked, I can finish all four by sprint end."
