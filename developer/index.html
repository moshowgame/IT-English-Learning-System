Welcome, Developer / 开发者你好

As a developer at HSBC, you communicate daily with team members in the UK, Hong Kong, mainland China, and India. This section focuses on the spoken English scenarios you actually face — from sprint standups to production incidents, from polite disagreement to vendor calls.

面向汇丰开发者，本节聚焦你日常真正遇到的口语场景，从Sprint站会到生产事故， 从礼貌表达不同意见到与海外Vendor沟通。每篇文章都包含真实场景、英文对话、中文翻译、关键句型与练习。

What you'll learn / 你将学到

  • ✅ Express progress clearly: on track / at risk / blocked

  • ✅ Politely disagree and push back on technical decisions
  • ✅ Communicate during production incidents under pressure
  • ✅ Use HSBC-specific banking and compliance terminology correctly

How to Use This Section / 学习建议

💡 Daily Practice Tip

Pick one article per day. Read the dialogue aloud twice, then try to paraphrase the key phrases from memory. After a week, you'll have the core patterns for that module locked in.

⚠️ For Real HSBC Work

Always confirm banking-specific terms with your local compliance team — terminology can vary between regions (UK, HK, mainland China, India). The terms here reflect common usage.
